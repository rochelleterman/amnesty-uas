"EXTRA 51/93 - South Africa (Venda): death penalty: Frans Netshirombeni, Wilson Nelukalo 
 EXTRA 51/93 - South Africa (Venda): death penalty: Frans Netshirombeni, Wilson Nelukalo | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 There is concern that the authorities in the nominally independent ""homeland"" of Venda intend to resume executions on Thursday 27 May 1993. The two men named above have been under sentence of death since 4 June 1990. In November 1992 the prisoners had their appeal dismissed, and their clemency petitions were dismissed in May 1993. "
