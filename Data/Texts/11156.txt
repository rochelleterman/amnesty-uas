"India: Further information on fear of torture / possible prisoner of conscience: Shabir Ahmad Shah 
 India: Further information on fear of torture / possible prisoner of conscience: Shabir Ahmad Shah | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Shabir Shah was released unconditionally from detention on the evening of 21 May 1995. He is reported to be well and to have attended a political rally on 22 May. There is still concern however that he may have been held in detention for nine days for political reasons."
