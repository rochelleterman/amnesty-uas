"Indonesia/East Timor: Fear for safety / possible ""disappearance"": Jacob Martins Reis fernandes 
 Indonesia/East Timor: Fear for safety / possible ""disappearance"": Jacob Martins Reis fernandes | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is seriously concerned for the      safety of Jacob Martins Reis Fernandes, head of the       Hatiola sub-district of Ermera district, East Timor. He   went into hiding in April 1999 following an attempt on    his life. Some reports indicate that he may have been     arrested on 17 May 1999. He has not been seen since.
