"Nepal: Further information on Fear for safety/fear of torture/possible 'disappearance ' 
 Nepal: Further information on Fear for safety/fear of torture/possible 'disappearance ' | Amnesty International 
 Bandhu Dev Pandey was released on 1 December. The reasons for his arrest and the place of his detention are unknown. He was reportedly arrested by four security personnel in plain clothes who came to the office of his printing press company in Naradevi, Kathmandu on 16 October.
