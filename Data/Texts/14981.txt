"Turkey: Fear for Safety/Torture and Ill-treatment, Sefik Yildirim 
 Turkey: Fear for Safety/Torture and Ill-treatment, Sefik Yildirim



 | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is seriously concerned for the safety of Sefik Yildirim. He has been arrested twice over the course of one week and is reportedly being held in incommunicado detention at a gendarmerie station in Diyarbak r province, southeast Turkey, where he is at risk of torture and ill-treatment.
