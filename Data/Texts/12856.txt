"Pakistan: Further information on Fear for safety, Akhtar Baloch. 
 Pakistan: Further information on Fear for safety, Akhtar Baloch. | Amnesty International 
 At around midnight on 25 March, Akhtar Baloch, a prominent member of the Human Rights Commission of Pakistan [HRCP] was released. He had been abducted by armed men two days earlier, as he returned from the annual general meeting of the HRCP. .
