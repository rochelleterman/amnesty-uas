"Indonesia / East Timor: Fear for safety: 36 families 
 Indonesia / East Timor: Fear for safety: 36 families | Amnesty International 
 Thirty-six families are being arbitrarily detained by the Indonesian Armed Forces in Dotik village (East Timor). They are believed to be detained as a means of discovering the identities of the perpetrators of an attack on a local military base on 9 November 1998.
