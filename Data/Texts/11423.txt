 Indonesia: Possible ""disappearance"" / fear for safety	 | Amnesty International 
 The six people named in this document are at grave risk of torture and ill-treatment after they were detained by police in Nanggroe Aceh Darussalam Province (NAD). At least five of them are human rights defenders, who are believed to have been arrested solely for their peaceful expression of their political beliefs, and their work in defence of human rights
