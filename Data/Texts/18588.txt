"Syria: Further information on fear of torture/Incommunicado detention/health concern/unfair trial/possible prisoner of conscience. Riad Drar al-Hamood (m) 
 Syria: Further information on fear of torture/Incommunicado detention/health concern/unfair trial/possible prisoner of conscience.   Riad Drar al-Hamood (m) | Amnesty International 
 Civil society activist Riad Drar al-Hamood, who is facing trial before the Supreme State Security Court (SSSC) on charges of ""inciting sectarian strife"", belonging to a ""secret organization"" and ""publishing false news"", has had his case adjourned until 5 March 2006. He was first brought before the court on 4 December 2005. His case has now been adjourned twice.
