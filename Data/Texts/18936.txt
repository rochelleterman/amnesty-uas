 Further information on EXTRA 102/93 (MDE 28/20/93, 25 November) - Algeria: death penalty / fear of execution: Didier-Roger Guyan (French national), and at least 150 others on death row under threat of execution | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 There have been reports that Algeria's Higher State Committee has decided to suspend the executions of people condemned to death under emergency ""anti-terrorist"" laws. However, until this decision is confirmed, there is concern that Didier-Roger Guyan and at least 150 other people on death row remain under threat of execution."
