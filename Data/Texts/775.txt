 Ethiopia: Fear of ill-treatment or torture/incommunicado  detention | Amnesty International 
 Around 60 people, including the named teachers, were arrested on 9 April in western Oromia Region, west of the capital, Addis Ababa. They are reportedly detained incommunicado in a police detention centre in Ambo Palace and Amnesty International fears they may be tortured there.
