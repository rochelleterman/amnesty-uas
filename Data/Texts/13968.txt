 Germany: Further information on fear of imminent refoulement: Fariz Simsek, rejected Kurdish asylum-seeker | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 On 31 January, following an application to the Federal Constitutional Court in Karlsruhe, a temporary injunction was issued halting Fariz Simsek's deportation. The Federal Constitutional Court is due to deliver its final decision on the deportation in a few days time."
