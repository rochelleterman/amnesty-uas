"Indonesia: Further information on fear of torture/ill-treatment 
 Indonesia: Further information on fear of torture/ill-treatment | Amnesty International 
 Yulius Petege, Yahia Pigome, Ben Kotouki and new name Andy Gobai: It is now believed that the six people taken into police custody following disturbances in Irian Jaya, on 22 August 1997, have all been released. The six were held for four hours and then released.
