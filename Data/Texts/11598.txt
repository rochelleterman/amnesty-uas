"Indonesia: Further information on possible ""disappearance"", Hubertus Wresman 
 Indonesia: Further information on possible ""disappearance"", Hubertus Wresman | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International has received information which indicates that Hubertus Wresman is being held by Kopassus officers stationed near his village. Hubertus Wresman is believed to be held incommunicado and is at high risk of torture or ill-treatment.
