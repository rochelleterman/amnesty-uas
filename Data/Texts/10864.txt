"China: Further information on Fear for safety/Incommunicado detention, Liu Di 
 China: Further information on Fear for safety/Incommunicado detention, Liu Di | Amnesty International 
 After just over one year in detention, Liu Di was reportedly released on bail on 28 November. However, she apparently remains under investigation and could still face trial. According to media reports, she has been warned not to speak to foreign journalists and her bail conditions stipulate that she must not travel outside Beijing.
