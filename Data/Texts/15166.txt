"EXTRA 56/94 - Turkey: fear of torture: Ali Ekber Kaya 
 EXTRA 56/94 - Turkey: fear of torture: Ali Ekber Kaya | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Ali Ekber Kaya, a civil servant with Tunceli Council and president of the Human Rights Association in Tunceli, was detained on the night of 22 September 1994. He is being held incommunicado at Tunceli Police headquarters and the police have acknowledged his detention. There is great concern for his safety."
