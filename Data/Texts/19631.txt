 Sudan: Further Information on UA 330/07: Fear for safety/ Incommunicado detention: Ammar Najm Eddine Jalak (m). | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Ammar Najm Eddine Jalak was released without charge at from the headquarters of the National Intelligence and Security Service (NISS) around 1am on 22 January 2008. He had been detained at Dabak Prison, north of Khartoum."
