 Sri Lanka: Ill-treatment/ Fear of Torture/ Medical concern/ Arbitrary detention | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Writer and publisher Vadivel Jasikaran  and his wife Valarmathi Jasikaran have been held without charge by the police Terrorist Investigation Division (TID),  in the capital, Colombo, since 6 March 2008. They are at risk of torture or other ill-treatment. According to local sources Valarmathi Jasikaran has not received the regular medical attention she requires following a stomach operation. "
