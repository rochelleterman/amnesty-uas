"USA (Florida): Further information on death penalty 
 USA (Florida): Further information on death penalty | Amnesty International 
 On 26 January 2000, less than two weeks after the Florida legislature voted to introduce lethal injection as an     execution method, Governor Jeb Bush ordered the           executions of Terry Sims and Anthony Bryan to go ahead.   They are scheduled to be executed at 7am on February 23   and 24 respectively.
