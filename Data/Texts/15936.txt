"Ukraine: Further information on death penalty: Maksim Artsyuk 
 Ukraine: Further information on death penalty: Maksim Artsyuk | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International fears that the above may have been executed in August 1996, despite the fact that his family has not been officially informed of his fate. He was transferred to Dnipropetrovsk prison on 27 August and it is believed that death row prisoners are usually executed only one or two days after their transferral to this prison.
