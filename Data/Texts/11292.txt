"Further information on UA 354/94 (ASA 20/35/94, 26 September) - India: fear for safety: Chakmas and Hajongs in Arunachal Pradesh 
 Further information on UA 354/94 (ASA 20/35/94, 26 September) - India: fear for safety: Chakmas and Hajongs in Arunachal Pradesh | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 The deadline of 30 September 1994 set by the All Arunachal Pradesh Students Union (AAPSU) for Chakmas and Hajongs in Arunachal Pradesh to leave the state, passed peacefully. There were no reports of killings of forcible expulsions."
