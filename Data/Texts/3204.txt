"Uganda: Jean-Francois David, Congolese citizen 
 Uganda: Jean-Francois David, Congolese citizen | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is concerned for the health of Jean-Francois David, a Congolese citizen and a member of  the Rassemblement congolais pour la democratie, who is in the detention of the Ugandan armed forces, and fears that his life may be at risk unless he receives urgent medical attention.
