 Indonesia: Fear of torture. | Amnesty International 
 Elias Tabuni and four other men were detained by the military in the town of Wamena in Papua Province on 6 April. They are being held at the Jayawijaya Military District Command (Kodim) in Wamena and have reportedly been beaten.
