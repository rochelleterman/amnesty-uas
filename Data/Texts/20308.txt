 Mexico: Fears detainee has been disappeared | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Jethro Ramsés Sánchez Santana was detained on 1 May in Cuernavaca, Morelos state, Mexico, by Cuernavaca Municipal Police, then passed to members of the Mexican Army. He has not been seen since and the authorities have failed to provide information on his whereabouts. His family are concerned that he is a victim of enforced disappearance.   "
