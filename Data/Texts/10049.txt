 USA (Oklahoma): Death penalty/Legal concern, Jay Wesley Neill | Amnesty International 
 Jay Wesley Neill is scheduled to be executed in Oklahoma  on 12 December 2002. He was sentenced to death for the    murder of four people committed during a bank robbery in  Geronimo, in southwest Oklahoma, in 1984. He was aged 19  at the time.
