 United States of America : Death penalty / Legal concern, Louis Jones. | Amnesty International 
 Former soldier Louis Jones is scheduled to be executed by the US Government on 18 March 2003 in the US Penitentiary in Terre Haute, Indiana. He was sentenced to death in     October 1995 for the murder of a white woman, Tracie Joy  McBride, eight months earlier.
