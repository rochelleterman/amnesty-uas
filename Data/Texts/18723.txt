"Syria: Further information on: Prisoners of conscience/incommunicado detention/fear of torture and ill-treatment 
 Syria: Further information on: Prisoners of conscience/incommunicado detention/fear of torture and ill-treatment | Amnesty International 
 Nizar Ristnawi is now being held in 'Adra prison, just outside the capital Damascus, where he was transferred to from Sednaya prison. He received a visit from his wife at the beginning of August and is reported to be in good health. He is being held in a large room with other detainees, who are also being held for political reasons.
