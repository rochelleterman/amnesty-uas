 Turkey: Fear of torture / fear of ""disappearance"": Imran Akdogan and others: | Amnesty International 
 On 9 December 1997 the police arrived at Imran Akdogan's home and occupied the flat for two days, monitoring all visitors and incoming phone calls, and not allowing the family outside. Then they took Imran with them. Since he is in police custody and it is not known whether his detention has been acknowledged. AI fears that he may be subjected to torture during interrogation.
