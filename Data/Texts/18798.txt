"United Arab Emirates (UAE): Fear of imminent execution: Zad Khan Shah 
 United Arab Emirates (UAE): Fear of imminent execution: Zad Khan Shah | Amnesty International 
 Zad Khan Shah, a Pakistani national, has reportedly had his death sentence upheld by the Federal Supreme Court in al-'Ain. He was convicted of the murder of an Indian national in December 1993. His case has now been referred to the President for ratification.
