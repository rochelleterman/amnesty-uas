"Russian Federation: Fear for Safety. 
 Russian Federation:  Fear for Safety. | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is concerned for the safety of all national and ethnic minorities in Russia as the anniversary of Adolf Hitler's birth on 20 April approaches. In previous years, this anniversary has been marked by an escalation of racially motivated harassment and violence.
