"Mexico: Extrajudicial execution / journalists' safety: Benjamin Flores Gonzalez, journalist 
 Mexico: Extrajudicial execution / journalists' safety: Benjamin Flores Gonzalez, journalist | Amnesty International 
 There is grave concern at the extrajudicial execution of Benjamin Flores Gonzalez. He had received several death threats prior to his murder as a result of the publication in his newspaper of articles concerning the alleged involvement of members of the local government with powerful drug cartels.
