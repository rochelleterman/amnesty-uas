"Egypt: Further information on: Incommunicado detention/Fear of torture or ill-treatment: Omar Yahya Youssef (m) 
 Egypt: Further information on: Incommunicado detention/Fear of torture or ill-treatment: Omar Yahya Youssef (m)
 | Amnesty International 
 Egyptian national Omar Yahya Youssef, aged 23, was released from detention in Egypt on 23 June. He had been detained for six days, and was reportedly interrogated during detention. He has now joined his family in the city of Alexandria.
