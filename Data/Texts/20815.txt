 Futher Information: Chinese activist sentenced to nine years | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Chen Wei, an activist detained in February 2011 during a crackdown to prevent ""Jasmine Revolution-inspired"" demonstrations in China, was sentenced to nine years in prison on 23 December 2011 on charges of “inciting subversion of state power”. Ding Mao was put under residential surveillance on 1 December 2011. Liang Haiyi remains detained incommunicado."
