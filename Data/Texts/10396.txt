"Bhutan: Cruel, Inhuman and Degrading Treatment / health concern: Kinzang Chozom (f) 
 Bhutan: Cruel, Inhuman and Degrading Treatment / health concern: Kinzang Chozom (f) | Amnesty International 
 Kinzang Chozom, who is in the final stage of pregnancy, has been held in incommunicado detention since 17 October 1997. She is not receiving medical care and is being denied access to her daughter.
