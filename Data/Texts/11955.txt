"Further information on UA 143/94 (ASA 23/03/94, 12 April) - Cambodia: arbitrary killings / fear for safety: Ethnic Vietnamese villagers in Kandal Province 
 Further information on UA 143/94 (ASA 23/03/94, 12 April) - Cambodia: arbitrary killings / fear for safety: Ethnic Vietnamese villagers in Kandal Province | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Seven men were reportedly arrested for the killing of 13 ethnic Vietnamese villagers in Kandal province in April 1994. However, thery were released on 2 June, reportedly because of lack of evidence. AI is calling for a renewed investigation into the killings."
