"Colombia: Fear for safety: 
 Colombia: Fear for safety: | Amnesty International 
 Mario Humberto Calixto, human rights activist, and members of Comite de Derechos Humanos de Sabana de Torres: On 23 December 1997, two armed men attempted to abduct Mario Humberto Calixto from his home. He narrowly escaped and remains in hiding. AI is concerned for his safety and for the safety of other members of the Comite de Derechos Humanos de Sabana de Torres.
