 Turkey: Further information on prisoner of conscience / fear of arrest as POC | Amnesty International 
 Akin Birdal, President of Turkish Human Rights Association; Mehmet Ihsan Arslan, vice-President of Mazlum-Der (human rights organization) and Fetullah Erbas, Member of Parliament: Amnesty International has been informed that Akin Birdal and Mehmet Ihsan Arslan have been released today, 6 September. There is no further information on the possible lifting of Fetullah Erbas' parliamentary immunity
