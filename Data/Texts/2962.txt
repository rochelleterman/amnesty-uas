"Sudan: Further information on Incommunicado detention / Fear of torture or ill-treatment, Adam Abdel Hamid Adam 
 Sudan: Further information on Incommunicado detention / Fear of torture or ill-treatment, Adam Abdel Hamid Adam | Amnesty International 
 Trainee advocate, Adam Abdel Hamid Adam was reportedly released from the Political Section of Kober prison on 24 July. He had been transferred from the offices of the National Security Agency in Khartoum to Kober prison on an unknown date. He had been held in incommunicado detention for more than four months, since his arrest on 3 March and has not been charged with any offence.
