"Uzbekistan: Further information on ""disappearance"" 
 Uzbekistan: Further information on ""disappearance"" | Amnesty International 
 Abduvali Mirzoyev, Islamic preacher, Ramazan Matkarimov, his assistant: There is still no news of the whereabouts of the above who ""disappeared"" on 29 August 1995 from the airport in Tashkent, reportedly after being detained by people believed to have been officers of the National Security Service (SNB).
