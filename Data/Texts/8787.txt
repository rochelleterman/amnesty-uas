 USA (Texas): Further Information on Death penalty / Legal concern. Scott Louis Panetti (m), white, aged 45 | Amnesty International 
 A federal judge has granted Scott Panetti a 60-day stay of execution in order that his competency for execution - whether he understands the reason for and reality of his punishment - can be examined. The Texas Board of Pardons and Paroles had already rejected clemency by 15 votes to one.
