"East Timor: Incommunicado detention 
 East Timor: Incommunicado detention | Amnesty International 
 Jose da Costa Soares, Agostinho Goncalves and Gil Ximenes: AI is concerned for the safety of three men arrested in recent days in East Timor. They are believed to be in custody at Police Resort, Dili, the capital of East Timor, and so far are thought to have been denied access to independent legal counsel.
