"USA (Ohio): Further information on Death penalty: Jay D. Scott 
 USA (Ohio): Further information on Death penalty: Jay D. Scott | Amnesty International 
 The Ohio Supreme Court blocked Jay Scott's execution about an hour before it was due to be carried out in order for more time to assess the issue of Scott's mental health. If Jay Scott is found competent for execution, the state would ask the Ohio Supreme Court to set a new date.
