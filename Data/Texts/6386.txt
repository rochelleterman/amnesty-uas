"UA 169/93 - Guatemala: threats / intimidation: Amilcar David Montejo Garcia 
 UA 169/93 - Guatemala: threats / intimidation: Amilcar David Montejo Garcia | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Amilcar David Montejo Garcia, a member of the Consultative Council of the Coordination of Secondary School Students (CEEM), has been threatened and intimidated during May 1993. He had recently made public statements on television questioning government policy. "
