"EXTRA 50/93 - USA (Texas): death penalty: John Selvage 
 EXTRA 50/93 - USA (Texas): death penalty: John Selvage | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 John Selvage, black, who has a history of mental illness, is scheduled to be executed in Texas on 8 June 1993. He was sentenced to death in February 1980 for his role in the murder of a police officer in July 1979. According to his current attorneys there is also considerable new evidence which shows that John Selvage may not have been the actual killer. "
