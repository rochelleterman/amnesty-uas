 Iran: Futher information on Death penalty / fear of imminent execution / possible prisoner of conscience: Morteza Firouzi | Amnesty International 
  The death sentence against Morteza Firouzi, editor of the English-language daily newspaper Iran News has been returned to the court of the first instance for re-examination. He is no longer therefore believed to be at risk of imminent execution.
