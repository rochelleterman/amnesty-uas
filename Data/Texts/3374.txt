"Democratic Republic of Congo: Prisoners of conscience /unfair trial. 
 Democratic Republic of Congo:   Prisoners of conscience     /unfair trial. | Amnesty International 
 Ten people are being detained in the southeastern town of Lubumbashi for their perceived criticism of the authorities. Although they are all civilians, they may face trial on contrived criminal charges before a notoriously unfair military tribunal.
