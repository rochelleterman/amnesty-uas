"Turkey: Further information on fear of torture: Sethettin Elci 
 Turkey: Further information on fear of torture: Sethettin Elci | Amnesty International 
 Sethettin Elci, the 19-year-old who was detained on 7 June 1996, was released without charge on 13 June. He was allegedly tortured in detention, including by the so-called ""Palestinian"" hanging.
