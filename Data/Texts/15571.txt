"Russian Federation/Chechnya: Further information on 'Disappearance'/Fear of torture/ Fear of extra-judicial execution 
 Russian Federation/Chechnya:  Further information on      'Disappearance'/Fear of torture/ Fear of extra-judicial execution | Amnesty International 
 The wife of Said-Magomed Imakaev, who ""disappeared"" from his home in the village of Novye Atagi on 2 June, has described two members of the Russian security forces who detained him and five other men. Tracing these soldiers could lead to information about the fate and whereabouts of the ""disappeared"" men - Said-Magomed Imakaev, Said-Khusein Imakaev, Ruslan Utsaev, Movsar Taisumov, Idris Abdulazimov and Aslambek.
