"Colombia: Further information on fear for safety 
 Colombia: Further information on fear for safety | Amnesty International 
 Misael Domico, indigenous leader; four other unidentified indigenous people: Misael Domico, who was abducted by armed men on 28 May 1997, was released at midday on 30 May. There is no further information on the other four indigenous people who were reportedly abducted by the same armed group on 29 May.
