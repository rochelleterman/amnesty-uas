"Jamaica: Death penalty 
 Jamaica: Death penalty | Amnesty International 
 The government of Jamaica has scheduled Peter Blaine, Milton Montique and Dalton Daley to be hanged on 26 and 27 November 1998.  These executions have been scheduled despite all three men having petitions pending before an international human rights body.
