 Colombia: Afro-descendent men forcibly disappeared | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Francisco Pineda and Everto González, two members of the community council of Caracolí, north-west Colombia, have been subjected to enforced disappearance by paramilitaries. Amnesty International fears their lives and the lives of other members of the Afro-descendant community may be at risk."
