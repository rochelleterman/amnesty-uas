"Colombia: Further Information on Fear for safety/ Death threats 
 Colombia: Further Information on Fear for safety/ Death threats | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is concerned for the safety of Ademir Luna, who works as a journalist for the non-governmental human rights organization CREDHOS. This follows an attack against another man who was mistaken for him and his home being watched by unknown individuals in the city of Barrancabermeja, Santander Department.
