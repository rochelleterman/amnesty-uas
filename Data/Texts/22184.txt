 Iran: Further information on imminent execution / legal concern: Mohammad Feda’i | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Juvenile offender Mohammad Feda’i was granted a one-month reprieve on 10 June 2008, the day before he was scheduled to be executed, to allow time for his family to negotiate with the family of the boy he was convicted of killing, and to agree on financial compensation in exchange for pardoning him."
