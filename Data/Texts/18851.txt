 Algeria: Torture / Fear of further torture   Said Zaoui | Amnesty International 
 Said Zaoui, a 70-year-old former member of a banned Islamist organization has reportedly been tortured while in security force detention. Amnesty International believes that he is at risk of further torture and fears for his safety.
