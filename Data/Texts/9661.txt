"USA: Further information on death penalty 
 USA: Further information on death penalty | Amnesty International 
 Thomas Mason, John Burks, Paul Nuncio and Shaka Sankofa   (formerly Gary Graham) were executed on 12, 14, 15 and 22 June 2000 respectively.  Texas now accounts for 23 of the 50 executions carried out in the USA this year.  Since    the USA resumed executions in 1977, 648 prisoners have    been put to death nationwide, 222 of them in Texas.
