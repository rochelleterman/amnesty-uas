"Cambodia: Further information on fear for safety 
 Cambodia: Further information on fear for safety | Amnesty International 
 Chao Sambath, Nhek Bun Chhay, Por Bun Sreu MP, Serey Kosal: Amnesty International has learned that Chao Sambath is already dead. It is believed that he was executed in custody. Fears for the safety of those in custody following the coup have been greatly heightened.
