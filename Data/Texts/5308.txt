"Colombia: Fear for Safety/Death Threats 
 Colombia: Fear for Safety/Death Threats | Amnesty International 
 Trade unionist Carmen Pungo and her cousin Ricaurte Yanten Pungo were reportedly killed by army-backed paramilitaries on 2 September. They were abducted on 2 September near their homes and allegedly taken to meet with local paramilitary leaders. Other trade unionists in the region are feared to be in danger.
