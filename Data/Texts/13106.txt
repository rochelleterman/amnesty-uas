 Philippines: Further information on ""disappearance"" / fear of ill-treatment: Romeo Cortez, peasant leader | Amnesty International 
 Romeo Cortez is still missing. He has not been seen since he was abducted on 1 April 1997 in San Roque, Pampanga province by three unidentified armed men.
