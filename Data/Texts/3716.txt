 Bolivia: Fear for safety/death threats | Amnesty International 
 An association of cattle ranchers and foresters has attacked the offices of a non-governmental organisation that represents indigenous and peasant farmers seeking their land rights, and threatened to burn lawyer Cliver Rochas alive. Amnesty International believes his life is in grave danger.
