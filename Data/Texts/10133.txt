"Venezuela: Further information on fear of torture 
 Venezuela: Further information on fear of torture | Amnesty International 
 Yuraima Lara (f), student, member of left-wing organization Bandera Roja:  A full investigation has been opened by the judicial authorities in Caracas into the arbitrary detention  of Yuraima Lara on 16 October 1997. She is reported to be recovering well from the brutal treatment she suffered during her detention.
