"Bangladesh: Further Information on Torture. 
 Bangladesh: Further Information on Torture. | Amnesty International 
 Dr. Mohiuddin Khan Alamgir remains in detention despite   repeated orders for his release on bail by the courts.    Bahauddin Nasim also remains in detention. Despite        credible reports that he had been severely tortured while in detention, the government has failed to institute an   independent and impartial investigation into these        allegations.
