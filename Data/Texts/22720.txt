 Syria: Further Information on UA 268/06 - Prisoner of conscience/ medical concern: Dr ‘Aref Dalilah  | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 ‘Aref Dalilah, a prisoner of conscience who has served half of a 10-year sentence in solitary confinement, is gravely ill with deep-vein thrombosis in one leg and a blood clot in his lungs. He may die if he is not allowed to be admitted to hospital immediately. "
