"Further information on EXTRA 41/94 (EUR 63/18/94, 29 July) - Bosnia-Herzegovina: ill-treatment: Civilian prisoners detained in Velika Kladusa, western Bosnia-Herzegovina 
 Further information on EXTRA 41/94 (EUR 63/18/94, 29 July) - Bosnia-Herzegovina: ill-treatment: Civilian prisoners detained in Velika Kladusa, western Bosnia-Herzegovina | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 The latest reports from western Bosnia-Herzegovina indicate that the forces loyal to Fikret Abdic are on the verge of surrender to Bosnian Government forces. The defeat of the forces loyal to Fikret Abdic should lead to the release of civilian prisoners in Velika Kladusa. AI will monitor further developments in the area."
