"Brazil: Apparent extrajudicial execution / fear for safety 
 Brazil: Apparent extrajudicial execution / fear for safety | Amnesty International 
 Francisco Gilson Nogueira de Carvalho, aged 34, lawyer, human rights defender, Roberto Monte, human rights defender, Luiz Gonzaga Dantas, human rights defender; unnamed witness: On 20 October 1996, Francisco Gilson Nogueira de Carvalho was shot dead in Natal by six men. Amnesty International believes that he was killed because of his work as a lawyer committed to exposing the involvement of members of the Rio Grande do Norte civil police in ""death squad"" activities. AI is concerned for both the safety of the unnamed witness to the killing and also of Carvalho's colleagues at the Centre for Human Rights and Collective Memory.
