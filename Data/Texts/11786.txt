"Indonesia: Further information on incommunicado detention: Andi Arief 
 Indonesia: Further information on incommunicado detention: Andi Arief | Amnesty International 
 Andi Arief who was kidnapped and detained incommunicado from 28 March to 17 April 1998, has now been granted access to a lawyer and is reportedly being treated well.. It remains unclear as to what charges he is being held under.
