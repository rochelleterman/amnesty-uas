"USA (Montana): Death penalty: David Thomas Dawson (m) 
 USA (Montana): Death penalty: David Thomas Dawson (m) | Amnesty International 
 David Dawson, white, aged 49, is scheduled to be executed in Montana on 11 August 2006. He was sentenced to death in April 1987 for the murder of Monica and David Rodstein and their 11-year-old son Andrew at a motel in Billings, Montana, in 1986. If the execution goes ahead, it would be the first judicial killing in the state of Montana for more than eight years.
