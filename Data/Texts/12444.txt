 Nepal: Further Information on Fear for safety/possible ""disappearance""/medical concern | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International has received reports that Naresh Maharjan killed himself on 9 January, one month after he was released. He appears to have taken his own life to escape threats and harassment by the security forces. There is no further information on the whereabouts of Bhagirath Kharel and Maha Prasad Angai.
