"Turkey: Fear of forcible return / fear for safety, Farideh Sohrabi Cheghakaboudi 
 Turkey: Fear of forcible return / fear for safety, Farideh Sohrabi Cheghakaboudi | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International fears that Farideh Sohrabi Cheghakaboudi, a Kurdish Iranian woman currently detained in Turkey, is in imminent danger of being forcibly returned to Iran. She would be at risk of arbitrary detention, torture or ill-treatment in Iran as a result of her political activism.
