 Libya: Libyan prisoner tortured | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Two brothers, who may be prisoners of conscience held solely for their interest in Amazigh culture, were arrested on 16 December 2010 in Tripoli. One has reported being tortured by the Libyan security forces in detention. Both are at risk of torture in Jdaida Prison where they are held.  "
