 Nepal: Fear for safety/ possible ""disappearance"" | Amnesty International 
 Man Bahadur Shrestha and Surya Bastakoti were reportedly arrested in Kathmandu municipality on 11 December 2003, and 22 March 2004 respectively. Their whereabouts remain unknown, and Amnesty International is concerned that they may have ""disappeared"".
