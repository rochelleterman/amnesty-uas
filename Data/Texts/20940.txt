 China: Uighur activist alleges torture  | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Ablikim Abdiriyim, a prisoner of conscience and son of Uighur activist Rebiya Kadeer, is being tortured in prison, in the Xinjiang Uighur Autonomous Region (XUAR) of China, according to his family. His health has deteriorated since he was placed in solitary confinement on 3 November 2010, and his family are seriously concerned for his well-being. "
