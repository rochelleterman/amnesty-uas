"USA (Delaware): Death penalty / legal concern: Justin L. Burrell 
 USA (Delaware): Death penalty / legal concern: Justin L. Burrell | Amnesty International 
 At a trial due to begin on 16 August 1999, the            prosecution will seek a death sentence against Justin     Burrell for a murder he is accused of committing when he  was 17 years old. International law forbids the use of    the death penalty for crimes committed by under           18-year-olds.
