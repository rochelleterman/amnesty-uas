"Colombia: Further information on Fear for safety/ death threats 
 Colombia: Further information on Fear for safety/ death threats | Amnesty International 
 A video apparently made by a new paramilitary group has identified journalist Hollman Morris and three others as ""military targets"", accusing them of being members or agents of guerrilla forces. The video amounts to a death threat, and Amnesty International fears that their lives are in grave danger.
