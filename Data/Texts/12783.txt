"Nepal: Further information on Fear of torture or ill-treatment/possible 'disappearance', Madhab Ghemere 
 Nepal: Further information on Fear of torture or ill-treatment/possible 'disappearance', Madhab Ghemere | Amnesty International 
 Madhab Ghemere was released from detention on 9 April. He had reportedly been arrested on 29 September 2003 by security personnel in civilian dress from outside the school where he worked in Kathmandu.
