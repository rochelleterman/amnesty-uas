"Pakistan: Death penalty/imminent execution, Manzoor Ali, Abdul Ghafoor 
 Pakistan: Death penalty/imminent execution, Manzoor Ali,  Abdul Ghafoor | Amnesty International 
 Two brothers convicted of murder are scheduled to be executed on 16 August. Manzoor Ali and Abdul Ghafoor have exhausted all their appeals, and will be hanged unless the heirs of their victims agree to pardon them.
