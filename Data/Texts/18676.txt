"Syria: Further information: Incommunicado detention/fear of torture/""disappearance""/medical concern, Aktham Nu'aysa 
 Syria: Further information: Incommunicado detention/fear of torture/""disappearance""/medical concern, Aktham Nu'aysa | Amnesty International 
 Aktham Nu'aysa was acquitted of all charges by the Supreme State Security Court (SSSC) on 26 June 2005. He was released on bail on 16 August 2004 pending his trial, which was attended by several representatives of foreign embassies in Syria.
