 Mexico: Further Information on Fear for safety | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Manuel Ponce Rosas and Raúl Lucas Lucía were found dead late at night on 20 February in Tecoanapa municipality, Guerrero State. Their families, members of the Organization for the Future of Mixtec Indigenous Peoples (Organización para el Futuro de los Pueblos Mixtecos, OFPM) and other human rights activists supporting the families may be at risk of reprisals."
