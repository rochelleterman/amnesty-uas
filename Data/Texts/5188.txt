 Colombia: Apparent extrajudicial execution / fear for safety: Fernando Carillo Villegas Como, civilians in the Vereda La Paz | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 There are serious fears for the inhabitants of the hamlet of La Paz following reports of the extrajudicial execution of Fernando Carillo Villegas Como by members of a paramilitary group who also threatened other local people. Many families have reportedly left the area since the killing and threats."
