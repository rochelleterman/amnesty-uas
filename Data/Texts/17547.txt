"Iraq: Death penalty/fear of imminent execution: Muhannad Hamza Matar (m) 
 Iraq: Death penalty/fear of imminent execution: Muhannad Hamza Matar (m)

 | Amnesty International 
 Muhannad Hamza Matar, aged 26, was sentenced to death by the Central Criminal Court of Iraq (CCCI) on 21 November 2006. He is now at risk of imminent execution, though he can still seek a pardon from the president.
