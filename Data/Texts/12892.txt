"Pakistan: Death penalty: Mutabar Khan 
 Pakistan: Death penalty: Mutabar Khan | Amnesty International 
 Mutabar Khan is being held in a death cell in Haripur prison, North West Frontier Province, Pakistan, after being sentenced to death for murder in 1998. He was reportedly 16 years old at the time of the crime, but is not able to prove his age. Pakistan is bound by national and international law forbidding the execution of those who were under the age of 18 at the time of the crime.
