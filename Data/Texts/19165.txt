"Tunisia: Further information on human rights defender / fear for safety: Sihem Ben Sedrine 
 Tunisia:  Further information on human rights defender / fear for safety: Sihem   Ben Sedrine | Amnesty International 
 Journalist Sihem Ben Sedrine was arrested on 26 June, when she returned from a two-week tour of Europe. In the past she has been beaten in police custody, and Amnesty International fears for her safety.
