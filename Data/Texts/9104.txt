"USA (Missouri):  Further information on death penalty on James Henry Hampton 
 USA (Missouri):  Further information on death penalty on James Henry Hampton | Amnesty International 
 James Hampton was executed in Missouri in the first few   minutes of 22 March 2000. James Hampton becomes the 26th  person executed in the USA this year and the 624th since  judicial killing resumed in the United States in 1977.    Forty-two of those executions  have been carried out in   Missouri.
