"Jordan: Further information on Death Penalty/Torture/Unfair trial 
 Jordan: Further information on Death Penalty/Torture/Unfair trial | Amnesty International 
 Yaser Muhammad Ahmad Salamah Abu Shannar was executed at  Swaqa Prison on 4 December, in connection with the        killing of Jordanian diplomat Na'ib 'Umran al-Ma'aytah in Beirut in 1994.  Yaser Muhammad Ahmad Salamah Abu Shannar had reportedly denied the charges against him and claimed to have confessed under torture during his interrogation.
