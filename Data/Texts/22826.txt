 Syria: Further information: Syrian rearrested and held incommunicado: Muhammad Yassin Al Hamwi | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Muhammad Yassin Al Hamwi, who was among four Syrian men originally detained in September 2011, was rearrested on 4 May, having been released on 25 February 2012. Two others, Muhammad Muhammad Al Hamwi and Ahmad Kuraitem, were released on 28 March. An acquaintance of theirs, Abd al-Akram al-Sakka, remains in detention. "
