"Iran: Further information on Medical concern/Fear for safety/incommunicado detention, Akbar Ganji 
 Iran: Further information on Medical concern/Fear for safety/incommunicado detention, Akbar Ganji | Amnesty International 
 Akbar Ganji was rushed to the Milad Hospital in Tehran on 17 July. He had been on hunger strike for 37 days, and so was in very poor health, but Tehran's Chief Prosecutor has reportedly said he was hospitalised in order to have surgery on his knee.
