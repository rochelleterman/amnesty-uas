"Guatemala: Further information on Fear for safety/Threats 
 Guatemala: Further information on Fear for safety/Threats | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International remains concerned for the safety of Otoniel De La Roca Mendoza and his family, after reports that his family was intimidated by a group of armed individuals at their home in USA. It is believed that this may be the latest incident in a campaign of threats and harassment against Otoniel De La Roca Mendoza.
