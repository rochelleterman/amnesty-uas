 Nepal: Further information on Possible ""disappearance"" / fear for safety | Amnesty International 
 Govinda Raj Rai was released on 12 October. He had not been ill-treated in custody. There is no further information about Ram Bahadur Parajuli, who was arrested by security forces personnel in plain clothes on 1 October, as he sold cigarettes on the street in Ason Tole, Kathmandu.
