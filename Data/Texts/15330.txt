"EXTRA 67/94 - Turkey: fear of torture: Aheste Akbilek 
 EXTRA 67/94 - Turkey: fear of torture: Aheste Akbilek | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Aheste Akbilek, a member of the Health Workers Union, Tum Saglik Sen, was arrested on 21 November 1994 by police from the Anti-Terror Branch of Ankara Security Headquarters. AI fears that she is being tortured in incommunicado detention. Aheste Akbilek was arrested previously in April 1994 and held for two days before being released without charge."
