 Viet Nam: Dr Nguyen Dan Que: No visitors or medical treatment for over three weeks since his arrest: Medical Action. | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Amnesty International is extremely concerned that in the  three weeks since his arrest on 17 March 2003, Dr Nguyen  Dan Que has not been allowed to receive any visitors. Dr  Nguyen Dan Que should be allowed immediate access to      family, a lawyer and medical treatment in accordance with international standards."
