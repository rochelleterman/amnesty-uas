"Iran: Further information on death penalty / possible prisoner of conscience: Morteza Firouzi 
 Iran: Further information on death penalty / possible prisoner of conscience: Morteza Firouzi | Amnesty International 
 The death sentence against Morteza Firouzi has reportedly been repealed following intervention by Mohammad Khatami, the President of Iran.  Morteza Firouzi remains in detention, as a possible prisoner of conscience, and it is believed that he will now face a re-trial.
