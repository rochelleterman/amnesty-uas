 China: Further information on arbitrary detention/ fear of torture and other ill-treatment: Gao Zhisheng (m) | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 According to the US-based Radio Free Asia, Gao Zhisheng was taken away from his home in Shaanxi Province by more than 10 security agents on 4 February 2009.  His whereabouts are unknown. Amnesty International believes he is at risk of torture and other ill-treatment."
