"Further information on UA 98/93 (EUR 60/06/93, 5 April) - Tadzhikistan: fear of torture: Bozo Sobirov 
 Further information on UA 98/93 (EUR 60/06/93, 5 April) - Tadzhikistan: fear of torture: Bozo Sobirov | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Amnesty International has learned that Bozo Sobirov has been charged with treason, an offence which carries a possible death sentence. No further details on the reason for the charges are available. Bozo Sobirov is also said to suffer from a serious but unspecified kidney illness. "
