"Guatemala: Fear for Safety/Death threats. 
 Guatemala: Fear for Safety/Death threats. | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is deeply concerned for the safety  of a missing group of residents who had been occupying    land in the suburb of Justo Rufino Barrios in the         capital, Guatemala City. Roly Escobar, the leader of the  CNSP, the National Popular Union Coordinating Body, the   organization that is filing the legal suits on behalf of  the families involved, and his family have also received  anonymous death threats.
