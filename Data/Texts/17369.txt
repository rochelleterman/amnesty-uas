"Iran: Imminent Execution: Rostam Tajik (m) 
 Iran: Imminent Execution: Rostam Tajik (m)
 | Amnesty International 
 Rostam Tajik, Afghan national, aged 20, is reportedly scheduled for execution on 10 December for a murder committed when he was 16. Iran is a state party to international treaties that expressly prohibit the use of the death penalty for crimes committed below the age of 18.
