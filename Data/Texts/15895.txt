"UA 240/94 - Ukraine: fear of imminent execution: Aleksandr Porokhnya 
 UA 240/94 - Ukraine: fear of imminent execution: Aleksandr Porokhnya | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Aleksandr Porokhnya is currently on death row in Donetsk. It is thought he was sentenced to death by Donetsk Regional Court towards the end of 1993. The Supreme Court has turned down his appeal and he is now awaiting the outcome of his petition for clemency."
