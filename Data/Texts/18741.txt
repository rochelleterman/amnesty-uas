"Syria: Further information on fear of torture/Incommunicado detention/health concern/unfair trial/possible prisoner of conscience. Riad Drar al-Hamood (m) 
 Syria: Further information on fear of torture/Incommunicado detention/health concern/unfair trial/possible prisoner of conscience.   Riad Drar al-Hamood (m) | Amnesty International 
 Civil society activist Riad Drar al-Hamood was brought before the Supreme State Security Court (SSSC) on 4 December, on charges of ""inciting sectarian strife"", belonging to a ""secret organization"" and ""publishing false news"". His case was adjourned until 15 December.
