"Nigeria: Fear for safety 
 Nigeria: Fear for safety | Amnesty International 
 On 19 May, armed men attacked Baridi Kpalap, the wife of Bari-ara Kpalap and his four children.  Bari-ara Kpalap is a staff member of the Movement for the Survival of the Ogoni People (MOSOP). Amnesty International believes that his life and those of his family may be at risk.
