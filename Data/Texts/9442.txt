 USA (New Hampshire): Further information on Death penalty / Legal concern | Amnesty International 
 On 10 May, Governor Craig Benson vetoed the bill, passed by both houses of the New Hampshire legislature, to raise from 17 to 18 the minimum age at which a person can be eligible for the death penalty in the state.
