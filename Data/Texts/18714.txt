"Syria: Further information on fear of torture/incommunicado detention/prisoners of conscience/unfair trial 
 Syria: Further information on fear of torture/incommunicado detention/prisoners of conscience/unfair trial | Amnesty International 
 Mahmoud 'Issa is reportedly being held in 'Adra prison near Damascus, following his arrest on 23 October 2006. Khalil Hussein and Suleyman Shummar are at risk of imminent arrest. Michel Kilo and Anwar al-Bunni have been detained for over five months. All are being held, or are at risk of re-arrest, in connection with signing the Beirut-Damascus Declaration.
