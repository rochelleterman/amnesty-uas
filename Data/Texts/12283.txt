 Malaysia: Further information on possible prisoners of conscience / ill-treatment / denial of medical treatment: 42 members of the Dayak Iban indigenous community | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International has just learned that all 42 members of the Dayak Iban indigenous community arrested on 25 June 1997 in connection with a dispute with an oil palm plantation company over their native customary land have now been released.
