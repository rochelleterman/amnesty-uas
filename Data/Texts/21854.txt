 Bahrain: Further information: Child moved from adult prison in Bahrain | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Sixteen-year-old Mohammad Mohammad ‘Abdulnabi ‘Abdulwasi was transferred from an adult prison to a juvenile detention centre and his family have been allowed to visit him. However, he remains held without charge and it appears that his lawyer has yet to visit him. "
