 Brazil: Fear for safety/torture/abduction | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is seriously concerned for the safety of a minor known as FC, Fabio Junior Gonzaga da Silva and Josivan Antonio dos Santos. This follows the alleged abduction, torture and attempted killing of FC, on 5 April by military police in the town of Gurupi, Tocantins state.
