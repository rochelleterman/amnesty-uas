 Turkey: Fear for torture | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Selahattin Bayram and Cemile Durmaz, who were arrested on 26 November, have reportedly been tortured at Diyarbakir  Police Headquarters. Amnesty International believes that  they are at risk of further torture, and that others are  facing arrest and torture in Diyarbakir."
