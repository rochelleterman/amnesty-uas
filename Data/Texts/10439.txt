"Myanmar: Further information on: Fear of Torture 
 Myanmar: Further information on: Fear of Torture	 | Amnesty International 
 The trial of Khun Htun Oo, Sai Nyunt Lwin and several others arrested with them is reported to have begun on 1 March in a closed court in Insein Prison in the capital Yangon, the main prison for political prisoners in Myanmar. The charges they face are not known. They are being held incommunicado, and the authorities have reportedly denied them access to lawyers.
