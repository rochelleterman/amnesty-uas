 Thailand: Further information on forcible return of Burmese refugees to Myanmar | Amnesty International 
 Ah Jwe, an ethnic Lahu headman and two other men who were taken by the Myanmar armed forces, have been released. The remaining villagers who were forcibly returned by the Thai authorities to Myanmar are said to be safe.
