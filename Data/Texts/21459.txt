 Russian Federation: One brother remaining in custody in Russia  | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Adam Tsechoev, one of two brothers held incommunicado and allegedly tortured by police in Ingushetia, Russia, was released on 17 June 2010. His older brother, Beslan Tsechoev, is still in police detention. According to his lawyer, he has not received adequate treatment for the injuries he sustained in custody. He is at risk of further torture or other ill-treatment.   "
