"Turkey: Fear of torture: Serpil Polat (f): 
 Turkey: Fear of torture: Serpil Polat (f): | Amnesty International 
 On 2 January 1998, Serpil Polat was detained at her home and taken to the Anti-Terror Branch of Izmir Police Headquarters. She was transferred to southeastern Turkey for interrogation by police on or around 4 January. It is feared that Serpil Polat is being subjected to torture during interrogation.
