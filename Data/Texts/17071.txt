 Iran: Further information on fear of ""disappearance""/fear for safety/health concern/possible prisoner of conscience: Saleh Kamrani (m), lawyer and human rights defender | Amnesty International 
 Iranian Azerbaijani lawyer and human rights defender, Saleh Kamrani was released on 18 September 2006.  Amnesty International believes that he was detained solely in     connection with his activities as a lawyer defending      Iranian Azerbaijanis and others and for the peaceful      exercise of his internationally recognized rights to      freedom of expression and association in support of       greater rights for the Iranian Azerbaijani community.
