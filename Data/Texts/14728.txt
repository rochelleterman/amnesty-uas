"EXTRA 33/94 - Turkey: fear of torture: Sevtap Yokus Veznedaroglu 
 EXTRA 33/94 - Turkey: fear of torture: Sevtap Yokus Veznedaroglu | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Sevtap Veznedaroglu, a research student at the Faculty of Law in Dicle University, Diyarbakir, was detained on 4 July 1994 by members of the Anti-Terror Branch of Diyarbakir Police Headquarters. She is the wife of Fevzi Veznedaroglu, former President of Diyarbakir Human Rights Association. Her detention was initially denied by the police authorities, but has now been confirmed. There are fears she may be being interrogated under torture."
