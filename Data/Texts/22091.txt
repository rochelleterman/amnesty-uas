 Iran: Further Information on Death penalty/ Stoning: Mokarrameh Ebrahimi (f) | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Mokarrameh Ebrahimi was released from Choubin prison in Iran on 17 March 2008, along with her son who was living in prison with her.  Mokarrameh Ebrahimi was sentenced to death by stoning after being convicted of adultery, and had been imprisoned for 11 years."
