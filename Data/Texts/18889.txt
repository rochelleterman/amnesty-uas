"Algeria: Further information on prisoner of conscience: Chawki Laamari 
 Algeria: Further information on prisoner of conscience: Chawki Laamari | Amnesty International 
 Chawki Laamari, the journalist arrested for drawing a cartoon of the Algerian flag, was tried on 29 July and sentenced to three years' imprisonment. The sentence was suspended and he was therefore released. The court also lifted the suspension order on La Tribune newspaper.
