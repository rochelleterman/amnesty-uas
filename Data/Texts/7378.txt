"Mexico: Fear for safety/Torture 
 Mexico: Fear for safety/Torture | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is seriously concerned for the safety of Hiram Oliveros, whose partner US citizen, Mario Medina was stabbed to death on 13 May at Nuevo Laredo prison in Tamaulipas State. They were accused of being involved in the murder of journalist Roberto Javier Mora and were reportedly tortured into confessing to the crime.
