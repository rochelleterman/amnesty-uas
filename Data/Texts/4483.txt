 Colombia: ""disappearance"" / fear for safety: Jorge Ivan Alarcon Sanchez, Edgar Monsalve | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 There is concern for the safety of Jorge Ivan Alarcon Sanchez and Edgar Monsalve, whose whereabouts have been unknown since they were last seen on 6 May 1995 being driven away in a vehicle with eight soldiers. The incident was witnessed in San Antonio de Prado, department of Antioquia."
