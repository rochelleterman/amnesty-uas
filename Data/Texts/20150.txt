 Honduras: Further Information on Fear for safety/Death threats: Bertha Oliva (f) | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Bertha Oliva was kept under surveillance on 11 March 2009 by a man who fled when he realised he had been seen. She has been receiving some police protection since 14 March, but it is inadequate, and she and her colleagues are still in danger."
