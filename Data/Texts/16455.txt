"Kosovo: Further information on: Fear for safety/forcible displacement 
 Kosovo: Further information on: Fear for safety/forcible displacement | Amnesty International 
 The threat that 47 members of the Ashkali ethnic minority group might be forcibly evicted from a French peace-keepers base has been lifted, following a wave of international protest. The Ashkali will now be allowed to stay in the base while arrangements are made for them to leave the country.
