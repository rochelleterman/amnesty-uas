 Iran: Further Information on Arbitrary Arrests/Fear of Torture or ill-treatment/Possible prisoners of conscience  | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Some of the students detained for their alleged role in demonstrations around the time of Iran's National University Students' Day, on 7 December 2007, have now been released.  Peyman Piran, Behrouz Karimizadeh and Ali Kantouri are still in custody and have been tortured.  Ali Kantouri is not receiving the medication he needs. "
