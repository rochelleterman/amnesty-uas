 UA 376/94 - Colombia: possible extrajudicial execution / fear for safety: Hernando Cuadros, Edgar Riano, Dario Lotero, Luis Hernandez, Monerge Sanchez, Bertina Calderon, Daniel Rico, Domingo Tovar, Victor Ramirez | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Hernando Cuadros, a leader of the Tibu branch of the oil workers' union USO, was abducted and killed on 10 October 1994. Unsuccessful attempts were made to abduct Edgar Riano, Dario Lotero, Luis Hernandez and Monerge Sanchez, activists in the same union. Death threats were made against the last four trade unionists named above in an anonymous 'phone call made on 11 October to the officers of the national Trade Union Confederation."
