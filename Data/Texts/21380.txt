 Turkey: Further information: New risk for Turkish conscientious objector: İnan S.  | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Turkish prisoner of conscience İnan S. has been moved to a prison in the western coastal city of İzmir, where he is now held pending trial on charges of ""infringement of leave"". When serving a previous sentence for ""desertion"" at the same prison, he reported being beaten by prison guards. He is at risk of torture or other ill-treatment. "
