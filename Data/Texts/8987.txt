 USA: Further information on fear of refoulement / fear for safety: Hani Abd al-Rahim al-Sayagh | Amnesty International 
 Hani Abd al-Rahim al-Sayagh, a Saudi Arabian national, who was deported to the USA on 17 June 1997, may now be at risk of refoulement to Saudi Arabia, where it is feared that he would be at risk of serious human rights violations.
