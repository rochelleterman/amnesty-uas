 Further information on EXTRA 31/92 (EUR 11/04/92, 24 March) - Albania: death penalty: Arben Elmaz Haxhiu | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Amnesty International has recently learned of the execution of Arben Haxhiu, which was probably carried out a few days after 1 June 1992, when his death sentence for murder was approved by the President of the Republic. "
