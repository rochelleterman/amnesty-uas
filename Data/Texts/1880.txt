 Rwanda: Further Information on Fear for safety/possible prisoner of conscience/detention without charge, Augustin Butorano (also known as Nkuba) | Amnesty International 
 Augustin Butorano, who was detained by police on 26 May, was released unconditionally around 9 June. He had been held without charge at Muhima police station in the capital, Kigali.
