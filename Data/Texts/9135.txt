"Further information on EXTRA 53/93 (AMR 51/45/93, 28 May) - USA (Missouri): death penalty: Chuck Lee Mathenia 
 Further information on EXTRA 53/93 (AMR 51/45/93, 28 May) - USA (Missouri): death penalty: Chuck Lee Mathenia | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Chuck Mathenia, who was scheduled to be executed on 4 June 1993, has been granted a 60-day stay of execution by Governor Carnahan, following a recommendation by the Missouri Board of Pardons and Paroles, in order that his attorney has time to prepare a clemency petition "
