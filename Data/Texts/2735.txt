 Sudan: Further information on Incommunicado detention/Possible Prisoner of Conscience.

 | Amnesty International 
 Dr Toby Madut was released on the morning of 21 June, three days after his arrest. Upon his release he returned to his family home. Amnesty International has received information that he was not ill-treated while in detention.
