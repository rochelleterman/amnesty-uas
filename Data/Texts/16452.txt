"Kosovo: Fear for safety/forcible displacement 
 Kosovo: Fear for safety/forcible displacement | Amnesty International 
 Forty-seven members of the Ashkali ethnic minority group, who have been sheltering in a French peacekeepers' base since they were driven from their homes by Albanian demonstrators, are to be evicted from the base on 12 August. Amnesty International fears their lives will be in danger unless they are given asylum in another country.
