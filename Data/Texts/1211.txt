 Kenya: Fear for safety / medical concern/ Fear of Torture and ill treatment / incommunicado detention | Amnesty International 
 On 4 December, high school students Darius Auka and Ibrahim Otaka Nyabere were arrested by police at a hospital in Trans Mara, northern Kenya, where they were receiving treatment for critical gunshot wounds sustained during a police operation. They are being held incommunicado at an unknown location. They may be at risk of torture and ill-treatment in detention, and their lives are in danger if they do not receive immediate medical care.
