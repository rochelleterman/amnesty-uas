 Colombia: Paramilitaries kill human rights defender: Eder Verbel Rocha  | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 On 23 March two paramilitaries shot at Orlando Enrique Verbel Rocha, Eder Verbel Rocha and his son in San Onofre municipality in Northern Colombia. Eder Verbel Rocha was killed in the attack. Amnesty International fears for the life of his family and other MOVICE members."
