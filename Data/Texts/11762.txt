"Indonesia: ""Disappearances"" 
 Indonesia: ""Disappearances"" | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is concerned for the safety of      Yacob Hamdan, Zainal Abdullah, Abdul Manaf, Ishak, Abdul  Mutaleb and Tgk M Saleh who have ""disappeared"" after they were arrested between 27 June and 8 July 1999, allegedly  by the Indonesian National Army.  AI urges the            authorities to establish and make public the whereabouts  of these men and to provide guarantees for their safety.
