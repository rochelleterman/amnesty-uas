"Pakistan: Further information on possible 'disappearance'/fear of torture 
 Pakistan: Further information on possible 'disappearance'/fear of torture | Amnesty International 
 Samiullah Baloch was released on 10 December. Sources close to him have reported that he was abducted by Pakistani intelligence agencies, he was tortured in the early stages of his unlawful detention and he was kept in solitary confinement.
