 Cameroon: Further information on torture and ill-treatment / health concern | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International remains seriously concerned about the health and safety of Ebenezer Akwanga, John Bah Atoh, Fon Peter Fonyam, Prisca Fonyam (f), Bika Iserisu, Grace Yaya Kwei (f), Wilson Che Neba, Philip Tete, Nseke Stanley Tete and Patrick Yimbu. Another prisoner, Lawrence Fai, died around 5 September 1998 and at least two others are reported to be seriously ill as a result of torture.
