 USA (Maryland): Death penalty: Vernon Evans Jr (m), black

 | Amnesty International 
 Vernon Evans Jr, black, aged 56, is scheduled to be executed in Maryland during the week beginning 6 February. He was sentenced to death in 1984 for the April 1983 shooting murders of two motel clerks, David Scott Piechowicz and Susan Kennedy, white.
