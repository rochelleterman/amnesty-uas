"Turkmenistan: Fear for safety / Torture or ill-treatment 
 Turkmenistan: Fear for safety / Torture or ill-treatment | Amnesty International 
 Three named people and many others, including men, women  and children, were arrested following an attack on the    Presidential motorcade on 25 November. The President was  unharmed, but the attack has led to a new wave of         repression against alleged government critics and their   families in Turkmenistan.
