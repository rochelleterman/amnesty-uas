 Sudan: Fear for safety / fear of torture or ill-treatment / incommunicado detention | Amnesty International 
 Abdul Majid Mosa Hassan and 27 other men were reportedly arrested by army personnel in the town of Kabkabyia, North Darfur state, between 4 and 5 August. All of them are being detained incommunicado and Amnesty International fears that they may be tortured or ill-treated in detention.
