"Nepal: Fear for safety/ incommunicado detention/ fear of torture 
 Nepal: Fear for safety/ incommunicado detention/ fear of torture | Amnesty International 
 Four named men were arrested on 1 February in the far west of Nepal. Several witnesses have reported that all four are being tortured in police custody. Relatives and lawyers have been denied access to the men, and so far the police have not presented them before any court.
