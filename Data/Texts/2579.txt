"Sudan: Fear for Safety, Dr Mudawi Ibrahim Adam (m) 
 NA 
 Human rights activist Dr Mudawi Ibrahim Adam was arrested at his home in Khartoum, the capital of Sudan at 8.30pm on 28 December 2003. He is now held in Kober Prison in Khartoum North and he is reportedly being interrogated by the National Security Forces. Amnesty International fears for his safety.
