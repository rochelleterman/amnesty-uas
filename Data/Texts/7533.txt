"Mexico: Fear for safety: Alejandro Gallardo Enriquez 
 Mexico: Fear for safety: Alejandro Gallardo Enriquez | Amnesty International 
 On 21 December 1998 a failed kidnap attempt against Alejandro Gallardo Enriquez, a leading campaigner for reform in the armed forces, took place. Although those involved have subsequently been arrested, Amnesty International remains concerned for the safety of him and his family.
