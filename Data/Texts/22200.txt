 Iran: Execution postponed again: Further information: Mohammad Reza Haddadi | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 The execution of Mohammad Reza Haddadi, scheduled for 16 July 2009, has been postponed, and the Head of the Judiciary is not known to have set a new execution date. This is the fourth time his execution has been postponed. There is no further news of Naser Qasemi who was also sentenced to death for crimes committed when he was under 18."
