"USA (Illinois): Further Information on Death penalty 
 USA (Illinois): Further Information on Death penalty | Amnesty International 
 Governor George Ryan of Illinois is considering whether   to commute any death sentences before he leaves office on 13 January 2003. Amnesty International believes that he   should commute all current death sentences in the state,  a total of around 160.
