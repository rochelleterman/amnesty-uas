"Philippines: Further information on imminent execution: Pablito Andan 
 Philippines: Further information on imminent execution: Pablito Andan | Amnesty International 
 The execution of Pablito Andan by lethal execution, which had been scheduled to take place at 12 pm on 28 May 1999, has been postponed until 28 June. President Estrada       granted the stay of execution.
