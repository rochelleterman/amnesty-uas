"Syria: Further information on Prisoner of conscience/fear of torture or ill-treatment: Muhammad Jum'ah Quban 
 Syria: Further information on Prisoner of conscience/fear of torture or ill-treatment: Muhammad Jum'ah Quban | Amnesty International 
 Muhammad Jum'ah Quban was released from jail on the evening of 16 June and has now been reunited with his family. He was reported as stating that he had been ill-treated while held in the custody of the Military Security.
