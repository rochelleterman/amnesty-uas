"UA 268/94 - Haiti: fear of torture / legal concern: Lunice Larochelle 
 UA 268/94 - Haiti: fear of torture / legal concern: Lunice Larochelle | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Lunice Larochelle has been detained unlawfully without charge or trial since his arrest on 23 June 1994. He is a member of the Justice and Peace office in Randel, Chardonnieres, Sud department. He is being held at the military barracks in Les Cayes. For the first two weeks of his detention he was reportedly denied visitors and food from his family."
