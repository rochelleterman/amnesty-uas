"Further information on EXTRA 95/93 (AMR 25/15/93, 5 November) - Cuba: death penalty: Felix Oviedo Aguilera 
 Further information on EXTRA 95/93 (AMR 25/15/93, 5 November) - Cuba: death penalty: Felix Oviedo Aguilera | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 The 4 August 1994 edition of the official Cuban newspaper Granma reports that Felix Oviedo Aguilera was executed after his appeal was rejected by the Supreme Court and the Council of State decided not to commute his sentence. The report did not say when the execution took place."
