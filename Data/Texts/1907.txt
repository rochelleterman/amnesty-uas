 Rwanda: Further information on: arbitrary arrest / incommunicado detention / fear of torture: Alex Hakuziyaremye | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International has learned that the above, a 25-year-old worker at Radio Rwanda, was released yesterday, 7 August 1996. He is reported to have been beaten while being interrogated at the Directorate of Military Intelligence in Kigali.
