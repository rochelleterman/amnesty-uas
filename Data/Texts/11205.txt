"Further information on UA 201/94 (ASA 20/18/94) - India: unacknowledged detention / fear of ""disappearance"": Sukhwinder Singh Bhatti 
 Further information on UA 201/94 (ASA 20/18/94) - India: unacknowledged detention / fear of ""disappearance"": Sukhwinder Singh Bhatti | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 On 17 June the Punjab and Haryana High Court directed the Central Bureau of Investigation (CBI) to inquire into the ""disappearance"" of Sukhwinder Singh Bhatti. The CBI was ordered to present its report within three months."
