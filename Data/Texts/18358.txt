"Saudi Arabia: Further information on fear of torture / possible prisoners of conscience 
 Saudi Arabia: Further information on fear of torture / possible prisoners of conscience | Amnesty International 
 Abdullah Abbas al-Ahmad, aged 40, employee of Aramco, Kamil Abbas al-Ahmad, aged 25, universitiy student, Muhammad Ibrahim al-Ibrahim, aged 40, employee of Aramco, Nasser 'Ali Rahim, 39, employee of Aramco, Ali Hassan al-Dawood, 32, employee of Aramco: Amnesty International has learned that Nasser 'Ali Rahim was released without charge on 20 July 1996. The others all reportedly remain without charge in incommunicado detention. Amnesty International fears that these detainees may be at risk of torture.
