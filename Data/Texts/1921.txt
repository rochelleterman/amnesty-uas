"Rwanda: Death penalty / unfair trial: Leonidas Ndikumwami 
 Rwanda: Death penalty / unfair trial: Leonidas Ndikumwami | Amnesty International 
 Leonidas Ndikumwami, who was sentenced to death after a grossly unfair trial on 20 January 1997 will have his case come before the Court of Appeal on 22 May. There is concern that his sentence may be confirmed.
