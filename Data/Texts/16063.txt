"Kyrgyzstan: Further information on fear of imminent execution: Nikolai Sokolov 
 Kyrgyzstan: Further information on fear of imminent execution: Nikolai Sokolov | Amnesty International 
 No final decision has yet been made on the case of Nikolai Sokolov.  Although it is reported that the Clemency Commission has already decided against recommending clemency, the possibility remains that President Askat Akayev may exercise his constitutional authority to commute the sentence.
