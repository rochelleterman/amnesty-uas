 Mexico: Human rights defender threatened in Mexico: Blanca Mesina Navarez (f): Further Information | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Human rights defender Blanca Mesina Navarez has received new threats related to her work documenting cases of torture in the municipality of Tijuana, northern Mexico. She is receiving no protection from the authorities, and her life could be in danger. "
