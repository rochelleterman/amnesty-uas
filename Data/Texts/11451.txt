"Indonesia: Further information on Imminent Execution 
 Indonesia: Further information on Imminent Execution | Amnesty International 
 Amrozi bin H. Nurhasyim, who was convicted of involvement in the 12 October 2002 bombings on the island of Bali, is facing imminent execution by firing squad.  A decision on the reviews of Ali Ghufron's and Imam Samudera's cases is expected imminently.
