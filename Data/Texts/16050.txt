"Further information on UA 101/93 (EUR 58/01/93, 6 April) - Kyrgyzstan: death penalty: Grigory Abramov 
 Further information on UA 101/93 (EUR 58/01/93, 6 April) - Kyrgyzstan: death penalty: Grigory Abramov | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 The Clemency Commission of Kyrgyzstan reviewed Grigory Abramov's petition for clemency on 18 June, but could not come to any decision, and a second clemency hearing has been scheduled for August, the exact date not known. Grigory Abramov had been sentenced to death on appeal by Bishkek City Court on 9 December 1992 for killing a state security guard "
