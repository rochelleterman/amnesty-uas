"India: Further information on fear of torture / fear for safety: Sheikh Mohammad Ashraf, lawyer 
 India: Further information on fear of torture / fear for safety: Sheikh Mohammad Ashraf, lawyer | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 The family of Sheihk Mohammad Ashraf was granted permission to visit him in detention in Baramulla on 2 July 1995. They were reportedly only allowed to see him for a few minutes and were still not informed of the charges under which he was being held. He has not been given access to a lawyer."
