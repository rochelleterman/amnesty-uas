 Djibouti: Further information on prisoner of conscience: Aref Mohamed Aref
 | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is calling for the immediate and    unconditional release of Aref Mohamed Aref, a human       rights defender and lawyer. On 15 February 1999 he was    sentenced to six months in prison after being  convicted  on politically motivated charges.
