 Nepal: Fear for safety / possible ""disappearance"" | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is concerned for the safety of Bhim Giri and Bhai Kaji Ghimire who were reportedly arrested on 3 December 2003 and Lok Prasad Bhurtel who was believed to have been arrested on 15 March 2004. Their whereabouts remain unknown.
