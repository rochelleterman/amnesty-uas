 Egypt: Death penalty - Imminent execution: Mati Bassilous Mati Hanna | Amnesty International 
 On 14 May 1998, the court of Cassation upheld Mati Bassilous Mati Hanna's  death sentence. As the Mufti, the highest religious authority in the country, has already approved the sentence, Mati Bassilous Mati Hanna's execution is now imminent.
