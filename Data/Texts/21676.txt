 Uzbekistan: Health condition of POC deteriorating, Norboi Kholzhigitov | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Norboi Kholzhigitov, a 60-year-old male human rights activist, is critically-ill in an Uzbekistan prison. Whilst in detention he has not been receiving the treatment he needs for a range of serious medical problems. He is a Prisoner of Conscience and his health has been continuously deteriorating since being imprisoned. There are now fears that if he is not treated he will die."
