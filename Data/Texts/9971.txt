"USA (Alabama): Further information on death penalty / legal concern: Gregory Wynn 
 USA (Alabama): Further information on death penalty / legal concern: Gregory Wynn | Amnesty International 
 On 17 September 1999, a Calhoun County Circuit Judge in   Anniston, Alabama, sentenced Gregory Wynn to die in the   state's electric chair. In doing so he upheld the         recommendation of the jury which had earlier decided that the death penalty was the appropriate sentence for the    teenager
