"Sudan: Fear for Safety/Prisoner of Conscience/Human Rights Defender. Saleh Mahmud Osman, lawyer 
 Sudan: Fear for Safety/Prisoner of Conscience/Human Rights Defender. Saleh Mahmud Osman, lawyer	 | Amnesty International 
 Human rights lawyer Saleh Mahmud Osman has been detained incommunicado since 1 February, and is at risk of torture or ill-treatment. It is not known why Saleh Mahmud Osman, who works in Nyala, in Darfur, in the west of Sudan, was arrested. However, he has provided legal aid to detainees, those facing capital punishment and victims of human rights abuses in Darfur.
