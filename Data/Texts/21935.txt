 Iran: Further Information on UA 334/07: Imminent execution: Raheleh Zamani (f) | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Raheleh Zamani was hanged on 2 January 2008 in Tehran's Evin Prison, along with seven men convicted of murder. She was sentenced to qesas-e nafs (retribution in kind) in October 2005 for the murder earlier the same year of her husband, Mohammad Zamani, whom she alleged was having an extra-marital affair. "
