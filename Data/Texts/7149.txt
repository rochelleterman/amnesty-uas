"Jamaica: Further information on death penalty: Kevin Mykoo and Leroy Lamey 
 Jamaica: Further information on death penalty: Kevin Mykoo and Leroy Lamey | Amnesty International 
 Kevin Mykoo and Leroy Lamey, who were scheduled to be hanged on 8 December 1998, were granted stays of execution pending the hearing of constitutional motions filed by each man after the execution warrants were issued.
