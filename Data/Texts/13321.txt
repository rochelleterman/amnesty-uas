"Singapore: Further information on Imminent Execution: Van Tuong Nguyen (m) 
 Singapore: Further information on Imminent Execution: Van Tuong Nguyen (m) | Amnesty International 
 Van Tuong Nguyen will be hanged on 2 December, according to a letter his family received on 17 November from the government of Singapore. Executions are usually carried out at dawn. The President had rejected an appeal for clemency on 21 October.
