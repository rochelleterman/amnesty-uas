"Indonesia: Further Information on fear of torture or ill-treatment/Incommunicado detention 
 Indonesia: Further Information on fear of torture or ill-treatment/Incommunicado detention | Amnesty International 
 The 13 named men and three unnamed men and women remain   in incommunicado detention at Manokwari Police Resort,    Manokwari town, Manokwari District, Papua Province. They  were also arrested on 27 November in connection with      activities to commemorate the fifth anniversary of a      declaration of Papuan independence and are at risk of     torture and ill-treatment.
