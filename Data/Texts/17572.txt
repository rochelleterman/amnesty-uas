 Iraq: Further information on Incommunicado detention / Fear of torture and ill-treatment: Zakaria Yehia Al-Zuhairi (m)

 | Amnesty International 
 Zakaria Yehia Al-Zuhairi, aged 30, an Iraqi and Libyan citizen, was released after officials working for the Prime Minister of Iraqi Kurdistan, Nechirvan Barzani, helped to secure his release. He returned to Libya on 24 July.
