"Malaysia: Further information on Fear of torture or ill-treatment / incommunicado detention / prisoners of conscience. 
 Malaysia: Further information on  Fear of torture or ill-treatment /             incommunicado         detention /   prisoners of          conscience. | Amnesty International 
 Tian Chua, Mohd Ezam Mohd Noor, Haji Saari Sungip and Hishamuddin Rais are to be detained for two years without charge or trial, and may have been subjected to prolonged, intense psychological pressure amounting to torture during interrogation by the police. Raja Petra Kamaruddin was released on 2 June, after N. Gobalakrishnan and Abdul Ghani Harun. The fate of the three remaining in police custody - human rights defender Badaruddin Ismail, Dr Badrul Amin Baharom and Lokman Nor Adam, still in police custody, is not yet known.
