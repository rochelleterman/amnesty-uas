 Colombia: Further information: Environmentalist receives death threats: Hildebrando Vélez | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Hildebrando Vélez, an environmentalist who has played a leading role in campaigning to establish the whereabouts of Sandra Viviana Cuellar Gallego, also an environmentalist, has received death threats. Sandra Viviana Cuellar Gallego went missing in Cali, western Colombia, on 17 February, and is feared to have been forcibly disappeared."
