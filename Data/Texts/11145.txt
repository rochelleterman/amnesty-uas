"India: Fear of torture 
 India: Fear of torture | Amnesty International 
 Four Nepalese nationals, Partha Chettri; Maheshwar Dahal; Aditi Shah (f); and Moti Prasad are currently in detention in India, and are facing imminent deportation to Nepal. Amnesty International fears that if they are forced to return, they could be at risk of torture.
