"USA (Nebraska): Death penalty: Randolph Reeves 
 USA (Nebraska): Death penalty: Randolph Reeves | Amnesty International 
 Randolph Reeves, a Native American, is scheduled to be executed by the state of Nebraska on 14 January 1999 for murder. He was granted an appeal in 1997 but it was overturned by the Supreme Court. The relatives of the two murder victims have spoken out publicly against the execution.
