"UAE: Further information on death penalty: Gamini Perera 
 UAE: Further information on death penalty: Gamini Perera | Amnesty International 
 Gamini Perera was executed on the morning of 8 November   1999 by firing squad. He had been sentenced to death in   October 1998 in connection with the murder of a young     Emirati national earlier that year.
