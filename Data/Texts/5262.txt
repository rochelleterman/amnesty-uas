"Colombia: Fear for safety 
 Colombia: Fear for safety | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is seriously concerned for the      safety of members of the Sindicato de Trabajadores        Oficiales de Antioquia (SINTRAOFAN), Union of Official    Workers of Antioquia, particularly the union's leaders    Arturo Ocampo and Carlos Zapata. Union members have       received death threats, and a threat to bomb their        headquarters.
