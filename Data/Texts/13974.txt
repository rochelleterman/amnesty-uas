"Germany: Further information on fear of refoulement / fear of torture: Fathelrahman Abdallah 
 Germany: Further information on fear of refoulement / fear of torture: Fathelrahman Abdallah | Amnesty International 
 The deportation of Sudanese asylum seeker Fathelrahman Abdallah has been postponed after his lawyer lodged an appeal. If the court's decision on appeal, expected on 29 or 30 June, goes against him, he may be deported immediately.
