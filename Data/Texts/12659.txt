"Nepal: Fear of torture/fear of ""disappearance"", Ramesh Gautam. 
 Nepal: Fear of torture/fear of ""disappearance"", Ramesh Gautam. | Amnesty International 
 Ramesh Gautam was reportedly arrested at about 10pm on 3  August in the capital Kathmandu by army personnel, who told him they were taking him for questioning. He has not been seen since then, and there are fears for his safety.
