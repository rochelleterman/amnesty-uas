 Israel: Further information on house demolition/forced eviction/medical concern 

 | Amnesty International 
 On 7 June the Jerusalem Court of Local Affairs ordered the Herbawi family to obtain a building permit for the part of their home which houses their disabled 24-year-old son Mansour by 7 December 2006, or have it demolished. It will be virtually impossible for the family to obtain such a permit.
