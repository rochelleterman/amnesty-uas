"Albania: Further information on legal concern / fear of ill-treatment: Klement Kolaneci 
 Albania: Further information on legal concern / fear of ill-treatment: Klement Kolaneci | Amnesty International 
 According to a report in the Albanian press of 24 April 1997 the charges against Klement Kolaneci and 25 others detained since 16 October 1996 have been dropped. Those detained had already escaped or been released from prison in March 1997.
