 Cameroon: Further information on fear of torture and ill-treatment / possible prisoners of conscience: | Amnesty International 
 Justin Pokam, Thomas Seme, Konga Philip Kuate, Victoria Musong (f), Gregoire Diboule, Oliver Finya, Wavie Asago, Richard Nyieka, Stephen Aka, Julius Mbenya and Chief Mondi; all members and supporters of the Social Democratic Front (SDF): Chief Mondi was, like all the others before him, released without charge on 17 December 1997. No further action is requested.
