 Nepal: ""Disappearance"" / Fear for Safety | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is concerned for the safety of Pushpa Lal Dhakal and Gopi Bhandari who were reportedly arrested by armed security force personnel on 13 January and 15 January respectively. The whereabouts of both these men are unknown.
