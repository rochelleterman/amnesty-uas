"Syria: Possible Prisoner of Conscience/Fear of torture or ill-treatment 
 Syria: Possible Prisoner of Conscience/Fear of torture or ill-treatment | Amnesty International 
 Musallam Shaykh Hasan, aged 35, a leading member of the Syrian Kurdish Democratic Unity Party was arrested on 7 May, by members of the political security forces. He is reportedly being held in incommunicado detention and is at risk of torture or ill-treatment.
