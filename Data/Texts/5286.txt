"UA 437/93 - Colombia: extrajudicial execution: Jesus Antonio Montoya Ospina 
 UA 437/93 - Colombia: extrajudicial execution: Jesus Antonio Montoya Ospina | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Jose Antonio Montoya, a lawyer and founding member of the Solidarity Committee with Political Prisoners, was killed by unidentified gunmen on 14 December 1993 in Cali, Valle del Cauca department, Colombia. As a lawyer, Jose Antonio Montoya had defended many trade union organizations and political prisoners and was and active member of the Communist Party and the Patriotic Union Party."
