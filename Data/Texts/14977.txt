 EXTRA 48/94 - Turkey: fear of torture: Berivan Kutluay, Safyetin Tepe, Nevzat Bulut, Mustafa Aladag | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 The four people named above were detained during a raid by Anti-Terror police on the Agri offices of the newspaper Ozgur Ulke (Free Country). The police also took papers and documents from the offices. No reason for the detention has been given. AI fears that the four journalists may be tortured in incommunicado detention."
