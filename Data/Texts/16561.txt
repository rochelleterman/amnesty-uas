"Bahrain: Fear of torture / possible prisoner of conscience: Muhammad Ahmad Jum'a Shafi'i 
 Bahrain: Fear of torture / possible prisoner of conscience: Muhammad Ahmad Jum'a Shafi'i | Amnesty International 
 Muhammad Ahmad Jum'a Shafi'i is reported to be in incommunicado detention where he remains at risk of torture or ill-teratment. The reason for his arrest is unclear, but it is believed to be connected with a telephone conversation with persons abroad.
