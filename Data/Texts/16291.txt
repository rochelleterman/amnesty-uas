"Uzbekistan: Further information on Fear of imminent execution 
 Uzbekistan: Further information on  Fear of imminent execution | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is concerned that Nigmatullo Fayzullayev is in imminent danger of execution and calls upon the Uzbek government to confirm Maksim Strakhov and Nikolay Ganiyev's stay of execution, following a request by the United Nations Human Rights Committee on 16 October.
