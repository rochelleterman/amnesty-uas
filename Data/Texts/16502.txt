 EXTRA 43/93 - Bahrain: fear of forced exile / fear for safety: al-Sayyid Hashem al-Musawi | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Amnesty International has received reports that a Bahraini national, al- Sayyid Hashem al-Musawi, is facing deportation to the United Arab Emirates on 1 May 1993. It is also feared that if deported, he may be sent to a third country, where he might be tortured or ill-treated. "
