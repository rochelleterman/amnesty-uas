"Guatemala: Fear of impunity: amnesty law 
 Guatemala: Fear of impunity: amnesty law | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is concerned that a new draft amnesty law, Ley de Reconciliacion Nacional, which is about to be debated by the Guatemalan Congress, may mean that those who committed gross human rights violations in the past will remain immune from prosecution.
