 Sri Lanka: Outspoken female journalist under threat | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Journalist and media rights activist, Dileesha Abeysundera is in danger after a gang of unidentified people threatened her and tried to forcefully gain access to her property in Sri Lanka.  Dileesha Abeysundera believes that the gang threatened her because of her work campaigning for media rights. "
