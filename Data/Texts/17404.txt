"Iran: Further information on Fear of imminent execution: Shahla Jahed (f) 
 Iran: Further information on Fear of imminent execution: Shahla Jahed (f) | Amnesty International 
 Shahla Jahed, aged 36, is once again facing imminent execution, after the Supreme Court reportedly upheld her death sentence for the second time. She was sentenced to death for murdering her husband's first wife in 2002. She may have been coerced into confessing to the murder.
