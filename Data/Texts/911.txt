 Further information on UA 364/94 (AFR 25/24/94, 3 October) - Ethiopia: fear of ill-treatment / legal concern: Lemma Sidamo, Getu Tenite, Daniso Borsamo and up to 300 other Sidama Liberation Movement (SLM) members in Awassa | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Lemma Sidamo was released in mid-November 1994 without charge. Getu Tenite, Daniso Borsamo and the others arrested in August are still being detained without charge in Awassa and Yirga Alem prisons. There have been reports of the torture or ill-treatment of detainees."
