"Further information on EXTRA 104/92 (AMR 51/128/92, 8 December) - USA (State of Washington): death penalty: Westley Allan Dodd 
 Further information on EXTRA 104/92 (AMR 51/128/92, 8 December) - USA (State of Washington): death penalty: Westley Allan Dodd | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Westly Allan Dodd was executed as scheduled, by hanging, on 5 January 1993 in the State of Washington. This is the first execution in the state since 1963 and the first to be carried out in the USA this year. "
