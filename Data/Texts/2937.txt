"Sudan: Further information on Fear for safety/fear of torture or ill-treatment / incommunicado detention 
 Sudan: Further information on Fear for safety/fear of torture or ill-treatment / incommunicado detention | Amnesty International 
 Gamar Eldin Mohammed Eisa was released without charge on 21 June after being held incommunicado by the security services since 10 June. However, Abbas El Tigani still remains in incommunicado detention and Amnesty International remains concerned for his safety as he may be at risk of torture or ill-treatment.
