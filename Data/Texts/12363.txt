 Malaysia: Further information on legal concern / health concern / possible prolonged detention without trial / possible prisoners of conscience / health concern | Amnesty International 
 Che Kamarulzaman Ismail is reported to be on hunger strike in protest at his continued detention without trial.  Amnesty International is concerned for his health amid fears that he may not have access to adequate medical care. He was arrested for 'deviationist' Islamic teaching in November 1997 along with Professor Lutpi Ibrahim, Fadzullah Shuib, Syed Sulaiman bin Syed Hassan, Zainal Adam, Said Muda and Ustaz Abdul Hassan, who are also still in detention
