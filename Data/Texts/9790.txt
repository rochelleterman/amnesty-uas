"USA (Florida): Further information on death penalty: Joseph Spaziano 
 USA (Florida): Further information on death penalty: Joseph Spaziano | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Joseph Spaziano who was scheduled to be exceuted in Florida on 21 September, has received a stay of execution. The hearing has now been rescheduled for mid-November to allow time for a new attorney to represent Spaziano to be found."
