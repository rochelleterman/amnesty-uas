 Indonesia: Further information on: Imminent execution: Astini

 | Amnesty International 
 Final preparations are being made for the execution of Astini, a mother of three. According to Indonesian press reports, a source in the Prosecutor's Office has confirmed that Astini will be executed in December, although the exact date and place of execution has not been announced.
