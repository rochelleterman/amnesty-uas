"UA 347/92 - Colombia: ""disappearance"": Leonardo Leyva 
 UA 347/92 - Colombia: ""disappearance"": Leonardo Leyva | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Leonardo Leyva, aged 29, was reportedly detained at 2pm on 30 October 1992 in La Belleza, Santander department, by troops from the Cobra 2 unit of the Bolivar Battalion based in Tunja, Boyaca department and has not been seen since. This unit has apparently been carrying out counter-insurgency operations in the area, in which attacks on civilian communities have been denounced. Despite attempts to obtain information about Leonarda Leyva, his detention has been denied by the military and other regional security and judicial authorities. AI is concerned for his physical safety. "
