 Iran: Further information on fear of death penalty / prisoners of conscience / fear of torture / health concern | Amnesty International 
 Three employees of the Iranian daily newspaper Tous,  Mashallah Shamsolva'ezin, Mohammad Javadi Hessar and Rahim Nabavi, who were arrested in September 1998 have been released on bail. A fourth journalist, Hamid Reza Jalaipour, was released on 13 October 1998.
