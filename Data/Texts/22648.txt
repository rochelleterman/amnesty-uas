 Saudi Arabia: Further information on fear of torture or ill-treatment / incommunicado detention: Fouad Ahmad al-Farhan | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Blogger Fouad Ahmad al-Farhan was released without charge on 26 April 2008. Fouad Ahmad al-Farhan, who owns a small IT company, had been arrested at his office on 10 December, and was held at Dhaban Prison, in the city of Jeddah."
