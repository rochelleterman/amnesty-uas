"Gambia: Incommunicado detention/Fear of                   ill-treatment/Health concern. 
 Gambia: Incommunicado detention/Fear of                   ill-treatment/Health concern. | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is seriously concerned for the      safety of Bisher Al-Rawi, an Iraqi national, and Jamil El Banna, a Jordanian national, who have     been held in    incommunicado detention in Banjul, the       capital      since their arrest by members of the National             Intelligence Agency (NIA) on 8 November.  Jamil El Banna  is believed to be a diabetic. Amnesty International is    concerned that he may have neither the medication he      requires, nor access to a doctor.
