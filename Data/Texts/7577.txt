 Mexico: Death threats/Fear for safety | Amnesty International 
 Peasant environmentalists (campesinos ecologistas) trying to protect the forests in Mexico's southern state of      Guerrero from overexploitation are reportedly facing      death threats from soldiers and so-called paramilitary    groups. Amnesty International is concerned for their      safety.
