 Chinese activists detained, at risk of torture: Ni Yulan and Dong Jiqin  | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Housing rights activist and former lawyer, Ni Yulan, and her husband, Dong Jiqin, have been detained in Beijing. They are being held on suspicion of “picking quarrels and provoking trouble”. They are at risk of torture and other ill-treatment. "
