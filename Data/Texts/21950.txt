 Iran: Iranian student detained without charge: Payam Jahangiri (m) | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Iranian student Payam Jahangiri was arrested on 5 December 2009 at his home in the city of Shiraz in southwestern Iran, two days before nationwide student protests against the government took place on 7 December.  Since then he has been held without charge in Shiraz and has had no access to a lawyer. He is at risk of torture or other ill-treatment."
