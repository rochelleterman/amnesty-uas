"USA (Ohio): Further information on Death Penalty: John Spirko (m) 
 USA (Ohio): Further information on Death Penalty: John Spirko (m) | Amnesty International 
 John Spirko was granted a six-month reprieve by Governor Taft on 9 January. He had been scheduled to be executed on 19 January. The reprieve was granted to allow more time for DNA testing.
