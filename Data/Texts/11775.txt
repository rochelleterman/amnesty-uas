 Indonesia: Fear for Safety for Human Rights activists     Rusli and Sofyan

 | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is concerned that two human rights  activists, Rusli and Sofyan, who were detained by police  in Aceh, are at risk of torture or ill-treatment. 
 Amnesty International is concerned that two human rights  activists, Rusli and Sofyan, who were detained by police  in Aceh, are at risk of torture or ill-treatment.
They were detained by police officers with the Police     Mobile Brigade. The reason for their detention   is not   known. Both are volunteers with the People's       Crisis Centre (PCC), a human rights monitoring and               humanitarian group.
