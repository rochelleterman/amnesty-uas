"Rwanda: Further information on  ""Disappearance"": Edouard Mpabuka 
 Rwanda: Further information on  ""Disappearance"": Edouard Mpabuka | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International has sought and received independent confirmation that Edouard Mpabuka, reported ""disappeared"" on 8 November 1999, is being held in pre-trial detention  in Gisenyi central prison, accused of participation in    the 1994 genocide.
