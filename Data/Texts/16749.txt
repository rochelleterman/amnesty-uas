"Egypt: Death penalty: Ahmad Hassan 'Ali Isma'il 
 Egypt: Death penalty: Ahmad Hassan 'Ali Isma'il | Amnesty International 
 Ahmad Hassan 'Ali Isma'il who had been sentenced to death in absentia in 1996 for murder, was reportedly arrested on 18 April 1997 in Cairo. There are fears that he is at risk of imminent execution.
