"People's Republic of China: Further information on death penalty / legal concern: Wang Jianye 
 People's Republic of China: Further information on death penalty / legal concern: Wang Jianye | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 The death sentence passed on Wang Jianye on 3 April 1995 is currently being reviewed by the Supreme People's Court and AI fears that Wang Jianye may face imminent execution. An appeal on 31 July was rejected and since then he has not been allowed to receive visits from his lawyer or receive and send letters to his lawyer and relatives."
