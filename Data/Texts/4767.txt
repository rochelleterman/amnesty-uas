 Colombia: Fear for safety, Civilian population of the     municipality of San Carlos, Antioquia Department | Amnesty International 
 Paramilitaries have attacked the town of San Carlos, killing at least 13 people, only days after the Colombian army withdrew from the town. Amnesty International is concerned for the safety of the civilian population in the municipality.
