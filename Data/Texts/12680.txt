"Nepal: Further information on Fear of torture or ill-treatment/ prisoner of conscience, Gagan Thapa 
 Nepal: Further information on Fear of torture or ill-treatment/ prisoner of conscience, Gagan Thapa | Amnesty International 
 Gagan Thapa appeared before the Special Court in Kathmandu on 14 August, where he was charged with sedition before being released pending trial. He had been in police custody since 27 July and was accused of shouting anti-monarchist slogans at a political demonstration.
