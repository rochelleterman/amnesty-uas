"Angola: Further information on fear of refoulement / fear for safety: Rwandese refugees on Zaire/Angola border 
 Angola: Further information on fear of refoulement / fear for safety: Rwandese refugees on Zaire/Angola border | Amnesty International 
 Fears continue for the safety of Rwandese refugees attempting to cross into Angola or who have been forcibly returned to Zaire. The authorities are continuing to deny the UN High Commissioner for Refugees access to the area.
