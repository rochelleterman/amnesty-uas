"Guatemala: Fear for safety 
 Guatemala: Fear for safety | Amnesty International 
 The offices of two organisations opposed to the Central America Free Trade Agreement (CAFTA) have been the target of raids in the capital Guatemala City, in which confidential information about their work and membership was stolen. Amnesty International believes that the members of these organisations may be in danger.
