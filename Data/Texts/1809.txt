 Zimbabwe: Unlawful detention/ fear of ill-treatment / possible prisoners of conscience (POCs)

 | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is seriously concerned at the harassment and unlawful detention of Zimbabwe Congress of Trade Unions (ZCTU) President Lovemore Matombo and Secretary General Wellington Chibebe as well as that of up to 50 trade union and human rights activists.
