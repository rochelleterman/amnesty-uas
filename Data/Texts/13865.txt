"Albania: Further information on possible prisoners of conscience 
 Albania: Further information on possible prisoners of conscience | Amnesty International 
 Sami Meta, Kristaq Mosko, Timoshenko Pekmezi and Tare Isufi: On 23 October 1996, the Court of Appeal in Tirana confirmed the sentences of imprisonment for Sami Meta and his three co-defendants. They had appealed against the length of the sentences on grounds of old age and alleged irregularities during the investigation.
