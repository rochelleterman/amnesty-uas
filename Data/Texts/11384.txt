"Indonesia: Further information on Death penalty / fear of imminent execution 
 Indonesia: Further information on Death penalty / fear of imminent execution | Amnesty International 
 Six people Ayodhya Prasad Chaubey (m), aged 56, Indian national, Suryadi Swabuana (m), aged 37, Nyonya Sumiarsih (f), Djais Adi Prayitno (m), Sungeng (m), Jurit bin Abdullah (m), convicted of drug trafficking in Indonesia may face imminent execution after their appeals for clemency were rejected by the President. Despite the decision to execute the six being announced to the media, the names and nationalities of those facing execution have not been made public.
