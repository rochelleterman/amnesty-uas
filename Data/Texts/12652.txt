"Nepal: Further information on: Fear for safety / fear of torture: Krishna Pahadi (m), founding chairman of the Human Rights and Peace Society (HURPES) 
 Nepal: Further information on: Fear for safety / fear of torture: Krishna Pahadi (m), founding chairman of  the Human Rights and Peace Society  (HURPES)

 | Amnesty International 
 Krishna Pahadi was released on 4 July at the Kathmandu district court. He had spent nearly five months as a prisoner of conscience, detained solely for carrying out his peaceful and legitimate work in defence of human rights.
