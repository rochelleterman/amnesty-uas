"UA 356/93 - Colombia: ""disappearance"": Jose Drigelio Diaz Fierro (known as ""Polo"") 
 UA 356/93 - Colombia: ""disappearance"": Jose Drigelio Diaz Fierro (known as ""Polo"") | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Jose Drigelio Diaz Fierro has been missing since 30 September 1993 when he was abducted by armed men in Ibague, Tolima department. He has worked at a local ironmongers since he was demobilized from the guerrilla organization, the 19 April Movement, Movimiento 19 de Abril (M-19). According to his family, there were no prior death threats or warnings issued against him. "
