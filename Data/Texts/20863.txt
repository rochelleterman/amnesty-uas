 China: Ill-health of Xiao Yunliang, prisoner of conscience: Medical Action. | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Amnesty International is concerned about the poor health  of Xiao Yunliang, a prisoner of conscience, held in       Liaoyang Municipal Detention Centre. Amnesty              International is calling for immediate appropriate        medical treatment for Xiao Yunliang, including            investigation and treatment of his respiratory and eye    problems and his immediate and unconditional release.
