 Mexico: Torture/ill-treatment/Fear for safety/Death threats: Jesús Arturo Torres Parra (m) | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Jesús Arturo Torres Parra was unlawfully detained, then beaten and threatened with death by federal police officers in Chihuahua City, Chihuahua state on 3 March 2009. He was released a few hours later, though he fears that he and his family are at risk of further attacks."
