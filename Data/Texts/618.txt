"Djibouti: Further information on fear of torture or ill-treatment 
 NA 
 Mohamed Kadamy Youssouf, Aicha Dabale Ahmed (f), Ali Mohamed Maki Houmed, Mohamed Daoud Chehem and Kamil Mohamed Ahmed: The five above-named people are now being held in Gabode prison in the capital. They have been taken to court and remanded in custody for investigation into charges including attempted murder and criminal association. They have access to their families and lawyer. Amnesty International remains concerned that Aicha Dabale, who is three months pregnant, and Kamil Mohamed Ahmed, who suffers from partial facial paralysis, are being denied medical care.
