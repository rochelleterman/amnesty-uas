"Further information on UA 148/94 (AFR 54/13/94, 14 April) - Sudan: legal concern / fear of ill-treatment and torture: Sara Nugdallah, Abdel Rasoul al-Nur 
 Further information on UA 148/94 (AFR 54/13/94, 14 April) - Sudan: legal concern / fear of ill-treatment and torture: Sara Nugdallah, Abdel Rasoul al-Nur | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Sara Nugdallah was released from Omdurman Women's Prison on 20 June 1994. Abdel Rasoul al-Nur was released from detention on 27 June because of his deteriorating health. He had been held in a secret detention centre known as a ""ghost house"" since April 1994, where he was ill-treated and held in poor conditions. He is now seriously ill in al-Mulazmein hospital in Omdurman."
