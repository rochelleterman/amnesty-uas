 Uganda: Fear of torture or ill-treatment/medical concern/prisoners of conscience | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 On 10 September 2008 lesbian, gay, bisexual and transgender (LGBT) human rights defenders, Oundo George and Kiiza were arrested in the village of Nabweru, Wakiso district, outside the capital city Kampala.  They are currently detained without charge at Nabweru Police Post and have not been brought before a court within the constitutional limit of 48 hours. "
