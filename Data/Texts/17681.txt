"Palestinian Authority: Prisoner of conscience / legal concern: Daoud Kuttab 
 Palestinian Authority: Prisoner of conscience / legal concern: Daoud Kuttab | Amnesty International 
 Daoud Kuttab, a well-known Palestinian journalist and broadcaster, was arrested by Palestinian police on 20 May and is being held in Ramallah police station. Amnesty International is calling for his immediate and unconditional release as a prisoner of conscience.
