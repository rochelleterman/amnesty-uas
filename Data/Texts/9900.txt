"USA (Indiana): Death penalty: Gary Burris 
 USA (Indiana): Death penalty: Gary Burris | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Gary Burris, black, is scheduled to be executed on 29 November 1995. He was sentenced to death in 1981 for murder. The two other men involved in the crime were sentenced to prison and one has since been released. The original death sentence against Burris was reversed but a later hearing affirmed the death sentence."
