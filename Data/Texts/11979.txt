"Cambodia: Further information on fear for safety / possible extrajudicial executions 
 Cambodia: Further information on fear for safety / possible extrajudicial executions | Amnesty International 
 Chao Sambath, Nhek Bun Chhay, Por Bun Sreu MP, Serey Kosal, Li Seng Hong, Krach Yeuam, Maen Bun Than, Chea Chhut, Thach Sang; and new name: Sam Norin: Amnesty International is concerned to learn that Krach Yeuam and Sam Norin, FUNCINPEC deputy commander of the Special Military Region, have been killed. Amnesty International has also learned that Nhek Bun Chhay, Li Seng Hong and Maen Bun Than are not in custody, but that the authorities are looking for them. The whereabouts of Serey Kosal is still unknown. Por Bun Sreu MP has managed to leave the country and Thach Sang is believed to be no longer at risk.
