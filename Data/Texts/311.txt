"Burundi: ""Disappearance"" / fear of extrajudicial execution 
 Burundi: ""Disappearance"" / fear of extrajudicial execution | Amnesty International 
 Paul Sirahenda, member of parliament and FRODEBU, Hamissi Ndimurukundu, driver: On 3 August 1997, the burnt out car of Paul Sirahenda and his driver Hamissi Ndimurukundo was found near the Tanzanian border. Neither man has been seen since and there are fears that they have been ""disappeared"" by members of the Burundi security forces.
