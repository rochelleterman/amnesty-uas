"Colombia: Fear for Safety - Further information on Forcibly displaced civilian communities 
 Colombia:   Fear for Safety - Further information on   Forcibly displaced civilian communities | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is seriously concerned for the safety of the Afro-Colombian civilian communities of the Cacarica River Basin (Cuenca del Cacarica) in the department of Choc— and the indigenous communities living in the same area, who have received reports that they may be facing an imminent paramilitary incursion.
