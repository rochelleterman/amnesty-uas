"Argentina: Fear for safety: Daniel Straga 
 NA 
 There are fears for the safety of Daniel Straga, a human rights lawyer and journalist, after he received a telephoned death threat on 14 February 1997. He works for the non-governmental organization Coordinadora contra la Represion Policial e Institucional (CORREPI) and is the legal representative for a number of families of victims of police violence. He has been subjected to death threats and intimidation in the past on account of his work.
