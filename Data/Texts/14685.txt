"Turkey: Fear of torture and ill-treatment 
 Turkey: Fear of torture and ill-treatment | Amnesty International 
 According to reports received by Amnesty International today, the Turkish police have raided offices of the People's Democratic Party (HADEP), a legally authorized parliamentary political party, throughout the country. Many people were detained and the police allegedly destroyed office archives.
