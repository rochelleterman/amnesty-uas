 Nepal: Further Information Fear for safety/fear of torture/possible 'disappearance ' | Amnesty International 
 Subindra Buda Magar was released from army custody on 19 November. He was reportedly arrested on 11 October at his home in Balaju, Kathmandu, by four plain clothes security personnel wearing masks. The reason for his arrest and detention is not known.
