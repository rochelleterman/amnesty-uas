 USA: Further information on death penalty / legal concern: Victor Kennedy | Amnesty International 
 Victor Kennedy was executed in Alabama's electric chair   in the first few minutes of 6 August 1999. He had been    sentenced to death in 1982 for the murder, during a       burglary, of 86-year-old Annie Laura Orr, on 24 December  1980.
