"USA (Georgia): Further information on Death penalty, Byron Ashley Parker 
 USA (Georgia): Further information on Death penalty, Byron Ashley Parker | Amnesty International 
 Byron Parker was executed as scheduled in Georgia on the evening of 11 December. He had been sentenced to death in 1984 for the murder of 11-year-old Christie Ann Griffith.
