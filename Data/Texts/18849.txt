"Algeria: ""Disappearance"" / fear for safety: Amine Amrouche 
 NA 
 Amine Amrouche, aged 22, ""disappeared"" from his home in Baraki on 30 January 1997. There were no eye-witnesses to his ""disappearance"", but he is believed to have been abducted. Several other youths are reported to have ""disappeared"" in Baraki during the same week. There is concern for his safety.
