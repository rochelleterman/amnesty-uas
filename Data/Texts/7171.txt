"Mexico: threats / fear for safety: Rocio Culebro and other human rights activists 
 NA 
 On 12 Januray 1996, Rocio Culebro (f), co ordinator of the National Network on Non-governmental Human Rights Organizations ""Full Rights for Everybody"", received a series of threatening phone calls. These came just before she was due to travel to the Inter-American Commission on Human Rights of the OAS in order to present a report on the massacre at Aguas Blancas in June 1995 where 17 peasants were killed by the security forces.
