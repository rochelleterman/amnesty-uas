 Indonesia/East Timor: Further information on fear of torture / health concern / legal concern | Amnesty International 
 Azito Freitas and new names: Evaristo Ximenes, Aniceto Ximenes, Armindo do Rosario, Manuel and Quintino Imanuel de Cristo: While Amnesty International has received no further information about Azito Freitas, the organization has received the names of five other people arrested in relation to the killing of Sergeant-Major Juliao Fraga, an East Timorese military commander in the Indonesian Armed Forces. All five are being held in police custody and two are reported to have been tortured.
