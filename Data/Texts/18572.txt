"Syria: Medical concern / Prisoner of conscience: Nizar Nayyuf 
 Syria: Medical concern / Prisoner of conscience: Nizar Nayyuf | Amnesty International 
 There is serious concern for prisoner of conscience and human rights defender, Nizar Nayyuf, detained since 1992, who is being denied urgent and specialist medical care following a diagnosis of Hodgkin's disease (cancer).
