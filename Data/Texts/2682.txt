 Sudan: Fear of torture or ill-treatment / incommunicado 			detention / prisoner of conscience, Faisal el   Bagir Mohamed, prominent    journalist and human rights   defender | Amnesty International 
 20/06/2001 00:00 
 Faisal el Bagir Mohamed, a prominent journalist and human rights defender, has been detained by Sudanese security   forces and is being held incommunicado. Amnesty           International believes him to be a prisoner of            conscience, and fears that he is at risk of torture or    ill-treatment."
