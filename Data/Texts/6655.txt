"Further information on UA 373/93 (AMR 34/74/93, 25 October) - Guatemala: ""disappearance"": Candelaria Nohemi Marroquin 
 Further information on UA 373/93 (AMR 34/74/93, 25 October) - Guatemala: ""disappearance"": Candelaria Nohemi Marroquin | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Candelaria Nohemi Marroquin has reappeared. She was believed to have been detained on 15 October 1993 when she and her family were followed by three men in olive-coloured uniforms. According to the latest information received she had managed to seek refuge at a friend's home. "
