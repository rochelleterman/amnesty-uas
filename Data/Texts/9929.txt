"USA: Further information on death penalty / legal concern: Michael Domingues 
 USA: Further information on death penalty / legal concern: Michael Domingues | Amnesty International 
 On 1 November 1999, the US Supreme Court announced that   it will not consider Michael Domingues's claim that his   death sentence is illegal under customary international   law and US treaty obligations. As a result, he and some   70 other child offenders on death row in the USA remain   under threat of execution.
