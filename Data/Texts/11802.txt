"Indonesia: Further information on possible ""disappearance"" / fear of torture or ill-treatment 
 Indonesia: Further information on possible ""disappearance"" / fear of torture or ill-treatment | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International has learnt that Izwar Puteh, who    was arrested on 17 July 1999, was released from military  custody on 22 July. He is believed to have been           ill-treated in custody. Ayub Hasan is in military         custody, and has reportedly been ill-treated. It is       thought that he has not yet had access to human rights    lawyers.
