"Sudan: Further information on Fear of Torture/Prisoners of conscience 
 Sudan: Further information on Fear of Torture/Prisoners of conscience | Amnesty International 
 Nureddin Mohammad Abdel Rahim and Bahr al-Din Abdullah Rifah were reportedly released on 11 May having been arrested in Kabkabiya, North Darfur state on 9 May. They were said not to have been tortured or ill-treated during their detention. There is no abstract for this document.
