"Russian Federation: further information on: Chechen Republic: fear for safety: civilians in Sernovodsk 
 Russian Federation: further information on: Chechen Republic: fear for safety: civilians in Sernovodsk | Amnesty International 
 AI is gravely concerned at recent reports of attacks by Russian forces on civilians in the village of Samashki.  According to reports, on 15 to 17 March 1996 Russian forces attacked the village where it is believed that there may be as many as 10,000 civilians, many of them women and children.
