"UA 02/94 - Socialist Republic of Viet Nam: death penalty: Nguyen Quang Thanh 
 UA 02/94 - Socialist Republic of Viet Nam: death penalty: Nguyen Quang Thanh | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Nguyen Quang Thanh, a former navy captain, was sentenced to death on 30 December 1993 by a military court in Danang City. He had been convicted of killing his wife in 1987."
