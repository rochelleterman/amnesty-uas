"Egypt: Death penalty: Ibrahim Mohammad 'Allam 
 Egypt: Death penalty: Ibrahim Mohammad 'Allam | Amnesty International 
 On 19 February 1997 the Cairo criminal court sentenced Ibrahim Mohammad 'Allam to death, after finding him guilty of murder. The sentence has been passed on to the Mufti, the highest religious authority in the country, for approval.
