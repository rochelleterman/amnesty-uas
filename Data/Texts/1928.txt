"Rwanda: Further information on fear for safety / fear of ill-treatment / arbitrary arrest 
 Rwanda: Further information on fear for safety / fear of ill-treatment / arbitrary arrest | Amnesty International 
 Amiel Nkuriza, director of Intego and editor of Le Partisan, Appolos Hakizimana, journalist at Intego, Isaie Niyoyita, editor of Intego: Amiel Nkuriza was released on 13 August. He was allegedly beaten when he was first detained on 6 August 1996 and again on his release. While in custody a soldier warned him that his life would be at risk if he wrote any further ""subversive"" articles. Appolos Hakizimana remains in military detention at the Brigade at Muhima and Isaie Niyoyita is believed to still be in hiding.
