 Mexico: Further information: Police allowing threats to migrants in Mexico | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Migrants and staff at the Belén Migrants shelter (Belén, Posada del Migrante) in the Mexican state of Coahuila are in danger because the state police are not properly protecting them. One migrant was recently threatened in full view of a policeman, who did not take any action."
