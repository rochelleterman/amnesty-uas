 Iran: Further information on fear of imminent execution: Delara Darabi (f) | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Juvenile offender Delara Darabi was granted a two month stay of execution by the Head of the Judiciary on 19 April 2009, the day before she was due to be executed. Delara Darabi remains at risk of being executed unless the family of the victim agrees on compensation."
