"Colombia: Fear for safety 
 Colombia: Fear for safety | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International fears for the safety of the         civilian population of Santo Domingo, Bolivar department, including the families of Otoniel Cardenas and Edilberto  Cardenas Agamez both of whom have been killed by          paramilitaries who have set up a new base nearby.         Thereare fears, following threats, that further killings  are planned.
