"Brazil: Further information on fear for safety / medical concern: Vagner Marcos da Silva 
 Brazil: Further information on fear for safety / medical concern: Vagner Marcos da Silva | Amnesty International 
 There are concerns for the deteriorating health of Vagner Marcos da Silva' who is awaiting trial on charges of drug-dealing and attempted homicide. He was shot by four military policeman during his arrest and has received only minimal medical treatment to date.
