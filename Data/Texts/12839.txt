"Pakistan: Fear for Safety: Dr Shazia Khalid (f), medical doctor 
 Pakistan: Fear for Safety: Dr Shazia Khalid (f), medical doctor | Amnesty International 
 Dr Shazia Khalid, who has been the victim of rape, is reportedly being held at a house in Karachi under the ""unofficial protection"" of the police and army. She is not allowed access to doctors, lawyers or visitors of her choice. Amnesty International is concerned for her safety.
