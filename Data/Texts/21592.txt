 Ukraine: Ukraine must not return asylum-seekers: Further information | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 The European Court of Human Rights (ECtHR) has ordered Ukraine not to forcibly return four men to Uzbekistan, where they would be at risk of torture or other ill-treatment. However, the four men could still be at risk of forcible return if Ukraine chooses to disregard this request. "
