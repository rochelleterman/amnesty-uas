"Sudan: Fear of torture / possible prisoners of conscience 
 Sudan: Fear of torture / possible prisoners of conscience | Amnesty International 
 Mudawi Ibrahim Adam, Kamal Abdel Rahman, Abdel Rahman al-Amin and Abdel Basit Abbas Hussein: Amnesty International fears that the named above are at risk of torture in the security wing of Kober Prison in Khartoum. They have been accused of having links with the National Democratic Alliance.
