 China: Fear of torture or ill-treatment/ prisoner of conscience: Bu Dongwei (also known as David Bu), (m)

 | Amnesty International 
 Bu Dongwei, aged 38, also known as David Bu, was assigned to two-and-a-half years' ""Re-education through Labour"" (RTL) on 19 June in connection with his activities as a member of the Falun Gong spiritual movement. He is detained at an undisclosed location, and Amnesty International considers him to be a prisoner of conscience. He is at serious risk of torture or ill-treatment.
