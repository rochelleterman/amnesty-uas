"UA 427/94 - Israel and the Occupied Territories: fear of torture: over 400 Palestinians 
 UA 427/94 - Israel and the Occupied Territories: fear of torture: over 400 Palestinians | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 The use of ""increased physical pressure"" during the interrogation of suspected members of the Islamic opposition group Hamas is reported to have been sanctioned by the Israeli authorities, raising fears for the safety of over 400 Palestinians taken into detention in the West Bank in recent weeks."
