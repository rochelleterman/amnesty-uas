"Further information on UA 05/94 (MDE 13/01/94, 12 January) - Iran: death penalty: Mehdi Dibaj 
 Further information on UA 05/94 (MDE 13/01/94, 12 January) - Iran: death penalty: Mehdi Dibaj | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 AI has received confirmation that Mehdi Dibaj was released from prison in Sari on 16 January 1994. He had earlier been sentenced to death on charges of apostasy (he converted to Christianity 45 years ago). However, according to some reports, he may face new charges."
