"USA (Indiana): Further information on death penalty / Legal concern: David Leon Woods (m) 
 USA (Indiana): Further information on death penalty / Legal concern: David Leon Woods (m) | Amnesty International 
 David Woods, aged 42,  was executed in Indiana on 4 May. He had been sentenced to death in 1985 for the murder of Juan Placencia in 1984. David Woods, who was 19 years old at the time of the crime, had spent more than half his life on death row.
