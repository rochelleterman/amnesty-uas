 Syria: Further information on Fear of torture and other ill-treatment/detention without charge: Ma’rouf Mulla Ahmed (m) | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Kurdish activist Ma’rouf Mulla Ahmed has been detained without charge in Branch 285 of State Security in Damascus since August 2007. He continues to be interrogated and is still at risk of torture or other ill-treatment. He may be a prisoner of conscience, held for his peaceful political activities."
