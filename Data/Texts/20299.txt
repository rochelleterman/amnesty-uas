 Mexico: Further information: Homes at risk of demolition in land dispute | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Fifteen armed men hired by a local landowning family threatened to demolish a couple's home in an area called Lomas del Poleo, Chihuahua state, northern Mexico, on 3 May. The inhabitants of Lomas del Poleo are at risk of further such attacks. "
