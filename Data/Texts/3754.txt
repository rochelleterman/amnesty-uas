"Bolivia: Fear for safety / Excessive use of force 
 Bolivia: Fear for safety / Excessive use of force | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is seriously concerned for the safety of demonstrators at mass protests around the country, following the apparent use of excessive force by the army and the police. Since 20 September, over 50 people, including three soldiers, have reportedly been killed and several hundred have been injured.
