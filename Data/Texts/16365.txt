"Bosnia-Herzegovina: Further information on fear for safety: non-Serbs in Bosnian Serb-controlled areas 
 Bosnia-Herzegovina: Further information on fear for safety: non-Serbs in Bosnian Serb-controlled areas | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 AI remains gravely concerned about the situation in northwest Bosnia-Herzogovina where Bosnian Serb forces and paramilitary forces from Serbia continue to commit gross human rights abuses against the remaining Muslims and Croats in the areas which they control. AI continues to receive reports of deliberate and arbitrary killings and the arbitrary detention of men of military age."
