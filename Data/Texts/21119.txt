 North Korea: Fears for forcibly returned North Koreans | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 At least nine North Koreans have reportedly been forcibly returned from China to North Korea where they are at serious risk of arbitrary detention, torture and other ill-treatment, enforced disappearance, forced labour, and death. Other North Koreans currently detained in China are also in danger of being forcibly returned to North Korea."
