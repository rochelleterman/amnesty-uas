"USA (Texas): Further information on death Penalty: Betty Lou Beets 
 USA (Texas): Further information on death Penalty: Betty Lou Beets | Amnesty International 
 Betty Lou Beets remains scheduled for execution in Texas  on 24 February 2000. On 18 February, her attorneys filed  a law suit against the Texas Board of Pardons and Paroles (BPP) asserting that the BPP violated its own regulations by failing to assess Betty Beets's case under a special   clemency review program that it established for all       battered women imprisoned for killing a family member.
