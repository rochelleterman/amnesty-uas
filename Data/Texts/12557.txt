 Nepal: Fear for safety/ possible ""disappearance"" | Amnesty International 
 Journalist Shakti Raj Bhattarai was reportedly arrested in Pokhara on 1 February. Lekhnath Sapkota was reportedly arrested in Kathmandu three days later. The men's whereabouts are now unknown, and Amnesty International is concerned for their safety.
