 Indonesia: Fear for safety / ill-treatment or torture | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is concerned for the safety of 24 people arrested in Lhokseumawe, North Aceh. They were arrested after a crowd of people were involved in the beating of a member of the security forces and the pulling down of an Indonesian national flag.
