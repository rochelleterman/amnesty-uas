"USA (Alabama): Death penalty / legal concern: Gregory Wynn 
 USA (Alabama): Death penalty / legal concern: Gregory Wynn | Amnesty International 
 At a trial due to begin in Alabama on 23 August 1999, the prosecution will seek a death sentence against Gregory    Wynn for a murder he is accused of committing when he was 17 years old. International law forbids the use of the    death penalty for crimes committed by under 18-year-olds.
