"Mexico: Fear for safety 
 Mexico: Fear for safety | Amnesty International 
 Juan Juarez Perez and Jose Lopez Hernandez, indigenous rights activists, and 50 families from the municipality of Sabanilla, Chiapas: At least four people, including children, have been killed by members of the paramilitary group Paz y Justicia (Peace and Justice) in the last month. AI is seriously concerned for the safety of more than 50 indigenous families, many of whom have had to flee in fear for their lives.
