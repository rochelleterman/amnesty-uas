 Colombia: Fear for safety: Human rights workers and trade  unionists in Medellín, Antioquia department.		

 | Amnesty International 
  
 There is serious concern for the safety of human rights   workers and trade unionists in Medellín, after two bombs  exploded outside the offices of a trade union and a human rights organisation in the city on 29 August 1999.
