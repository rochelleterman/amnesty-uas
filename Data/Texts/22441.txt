 Israel/Occupied Palestinian Territories: Further information: Administrative detainee transferred to Gaza: Hana Shalabi | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Palestinian woman Hana Shalabi ended her hunger strike on 28 March. She has been transferred to the Gaza Strip in what may amount to forcible transfer or deportation. Hana Shalabi spent 43 days on hunger strike in protest against her detention without charge or trial. "
