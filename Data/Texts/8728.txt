 USA  (California:) Further information on Stephen Wayne Anderson (m), white, aged 48 | Amnesty International 
 Stephen Wayne Anderson was executed by lethal injection in the early hours of 29 January 2002. Convicted of the murder of 81-year-old Elizabeth Lyman, Anderson had spent 20 years on death row.
