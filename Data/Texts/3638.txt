"Argentina: Fear for Safety/ Death threats: Marcelino ALTAMIRANO 
 Argentina: Fear for Safety/ Death threats: Marcelino ALTAMIRANO | Amnesty International 
 Marcelino Altamirano has been subject to further threats and intimidation, reportedly in relation to his work as coordinator of the street children's home La Casita del Puente Afectivo (The Little House of the Bridge of Affection) in the city of Mendoza. Amnesty International remains seriously concerned for his safety.
