"UA 162/93 - Colombia: fear of torture / ""disappearance"": Samuel Ramirez Rincon 
 UA 162/93 - Colombia: fear of torture / ""disappearance"": Samuel Ramirez Rincon | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 There is concern for the safety of Samuel Ramirez Rincon, who was reportedly detained with another man, by members of the Colombian armed forces on 3 May 1993 in the town of Cucuta, Norte de Santander department. The second man, following his release, claimed that Samuel Ramirez had been tortured in detention. A petition of habeas corpus was presented on his behalf to the First Municipal Penal Judge of Cucuta and copied to the local Human Rights Ombudsman. However the judge responded that despite making inquiries to all security forces in the area, he was unable to find any information about the detention of Samuel Ramirez and his whereabouts reamin unknown.
