 Liberia: Further Information on Fear for safety/risk of torture or ill-treatment/Incommunicado detention/health concern | Amnesty International 
 Hassan Bility and Sheikh K.M. Sackor are believed to have been moved between various places of detention in the     capital, Monrovia, including the National Bureau of       Investigation and the National Security Agency. There has been no official confirmation of where they are being     held and they have consistently been denied access to     lawyers, relatives and medical care.
