"Colombia: Fear for safety/possible ""disappearances"" 
 Colombia: Fear for safety/possible ""disappearances"" | Amnesty International 
 Paramilitary gunmen have reportedly abducted five men in a raid on a village in Tolima department. They have also threatened to kill members of the ANTHOC health workers' union in Tolima, and are believed to have abducted one member of the union.
