"Indonesia: Fear for safety/torture or ill-treatment 
 Indonesia: Fear for safety/torture or ill-treatment | Amnesty International 
 Zulkarnain Ismail and Abdul Muthaleb Abdullah were arrested in the early hours of 25 July by military and police in Pidie District, Aceh Province. They are being detained at Mereudu Police Sector, (Polsek Mereudu), where both have reportedly been tortured.
