 Colombia: Fear for Safety / possible ""Disappearances"" | Amnesty International 
 A tradesman has ""disappeared"" after threats from paramilitaries allied to the armed forces who have        ordered several communities to leave their homes in the heavily militarised  region in central  Colombia. In      light of the continued failure of the armed  forces to    protect the civilian population of the region   Amnesty   International fears for its safety.
