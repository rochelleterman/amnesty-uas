 Indonesia: Fear of torture or ill-treatment / medical concern | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is concerned for the safety of twenty-four people who are being detained in South Sulawesi Province. There are reports that several of them have been beaten in custody and that they have not received adequate medical care for injuries sustained at the time of their arrest.
