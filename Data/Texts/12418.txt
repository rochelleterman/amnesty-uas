"Nepal: Further Information on Fear for safety/possible ""disappearance"" 
 Nepal: Further Information on Fear for safety/possible ""disappearance"" | Amnesty International 
 Kumar Prasad Pant was released from the Hanumandokha District Police Office (DPO) in Kathmandu on 6 January at 1pm. He was handed over to his family on condition that he report back to the DPO every three days. There is no further information on the whereabouts of the other men, who were all arrested in Kathmandu district between 31 August and 1 December.
