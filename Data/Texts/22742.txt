 Syria: Further information on Fear of torture or other ill-treatment: Maryam Kallis (f) | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Another UK national is now known to have been arrested shortly after Maryam Kallis. Like her, Yasser Zahur Ahmed is being held without access to a lawyer or to his family and without charge in a detention centre in the Kafr Sousa district of Damascus. Both are now known to be accused of involvement in terrorism."
