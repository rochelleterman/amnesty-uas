"UA 83/93 - Iran: fear of torture: Shahpour Naroui, Hamid Naroui 
 UA 83/93 - Iran: fear of torture: Shahpour Naroui, Hamid Naroui | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Two Naroui tribesmen, Shahpour Naroui and Hamid Naroui, were reportedly arrested approximately one week ago in Kerman. The reasons for their arrest and their present whereabouts are not known. AI remains concerned about continuing reports of arbitrary arrests, detention, torture and execution of members of the Naroui tribe in Baluchistan-Sistan region of Iran. "
