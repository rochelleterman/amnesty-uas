 Yemen: Imminent execution: Bashir Sultan Mohamed al-Ja'fari  | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Bashir Sultan Mohamed al-Ja'fari, who is a solider in the Yemeni army, is facing imminent execution. According to information received by Amnesty International, he could be executed as early as 4 February 2009. He has exhausted all his appeals and the death sentence against him was ratified by the President in October 2008."
