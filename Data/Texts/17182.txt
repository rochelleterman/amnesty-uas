"Iran: Death penalty / flogging / torture / unfair trial 
 Iran: Death penalty / flogging / torture / unfair trial | Amnesty International 
 Hedayatollah Zendehdel, businessman (photo, standing), Abolghasem Majd-Abkahi, businessman, Alireza Yazdanshenas, former air force officer, Kurosh Nikakhtar, 28 years old, journalist: AI is gravely concerned at reports that, in two separate cases, the four men named above have been sentenced to death after Islamic Revolutionary Court trials in Tehran. Kurosh Nikakhta was arrested in connection with an incident in which shots were fired at President Ali Akbar Hashemi Rafsanjani. The others were charged with gathering classified military information during the Iran-Iraq war and passing in to foreign countries.
