 Russia (Chechen Republic): Further information on Fear of torture or ill-treatment / Arbitrary arrest / Incommunicado detention | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 The four members of Dr. Omar Khambiev's medical team:     Abubakar Ismailov, Samrudy Djabrailov, Aslanbek           Zelimkhanovich Djankhutov and Mussa Goitaevnamed are      reportedly still detained incommunicado in ""filtration    camps"", where Amnesty International fears they may be     tortured or ill-treated.  Apart from the four men named   above, the rest of the medical team have all reportedly   since been released. There is no information about the    current whereabouts of the 81 civilian patients.
