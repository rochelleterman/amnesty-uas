"Colombia: Fear for safety 
 Colombia: Fear for safety | Amnesty International 
 Paramilitary groups are threatening to kill representatives of the forcibly displaced communities of  the Cacarica River Basin, who are preparing to return to  the homes they were forced out of by a joint army and     paramilitary offensive in 1997. Amnesty International is  concerned for their safety.
