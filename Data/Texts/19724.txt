 Sudan: Act now to end the human rights crisis in Darfur: Closing text | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 The Government of Sudan now facilitates full and free     access to humanitarian agencies in Darfur. United Nations human rights monitors and African Union ceasefire         monitors are in place although their numbers and mandate  are limited. An increased international presence has been agreed in Darfur to ensure the protection of civilian     populations, including internally displaced persons in    camps.
