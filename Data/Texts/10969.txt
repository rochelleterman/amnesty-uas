 China: Further information on: Fear of execution | Amnesty International 
 The Chinese authorities have not yet made any official announcement about the fate of Tenzin Deleg Rinpoche, amid conflicting reports about when the two year period of suspension on his death sentence comes to an end. Mass appeals now could help to save his life.
