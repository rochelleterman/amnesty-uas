"Israel/Occupied Territories: Further information on Fear of torture/legal concern/possible prisoner of conscience, Daoud al-Dir'awi 
 Israel/Occupied Territories: Further information on  Fear of torture/legal concern/possible prisoner of conscience, Daoud al-Dir'awi | Amnesty International 
 A judge has ordered that Daoud al-Dir'awi be released on bail, but the army has responded by issuing a six-month administrative detention order, renewable indefinitely. He is now held in Megiddo Prison, in Israel.
