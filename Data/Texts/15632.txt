"Russian Federation: Fear of torture / ill-treatment, Viktor Knaus 
 Russian Federation: Fear of torture / ill-treatment, Viktor Knaus | Amnesty International 
 Fifteen-year-old Victor Knaus has reportedly been beaten and threatened by Russian police in order to force him to confess to the murder of two young boys. Evidence allegedly extracted under duress may be used in the trial against him, which is likely to begin within weeks.
