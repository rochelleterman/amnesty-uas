 Iran: Further information on: Fear of imminent execution/fear of flogging, Leyla  M | Amnesty International 
 Leyla Mafi, who was sentenced to death for ""morality-related"" offences arising from her being forced into prostitution as a child, is no longer in imminent danger of execution. The Iranian authorities are reviewing her case, in an attempt to investigate press reports that Leyla Mafi has a mental age of eight.
