"People's Republic of China (PRC): Further information on Death penalty 
 People's Republic of China (PRC): Further information on Death penalty | Amnesty International 
 Four men, Jin Ruchao, Wang Yushun, Hao Fengqin, and Hu Xiaohong were reportedly executed on 29 April. Amnesty International was concerned that they may have been executed and convicted without full investigation of the crime or fair trial as the authorities were under pressure to solve this high-profile crime quickly.
