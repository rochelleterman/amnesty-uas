 Colombia: Fear for safety/possible ""disappearance"",  Enrique Valencia | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is concerned for the safety of Enrique Valencia, a peasant farmer from the Cimitarra River Valley in central Colombia. He has not been seen since 27 May and it is feared he may have ""disappeared"".
