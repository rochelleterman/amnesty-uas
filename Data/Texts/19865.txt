 Chile: Further information on intimidation/ harassment: Elena Varela López | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Documentary filmmaker Elena Varela López was released on 13 August 2008, after more than three months in custody. She is now subject to aresto domiciliario nocturna (house arrest between 10pm and 8am), and arraigo nacional (she cannot leave the country)."
