"USA (Maryland): Further Information on Death penalty, Steven Howard Oken 
 USA (Maryland): Further Information on Death penalty, Steven Howard Oken | Amnesty International 
 Steven Oken was executed in Maryland shortly after 9pm local time on 17 June 2004. He was sentenced to death in 1991 for the 1987 rape and murder of 20-year-old Dawn Marie Garvin. He was the first person executed in Maryland since November 1998.
