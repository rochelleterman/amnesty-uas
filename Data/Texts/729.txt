"Ethiopia: Further information on health concern: Asrat Woldeyes 
 Ethiopia: Further information on health concern: Asrat Woldeyes | Amnesty International 
 On 27 December 1998 the Ethiopian government finally      allowed Dr (Professor) Asrat Woldeyes to go abroad for    urgently needed medical treatment. Shortly after, the     prosecutor withdrew the charges against him, saying he    was physically unfit to stand trial.
