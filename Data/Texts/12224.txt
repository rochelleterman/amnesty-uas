"Malaysia: Fear of torture or ill-treatment/incommunicado detention, Abdul Manaf Kasmuri. 
 Malaysia:  Fear of torture or ill-treatment/incommunicado detention, Abdul Manaf Kasmuri. | Amnesty International 
 Abdul Manaf Kasmuri was arrested on the night of 20 February 2003 by the Malaysian police under the Internal Security Act (ISA), which allows for indefinite detention without charge or trial. Amnesty International is concerned that Abdul Manaf Kasmuri is at risk of torture or ill-treatment during police detention.
