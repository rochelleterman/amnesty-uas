 Turkmenistan: Fear of imminent execution / legal concern: Anton Yuryevich Chernov
 | Amnesty International 
 Anton Yuryevich CHERNOV is believed to be in imminent danger of execution. He was sentenced to death for murder on 24 November 1997 and has alleged that his confession was signed under duress.
