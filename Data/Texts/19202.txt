"Tunisia:Health concern for hunger strike prisoners 
 Tunisia:Health concern for hunger strike prisoners | Amnesty International 
 There is serious concern for the health of prisoners in   Tunisia currently on hunger strike, Fathi                 Karraoud,Abdellatif Bouhajila,  Yassine Ben Zarti, Ahmed  Amari,  Zuhair Yacoub,  Abdallah Drissa, Noureddine       Amdouni and at least ten others   have been on hunger     strike repeatedly in recent months. Prison conditions are extremely poor and prisoners do not have access to        medical care.
