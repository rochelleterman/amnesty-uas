"Indonesia: Fear for safety / possible ""disappearance"" / extrajudicial execution 
 Indonesia: Fear for safety / possible ""disappearance"" / extrajudicial execution | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is concerned for the safety of 18   people, who ""disappeared"" after they were arrested in     Blang Merandeh Village, in the province of Aceh, northern Sumatra, on 23 July 1999. The soldiers who arrested them  had reportedly just killed at least 29 people, including: Abdul Manaf; Abdul Wahid,  Adnan,  Ali,  Amir, Effendi    Abdullah, Husaini, Imum Chik, Jamalul Ade, Maddin, M Amin N, Ma'ruf, M Harun, M Husen, M Muin Bahrum, Muamin,       Muhammad Ali, Muhammad Din, Munir, M. Yusuf, Saidi,       Syamsuar, Syamsul, Tarmizi Daud, Tengku Bantakiah, Tengku Ni, Tengku Syamsuar, Usman bin Bantakiah and Zubir.
