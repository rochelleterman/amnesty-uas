"Jamaica: Further information on death penalty 
 Jamaica: Further information on death penalty | Amnesty International 
 Samuel Lindsay, Henry McKoy, Lansford James: Amnesty International has learned that Henry McKoy and Lansford James are scheduled to be executed on 27 May and 29 May 1997 respectively in what would be the first executions in Jamaica since 1988. Samuel Lindsay has been granted a stay of execution.
