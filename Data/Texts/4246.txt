 Colombia: Further information on Fear for Safety/Possible ""Disappearances"" | Amnesty International 
 Army-backed paramilitaries have released a number of people, Nelson Yaguilu, Erminia Encinosa and her two sons Ramon and Pedro Encinosa, as well as Ramon Aguila, Maria Elena and Maria Cecilia Gaitan among others, who were abducted in October.
