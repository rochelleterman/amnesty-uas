"Turkey/Greece: Fear for safety, torture, ill-treatment 
 Turkey/Greece: Fear for safety, torture, ill-treatment | Amnesty International 
 A large group of African nationals has been deported from Istanbul to the Ipsala border area between Turkey and Greece where they remain stranded without food, water or shelter. There are reports of ill-treatment and torture, including rape, and that three people have already died.
