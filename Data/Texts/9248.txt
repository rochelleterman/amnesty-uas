"USA (Texas): Further information on: death penalty: Thomas Joe Miller-El 
 USA (Texas): Further information on: death penalty: Thomas Joe Miller-El | Amnesty International 
 The above, a black man previously scheduled to be executed in October 1995 and January 1996, is now scheduled to be executed on 30 July 1996. Thomas Miller-El was sentenced to death for murder. According to information received by AI, he was in extremely bad health during his trial and was still suffering from the effects of being shot by police at the time of his arrest. The trial judge had ordered his medication to be stopped and he drifted in and out of consciousness for much of the three-month hearing.
