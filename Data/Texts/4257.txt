"Colombia: Further information on ""disappearance"" / apparent extrajudicial executions / fear for safety: Civilian population of the communities of northeastern Choco 
 NA 
 Amnesty International remains seriously concerned for the safety of members of the civilian communities in the northeast of the Choco department where paramilitary groups allied to the security forces continue to operate and commit serious human rights violations. Amongst the information that has been received are details of the killings of Dagoberto Martinez and a man identified only as Francisco, who were reportedly abducted by paramilitary forces and subsequently tortured and killed.
