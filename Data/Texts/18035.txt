 Further information on UA 288/93 (MDE 17/07/93, 24 August) - Kuwait: death penalty: Muhammad 'Ali Qulaib al-Rashidi (please note full name) | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 On 4 June 1994 the Court of Cassation upheld the death sentence imposed on Muhammad 'Ali Qulaib al-Rashidi on 21 August 1993 by the Criminal Court. His sentence may now be carrried out if it is ratified by the Amir of Kuwait."
