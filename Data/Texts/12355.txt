 Malaysia: Further information on Fear of torture or ill-treatment/Fear of forcible return | Amnesty International 
 The eight asylum seekers, Rusli bin Abas (m), Muhamad Hanafiah bin Abdullah, (m), Sauri bin Sabran, (m), Muhamad Iqbal bin Yusri (m), Saba Sabirin bin Mudadia (m), Zulkifly bin Basyah (m) and one woman and her 13-month- old child (names unknown) are no longer facing imminent forcible repatriation to Nanggroe Aceh Darussalam (NAD) province in Indonesia, where they would be at risk of serious human rights violations.
