 Turkey: Further information on torture/ill-treatment 

 | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 A delegation of Kurdish women  (members of the Peace  Mothers Initiative) from eastern Turkey have reportedly   been tortured and ill-treated, after they      travelled  to the Kurdish regio in North Iraq to mediate in the      civil conflict. They were interrogated, sexually abused   by gendarmerie, as well as  torture and ill-treatement .  They are still in custody."
