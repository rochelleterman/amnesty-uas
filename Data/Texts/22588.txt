 Saudi ""Day Of Rage"" Protester To Go On Trial: Khaled al-Johani | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 The only man to demonstrate on the “Day of Rage” in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia, on 11 March 2011, is scheduled to stand trial on 22 February. Amnesty International considers him to be a prisoner of conscience, held solely for peacefully exercising his rights to freedom of expression and assembly."
