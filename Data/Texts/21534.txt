 Belarus: Further Information on Torture and other ill-treatment/health concern: Emanuel Zeltser | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Emanuel Zeltser's trial began on 30 July 2008. He is being tried on charges of “using forged documents”, “possession and smuggling of illegal drugs” and “commercial espionage.” The trial is being conducted behind closed doors despite requests from his family and representatives of the US State Department to be allowed to attend."
