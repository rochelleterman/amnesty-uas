"Sudan: Further information on Incommunicado detention/Detention without charge 
 Sudan: Further information on Incommunicado detention/Detention without charge | Amnesty International 
 Hashem al-Tayeb Yusuf was reportedly released on 15 January 2003. Al-Shafi' al-Tayeb Yusuf remains detained   without charge or access to lawyers or judicial review at Kober Prison in Khartoum North. Their brother, Lenin      al-Tayeb Yusuf, has now been detained and Amnesty         International fears he is at risk of torture and          prolonged incommunicado detention.
