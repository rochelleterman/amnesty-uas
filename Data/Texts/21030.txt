 India: Further information: Indian environmentalist released from jail | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Dr Harihar Patel, one of the two human rights defenders and environmental rights campaigners who have spent two months in the state of Chhattisgarh, has been released on bail by a local court. He and Ramesh Agrawal had been arrested on false charges on 28 May 2011."
