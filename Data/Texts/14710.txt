 Turkey: Torture & ill-treatment/fear for safety/prisoner of conscience | Amnesty International 
 Leading human rights defender Osman Baydemir has been arrested while he was with a delegation investigating human rights abuses in southeast Turkey. His whereabouts are now unknown, and he is at grave risk of torture. Eighteen-year-old Rasim Aar was arrested after he testified to the delegation, and has reportedly been tortured in custody.
