 Turkey: Further information on fear of ""disappearance"" / torture: Kenan Bilgin | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 No further news has been heard of Kenan Bilgin. His family and lawyers have made repeated applications for information to the Security Headquarters and the State Security Court Prosecutor. Both continue to deny any knowledge of Kenan Bilgin. He has ""disappeared"" in detention."
