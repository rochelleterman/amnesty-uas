"Sudan: Fear of Amputation, Mohamed Hassan Hamdan 
 Sudan: Fear of Amputation, Mohamed Hassan Hamdan | Amnesty International 
 On 14 October, sixteen-year-old Mohamed Hassan Hamdan was sentenced to ""cross amputation"" (amputation of the right hand and left leg) by a Special Court in the city of Nyala, South Darfur state. Such a punishment constitutes torture and contravenes international human rights standards ratified by the Sudan
