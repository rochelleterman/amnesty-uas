"Turkey: Further information on fear of torture / health concern 
 Turkey: Further information on fear of torture / health concern | Amnesty International 
 Recep Marasli, publisher, Nuran Marasli (f), Levent Bakanay: Recep Marasli and his wife Nuran Marasli were taken before a judge on 12 March 1997. Recep Marasli was committed to prison, as there was an arrest warrant in force for him in connection with his publications and writings. Nuran Marasli was released. Neither had been ill-treated. According to an unconfirmed report, Levent Bakanay had been released earlier.
