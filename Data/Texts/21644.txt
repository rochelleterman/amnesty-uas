 Kyrgyzstan: Further information: Human rights defender jailed for life: Azimzhan Askarov | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Azimzhan Askarov, director of the human rights organization Vozdukh (Air) in Kyrgyzstan, has been sentenced to life imprisonment after an unfair trial Amnesty International considers him to be a prisoner of conscience, targeted in response to his legitimate human rights activities."
