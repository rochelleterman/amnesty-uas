 Tajikistan: Journalist arrested and reportedly tortured | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Tajikistani BBC journalist Urunboy Usmonov is awaiting trial after being charged with participation in the banned Islamic movement Hizb-ut-Tahrir. Amnesty International believes that he has been detained due to his journalistic work on Hizb-ut-Tahrir. He may have been tortured or ill-treated in detention. "
