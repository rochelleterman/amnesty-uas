 Indonesia: Death penalty / fear of imminent execution | Amnesty International 
 Six people convicted of drug trafficking in Indonesia may face imminent execution after their appeals for clemency  were rejected by the President. Despite the decision to   execute the six being announced to the media, the names   and nationalities of those facing execution have not been made public.
