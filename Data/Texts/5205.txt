"Colombia: Further Information on Fear for Safety 
 Colombia: Further Information on Fear for Safety | Amnesty International 
 Domingo Tovar Arrieta, Director of the Human Rights Department of the Central Unitaria de Trabajadores, (CUT), Colombian Trade Union Confederation has reportedly received a renewed death threat. Amnesty International is concerned for his safety and that of other members of the CUT.
