 Iran: Death Penalty/Fear of Imminent Execution

 | Amnesty International 
 A woman and a man identified only as Akram and Behnam are at imminent risk of execution. Their death sentences have been upheld by the Supreme Court. At this stage the Head of the Judiciary has the power to order a stay of execution and a review of the case.
