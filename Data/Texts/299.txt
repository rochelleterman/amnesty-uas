"Burundi: Further information on fear of torture and ill-treatment / deaths in custody 
 Burundi: Further information on fear of torture and ill-treatment / deaths in custody | Amnesty International 
 Detainees at Socarti military camp, Kamenge district, Bujumbura, including: Jerome Pazirupwabo, Pascal ""Le Vieux"", Georges Nimbona, Bosco Ndikumana, Alexis, Omar, Samuel Ndayishiniye, Gihonga, Alexis ""Papi"", Berahino, Elie Ngendabadashaka, Eric: Amnesty International has learned that the above men have been transferred to Mpimba central prison, Bujumbura and that their cases have been forwarded to the judiciary, although they are yet to be formally charged in court. There continues to be concern, however, for the remaining detainees in Socarti military camp.
