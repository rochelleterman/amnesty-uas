"Iran: Further information on: Medical concern/Fear for safety/incommunicado detention, Akbar Ganji 
 Iran: Further information on: Medical concern/Fear for safety/incommunicado detention, Akbar Ganji | Amnesty International 
 Investigative journalist Akbar Ganji has been on hunger strike for 33 days in protest at being denied access to medical care for chronic asthma, despite specialist recommendation that he be treated. Amnesty International considers him a prisoner of conscience, detained solely for his peaceful criticism of the authorities.
