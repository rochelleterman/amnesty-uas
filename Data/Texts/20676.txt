 USA: Further information on Death penalty/Legal concern: Charles Dean Hood (m) | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 On 9 September 2008, the Texas Court of Criminal Appeals issued a stay of execution for Charles Hood, who was scheduled to be executed on 10 September. He was sentenced to death in 1990 for the murders of Ronald Williamson and Tracie Lynn Wallace in 1989. "
