"Rwanda: Further information on fear for safety: Fidele Uwizeye 
 Rwanda: Further information on fear for safety: Fidele Uwizeye | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International has learnt that Fidele Uwizeye is currently detained in Kimironko where he is facing charges of endangering the security of the state. His health reportedly suffered as a result of the poor conditions he was held in during his initial period of detention.
