"Syria: Further information on Prisoner of conscience 
 Syria: Further information on  Prisoner of conscience | Amnesty International 
 On 12 September, Syrian Political security agents (al-Amn al-Siyassi) arrested prisoners of conscience, Habib Isa and Fawaz Tello as part of a continuing crackdown on the activities of political dissents who criticise the government. Both men are being held incommunicado and are at risk of torture or ill-treatment.
