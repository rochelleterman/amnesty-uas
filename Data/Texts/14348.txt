"Turkey: Incommunicado detention/fear of torture or ill-treatment: Ihsan Tekes (m) 
 Turkey: Incommunicado detention/fear of torture or ill-treatment: Ihsan Tekes (m) | Amnesty International 
 Ihsan Tekes, an alleged member of the armed separatist group, the Kurdistan Workers Party (PKK), was reportedly detained by the Turkish Army on 19 September 2007. It is not known where he is being held and he may be at risk of torture or other ill-treatment.
