"UA 359/94 - Colombia: possible ""disappearance"" / fear for safety: Jairo Barahona, Elena Marquez 
 UA 359/94 - Colombia: possible ""disappearance"" / fear for safety: Jairo Barahona, Elena Marquez | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Jairo Barahona was abducted violently from his home by two men on 29 September 1994. His detention has not been acknowledged and there is concern for his safety. His wife and children were threatened by the men, who identified themselves as member sof the security forces. Jairo Barahona is a well-known community leader and founder of the local Human Rights Committee. He is financial and commercial head of a public services operation in Pailitas."
