"Argentina: Fear for Safety/Death threats, Clara Britos. 
 Argentina: Fear for Safety/Death threats, Clara Britos. | Amnesty International 
 On the evening of 14 February 2003 Clara Britos was violently accosted near her house by three unidentified individuals who threatened to kill her and her family. This is the latest in a series of threats and intimidating behaviour that Clara Britos has suffered. Amnesty International is gravely concerned for her safety and that of her family.
