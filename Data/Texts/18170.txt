"Palestinian Authority: Death Penalty / Unfair trial 
 Palestinian Authority: Death Penalty / Unfair trial | Amnesty International 
 Two men, Muhammad Dayf Allah al-Khatib and Husam al-Din Musa Hamid have been sentenced to death after an unfair trial. They are not entitled to appeal against the sentence. If the sentence is ratified by President Yasser Arafat, they could be executed at any time.
