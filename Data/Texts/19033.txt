"Tunisia: Further information on ""disappearance"" / fear of torture / possible prisoner of conscience: Moncef Zidi 
 NA 
 Amnesty International has learned that Moncef Zidi, who had ""disappeared"" after his arrest on 30 December 1996, was released on 9 January 1997. He was reportedly detained in a police station in Nabeul and was tortured and ill-treated during the first days of his detention.
