 Sri Lanka: Demand investigation into missing journalist | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Sri Lankan journalist and political analyst Prageeth Eknaligoda went missing on 24 January 2010 from Homagama, near the capital Colombo. His wife, Sandya Eknaligoda, was taken into custody when she went to lodge a complaint with the Homagama Police on 25 January. She has since been released. "
