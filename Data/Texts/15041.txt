"Turkey: Fear of disappearance / torture: Gulbahar Nifak, nee Alici (f), Lezgin Alici 
 Turkey: Fear of disappearance / torture: Gulbahar Nifak, nee Alici (f), Lezgin Alici | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 AI is concerned for the safety of Gulbahar Nifak, aged 19, and her brother Lezgin Alici, aged 20, detained by the police in the early hours of 4 September 1995. Reports indicate that Gulbahar Nifak has already been tortured and the detention of both has so far been unacknowledged by the authorities."
