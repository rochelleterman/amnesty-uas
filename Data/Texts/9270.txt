"USA: Further information on: Prisoner of conscience: Abdullah William Webster 
 USA: Further information on: Prisoner of conscience: Abdullah William Webster | Amnesty International 
 Abdullah Webster, imprisoned in the USA on account of his conscientious objection to the war in Iraq, is now receiving appropriate medical care. He had not been getting the regular blood tests required to check for the side effects of a drug he needs to take for high blood cholesterol.
