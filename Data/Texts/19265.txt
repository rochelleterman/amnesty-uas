"Tunisia: Further information on ""disappearance"" / fear of ill-treatment / prisoner of conscience / health concern: Abdelmajid Sahraoui 
 Tunisia: Further information on ""disappearance"" / fear of ill-treatment / prisoner of conscience / health concern: Abdelmajid Sahraoui | Amnesty International 
 There is concern that Abdelmajid Sahraoui has been held in secret detention since his arrest on the morning of 17 February 1997 at the Palais de Justice (law courts). His current whereabouts are unknown and there is concern for his safety.
