"Malaysia: Fear of torture or ill-treatment / incommunicado detention / prisoners of conscience 
 Malaysia:  Fear of torture or ill-treatment /             incommunicado         detention /   prisoners of          conscience 
 | Amnesty International 
 Malaysian police have detained at least seven leading members of one of Malaysia's main opposition parties, Parti Keadilan Nasional, National Justice Party. They are being held incommunicado at the Bukit Aman national police headquarters in the capital, Kuala Lumpur, where they are at risk of torture or ill-treatment.
