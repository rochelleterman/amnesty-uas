"Zaire: Fear for safety: Members of the Tutsi ethnic group 
 Zaire: Fear for safety: Members of the Tutsi ethnic group | Amnesty International 
 Floribert Chebeya Bahizire, human rights activist, Harouna Mbongo, human rights activist, Bashi Nabukili, human rights activist: The above human rights activists are reported to have been arrested because they were inquiring about human rights abuses against members of the Tutsi ethnic group. Amnesty International has not been able to establish their place of detention and fears that they may be ill-treated.
