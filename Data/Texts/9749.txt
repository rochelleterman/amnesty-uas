"USA: Further information on Death Penalty / Legal concern: Juan Raul Garza 
 USA: Further information on Death Penalty / Legal concern: Juan Raul Garza | Amnesty International 
 President Clinton stayed the execution of Juan Raul Garza on 2 August, three days before he was scheduled to die.   The execution would have been the first under federal law since 1963. Juan Raul Garza's execution has now been      scheduled for 12 December 2000.
