"Mexico: ""Disappearance""/fear of torture/extrajudicial execution 
 Mexico: ""Disappearance""/fear of torture/extrajudicial execution | Amnesty International 
 Angel Leal Alonso, Roman Morales Acevedo and Carlos Alberto Lopez Ines, ""disappeared""; Daniel Colin Enciso, Oscar Ivan Mora and Juan Carlos Romero Peralta (17), extrajudicially executed: On 8 September 1997, the six young men were detained by police force agents. AI is extremely concerned for the safety of the three ""disappeared"" and fears that they may be subjected to torture if in detention.
