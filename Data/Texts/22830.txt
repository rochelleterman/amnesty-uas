 Syria: Brother of rights activist held incommunicado: Yassin Ziadeh | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Yassin Ziadeh, brother of exiled Syrian human rights activist Radwan Ziadeh, has been held in incommunicado detention since his arrest on 30 August 2011 in Daraya, near the capital Damascus. He is at risk of torture and other ill-treatment and is likely to be a prisoner of conscience. "
