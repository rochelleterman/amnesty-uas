"EXTRA 109/93 - Sri Lanka: fear of execution: Gopalaswamy Mahendrarajah and 120 others 
 EXTRA 109/93 - Sri Lanka: fear of execution: Gopalaswamy Mahendrarajah and 120 others | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 According to a government-owned newspaper, former deputy leader of the Liberation Tigers of Tamil Eelam (LTTE) and 120 of his followers being held by the LTTE in Jaffna, are due to be executed by the LTTE on 16 January 1994. Gopalaswamy Mahendrarajah was reportedly arrested in August, apparently by the Ltte of having informed the Indian government about an arms shipment."
