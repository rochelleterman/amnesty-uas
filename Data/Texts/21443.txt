 Russian Federation: Vasily Stetsik, Human Rights Activist held  in psychiatric hospital : Medical Action. | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Vasily Michaelovich Stetsik has been held in psychiatric  hospitals since the end of 1998, without him or his       family receiving any information on the reason for his    detention. Neither he nor his family have been given a    diagnosis of his condition. Amnesty International         received information that he may have been given          medication which has had a damaging influence to his      health."
