"USA (Texas): Further information on Death Penalty 
 USA (Texas): Further information on Death Penalty | Amnesty International 
 Marcus Cotton was executed in Texas on 3 March 2004. He was sentenced to death in November 1997 for the murder of a 27-year-old Texas county prosecutor, Assistant District Attorney Gil Epstein, who was shot during a robbery in September 1996.
