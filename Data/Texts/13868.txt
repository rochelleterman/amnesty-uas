"Albania: Further information on legal concern / fear of ill-treatment: Klement Kolaneci 
 Albania: Further information on legal concern / fear of ill-treatment: Klement Kolaneci | Amnesty International 
 Klement Kolaneci was still in detention on 6 November 1996, in violation of  Albanian law, which stipulates that a court must hear a detainee's appeal against detention within ten days of the appeal being filed. It was also stated that his access to his lawyers continues to be severely restricted.
