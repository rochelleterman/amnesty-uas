"Argentina: Further information on Fear for Safety/Death threats, Clara Britos. 
 Argentina:  Further information on Fear for Safety/Death threats, Clara Britos. | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is seriously concerned for the safety of Clara Britos following her recent temporary abduction. Together with her family, the owner and editor of the monthly newspaper La Tapa in Guernica, Buenos Aires Province has been continuously subjected to threats, which are believed to be related to her work as a journalist.
