"Tunisia: Fear of torture / legal concern / health concern: Hafedh Ben Gharbia 
 Tunisia: Fear of torture / legal concern / health concern: Hafedh Ben Gharbia | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Hafedh Ben Gharbia has been held incommunicado since his reported arrest on 21 April. A student of electrical engineering in Germany, he was arrested on his arrival at Monastir airport, when on a return visit to see his family. He is reported to suffer from nervous disorders and there is additional concern that he may not be allowed access to necessary medical treatment."
