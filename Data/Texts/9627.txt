"USA: Death penalty/legal concern: Sean Sellers 
 USA: Death penalty/legal concern: Sean Sellers | Amnesty International 
 Sean Sellers is scheduled for execution on 4 February 1999 in Oklahoma for crimes committed when he was 16. He has been diagnosed as suffering from mental health problems. His death sentence violates international law banning the death penalty for crimes committed by persons under 18.
