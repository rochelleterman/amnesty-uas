"Viet Nam: Further information on death penalty / torture: Duong The Tung 
 Viet Nam: Further information on death penalty / torture: Duong The Tung | Amnesty International 
 Duong The Tung, aged 19, was executed by firing squad on 24 April 1997. He had been tortured by police armed with electric batons in an anteroom at the Ha Noi People's Court on 16 April 1996, while awaiting the verdict in his trial.
