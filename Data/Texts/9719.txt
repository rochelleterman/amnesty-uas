"USA : Further information on fear of indiscriminate mass killing of civilians in Iraq 
 USA : Further information on fear of indiscriminate mass killing of civilians in Iraq | Amnesty International 
 At least two hospitals and a rice storage house are reported to have been hit as a result of US and UK air strikes in Baghdad. International Humanitarian Law prohibits attacks which can cause indiscriminate and disproportionate impact on civilians.
