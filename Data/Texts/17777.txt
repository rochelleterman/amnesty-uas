"Israel/OT: Further information on Incommunicado detention/harsh conditions of detention - Jihad Shaker Abu Ayesh and Hussam Sameh Zeitun 
 Israel/OT: Further information on Incommunicado detention/harsh conditions of detention -  Jihad Shaker Abu Ayesh and Hussam Sameh Zeitun | Amnesty International 
 On 15 June, Jihad Shaker Abu Ayesh and Hussam Sameh Zeitun were released from Huwara military base, near Nablus in the West Bank. They had been arrested on 31 May by Israeli soldiers at a checkpoint in Huwara, where they were selling refreshments to Palestinians queuing at the checkpoint. During their period of detention, they were denied access to a lawyer and to their families. They have not been charged with any offence.`
