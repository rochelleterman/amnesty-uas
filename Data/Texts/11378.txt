"Indonesia: Imminent Execution 
 Indonesia: Imminent Execution | Amnesty International 
 The three men (Amrozi bin H. Nurhasyim (m); Ali Ghufron alias Mukhlas (m); Imam Samudera (m)) above are facing imminent execution. They were convicted of involvement in the 12 October 2002 bombings on the island of Bali, which killed 202 people and injured a further 209. The Bali Prosecutors' Office has announced that it has ""completed preparations"" for the execution, and they could now be put to death at any time.
