"Colombia: Further information on fear for safety: Luis Guillermo Perez Casas, Katia Karina Nino Vanegas, his wife and their two children 
 Colombia: Further information on fear for safety: Luis Guillermo Perez Casas, Katia Karina Nino Vanegas, his wife and their two children | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 During the past few days, the name of Luis Guillermo Perez' and Katia Karina Nino Vanegas' young son has been written on the wall of the building where Katia Karina Nino and her children have been staying. This new incident suggests that their place of hiding is known. There is further concern for their safety. Human rights lawyer Luis Guillermo Perez has left Colombia."
