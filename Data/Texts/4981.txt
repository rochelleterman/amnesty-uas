"Colombia: Further information on death threats / fear for safety and new concern: possible extrajudicial execution: Josue Giraldo Cardona 
 Colombia: Further information on death threats / fear for safety and new concern: possible extrajudicial execution: Josue Giraldo Cardona | Amnesty International 
 On 13 October 1996, Jose Giraldo Cardona, President of the Meta Civic Human Rights Committee and an activist with the Patriotic Union was shot dead outside his home. His killing renews fears for the safety of other members of the committee and the UP, many of whom have received death threats.
