"Colombia: ""Disappearance"" / fear for safety: Jorge Aurelio Artega Jimenez 
 Colombia: ""Disappearance"" / fear for safety: Jorge Aurelio Artega Jimenez | Amnesty International 
 There is grave concern for the safety of Jorge Aurelio Artega Jimenez who has ""disappeared"" since his abduction by armed men from a bus on 12 July 1997 in Toledo, department of Antioquia. Earlier that day he had been arrested by police.
