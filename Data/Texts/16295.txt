"Uzbekistan: Further information on fear for safety / incommunicado detention / unfair trial: Rashid Bekzhon 
 Uzbekistan: Further information on fear for safety / incommunicado detention / unfair trial: Rashid Bekzhon | Amnesty International 
 Rashid Bekzhon has been sentenced to 12 years'            imprisonment. The verdict was announced on 18 August.     Five others who went on trial with him were also          sentenced to long periods of imprisonment. Rashid Bekzhon claims he was tortured and forced to confess to           fabricated charges while in pre-trial detention.
