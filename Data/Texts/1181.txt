"Kenya: Prisoner of conscience / possible prisoner of conscience 
 Kenya: Prisoner of conscience / possible prisoner of conscience | Amnesty International 
 There are serious concerns for Njuguna Mutahi and Wahome Karengo who were arrested on or around 15 May 1998. Njuguna Mutahi, a human rights activist is accused of stealing a copy of a confidential report which he claims was given to him by Wahome Karengo, a journalist.
