"Gambia: Further Information on Incommunicado detention/Fear of ill-treatment/Health concern. 
 Gambia: Further Information on Incommunicado detention/Fear of ill-treatment/Health concern. | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International has received confirmation that Bisher Al-Rawi and Jamil Al-Banna have been transferred   to a US airbase in Bagram, north of Kabul, Afghanistan.   The men were originally arrested in Gambia on 8 November  2002 on suspicion of links with al-Qa'ida. They were held incommunicado until their transfer from Gambia, which is  thought to have been in early January 2003.
