"UA 71/93 - Guatemala: threats / intimidation: Demetrio Antonio Perez Ordonez 
 UA 71/93 - Guatemala: threats / intimidation: Demetrio Antonio Perez Ordonez | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Demetrio Antonio Perez, a counsellor with the refuge for street children Covenant House (Casa Alianza), has recently been receiving anonymous, threatening telephone calls and has reported seeing a car with tinted windows and no licence plates outside a Covenant House group home and later near his own home. AI is concerned for his safety. Covenant House workers have been subjected to intimidation since they started to denounce human rights violations against street children in Guatemala over two years ago. "
