 Mexico: UA 19/08 - Fear for safety | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Mariana Villareal Contreras, a member of the Diocesan Centre for Human Rights “Fray Juan de Larios” (Centro Diocesano para los Derechos Humanos “Fray Juan de Larios”) in Coahuila state, was attacked by two unknown men at the organization's office on 20 December 2007. Other members of the organization, including Raúl Vera, the Bishop of Saltillo, have been threatened in recent months. "
