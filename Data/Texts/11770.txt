 Indonesia: Fear of torture of  Human rights activist, M   Yusuf


 | Amnesty International 
 M Yusuf a human rights activist believed to be in police  detention in Aceh is at serious risk of torture. 
 M Yusuf a human rights activist believed to be in police  detention in Aceh is at serious risk of torture.
He was detained by the Indonesian security forces  during a stop and search     operation near Bagok, Nurussalam    Sub-district, East Aceh. He is thought to be held at      Nurussalam Police Sector. Representative of East Aceh     Police  informed local human rights monitors, that there  is   no record of the arrest."
