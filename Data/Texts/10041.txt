"USA (Pennsylvania): Further information on: Death penalty / Legal concern, George Banks 
 USA (Pennsylvania): Further information on: Death penalty / Legal concern, George Banks | Amnesty International 
 George Banks had his execution stayed on 1 December, the day before he was scheduled to be put to death. The Pennsylvania Supreme Court sent his case back to a trial-level court for a hearing on the question of whether he is mentally competent for execution.
