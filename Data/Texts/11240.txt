"India: Fear for safety 
 India: Fear for safety | Amnesty International 
 There are fears that members of the Buddhist Chakma community in Arunachal Pradesh state may be in imminent danger of physical attack by members and supporters of the All Arunachal Pradesh Students Union (AAPSU). There are also concerns that the authorities have not taken appropriate steps to protect the Chakmas.
