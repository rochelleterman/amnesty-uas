"USA (Indiana): Death penalty: Donald Ray Wallace 
 USA (Indiana): Death penalty: Donald Ray Wallace

 | Amnesty International 
 Donald Wallace is scheduled to be executed in Indiana on 10 March 2005. He was sentenced to death in 1982 after being convicted of killing four members of the same family in 1980. He has been on death row for more than two decades.
