 Tunisia: Jailed Tunisian journalist's health at risk  | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Tunisian prisoner of conscience Fahem Boukadous, aged 40, has been on hunger strike since 8 October 2010 in protest against his prison conditions and his continued detention in Gafsa Prison. He is refusing to take his medication without which his life may be in danger. "
