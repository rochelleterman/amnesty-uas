 Mexico: Fear for safety/Death threat | Amnesty International 
 Further death threats have been issued against human rights activists and lawyers working on behalf of those accused of the killing of 26 indigenous people in Agua Fria in May 2002. Amnesty International remains seriously concerned for their safety.
