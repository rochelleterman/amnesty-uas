"USA (Texas): Death penalty: Patrick Bryan Knight (m) 
 USA (Texas): Death penalty: Patrick Bryan Knight (m)

 | Amnesty International 
 Patrick Knight, white, aged 39, is scheduled to be        executed in Texas on 26 June. He was sentenced to death in Randall County in 1993 for the murder of Mary Ann and Walter Werner in 1991. Patrick Knight, who was 23 years old at the time of the crime, has been on death row for more than one third of his life.
