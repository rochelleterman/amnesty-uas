"Indonesia/East Timor: Further information on fear of torture / medical concern 
 NA 
 Of those arrested in the Viqueque district of East Timor in relation to disturbances between 7 and 11 February, 105 have now been released, leaving at least four people (names unknown) in detention. According to reports, many of those that have been released were tortured or ill-treated while in custody. There is serious concern for the safety of those still in detention.
