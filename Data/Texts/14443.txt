"Further information on EXTRA 16/94 (EUR 44/21/94, 24 March) - Turkey: fear of torture: Masallah Duygu, Metin Elci, Veysel Ugurlu 
 Further information on EXTRA 16/94 (EUR 44/21/94, 24 March) - Turkey: fear of torture: Masallah Duygu, Metin Elci, Veysel Ugurlu | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Masallah Duygu and Metin Elci have been charged and formally arrested. They are now in prison in Mersin awaiting trial at the State Security Court. It is alleged that both were tortured. Veysel Ugurlu was released on 25 March 1994."
