"Mexico: Further information on fear for safety 
 Mexico: Further information on fear for safety | Amnesty International 
 Father Samuel Ruiz, Bishop of San Cristobal de las Casas; Raul Vera, auxiliary bishop; and new names: Maria de la Luz Ruiz (f), sister of Bishop Ruiz; Jose Pedro Perez Perez, Jose Vazquez Perez and Manuel Perez Perez, all priests: Maria de la Luz Ruiz was attacked on 6 November and is in hospital in serious condition. The three priests were injured during the attack against Bishop Ruiz.
