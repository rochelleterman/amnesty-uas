 Further information on UA 421/93 (ASA 36/08/93, 30 November; and follow-up ASA 36/03/94, 1 March) - Singapore: death penalty: Fung Yuk Shing, and new names: Nathan Tse Po Chung, Cheuk Mei Mei, Manit Changthong | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Fung Yuk Shing was executed, as scheduled, on 4 March 1994. He had been sentenced to death on 5 December 1992 for drug trafficking. Three other people, named above, were executed on the same day. They had also been sentenced to death for drug-related offences."
