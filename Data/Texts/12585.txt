"Nepal: Fear for safety/Fear of Torture/ Ill-treatment 
 Nepal: Fear for safety/Fear of Torture/ Ill-treatment | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is concerned for the safety of lawyer Tikajung Shahi. He was arrested at about 4.30pm on 29 May by four army personnel in civilian clothes. The reason for his arrest is unknown and he is at risk of torture or ill-treatment.
