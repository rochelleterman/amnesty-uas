"Further information on EXTRA 84/93 (EUR 44/90/93, 4 October; and follow-up EUR 44/91/93, 6 October) - Turkey: fear of torture: Nilufer Koc 
 Further information on EXTRA 84/93 (EUR 44/90/93, 4 October; and follow-up EUR 44/91/93, 6 October) - Turkey: fear of torture: Nilufer Koc | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Nilufer Koc, a Kurdish interpreter and student in Germany, was released on 5 October 1993. She had been detained in Uludere, Sirnak province, on 29 September, while accompanying a German delegation to Turkey. After her return to Germany, Nilufer Koc described the torture to which she was subjected while in detention at the Gendarmerie Headquarters in Sirnak. "
