 Yemen: Further information on limited medical action: 'Ali 'Abdul-Fattah Hashim | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 'Ali 'Abdul-Fattah Hashim , who was arrested on a charge of apostasy in April 1992, was released from Sana'a Central Prison in June 1993, into the care of his brother. However the charge of apostasy, which carries a sentence of death, has not been dropped, and his release was reportedly made conditional upon his brother agreeing that he would, as guardian, be responsible for 'Ali 'Abdul-Fattah Hashim's actions"
