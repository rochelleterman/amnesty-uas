"USA (Tennessee): Further information on Death penalty / Legal concern: Paul Dennis Reid (m) 
 USA (Tennessee): Further information on Death penalty / Legal concern: Paul Dennis Reid (m) | Amnesty International 
 The execution of Paul Reid, who has a history of mental illness, has been stayed indefinitely. He had been scheduled to be executed in Tennessee on 28 June. He has given up appeals against his death sentence.
