"People's Republic of China: death penalty: Shen Jianfeng 
 People's Republic of China: death penalty: Shen Jianfeng | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 The ""Liberation Daily"" newspaper reports that Shanghai Municipal Intermediate People's Court sentenced Shen Jianfeng to death on 6 March 1995. He was convicted of murder. It is not known if he has appealed against the sentence."
