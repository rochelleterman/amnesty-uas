"Tunisia: Further information on prisoner of conscience / fear of ill-treatment: Abdel Moumen Belanes 
 Tunisia: Further information on prisoner of conscience / fear of ill-treatment: Abdel Moumen Belanes | Amnesty International 
 Prisoner of conscience Abdel Moumen Belanes has begun an unlimited hunger-strike on 8 December 1997 to protest at his detention. AI is concerned for his health and fears that he may be subjected to ill-treatment or torture.
