"Tunisia: Further information on Prisoner of conscience / health concern: Mohamed Mouadda 
 Tunisia: Further information on Prisoner of conscience / health concern: Mohamed Mouadda | Amnesty International 
 There are concerns for the health of Mohamed Mouadda, former leader of opposition party, Mouvement des Democrates Socialistes (MDS), since he went on hunger strike in protest at his contined house arrest. He was admitted to hospital on 8 June 1998.
