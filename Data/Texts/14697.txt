"Turkey: Fear of torture: Hakki Alpan 
 Turkey: Fear of torture: Hakki Alpan | Amnesty International 
 There is concern that Hakki Alpan, aged 43, is at risk of torture in police custody. He was detained on 29 June     1999 at the Ipsala border post, when he tried to enter    Turkey from Greece using a false passport. He has         subsequently been transferred to Tunceli Police           Headquarters.
