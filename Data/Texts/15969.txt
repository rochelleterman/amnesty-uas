"EXTRA 12/93 - Azerbaydzhani Republic: death penalty: Sergey Aleksandrovich Grebenkov 
 EXTRA 12/93 - Azerbaydzhani Republic: death penalty: Sergey Aleksandrovich Grebenkov | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Sergey Aleksandrovich Grebenkov, of Armenian-Russian parentage, was sentenced to death by the Military Collegium of the Azerbaydzhani Supreme Court on 29 January 1993. He has no right of appeal against the sentence. He was convicted of premeditated, aggravated murder, illegal possession of arms, theft and forging official documents. It is possible that the charges relate to the current conflict in Nagorno-Karabakh. AI fears that his execution may be imminent. "
