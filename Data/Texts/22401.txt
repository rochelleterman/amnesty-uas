 Iraq: Stop ""honour killings"" in Iraq | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 A 17-year-old girl was publicly stoned to death for an  ""honour crime"" in the town of Bashika, near the northern  Iraqi city of Mosul, on 7 April 2007. Du'a Khalil Aswad,  from the village of Bahzan was a member of the minority   non-Muslim Yezidi community in Iraq and had been involved in a relationship with a Muslim boy. She had been missing from her home for one night"
