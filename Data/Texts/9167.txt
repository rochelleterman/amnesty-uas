 EXTRA 56/93 - USA (Texas): death penalty: Denton Crank, Markham Duff-Smith | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 The above named men both face imminent execution. Denton Crank, white, is scheduled to be executed on 15 June 1993. Markham Duff-Smith, white, is scheduled to be executed on 29 June 1993. A co-defendant of Markham Duff- Smith is currently awaiting retrial after his death sentence was reversed on appeal. "
