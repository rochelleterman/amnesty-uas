"Philippines: Imminent execution 
 Philippines: Imminent execution | Amnesty International 
 Josefina Esparas is scheduled for execution on 16 August  1999. She was sentenced to death for drug trafficking in  April 1995. If the execution goes ahead, she will be the  first woman to be executed in the Philippines since the   death penalty was restored in 1994.
