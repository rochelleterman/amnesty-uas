 Further information on EXTRA 46/93 (EUR 55/18/93, 14 May) - Azerbaydzhan: death penalty: Vladislav Kudinov, Konstantin Tukish, Yaroslav Yevstigneyev, Andrey Filippov, Mikhail Lisovoy | Amnesty International

 The Azerbaydzhani parliament voted on 3 September 1993 to hand over to Russia the five servicemen named above, who had been sentenced to death by the Military Collegium of the Supreme Court of Azerbaydzhan on 12 May 1993. It is thought that their death sentences will not be carried out in Russia. "
