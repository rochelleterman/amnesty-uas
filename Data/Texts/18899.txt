"UA 257/93 - Algeria: fear of torture in prolonged incommunicado detention: Ali Derouiche, Mohieddine Derouiche, Tahar Derouiche, Mohamed Yassine Simozrag 
 UA 257/93 - Algeria: fear of torture in prolonged incommunicado detention: Ali Derouiche, Mohieddine Derouiche, Tahar Derouiche, Mohamed Yassine Simozrag | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Mohieddine Derouiche, a former Imam and a member of the banned Islamic Salvation Front (FIS), and his brothers Ali and Tahar, were arrested on 19 July. A fourth brother, Ahmed Derouiche, was arrested on 27 July. Mohamed Yassine Simozrag, the 22-year-old son of a lawyer active in political cases, was arrested on 23 July. All are held in prolonged incommunicado detention, and there is grave concern that they may be at risk of torture or ill-treatment. "
