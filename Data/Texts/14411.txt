 Further information on UA 131/94 (EUR 44/22/94, 30 March) - Turkey: ""disappearance"": Hatice Ciftci, Huseyin Taskaya, Nazim Babaoglu, Cuneyt Aydinlar | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Hatice Ciftci was released from the Adana Police Headquarters on 4 April 1994. No further information has been received on the whereabouts of the other three named above who remain ""disappeared"" following their detention by police."
