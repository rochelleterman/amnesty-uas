 Malaysia: Fear of torture or ill-treatment/incommunicado  detention: Noralwiseh Lee Abdullah


 | Amnesty International 
 Noralwiseh Lee Abdullah is being held by the Malaysian police under the Internal Security Act (ISA), which allows for indefinite detention without charge or trial. Amnesty International is concerned that she is at risk of ill-treatment and possible torture during police detention at an unknown location.
