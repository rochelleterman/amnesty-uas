"Ethiopia: Further information on health concern: Asrat Woldeyes 
 Ethiopia: Further information on health concern: Asrat Woldeyes | Amnesty International 
 Dr Asrat Woldeyes, 70, a political prisoner since 1994, is seriously ill in custody in Black Lion Hospital, Addis Ababa, and his life may be at risk, according to reliable information received by Amnesty International.
