 USA: Former jurors oppose Oklahoma execution: Richard Smith | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Richard Smith, a 47-year-old man, is due to be executed in the US state of Oklahoma on 8 April. He was convicted of murder in 1987. He has been on death row for more than half of his life. A number of jurors from his trial now oppose his execution, as does a brother of the victim."
