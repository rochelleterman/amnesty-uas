 Further information on UA 93/94 (AFR 24/05/94, 8 March) - Equatorial Guinea: arbitrary arrest / torture / medical concern: Norberto ""Tito"" Mba Nze | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Norberto ""Tito"" Mba Nze was reportedly released sometime between 15 and 22 March 1994. He was severely tortured and sustained kidney damage for which he was denied medical treatment."
