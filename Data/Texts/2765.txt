 Sudan: Further Information on Fear for Safety and new concern: Fear of Unfair Trial, Dr Mudawi Ibrahim Adam (m) | Amnesty International 
 Human rights activist Dr Mudawi Ibrahim Adam has been charged with offences that carry the death penalty, and is at risk of an unfair trial. Amnesty International considers him a prisoner of conscience, held solely for his activities in defence of human rights
