"Nepal: Further information on Fear of torture/fear for safety, Ramnath Mainali, lawyer 
 Nepal: Further information on Fear of torture/fear for safety, Ramnath Mainali,  lawyer | Amnesty International 
 Ramnath Mainali, a lawyer and member of the Supreme Court Bar Unit of the Nepal Bar Association, was reportedly transferred to Dillibazar Jail in Kathmandu on 4 April. Amnesty International is concerned for his safety as those detained in army custody have reportedly been subjected to torture and ill-treatment.
