"Guatemala: Fear for safety 
 Guatemala: Fear for safety | Amnesty International 
 Armando Mejia y Mejia and some 100 other flour mill workers involved in a peaceful protest at a flour mill in Guatemala City have been subjected to death threats and acts of intimidation by alleged members of the security forces or private security guards operating under the licence of the Minister of the Interior. There is serious concern for the workers' safety.
