"USA: Further information on death penalty / Legal concern, Ernest Paul McCarver 
 USA: Further information on death penalty / Legal concern, Ernest Paul McCarver

 | Amnesty International 
 The US Supreme Court stopped Ernest McCarver's execution less than seven hours before he was due to be lethally injected on 2 March 2001. A few minutes earlier, the Governor of North Carolina had announced that he had decided to deny clemency.
