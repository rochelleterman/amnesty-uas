"EXTRA 03/94 - USA (Texas): death penalty: Cesar R Fierro 
 EXTRA 03/94 - USA (Texas): death penalty: Cesar R Fierro | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Cesar Fierro is scheduled to be executed in Texas on 15 February 1994. A Mexican national, he was sentenced to death on 15 February 1980 for the murder of a white taxi cab driver during a robbery in February 1979. Amnesty International is investigating serious allegations that Cesar Fierro confessed to the crime under duress."
