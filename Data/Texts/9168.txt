"EXTRA 40/94 - USA (Texas): death penalty: Robert Nelson Drew, Lesley Gosch 
 EXTRA 40/94 - USA (Texas): death penalty: Robert Nelson Drew, Lesley Gosch | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Robert Drew, white, was sentenced to death in December 1983 for the murder of a white man, Lesley Gosch, also white, was sentenced in 1986 for the murder of a white female. Robert Drew is scheduled ot be executed in Texas on 2 August 1994 and Lesley Gosch on 15 August 1994. AI is becoming increasingly concerned about the number of executions being scheduled in Texas."
