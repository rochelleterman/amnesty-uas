"Colombia: death threats / fear for safety: Carlos Emilio Ramirez Montoya, radiologist 
 Colombia: death threats / fear for safety: Carlos Emilio Ramirez Montoya, radiologist | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Carlos Emilio Ramirez Montoya has received death threats following recent press articles in which he was accused of being a member of the guerrilla group ELN. Such accusations are often followed by ""disappearance"" or extrajudicial execution; there is serious concern for his safety. He is reportedly being kept under constant surveillance by security force agents."
