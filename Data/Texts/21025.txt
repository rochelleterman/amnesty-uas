 India: Further information: Indian environmentalists denied bail again | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Two human rights defenders and environmental rights campaigners, Ramesh Agrawal and Dr Harihar Patel, have been denied release on bail by the high court. They have been jailed for a month in state of Chhattisgarh after their arrest on false charges on 28 May 2011. "
