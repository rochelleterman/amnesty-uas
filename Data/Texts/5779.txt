"El Salvador: death threats/ fear for safety: Adrian Esquino Lisco 
 NA 
 On 6 January 1996, Adrian Esquino Lisco, member of the Salvodorean National Indigenous Association (ANIS), was threatened with death if he did not leave El Salavador within 72 hours.  Memebers of his family have also been attacked or harassed as a result of their activism in ANIS in recent months.
