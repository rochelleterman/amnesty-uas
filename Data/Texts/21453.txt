 Russian Federation: Further information: Abducted man found dead in Ingushetia | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 The body of Ilez Gorchkhanov, 26 years old, was found on 19 April 2011. He was abducted in Nazran in the Republic of Ingushetia, Russian Federation, on 21 March by around 15 men wearing masks, believed to be members of law enforcement agencies. "
