"USA (Virginia): Death penalty: Dennis Waldon Stockton 
 USA (Virginia): Death penalty: Dennis Waldon Stockton | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Dennis Stockton is scheduled to be executed in Virginia on 27 September 1995. He was sentenced to death for the murder of Kenneth Ardner in 1983. The testimony of a witness was the state's main evidence against Dennis Stockton and this has been continually challenged."
