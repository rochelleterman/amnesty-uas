"Turkey: Further information on fear of torture: Gulcin Ozgur (f) 
 Turkey: Further information on fear of torture: Gulcin Ozgur (f) | Amnesty International 
 On 21 June 1996, the above, a 15-year-old girl, was brought before a judge, formally arrested and committed to prison in Mersin. On 23 June she was transferred to a prison in Konya to await trial on charges of PKK (Kurdish Workers' Party) membership. She alleged that in police custody in Mersin she had been tortured again.
