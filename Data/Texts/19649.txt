 Sudan: Sudanese academic released without charge: Mohamed Zain Al-Abideen | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Professor Mohamed Zain Al-Abideen was released without charge on 4 March after being held incommunicado in Sudan for 15 days. He believes he was arrested for writing an article in which he criticized Sudanese President Omar al-Bashir. "
