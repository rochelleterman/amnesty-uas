 Palestinian Authority: Fear of torture and ill-treatment / Incommunicado detention / Legal concern | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is concerned for the safety of      about 30 students and one staff member from Birzeit       University in the West Bank who are being held            incommunicado. This follows mass arrests which     took   place after violent demonstrations broke out at the       university on 26 February 2000.
