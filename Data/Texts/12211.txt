"Malaysia: Fear of torture or ill-treatment  Wan Min Wan Mat. 
 Malaysia: Fear of torture or ill-treatment  Wan Min Wan Mat. | Amnesty International 
 On 27 September, Malaysian police arrested Wan Min Wan    Mat, a former lecturer at Malaysia Technology University  Internal Security Act (ISA). It is not known where he is  being held and whether he has access to family or legal   representation. Amnesty International is concerned that   he is at risk of torture and ill-treatment.
