"Solomon Islands: Fear for safety 
 Solomon Islands: Fear for safety | Amnesty International 
 Armed groups from Malaita island have twice this week     killed patients in clinics on the main island of          Guadalcanal where their opponents in the Solomon Islands' ethnic conflict have been treated. Patients and staff of  the clinics, particularly in rural areas, are in grave    danger.
