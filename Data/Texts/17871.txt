"Israel/Occupied territories: Further information on medical concern 
 Israel/Occupied territories: Further information on medical concern | Amnesty International 
 In a letter dated 24 July, the Military Attorney General gave permission for the Mansur family to travel to the Allenby Bridge leading to Jordan. They are presently waiting for transport to take them to the border, from where they will now be able to travel to the United States for Munjid Mansur's medical treatment.
