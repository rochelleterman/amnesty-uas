 USA (Connecticut): Further information: Death penalty: Michael Bruce Ross (m)


 | Amnesty International 
 The execution of Michael Ross, white, aged 45 has been delayed indefinitely. In recent days he had a series of execution dates scheduled, and in the early hours of 29 January was an hour away from becoming the first person to be put to death in Connecticut in 45 years.
