"Indonesia: Possible 'disappearance' /fear for safety/possible extrajudicial execution - Abdussalam Muhamad Deli. 
 Indonesia: Possible 'disappearance' /fear for safety/possible        extrajudicial execution - Abdussalam Muhamad Deli. | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is concerned for the safety of Abdussalam Muhamad Deli, a member of the East Aceh branch of the Human Rights and Legal Aid Post (PB-HAM) and the Indonesian Red Cross (Palang Merah Indonesia, PMI), who has not been seen since 11 May. Another human rights defender Raja Ismail (m), aged 50 who went missing on the same day was found dead on 13 May.
