"Mexico: ""Disappearance"": Angel Valdovinos Garza 
 Mexico: ""Disappearance"": Angel Valdovinos Garza | Amnesty International 
 Angel Valdovinos Garza has not been seen since 26 January 1996. AI is concerned for his safety as it is feared that he may have been abducted because of his leadership role on behalf of a group of 40 peasant familes who face eviction from their land.
