 Peru: Fear for safety / Death in custody/ Torture and ill-treatment | Amnesty International 
 Police officers have been harassing the family of Jenard Lee Rivera San Roque, who died in police custody on 9 May as a result of torture and ill-treatment, their lawyer, Gina Requejo, and others protesting at his death. Amnesty International is concerned for their safety.
