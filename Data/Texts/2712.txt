 Sudan: Fear of torture or ill-treatment/ Incommunicado detention  

 | Amnesty International 
 Osman Al Magdum, Abdel Rahman Zaidan, Abdel Aatti Abdel Khair, Hassan Sidiq Atolabi, Haitham Aldusogi and Ahmed Abdel Fatah Gabriel, most of whom are members of the Manasir Association Executive Committee, are reportedly being held incommunicado without charge in Kober prison in the capital, Khartoum. They are at risk of torture and ill-treatment.
