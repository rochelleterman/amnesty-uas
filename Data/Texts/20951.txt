 China: Fear of torture or ill-treatment/ fear of unfair trial:  | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Ye Mingjun, aged 25, son of imprisoned Beijing housing rights activist Ye Guozhu, was released on bail on 30 October 2007 after a month in detention. He remains under surveillance and has been told he should not speak to the media. Ye Guoqiang, aged 47, the brother of Ye Guozhu, continues to be detained incommunicado at an undisclosed location, and is at risk of torture or ill-treatment. Amnesty International is concerned for the safety of Ye Guoqiang, Ye Mingjun and Ye Guozhu."
