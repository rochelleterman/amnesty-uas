 Iran: Fear of torture or ill-treatment/ Incommunicado     detention | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is concerned for the safety of Taher Mahmoud Tamimi, Mohammad Jalali and at least 37 others who are all members of Iran's Arab minority. They may be held in incommunicado detention and are at risk of torture or ill-treatment.
