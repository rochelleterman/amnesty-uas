 Zimbabwe: Further information: Social justice activists no longer at risk | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Women of Zimbabwe Arise (WOZA) leaders are no longer in hiding after a police search for them appears to have been called off. Ten members of WOZA, who were arrested in three separate incidents between 28 February and 7 March, had all charges against them withdrawn by a magistrates court in Bulawayo, on 10 May. "
