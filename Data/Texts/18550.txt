"Syria: Fear of torture / legal concern: Musa 'Alika 
 Syria: Fear of torture / legal concern: Musa 'Alika | Amnesty International 
 Musa 'Alika who has lived in Germany as a political refugee for several years was arrested on 11 July 1998 on his return to Syria. AI is concerned that he may be at risk of torture and ill-treatment by members of the Syrian military intelligence.
