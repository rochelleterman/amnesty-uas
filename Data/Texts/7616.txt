"Mexico: Death threats / fear for safety: Juan Salgado 
 Mexico: Death threats / fear for safety: Juan Salgado | Amnesty International 
 Juan Salgado, a member of the Human Rights Academy, based in Mexico City, has received an anonymous death threat and has also been closely followed by unidentified individuals. The death threat is believed to be the result of his active role in a campaign to stop the numerous death threats against the Human Rights Centre Agustin Pro-Juarez.
