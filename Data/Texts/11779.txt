 Indonesia: Possible ""disappearance"" / fear of torture or ill-treatment: Jamaluddin Umar | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is concerned for the safety of      Jamaluddin Umar who has ""disappeared"" since he was        arrested on 20 July 1999 in the province of Aceh,         northern Sumatra. He could be at risk of torture,         ill-treatment or death.
