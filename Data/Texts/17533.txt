"Iraq: Incommunicado detention/fear of torture, Rebwar Arif 
 Iraq: Incommunicado detention/fear of torture, Rebwar Arif | Amnesty International 
 Rebwar Arif has been held incommunicado since 5 May. Amnesty International fears that he may be at risk of torture or ill-treatment as he is believed to be detained at the headquarters of the Asayish (security forces) in Sulaimaniya, northern Iraq.
