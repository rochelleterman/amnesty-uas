 Democratic Republic of Congo: Stop the Use of Child Soldiers | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Over the past five years, tens of thousands of children have been forced to fight alongside government armed      forces and armed political groups in the Democratic       Republic of Congo (DRC). From the beginning to the end of their military service, these children are subjected to   harrowing and dehumanising experiences, including         beatings, rape and other forms of torture"
