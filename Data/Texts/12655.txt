 Nepal: Further Information on Fear for safety / possible 'disappearance' | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International has received information that Bal Krishna Devkota and Dhanajay Khanal were released on 26 February. They were reportedly both arrested at different locations at around 11pm on 21 February. Amnesty International has no further information about where they were held, or in what conditions.
