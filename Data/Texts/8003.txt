"UA 318/94 - Peru: death threat: Henry Halanoca Hauyta 
 UA 318/94 - Peru: death threat: Henry Halanoca Hauyta | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 The mayor of Lampa province, Puno department, and a member of staff from the municipality's Public Relations Office, have reportedly threatened to attack or kill radio journalist Henry Halanoca Hauyta and his family. Henry Halanoca Hauyta had publicly exposed corruption at the town hall in his program Lampa y Realidad broadcast on Radio Qollasuo."
