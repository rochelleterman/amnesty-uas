 Further information on UA 273/94 (MDE 12/12/94, 18 July and follow-up MDE 12/13/94, 28 July) - Egypt: death penalty: Mohammad Rashad 'Abd al-Hamid Higazy, Mohammad 'Abd al-'Alim Khalifa, Mas'ud al-'Arif Ibrahim, Tariq Ahmad 'Abd al-Samad, Usama Mohammad R | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 The five men named above were executed in Cairo on 22 August 1994. They had been sentenced to death by the Supreme Court in the case known as ""the attempted assassination of the Interior Minister, General Mohammad Hassan al-Alfy""."
