"Cambodia: Fear for safety: Kong Sotha (f) 
 Cambodia: Fear for safety: Kong Sotha (f) | Amnesty International 
 There is concern for the safety of Kong Sotha, a Member of Parliament from Kratie Province, who has not been seen by her family since 19 April 1997. She is one of a group of 12 MPs from the FUNCINPEC political party who signed a statement which expressed no confidence in party leader and First Prime Minister Prince Norodom Ranariddh.
