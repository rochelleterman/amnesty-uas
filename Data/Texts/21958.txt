 Iran: Further information on death penalty: Bahman Salimian (m) | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 The execution of juvenile offender Bahman Salimian, scheduled to be carried out on 5 February 2009, was halted late on 4 February by order of the Head of the Judiciary, Ayatollah Mahmoud Shahroudi. However, it is feared that it could be rescheduled at any time. "
