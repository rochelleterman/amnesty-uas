"Singapore: imminent execution: Oh Laye Koh 
 Singapore: imminent execution: Oh Laye Koh | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Oh Laye Koh, a Singaporean bus driver believed to be in his early 40s, is scheduled to be hanged on 19 May 1995. In 1992 he was acquitted of the murder in 1989 of a 17-year-old schoolgirl, but was subsequently sentenced to death in May 1994 when the case was reopened. He lost his appeal in July 1994."
