 EXTRA 37/93 - United Arab Emirates: death penalty: two men (names unknown) | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Two men sentenced to death in the United Arab Emirates are at imminent risk of execution. According to press reports the two men, described only as Arab nationals, were sentenced to death in July 1992 on charges of rape, and these sentences have now been confirmed by the Federal High Court. "
