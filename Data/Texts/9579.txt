"USA (Ohio): Further information on Death penalty 
 USA (Ohio): Further information on Death penalty | Amnesty International 
 William Zuern was executed in Ohio on 8 June. He was sentenced to death in October 1984 for the murder of a jail guard, Phillip Pence, in the Community Correctional Institute in Hamilton County four months earlier. William Zuern had been on death row for almost two decades
