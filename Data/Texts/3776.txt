"Brazil: Fear for Safety 
 Brazil: Fear for Safety | Amnesty International 
 Settlers have invaded a Catholic mission in an indigenous reserve in Roraima State, in protest at government plans to guarantee the land to indigenous peoples. They held three missionaries hostage for three days, and reportedly subjected them to psychological torture and humiliation.
