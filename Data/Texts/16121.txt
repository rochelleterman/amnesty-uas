"Turkmenistan: Fear of torture - Farid Tukhbatullin 
 Turkmenistan: Fear of torture - Farid Tukhbatullin | Amnesty International 
 Civil society activist and ecologist Farid Tukhbatullin   was arrested on 23 December 2002 in the city of Dashoguz, in Northern Turkmenistan. He  is currently being held at  the Ministry of   National Security, an is at risk of     torture.
