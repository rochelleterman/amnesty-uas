"Brazil: Fear for safety: Cecilia Coimbra 
 Brazil: Fear for safety: Cecilia Coimbra | Amnesty International 
 There is serious concern for the safety of members of the human rights organization, Grupo Tortura Nunca Mais, (GTNM) Torture Never Again Group, after they recently received a number of anonymous death threats and suffered other forms of intimidation. The group have been refused police protection.
