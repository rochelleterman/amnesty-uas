"Malaysia: Fear of imminent execution 
 Malaysia: Fear of imminent execution | Amnesty International 
 Yip Kim Seng, aged 49, shoemaker, Lam Hok Sung, aged 41, cook: These two men are facing imminent execution following the rejection of their appeals by the Federal Court on 26 March 1997. Their only hope of commutation now lies with the King. Both men were convicted of firearms offences.
