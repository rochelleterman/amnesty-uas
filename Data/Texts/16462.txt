 Arrests of ethnic Albanian police officers are continuing. Latest reports indicate that at least 110 former officers have been arrested in Kosovo since 17 November 1994 and there have been more allegations of torture. Lawyers for Ylmet Fondaj, arrested 26 November and Blerim Olloni, arrested 20 November, have alleged that their clients were tortured."
