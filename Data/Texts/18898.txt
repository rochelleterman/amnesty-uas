"UA 410/92 - Algeria: fear of torture: Ahmed Bentchikou 
 UA 410/92 - Algeria: fear of torture: Ahmed Bentchikou | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Ahmed Bentchikou, a 21-year-old builder's merchant, was arrested on 29 November 1992 and has been held incommunicado since. The legal maximum period under anti-terrorist legislation for garde-a-vue detention is 12 days; Ahmed Bentchikou has now been held beyond this period. Ahmed Bentchikou's family has inquired since about his whereabouts but has not been told where he is being held or given any reason for his continued detention. "
