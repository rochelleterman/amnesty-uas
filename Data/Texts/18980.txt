"Morocco: Fear of torture and ill-treatment / fear for safety 
 Morocco: Fear of torture and ill-treatment / fear for safety | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is very concerned for the safety of demonstrators, mainly students and predominantly          Sahrawis, who have been detained in Laayoune, Western     Sahara, in the past week. This follows reports that       dozens of Sahrawis who have since been released were      tortured and ill-treated in detention.
