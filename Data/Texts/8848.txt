 USA: Death penalty / Legal concern: Donald Anthony Miller (m)

 | Amnesty International 
 Donald Miller, white, aged 44, is scheduled to be executed in Texas on 27 February 2007. He was sentenced to death in 1982 for the murder of Michael Mozingo earlier that year. Donald Miller was 19 years old at the time of the crime. He has been on death row for nearly 25 years.
