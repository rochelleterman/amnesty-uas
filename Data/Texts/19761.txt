 Democratic Republic of Congo: Further Information on Incommunicado detention/Fear of torture and other ill-treatment/health concern: Bernardin MBANDI (m) | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Retired civil servant Bernardin Mbandi was released from Agence Nationale de Renseignements (ANR), National Intelligence Agency detention on 14 March 2009.  He held an influential post during the government of President Mobutu (1965-1997) and was arrested on 22 January at his home in the capital, Kinshasa."
