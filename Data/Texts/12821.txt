"Nepal: Further information: Fear for safety/""disappearance"" 
 Nepal: Further information: Fear for safety/""disappearance"" | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International has received information from the government that Bhuwan Sen, (also known as Chham Bahadur Sen), is currently being detained under the Terrorist and Disruptive Activities (Control and Punishment) Act, 2002 (TADA), at a prison in Baglung District. There is no further information on Radha Baitha who was arrested on 5 April 2004, by a group of 15 security force personnel at his residence.
