"Algeria: Further information on ""disappearance"": Dr Messaoud Ouziala 
 Algeria: Further information on ""disappearance"": Dr Messaoud Ouziala | Amnesty International 
 Dr Messaoud Ouziala, a kidney transplant surgeon who ""disappeared"" on 8 July 1997, was released on 22 July after two weeks in secret detention. He was not reportedly ill-treated.
