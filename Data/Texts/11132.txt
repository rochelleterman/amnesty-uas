"UA 60/93 - India: torture: Manzoor Ahmed Ganai 
 UA 60/93 - India: torture: Manzoor Ahmed Ganai | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Manzoor Ahmed Ganai, aged 20, has allegedly had to have had the lower parts of both legs amputated as a result of torture by the security forces in Kashmir. He was reportedly arrested on 7 January 1993 along with 14 others by the 10th Bihar Regiment during a search operation in his village. He was reportedly tortured for 10 days. His relatives claim that he was released on 27 January in a coma and was taken immediately to hospital. AI is not aware of any official inquiry into the torture allegations. "
