"Yemen: Further information on incommunicado detention/ fear of torture or ill-treatment/ fear of forcible return: Mohammed Abdel Qadir (m) 
 Yemen: Further information on incommunicado detention/ fear of torture or ill-treatment/ fear of forcible return: Mohammed Abdel Qadir (m) | Amnesty International 
 Mohammed Abdel Qadir was released from detention in Political Security in the capital Sana'a in June 2006. He had been detained for more than ten months. He was not charged with any offence and was not brought before a court at any time during his detention.
