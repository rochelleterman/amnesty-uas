"Saudi Arabia: Further information on Incommunicado detention/Fear of torture/Possible prisoner of conscience, Muhammad Rajkhan 
 Saudi Arabia: Further information on Incommunicado detention/Fear of torture/Possible prisoner of conscience, Muhammad Rajkhan | Amnesty International 
 Possible prisoner of conscience, Muhammad Rajkhan has reportedly been tortured, whilst being held in incommunicado detention at al-Ruwais prison in Jeddah. This may have resulted in him suffering from a burst eardrum and substantial weight loss.
