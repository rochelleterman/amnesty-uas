"Burundi: Fear for safety / fear of ill-treatment: Mr Pascal Birari 
 Burundi: Fear for safety / fear of ill-treatment: Mr Pascal Birari | Amnesty International 
 There are concerns for the safety of Pascal Birari who was arrested on 30 August 1998 and whose place of detention is unknown. Around 15 other civilians were also arrested on 25 August during an identity check.
