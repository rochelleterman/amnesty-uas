"Kazakstan: Further information on: Fear of forcible return, Lutfullo Shamsuddinov 
 Kazakstan: Further information on: Fear of forcible return, Lutfullo Shamsuddinov | Amnesty International 
 Human rights defender Lutfullo Shamsuddinov was released on 4 July, and handed over to the United Nations High Commissioner for Refugees (UNHCR). The UNHCR have flown him and his family temporarily to a European country, where they will stay until they are permanently resettled.
