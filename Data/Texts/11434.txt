 Indonesia: Further information on: Possible ""disappearance""/fear for safety | Amnesty International 
 Nirmala Ohee, wife of human rights activist Demy Bebari, is safe and well after returning to her family in Papua province. Amnesty International currently has no information on what happened after she went missing on 12 May.
