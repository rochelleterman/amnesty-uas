 Nepal: Fear for safety/ possible ""disappearance"". Hira Bahadur Rokka (m), aged 34, street trader | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is concerned for the safety of Hira Bahadur Rokka who was reportedly arrested by security forces personnel in plain clothes on 6 December 2003 in Kathmandu. The reason for Hira Bahadur Rokka's arrest is not known as are his whereabouts.
