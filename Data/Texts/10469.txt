"Myanmar: Fear for safety/possible ""disappearance"" 
 Myanmar: Fear for safety/possible ""disappearance"" | Amnesty International 
 Aung San Suu Kyi, leader of the opposition party the National League for Democracy (NLD), is among over 100 political activists, including monks and students who have not been seen since a reported violent incident on 30 May. It is thought they are being held in detention, and there are fears for their safety.
