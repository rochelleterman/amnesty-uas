"United Arab Emirates:Further information on: Incommunicado detention/Fear of torture or ill-treatment: Salem Abdul Rahman Nawab al Baloushi 
 United Arab Emirates:Further information on: Incommunicado detention/Fear of torture or ill-treatment: Salem Abdul Rahman Nawab al Baloushi | Amnesty International 
 On 11 June, Salem al-Baloushi was allowed to make a telephone call to his family. The conversation lasted for about 15 minutes during which he asked about his family's wellbeing. His family thought he sounded as if he was in good health, though he is still being held without charge and without access to lawyers at an unknown location.
