 Mexico: Further information on ""Disappearance"" / torture / fear for safety and new concern: possible extrajudicial execution: | Amnesty International 
 Aureo Mendoza Rosales, Jesus Leyva and new name: Sixta Reza Delgado, wife of Aureo Mendoza Rosales: On 24 October 1997, Aureo Mendoza Rosales was found dead in Cuautla, Morelos State. An autopsy revealed that he had received 55 bullet wounds. His wife, her family and Jesus Leyva decided to leave the region on 22 November 1997 fearing for their safety.
