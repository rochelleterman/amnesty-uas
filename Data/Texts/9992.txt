"USA: Further information on death penalty / legal concern: Bobby Purcell 
 USA: Further information on death penalty / legal concern: Bobby Purcell | Amnesty International 
 On 17 September 1999, a Maricopa County Superior Court    judge in Phoenix, Arizona, sentenced Bobby Purcell to     life in prison without the possibility of release.The UN  Convention on the Rights of the Child prohibits the use   of this punishment for those convicted of crimes          committed when under 18.
