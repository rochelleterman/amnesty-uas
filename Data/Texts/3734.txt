"Bolivia: Fear for safety 
 Bolivia: Fear for safety | Amnesty International 
 Adalberto Rojas, lawyer and President of Bolivian non-governmental organization, the Asamblea Permanente en Santa Cruz de la Sierra (APDH), Permanent Human Rights Assembly in Santa Cruz de la Sierra, Santa Cruz Department, has been threatened and intimidated. His life, and those of others working for APDH, may be in danger.
