 Iran: Further information on fear for safety and torture | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International has just learned of the release     from detention of  Hossein Kashani, the Director of the   newsletter Hoveyat-e-Khish (Our Identity), in July 1999,  and Heshmatollah Tabarzadi, who was released on bail on 5 November.The organization is now trying to get more       information about Mohammad Massod Salamati, Seyed Djavad  Emami and Parviz Safari, associates of Heshmatollah       Tabarzadi and Hossein Kashani, arrested in July.
