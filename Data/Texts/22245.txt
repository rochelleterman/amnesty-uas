 Iran: Fear of torture or other ill-treatment: Saman Rasoulpour (m) | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Kurdish human rights activist Saman Rasoulpour was arrested on 25 July 2008 at his home in the city of Mahabad, in West Azerbaijan province, and is in danger of torture. He is a senior member of an organisation that campaigns for better treatment of Iran's Kurdish minority, the Human Rights Organisation of Kurdistan (HROK), based in the city of Sanandaj, in the western province of Kordestan."
