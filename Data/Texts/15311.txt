"Further information on EXTRA 106/92 (EUR 44/131/92, 17 December) - Turkey: fear of torture: Gulay Toraman 
 Further information on EXTRA 106/92 (EUR 44/131/92, 17 December) - Turkey: fear of torture: Gulay Toraman | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Gulay Toraman, detained on 12 December 1992, was formally arrested on the evening of 18 December by Istanbul State Security Court. The charges against her are not known. She was not ill-treated during interrogation. "
