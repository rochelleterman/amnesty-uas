 Turkey:Fear of torture and ill-treatment | Amnesty International 
 Thirteen people, including the local and provincial heads of the pro-Kurdish People's Democracy Party (HADEP), have been arrested in southeastern Turkey and may be at risk of torture. No official reason has been given for their arrest.
