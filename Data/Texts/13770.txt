"UA 307/94 - Socialist Republic of Viet Nam: death penalty: Le Thi Thu Ha 
 UA 307/94 - Socialist Republic of Viet Nam: death penalty: Le Thi Thu Ha | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 On 22 August 1994 a Vietnamese court sentenced Le Thi Thu Ha, a police captain, to death for fraud. She may appeal the death sentence to an appellate court."
