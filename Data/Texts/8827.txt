"USA (Maryland): Further information on death penalty: Flint Gregory Hunt 
 USA (Maryland): Further information on death penalty: Flint Gregory Hunt | Amnesty International 
 Flint Gregory Hunt, black, is scheduled to be executed in Maryland on 30 June 1997. He was sentenced to death for the murder of a police officer in November 1985. There is concern at the racial disparity in the application of the death penalty in Maryland.
