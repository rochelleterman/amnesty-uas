"Colombia: Fear for safety 
 Colombia: Fear for safety | Amnesty International 
 There are fears for the safety of human rights defenders attached to the Comision Intercongregacional de Justicia y Paz (CIJP) following a search by soldiers of their Bogota offices. The search was based upon a report that the organization held information relating to a guerilla network.
