"Saudi Arabia: Fear of torture and ill-treatment / possible prisoners of conscience 
 Saudi Arabia: Fear of torture and ill-treatment / possible prisoners of conscience | Amnesty International 
 Ariel Ordona, Angelito Sison, Juanito Manalili, Ruban Aguire, Wim Den Hartog and several other Christian people working in Saudi Arabia were arrested between 6 and 17 June 1998, possibly only because of their religious beliefs. Most of them are said to be held incommunicado and may be at risk of torture or ill-treatment.
