 Mexico: Fear for safety | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Amnesty International is concerned for the safety of members of the human rights organisation Ciudadanos en Apoyo a los Derechos Humanos A.C., and especially for their Director, Sister Consuelo Morales, who has been followed, threatened and subjected to surveillance by unidentified men, in an attempt to intimidate her and stop her legitimate activities in defence of human rights.
