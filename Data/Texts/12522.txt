"Nepal: Further information on ""Disappearance"" / fear of torture: Bishnu Pukar      Shrestha 
 Nepal: Further information on ""Disappearance"" / fear of torture: Bishnu Pukar      Shrestha | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International continues to be gravely concerned   for the safety of Bishnu Pukar Shrestha who is reported   to be held in unacknowledged detention at the Maharajgunj Police Training Academy, Kathmandu, an unofficial place   of detention, where he may be at risk of torture and      possibly death.
