 Mexico: Campaigner's family in danger after killing | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Peace and justice campaigner Nepomuceno Moreno Núñez was killed on 28 November 2011 in the city of Hermosillo in northwestern Mexico. He appears to have been killed after seeking justice for his son, who was subjected to possible enforced disappearance. The family of Nepomuceno Moreno Núñez are in danger."
