"Burundi: Further information on Fear of Torture/incommunicado detention 
 Burundi: Further information on Fear of Torture/incommunicado detention | Amnesty International 
 Four detainees, including Daniel Nsanzurwimo and Berchmans, were released on 17 January. The other detainees, Bernard Barayegeranije, Nathanael Bisesere, Barnab_ Barayegeranije, Tite Mpawnimana, ""Mama Richard"" Niyikundana, Simon Nani, R_m_gie Ntuyahaga, and Seraphine Nduwimana remain in police custody at three different locations in Bujumbura. Amnesty International is concerned that the detainees remain at risk of torture or cruel, inhuman or degrading treatment.
