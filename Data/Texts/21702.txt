 Serbia: Further information: Eviction in Belgrade leaves families homeless | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Five Roma families who lived in privately owned buildings in 55 Skadarska Street, Belgrade, Serbia were evicted on 11 August. The eviction was carried out on the basis of a decision made by a Belgrade court on 29 June, which stated that the residents had to vacate the property and remove all their possessions. "
