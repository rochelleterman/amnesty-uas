 Ethiopia: Fear for safety / Use of excessive force by     security forces | Amnesty International 
 At least two students have reportedly died and many others have been injured in violent clashes between riot police and demonstrators in the Ethiopian capital, Addis Ababa. Amnesty International is concerned that Ethiopian security forces will continue to use what appears to be excessive force against students and other demonstrators.
