"USA (Virginia): Death penalty / legal concern: Dwayne Allen Wright 
 USA (Virginia): Death penalty / legal concern: Dwayne Allen Wright | Amnesty International 
 Dwayne Allen Wright is scheduled to be executed on 14 October 1998 for a crime committed when he was under 18. His execution would violate international human rights law which bans the use of death penalty for crimes committed by those under 18.
