 Nepal: Fear for safety/possible ""disappearance"" | Amnesty International 
 Hari Prasad Paudel and Chandra Kanta Dhakal are believed to have been arrested by security forces personnel on 13 and 16 September respectively. Amnesty International is concerned that their whereabouts remains unknown and fears that they may be at serious risk of torture or ""disappearance"".
