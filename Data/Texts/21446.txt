 Russian Federation: Further information: Detained Ingush activist released: Magomed Khazbiev  | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Activist Magomed Khazbiev was released on 26 March after having been arrested on 23 March and held in administrative detention in Ingushetia. He was repeatedly beaten by police, and denied access to medication he relied on.  "
