 Guatemala: Further information on Fear for safety: David Herrera (m), journalist  

 | Amnesty International 
 David Hererra is now safely in exile abroad. He had been abducted by gunmen on 10 April, but managed to escape. The abduction appears to have been linked to his work helping foreign journalists coming to the country to investigate human rights abuses.
