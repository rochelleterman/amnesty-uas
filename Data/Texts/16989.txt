 Further information on UA 107/93 (MDE 13/05/93, 8 April; and follow-up MDE 13/08/93, 7 June) - Iran: death penalty: Salim Saberniah, Mustafa Ghaderi | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 According to reports Salim Saberniah and Mustafa Ghaderi may be in danger of imminent execution. Their cases have been reviews by the Islamic Revolutionary Court in Tabriz and the death sentences have been upheld. "
