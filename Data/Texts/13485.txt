"Sri Lanka: Fear for safety: Lasantha Wickrematunge 
 Sri Lanka: Fear for safety: Lasantha Wickrematunge | Amnesty International 
 There is concern for the safety of journalist and editor Lasantha Wickrematunge whose house was fired upon with anti-tank bullets on 17 June 1998. The Sri Lankan government has called for a thorough police investigation.
