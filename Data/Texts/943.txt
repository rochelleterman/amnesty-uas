"Gambia: Further information on incommunicado detention/fear of torture or ill-treatment 
 Gambia: Further information on incommunicado detention/fear of torture or ill-treatment | Amnesty International 
 Arrests have continued over the last few days in connection with an attempted coup that allegedly took place on 21 March. Furthermore, Abdoulie Kujabi, who has been held incommunicado since 27 March 2006, was reportedly brought to hospital in Banjul, the capital, on 6 April and is said to be in a ""critical physical condition"".
