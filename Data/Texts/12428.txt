 Nepal: Fear of torture/ medical concern: Bablu Rai (m)

 | Amnesty International 
 Bablu Rai, aged 34, has reportedly been tortured in police detention and is at risk of further torture or ill-treatment. The authorities are reportedly denying him access to medical treatment for the injuries he has sustained.
