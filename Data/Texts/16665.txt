 Egypt: Death penalty: Mohammad Fawzi Mohammad Ibrahim, 29, Ahmad 'Abd al-Qadir Bakri, 24, 'Izzet al-Shahhat Mohammad (in absentia) | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 AI is concerned that the three men mentioned above were sentenced to death on 6 June 1995 and that they could be executed if their sentences are upheld. The three, alleged members of the al-Gama'a al-Islamiya, were charged with, inter alia, killing a policeman and injuring two others in October 1994 and of possession of materials reportedly calling for the overthrow of the government."
