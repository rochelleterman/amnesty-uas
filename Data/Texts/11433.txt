"Indonesia: Further Information on Possible ""disappearance"" / fear for safety 
 Indonesia: Further Information on Possible ""disappearance"" / fear for safety | Amnesty International 
 Three of the six people believed to be at grave risk of torture and ill-treatment following their detention in Nanggroe Aceh Darussalam Province (NAD) have now been released. However, Masrizal, Iwan Irama Putra and Harlina remain in detention. They have reportedly been beaten and are at risk of further torture or ill-treatment. They have not had access to lawyers or family members.
