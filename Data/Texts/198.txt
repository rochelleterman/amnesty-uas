"Burundi: Further information on fear of ""disappearance"" / fear for safety: Jean Nepomuscene Minani 
 Burundi: Further information on fear of ""disappearance"" / fear for safety: Jean Nepomuscene Minani | Amnesty International 
 AI remains concerned for the safety of the above-named individual who was arrested by soldiers at Kwipera military position, Gasarara zone, Kanyosha commune, Rural Bujumbura on 28 November 1997.He has not been seen since. His whereabouts remain unknown despite attempts by human rights organizations to establish his whereabouts.
