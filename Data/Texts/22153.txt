 Iran: Further information: Iranian juvenile faces execution on 16 July: Mohammad Reza Haddadi (m) | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 The execution of 20-year-old Mohammad Reza Haddadi has been scheduled in the city of Shiraz, southern Iran, on 16 July 2009. He has been sentenced to death for a crime he allegedly committed while under the age of 18. This is the third time his execution has been scheduled."
