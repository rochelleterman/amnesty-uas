 Nepal: Possible ""Disappearance""/Fear for safety/Fear of torture or ill-treatment | Amnesty International 
 On 5 September, Radha Bhusal and Geeta Nepali were re-arrested by police shortly after they were released before the district court in Kapilvastu, in southern Nepal, close to the border with India. The reasons for their re-arrest are not known. They are at risk of torture, ill-treatment or ""disappearance"".
