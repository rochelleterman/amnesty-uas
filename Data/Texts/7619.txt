"Mexico: ""Disappearance"" / fear for safety: Felipe Sanchez Rojas 
 Mexico: ""Disappearance"" / fear for safety: Felipe Sanchez Rojas | Amnesty International 
 Felipe Sanchez Rojas, an Indian rights activist and chairperson of the non-governmental Indigenous Regional Development Centre, was abducted by unidentified individuals on 29 October 1996. He remains ""disappeared"" and Amnesty International fears for his safety.
