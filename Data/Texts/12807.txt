"Nepal: Further information on: Fear for safety/possible ""disappearance"" 
 Nepal: Further information on: Fear for safety/possible ""disappearance"" | Amnesty International 
 Yuba Raj Chaulagain was released on 29 September, but re-arrested on 1 October, at his home in Baneswor, Kathmandu, by seven people who told him they were security forces personnel. According to a witness he was taken away in a white van. His whereabouts are now unknown, and Amnesty International is concerned for his safety.
