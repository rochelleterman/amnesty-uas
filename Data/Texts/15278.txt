 Turkey: Fear of torture: Necmiye Aslanoglu (f), Metin Acet, Nuran Tekdag (f) | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 On 3 November 1995, the three Kurdish journalists named above were stopped at a roadblock and taken into detention. It is feared they are being interrogated under torture. It is not known whether the authorities have acknowledged the detentions."
