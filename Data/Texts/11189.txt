"India: Further information on death penalty / legal concern 
 India: Further information on death penalty / legal concern | Amnesty International 
 S Nalini (f), Murugan, Santhan, Perarivalan. The four     people named above are scheduled for execution on 9 June  1999. They were among 26 people sentenced to death for    murder and conspiracy to murder the Prime Minister, Rajiv Gandhi, who died in a bomb explosion in Tamil Nadu in     1991. The other 22 have had their sentences quashed or    commuted.
