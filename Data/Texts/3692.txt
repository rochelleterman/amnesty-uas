"Barbados: Further information on death penalty 
 Barbados: Further information on death penalty | Amnesty International 
 Leston Leroy Harewood and Vincent Murrell, who had been scheduled to be executed on 10 September 1996, have both had their sentences commuted to life imprisonment by the Barbados Supreme Court.
