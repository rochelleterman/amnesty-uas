"Syria: Further information on Legal concern/Incommunicado detention/ Fear of torture 
 Syria: Further information on Legal concern/Incommunicado detention/ Fear of torture | Amnesty International 
 Returned exile, Muhammad Ghazi Hubayib was released by the Syrian authorities on 11 May. He had been held in incommunicado detention for almost four weeks following his arrest on 16 April at Damascus airport. Details about his conditions of detention are not known at this time.
