 Colombia: fear for safety / ""disappearance"": Conrrado Urrego Cifuentes | Amnesty International 
 Conrrado Urrego Cifuentes has not been seen since he was abducted on 26 February 1996 by ten armed men from his house in Medellin, Antioquia Department. The men had claimed to be from Fiscalia, the Attorney General's Office.
