"Sudan: Fear for safety/fear of torture or ill-treatment/incommunicado detention, El Fadil Adam Mohammed Ahmed Noorein 
 Sudan: Fear for safety/fear of torture or ill-treatment/incommunicado detention, El Fadil Adam Mohammed Ahmed Noorein | Amnesty International 
 El Fadil Adam Mohammed Ahmed Noorein is being held incommunicado and without charge at the headquarters of the security forces in the town of Nyala, South Darfur province, western Sudan. Amnesty International fears that he may be tortured or ill-treated in detention.
