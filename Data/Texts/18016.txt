"Kuwait: Death penalty 
 Kuwait: Death penalty | Amnesty International 
 Hassan Mohamed Helal, Hamdi Abdel Azim Khalil, Egyptian nationals: Amnesty International has received reports that a court of appeal in Kuwait has confirmed the death sentences handed down to two Egyptian nationals convicted of pre-meditated murder. Their sentences are thought to have been sent to the Amir for ratification.
