 Brazil: Fear for safety: Joilce Gomes Santana | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is concerned for the safety of      lawyer Joilce Gomes Santana, who continues to suffer      persecution by members of a death squad. Joilce Gomes     Santana, who acts on behalf of victims of death squad     violence, has been threatened with death in the past.
