 Tunisia: Further information on fear of torture / ill-treatment and legal concern: Mehdi (Khiareddine) Jarrar | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Mehdi Jarrar was transferred on 19 April from an unknown detention centre to the 9 Avril Prison in Tunis. The prison authorities have reportedly confirmed his detention but he is still being held incommunicado."
