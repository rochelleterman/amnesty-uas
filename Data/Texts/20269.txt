 Mexico: Staff at migrants' shelter at risk after attack | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Father José Guadalupe Valdés, who runs the migrants’ shelter, Albergue Frontera Digna, in Piedras Negras, Coahuila state, on Mexico’s northern border, and the volunteers and migrants at the shelter, are at risk after a member of staff was attacked and threatened on 9 May."
