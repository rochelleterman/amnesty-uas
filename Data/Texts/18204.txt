 Palestinian Authority: Further information on prisoner of conscience / legal concern / incommunicado detention / medical concern: Fathi Subuh | Amnesty International 
 Prisoner of conscience Fathi Subuh remains in detention in Gaza Central Prison. AI has been informed by Colonel Muhammad Dahlan, head of PSS in the Caza Strip, that Fathi Subuh was being held on charges that included rape and spying.
