"Honduras: Further information on Fear for safety/death threats/unlawful killing 
 Honduras: Further information on Fear for safety/death threats/unlawful killing | Amnesty International 
 Police in Tegucigalpa have reportedly used excessive force against demonstrators protesting at the killing of Carlos Roberto Flores, leaving at least 30 people injured. Amnesty International is concerned for the safety of the some 300 demonstrators, who continue their vigil outside the National Congress building..
