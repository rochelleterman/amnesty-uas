 Iran: Further information on imminent execution: Behnoud Shojaee  | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Juvenile offender Behnoud Shojaee was granted a one-month reprieve on 10 June 2008, the day before he was scheduled for execution, to allow time for his family to negotiate financial compensation with the family of the boy he was convicted of stabbing to death, in exchange for a pardon."
