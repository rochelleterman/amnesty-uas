"Russian Federation (Chechen Republic): Fear for safety: Civilians trapped in Grozny 
 Russian Federation (Chechen Republic): Fear for safety: Civilians trapped in Grozny | Amnesty International 
 Hundreds and possibly thousands of people are reported to have been killed since 6 August 1996, when heavy fighting broke out in Grozny. Amnesty International is gravely concerned for the safety of civilians trapped in the capital and the surrounding areas, and of those trying to flee.
