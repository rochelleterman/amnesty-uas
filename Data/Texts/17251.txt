 Iran: Further information on Fear of imminent execution, Afsaneh Nouroozi | Amnesty International 
 Iran's Supreme Court has quashed the death sentence of Afsaneh Norouzi, a woman convicted of the murder of a senior police official, who she alleged had tried to rape her. According to her lawyer, quoting from the verdict, the Supreme Court ""found deficiencies in her case"".
