"Guatemala: Fear for safety: Maria Francisca Ventura Sican (f) 
 Guatemala: Fear for safety: Maria Francisca Ventura Sican (f) | Amnesty International 
 Maria Francisca Ventura Sican and her family have in the last few days been subjected to acts of intimidation by individuals believed to be linked to the security forces and there is grave concern for their safety. It is believed she is being targeted because she gave testimony to an Amnesty International delegation in April 1997.
