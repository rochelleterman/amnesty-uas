 Equatorial Guinea: Enforced disappearance/ incommunicado detention/ fear of torture or ill-treatment: Cipriano Nguema Mba (m) | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 The whereabouts of former Equatorial Guinea army colonel Cipriano Nguema Mba have been unknown since he was illegally arrested in Cameroon.  He is at risk of enforced disappearance, torture or ill-treatment and might be held incommunicado in Black Beach prison in the capital Malabo (Equatorial Guinea)."
