 Indonesia: Possible ""disappearance"" | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is concerned for the safety of      Eurico Pereira, Zelia Fernandes and Rogerio Soares. They  have been missing since 11 August 1999 when a local       militia group opened fire on the office of the Dewan      Solidaritas Mahasiswa Timor Timur (DSMTT), Student        Solidarity Council of East Timor, killing at least two    students and reportedly injuring others.
