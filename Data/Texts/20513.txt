 USA: Further information: Execution in Ohio as governor denies mercy: Mark Wiles | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Mark Wiles was executed by lethal injection in the US state of Ohio on 18 April for a murder committed in 1985. The governor denied clemency on 11 April. Mark Wiles had been on death row for more than a quarter of a century."
