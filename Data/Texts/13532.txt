"Sri Lanka: Fear of torture 
 Sri Lanka: Fear of torture | Amnesty International 
 A Rasaratnam, E Nayavel (Amudan), Thirugnanam Sambandan, Kasinathan Naguleswaran and Rajendran Sudarsan have not been seen since being taken prisoner by the Liberation Tigers of Tamil Eelam (LTTE). There are fears that they are being tortured during interrogation.
