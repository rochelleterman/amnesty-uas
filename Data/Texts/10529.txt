"Myanmar: Fear for safety / fear of ill-treatment 
 Myanmar: Fear for safety / fear of ill-treatment | Amnesty International 
 Khin Maung Win alias Ko Sunny, Cho Aung Than, Daw Khin Ma Than (f), U Shwe Myint Aung, U Ohn Myint: There is concern for the safety of the above five supporters of the National League for Democracy (NLD) whose whereabouts are unknown. The reasons for their arrest are unknown, but unofficial sources have stated that they were arrested for allegedly smuggling abroad video-taped speeches by the NLD leader Daw Aung San Suu Kyi.
