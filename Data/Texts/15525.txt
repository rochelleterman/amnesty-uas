"Chechen Republic, Russian Federation: Fear of imminent execution / death penalty 
 Chechen Republic, Russian Federation: Fear of imminent execution / death penalty | Amnesty International 
 Two people arrested on suspicion of kidnapping and hostage-taking in the Chechen Republic face imminent execution. Another 46 detainees  have received various measures of punishment including a woman, who along with nine others received a life sentence for kidnapping.
