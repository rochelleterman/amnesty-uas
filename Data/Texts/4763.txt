"Further information on UA 40/94 ( AMR 23/14/94, 7 February) - Colombia: ""disappearance"": Julio Cesar Payan de la Roche 
 Further information on UA 40/94 ( AMR 23/14/94, 7 February) - Colombia: ""disappearance"": Julio Cesar Payan de la Roche | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Dr Julio Cesar Payan del la Roche was released unharmed in April 1994. It appears that he was kidnapped and not ""disappeared"", as was widely believed."
