 Cameroon: Further information on prisoner of conscience / health concern / cruel, inhuman or degrading treatment: Pius Njawe, journalist, director of Le Messager: | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International remains seriously concerned about the health of prisoner of conscience Pius Njaw? who continues to be held in Central Prison, New Bell, Douala.  Conditions at the prison are particularly harsh and although Pius Njaw? has deteriorating eye-sight he is being refused access to specialist treatment.
