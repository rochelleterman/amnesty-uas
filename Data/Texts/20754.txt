 Venezuela: Further information: Dad attacked as he calls for justice for son: Victor Martínez  | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Victor Martínez, the father of a 23 year-old man who was shot dead last year, Mijail Martínez, has been attacked. He was handing out flyers alleging that police and local officials were involved in his son's death. He, his family and his colleagues may be at risk of further attacks."
