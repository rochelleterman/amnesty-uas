"Russian Federation: Prisoner of conscience: Vitaly Vladimirovich Gushchin 
 Russian Federation: Prisoner of conscience: Vitaly Vladimirovich Gushchin | Amnesty International 
 Vitaliy Vladimirovich Gushchin, a 22-year-old Jehovah's Witness from Kurchatovo, Kursk Region, is currently serving a one and a half year prison sentence for refusing to carry out military service, because of his religious beliefs. AI considers him to be a prisoner of conscience.
