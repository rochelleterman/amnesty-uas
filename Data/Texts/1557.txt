"Niger: Further information on impunity: proposed new constitution which provides an amnesty for those responsible for human rights violations 
 Niger: Further information on impunity: proposed new constitution which provides an amnesty for those responsible for human rights violations | Amnesty International 
 On 18 July 1999 a new Constitution was accepted by        popular referendum in Niger. Article 141 of the           constitution provides an amnesty for the authors of the   human rights violations committed during the military     coups of January 1996 and April 1999.
