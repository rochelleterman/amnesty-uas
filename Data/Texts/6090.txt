"Guatemala: Further information on fear for safety/death threats 
 Guatemala: Further information on fear for safety/death threats
 | Amnesty International 
 Two members of the team, Rodrigo Salvado and Mario Domingo, investigating the murder of a Guatemalan bishop have been receiving death threats. The threats began when a judge ruled that three high-ranking military officers should stand trial for the murder. Many others involved in the investigation have faced death threats, and several have fled the country.
