"Sudan: Further information on Incommunicado detention/Detention without charge 
 Sudan: Further information on Incommunicado detention/Detention without charge | Amnesty International 
 Khidder al-Tayeb Yusuf was released on 16 December        without charge. Members of the Sudanese security services reportedly tortured him in pre-trial detention. His       brothers, al-Shafi' and Hashem al-Tayeb Yusuf remain in   incommunicado detention.
