 Indonesia (East Timor): Fear for safety / arbitrary detention: Iskandar Amin | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is concerned for the safety of      Iskandar Amin, Village Head and Asnawithe, who are in     police custody in the province of Aceh, northern Sumatra. They are at risk of torture or ill-treatment.
