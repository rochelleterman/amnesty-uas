"Rwanda: Further information on fear for safety / ""disappearance"" 
 Rwanda: Further information on fear for safety / ""disappearance"" | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Jean Rubaduka, Abbe Andre Sibomana, Jean-Baptiste Barambirwa: Jean-Baptiste Barambirwa, the president of the human rights organisation CLADHO, was released on 12 December, apparently as a result of international pressure. However, his case is still under review by the Public Prosecutor's office.  It is not yet known whether he will face formal charges."
