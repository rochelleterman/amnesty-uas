"Turkey: Fear of torture or ill-treatment 
 Turkey: Fear of torture or ill-treatment | Amnesty International 
 On 13 April 1998  Semdin Sakik, a former leading commander of the Kurdish Workers' Party (PKK), and his brother, Hasan Sakik, were abducted by Turkish Special Forces in Northern Iraq.  It is believed they have been taken for interrogation and could therefore be at great risk of torture or ill-treatment.
