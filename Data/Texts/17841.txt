"Israel: Fear of imminent house destruction 
 Israel: Fear of imminent house destruction | Amnesty International 
 At least 40 persons, including elderly persons and children living in four houses in Asira al-Shamaliya in the northern West Bank, are facing the imminent destruction or sealing of their homes because four male relatives, who have not lived in the houses for years, have been accused of involvement in recent suicide bombings.
