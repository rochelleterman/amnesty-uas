"USA (Oklahoma): Further information on death penalty: Robert Brecheen 
 USA (Oklahoma): Further information on death penalty: Robert Brecheen | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Robert Brecheen was executed in Oklahoma on 11 August 1995. He had been found unconscious in his cell about three hours before the execution, apparently from an overdose of an unknown substance. He was taken to hospital where he was revived and then returned to the prison and executed."
