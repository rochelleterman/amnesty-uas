"USA (Florida): Further Information on Legal concern, Lionel Tate. 
 USA (Florida): Further Information on Legal concern, Lionel Tate. | Amnesty International 
 On 10 December, Human Rights Day, a Florida court overturned the conviction of Lionel Tate. In 2001, he was sentenced to life imprisonment without the possibility of parole for the 1999 murder of six-year-old Tiffany Eunick committed when Lionel Tate was 12 years old. The state has until 25 December to appeal against the decision.
