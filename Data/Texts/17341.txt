"Iran: Incommunicado detention/Fear of torture or ill-treatment 
 Iran: Incommunicado detention/Fear of torture or ill-treatment | Amnesty International 
 Hamid Qate'Pour (m), Emad Rafi'I (m) and Mohammad Hezbawi (also known as Hezbaee Zadeh) (m), members of Iran's Arab minority, have all reportedly been detained in or around the city of Ahvaz, southwestern Iran. They are being held incommunicado at unknown locations and are reportedly at risk of torture or ill-treatment
