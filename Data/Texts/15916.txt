"Ukraine: Fear of imminent execution: Vladimir Vitalyevich Sorochinsky 
 Ukraine: Fear of imminent execution: Vladimir Vitalyevich Sorochinsky | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Vladimir Sorochinsky, aged 22, was sentenced to death for premeditated murder by the Vinnitsa regional court on 23 November 1994. His appeal against his death sentence was rejected by the Ukrainian Supreme Court on 9 February 1995."
