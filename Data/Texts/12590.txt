 Nepal: Further Information on Fear for safety/ possible ""disappearance"" | Amnesty International 
 Chet Bahadur Thakuri was reportedly arrested on 13 January and his whereabouts are now unknown. Amnesty International has received no further information about Deepak Thakuri or Yag Prasad Dulal, who were reportedly arrested on 27 January and 6 February respectively, and is increasingly concerned for their safety.
