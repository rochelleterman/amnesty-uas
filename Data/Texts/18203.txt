 Palestinian Authority: Further information on fear for safety: Alan Johnston | Amnesty International 
 On 1 June, a Gaza-based Palestinian armed group calling itself  ""Army of Islam"" released a video showing BBC news  reporter Alan Johnston, who has been missing since his    abduction in Gaza City on 12 March. This has increased    fears for Alan Johnston's safety.
