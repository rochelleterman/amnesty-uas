"UA 179/94 - Georgia: fear of imminent execution: Sergo Tidilov and Kakusha Grigolova 
 UA 179/94 - Georgia: fear of imminent execution: Sergo Tidilov and Kakusha Grigolova | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Sergo Tidilov and Kakusha Grigolova face imminent execution after reports on 5 May 1994 that parliament has lifted a two-year moratorium on executions. Both men had been sentenced to death for premeditated, aggravated murder, but no further details are known."
