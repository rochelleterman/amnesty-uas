 Syria: Further information on Prisoners of conscience/incommunicado detention/fear of torture and ill-treatment | Amnesty International 
 Human rights defender Nizar Ristnawi is now believed to be held at the Military Security branch in his home town of Hama, in western Syria. He is still held incommunicado and without charge, and is in grave danger of torture and other ill-treatment.
