 USA (Oklahoma): Further information on Death penalty: Kevin Young (m) | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 On 8 July 2008, the Oklahoma Pardon and Parole Board voted 4-1 to recommend clemency for Kevin Young, who is due to be executed on 22 July. The recommendation goes to Governor Henry, who can accept or reject it. Since taking office in 2003, he has granted clemency once from three previous clemency recommendations."
