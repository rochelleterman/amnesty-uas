"Albania: Further information on death penalty: Laver Goga 
 Albania: Further information on death penalty: Laver Goga | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 According to a recent press report, Laver Goga has received a presidential pardon which commuted his death sentence to life imprisonment. He has reportedly been on hunger-strike for three days, demanding to be transferred from ""the cell to prison""."
