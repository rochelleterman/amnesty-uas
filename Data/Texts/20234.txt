 Mexican migrants rights defender attacked: Ignacio Muñiz Zamora  | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Migrant rights defender Ignacio Muñiz Zamora has been attacked, robbed and threatened by armed men in Tamaulipas state in northern Mexico. He and his colleagues at the Beato Juan Bautista Scalabrini Migrants Rights Centre are at risk of further attacks. "
