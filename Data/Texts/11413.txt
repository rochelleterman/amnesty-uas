"Indonesia: Further Information: Imminent execution: Astini (f) 
 Indonesia: Further Information: Imminent execution: Astini (f) | Amnesty International 
 Astini, aged 49, Indonesian national, was executed by firing squad on 20 March. It is not known where she was put to death, although there have been some reports that it was in West Surabaya, approximately 90km from the prison where she was held.
