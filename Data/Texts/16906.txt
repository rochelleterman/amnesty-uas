"Iran: Further information on fear of torture: Retired General Azizollah Amir Rahimi, Mehrdad Amir Rahimi, his son 
 Iran: Further information on fear of torture: Retired General Azizollah Amir Rahimi, Mehrdad Amir Rahimi, his son | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 According to press reports of 3 March 1995, Mehrdad Amir Rahimi was released on bail on 1 March 1995. AI remains seriously concerned about his father, Retired General Azizollah Amir Rahimi. It is reported that he is to be tried on unspecified charges related to his criticism of the Iranian government. AI believes him to be a prisoner of conscience who should be released immediately and unconditionally."
