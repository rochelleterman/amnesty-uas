 Nepal: Incommunicado detention/fear of torture or ill-treatment   Ram Bahadur Limbu (m), aged 47, social activist | Amnesty International 
 Ram Bahadur Limbu, an activist who campaigns on behalf of the Limbu ethnic group, is being held incommunicado by security forces at an unknown location. Amnesty International believes he may be at risk of torture or ill-treatment in detention. .
