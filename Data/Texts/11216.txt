"India: Further information on Imminent Execution, Dayanidhi Bisoi (m) 
 India: Further information on Imminent Execution, Dayanidhi Bisoi (m) | Amnesty International 
 On 16 August, Dayanidhi Bisoi was granted clemency by the Governor of Orissa state, and his death sentence was commuted to life imprisonment. He had been scheduled to be executed on 23 August.
