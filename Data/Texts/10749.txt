 People's Republic of China: death penalty: Wu Decheng, unemployed | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 According to the newspaper Liaoshen Wanbao, on 22 March 1995 the Shenyang Municipal Intermediate People's Court, Liaoning province, sentenced Wu Decheng to death for theft and trafficking in counterfeit national currency. It is not known whether he has appealed against his sentence."
