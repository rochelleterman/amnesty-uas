 UA 189/94 - Cambodia: ""disappearance"" / fear of extrajudicial execution: Three villagers (names unknown) from Sei La Keyo village, Kom Ping Poi district, Battambang Province. | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 On 7 April three villagers were taken from Sei La Keyo village by a senior officer of the Royal Cambodian Armed Forces (RCAF) in connection with a land dispute. They have not been seen since and there is serious concern for their safety."
