 Iran:  Forcible return/ Prisoners of conscience/ Fear of torture and ill-treatment | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Ma’soumeh Ka’bi and her five children, all members of the Arab minority in Iran, were forcibly returned by the Syrian Government in breach of international law on 27 September 2008 from Syria to Iran, where they are all now said to be in custody and may be at risk of torture and other ill-treatment."
