 Iran: Prisoner of conscience / medical concern: Emadeddin Baghi (m) | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Journalist and human rights defender Emadeddin Baghi suffered a seizure on 7 May 2008 in Tehran's Evin prison; he was treated in the prison hospital and sent back to his cell the same day. Amnesty International is concerned that Emadeddin Baghi is not receiving adequate medical treatment, putting his life in danger."
