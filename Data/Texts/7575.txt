"Mexico: Arbitrary arrest / ""disappearance"" / fear for safety: Silverio Lopez Ramos, peasant rights activist 
 Mexico: Arbitrary arrest / ""disappearance"" / fear for safety: Silverio Lopez Ramos, peasant rights activist | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is concerned for the safety of Silverio Lopez Ramos who was arrested - reportedly violently - on 4 September by members of the state police, the Mexican army and the federal judicial police. His whereabouts remain unknown.
