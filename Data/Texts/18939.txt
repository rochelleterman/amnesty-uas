"Algeria: Further information on disappearance/ fear of refoulement: Mohamed Naceur el-Hani 
 Algeria: Further information on disappearance/ fear of refoulement: Mohamed Naceur el-Hani | Amnesty International 
 Mohamed Naceur el-Hani, a lawyer, was released from detention by the Tunisian authorities on 25 April 1998, 10 days after being handed over to them by Algerian security forces.  During his interrogation he was beaten.
