 Nigeria: Human rights defender receives death threat: Patrick Naagbanton | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Patrick Naagbanton, Coordinator of the Centre for Environment, Human Rights and Development (CEHRD) in Port Harcourt, in Rivers State, southern Nigeria, has received a number of death threats in the last two weeks. Amnesty International believes his life to be in danger. "
