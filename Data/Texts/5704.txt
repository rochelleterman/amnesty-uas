"Further information on UA 316/92 (AMR 28/04/92, 12 October) - Ecuador: fear of refoulement: Oscar Amaris 
 Further information on UA 316/92 (AMR 28/04/92, 12 October) - Ecuador: fear of refoulement: Oscar Amaris | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 AI has been given assurances by Ecuadorean Embassies in several countries that Oscar Amaris, a Colombian political refugee, is not to be returned to Colombia. The Ecuadorean Government has asked the UNHCR to find another country of asylum for Oscar Amaris. "
