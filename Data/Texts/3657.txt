"Bahamas: Further information on death penalty: Trevor Nathaniel Fisher 
 Bahamas: Further information on death penalty: Trevor Nathaniel Fisher | Amnesty International 
 Trevor Nathaniel Fisher is scheduled to be hanged in The Bahamas on Thursday 2 April 1998. The execution was scheduled despite the fact that Trevor Fisher has a petition pending with the Inter-American Commission on Human Rights (IACHR).
