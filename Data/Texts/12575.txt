 Nepal: Possible ""disappearance"" / fear for safety | Amnesty International 
 The three people Sho Prasad Khatiwada, Laxman Prasad Aryal and Jeetaman Basnet were all reportedly arrested by security forces personnel in January and February, and their whereabouts remain unknown. Amnesty International is concerned for their safety and that they may have ""disappeared"" as their families have not been informed of their whereabouts.
