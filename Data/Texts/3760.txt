 Bolivia: Further information on fear of torture / ""disappearance"" / legal concern: Quintin Vargas, Delfin Olivera, Esteban Quispe, Teodocio Romero | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Amnesty International has received information that in the last few days Delfin Olivera was released for lack of evidence, and Quintin Vargas and Esteban Quispe were provisionally released. There is no longer concern for the safety of Teodocio Romero."
