"Argentina: Further information on Fear for Safety/Death threats, Clara Britos. 
 Argentina:  Further information on Fear for Safety/Death threats, Clara Britos. | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International continues to be seriously concerned for the safety of Clara Britos and her family. As the owner and editor of the monthly newspaper La Tapa in Guernica, Buenos Aires Province, she is continuously being threatened as a result of her work as a journalist.
