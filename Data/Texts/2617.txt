"Sudan: Possible prisoners of conscience / Fear of torture an d ill-treatment 
 Sudan: Possible prisoners of conscience / Fear of torture an d ill-treatment | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is deeply concerned that Father Lino Sebit and Father Hillary Boma are at risk of ongoing torture and ill-treatment while in detention.  This follows recent reports that both have confessed, under duress, to being involved in the bombings in the Sudanese capital, Khartoum, on 30 June 1998.
