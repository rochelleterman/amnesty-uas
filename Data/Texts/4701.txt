"Further information on UA 134/93 (AMR 23/28/93, 26 April) - Colombia: fear of ""disappearance"": Reinaldo Amorocho 
 Further information on UA 134/93 (AMR 23/28/93, 26 April) - Colombia: fear of ""disappearance"": Reinaldo Amorocho | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Amnesty International has been informed that the body of Reinaldo Amoroco has been found. Military sources have reportedly claimed that Reinaldo Amorocho was killed on 7 April 1993 in a confrontation between the Colombian armed forces and guerrillas from the People's Liberation Army, in Santander department. However eye-witnesses have reported seeing his detention on 7 April by soldiers from the army's Guane Battalion "
