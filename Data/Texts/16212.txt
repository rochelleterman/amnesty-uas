"Further information on UA 210/93 (EUR 62/16/93, 24 June) - Uzbekistan: fear of imminent execution: Dmitry Vasilyevich Rassokhin 
 Further information on UA 210/93 (EUR 62/16/93, 24 June) - Uzbekistan: fear of imminent execution: Dmitry Vasilyevich Rassokhin | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Dmitry Rassokhin has been transferred from the prison in Tashkent where he was on death row. His current whereabouts are unknown, but it is feared that his move may be to another prison where his sentence will be carried out. He was sentenced to death in July 1992. He is reported to have been seriously ill in recent months."
