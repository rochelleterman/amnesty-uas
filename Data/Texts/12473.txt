 Nepal: Possible ""disappearance""/fear for safety | Amnesty International 
 Four men were arrested by security force personnel in Kathmandu between 4 and 10 January 2005. Their whereabouts are now unknown, and Amnesty International is concerned that they may be tortured, or ""disappear"".
