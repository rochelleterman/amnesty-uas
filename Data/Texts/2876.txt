"Sudan: Further information on Incommunicado detention/Detention without charge. 
 Sudan: Further information on Incommunicado detention/Detention without charge. | Amnesty International 
 Al-Shafi' al-Tayeb Yusuf was released on 5 May 2003 from Kober prison. His brother, Lenin al-Tayeb Yusuf remains in detention and was seen in the political section of Kober prison by other detainees. Though held in the same prison, the two brothers were not able to meet.
