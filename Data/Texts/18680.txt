"Syria: Prisoner of conscience/fear of torture or ill-treatment: Muhammad Jum'ah Quban 
 Syria: Prisoner of conscience/fear of torture or ill-treatment: Muhammad Jum'ah Quban | Amnesty International 
 Prisoner of conscience, Muhammad Jum'ah Quban is being held in incommunicado detention at an unknown location where he is at risk of torture or ill-treatment. As a former leading member of the banned Democratic B'ath Party, Muhammad Jum'ah Quban has been arrested by two different security departments within a short space of time in a governmental crack-down on freedom of expression and association.
