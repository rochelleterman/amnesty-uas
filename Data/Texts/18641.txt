 Syria: Fear of torture and ill-treatment/Fear of incommunicado detention | Amnesty International 
 Four named Kurdish school children were reportedly arrested by members of the political security department at al-Talane' school, in the city of Qamishli, north-eastern Syria on 6 April. They were taken to a detention centre in the town of al-Hasaka, about 80km away.
