"United States of America (Oklahoma): Further information on Death penalty / Legal concern, Scott Allen Hain. 
 United States of America (Oklahoma): Further information on Death penalty / Legal concern, Scott Allen Hain. | Amnesty International 
 Scott Hain was executed in Oklahoma on 3 April for a double murder committed when he was 17 years old. International law, respected in almost every country in the world, prohibits the use of the death penalty against people who were under 18 at the time of the crime.
