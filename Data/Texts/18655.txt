"Syria: Further Information on fear of torture/incommunicado detention/prisoners of conscience and new concern: unfair trial 
 Syria: Further Information on fear of torture/incommunicado detention/prisoners of conscience and new concern: unfair trial | Amnesty International 
 Eight men ('Ali Nizar 'Ali (m), Husam 'Ali Mulhim (m), Tarek Ghorani (m), Maher Ibrahim (m), 'Ayham Saqr (m), 'Alam Fakhour (m), 'Omar 'Ali al-'Abdullah (m), Diab Sirieyeh (m)) were reportedly moved about 11 days ago to Sednaya prison, on the outskirts of Damascus. They have all been held in incommunicado detention since their arrests which took place between 26 January and18 March 2006. They remain at serious risk of torture and ill-treatment.
