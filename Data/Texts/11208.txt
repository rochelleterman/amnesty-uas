"India: Further information on fear for safety - Mohammad Yousuf Sofi 
 India: Further information on fear for safety -   Mohammad Yousuf Sofi

 | Amnesty International 
 According to reliable reports, Mohammad Yousuf Sofi has been moved to a police station and is being allowed regular access to his family and lawyer. However, he is still at risk of torture and ill-treatment. He has not been charged with any offence, or told why he is in custody
