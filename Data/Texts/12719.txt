 Nepal: Further information on ""Disappearance"" / Fear for Safety and new concern: Arbitrary detention | Amnesty International 
 Gopi Bahadur Bhandari has been released. Pushpa Lal Dhakal, who was thought to have ""disappeared"", is now reported to be in jail. Amnesty International is concerned that he appears to have been detained arbitrarily.
