"USA (Arkansas): Death penalty: William Frank Parker (aka Frankie Parker) 
 USA (Arkansas): Death penalty: William Frank Parker (aka Frankie Parker) | Amnesty International 
 William Frank Parker, white, is scheduled to be executed in Arkansas on 8 August 1996. While in prison, Parker has become a devout Buddhist, and according to press reports, Buddhists worldwide have appealed for clemency on his behalf.
