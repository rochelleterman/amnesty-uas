 USA (Missouri): Further information on death penalty: John Clayton Smith


 | Amnesty International 
 John Clayton Smith is scheduled to be executed in Missouri on 29 October 2003. He was sentenced to death in 1999 for the murder of his former girlfriend Brandie Kearnes and her stepfather, Wayne Hoewing, in 1997.
