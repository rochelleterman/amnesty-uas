"Kyrgyzstan: Fear of imminent execution: Vasily Skvortsov 
 Kyrgyzstan: Fear of imminent execution: Vasily Skvortsov | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Vasily Skvortsov was sentenced to death for murder on 9 June 1994. It is believed that his appeal was refused. A petition for clemency has been lodged with the Clemency Commission of Kyrgyzstan and the result is expected at any time. If this is refused, AI fears that he could be executed imminently."
