"EXTRA 77/93 - USA (Texas): death penalty: Antonio Bonham 
 EXTRA 77/93 - USA (Texas): death penalty: Antonio Bonham | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Antonio Bonham, black, is scheduled to be executed in Texas on 28 September 1993. He was sentenced to death in October 1991 by a jury from Harris County, Texas, which is notorious for its disparate treatment of African Americans in the criminal justice system. "
