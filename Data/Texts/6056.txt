 Guatemala: Fear for safety | Amnesty International 
 An attempt on the lives of two environmentalists, Carlos Albacete Rosales and his wife Piedad Espinosa Albacete, has been made in Guatemala City. Amnesty International believes that their lives and those of other colleagues are in grave danger.
