"USA (South Carolina): Further information on death penalty / legal concern: William Downs (m) 
 USA (South Carolina): Further information on death penalty / legal concern: William Downs (m) | Amnesty International 
 William Downs, white, aged 38, was executed in South Carolina on the evening of 14 July. He was sentenced to death in 2002 for the rape and murder of Keenan O'Mailia, a six-year-old boy. William Downs had given up appeals against his conviction and death sentence.
