"Turkey: Fear of ""disappearance"" / fear of extrajudicial execution 
 Turkey: Fear of ""disappearance"" / fear of extrajudicial execution | Amnesty International 
 Fatih Turhalli, Kurd: Fatih Turhalli was reportedly detained by plainclothes police officers at a bus terminal in Diyarbakir. He is thought to be detained at the Cevik Kuvvet detention centre where he would apparently have been tortured. Amnesty International is extremely concerned for his safety and fears that he may be extrajudicially executed.
