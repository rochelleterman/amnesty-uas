 Further information on UA 174/94 (EUR 44/32/94, 3 May; and follow-up EUR 44/36/94, 11 May) - Turkey: ""disappearance"" / fear for safety / fear of torture: Yavuz Yilmaz | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Yavuz Yilmaz was interrogated in incommunicado detention in Elazig Police Station until brought to court and formally arrested on or about 23 May. He is now held in Elbistan Prison. It is not known how he was treated in police custody or what charges have been brought against him."
