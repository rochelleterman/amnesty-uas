 Hojjatoleslam val muslimin Mohammad Taqi al-Dhakeri, Hojjatoleslam Fu'ad Fujian, Hadi Dhakeri, Hojjatoleslam Mohammed Saleh Hedayati and 7 others; and new names: Hojjatoleslam val muslimin Sheikh Mohammad Amin Ghafoori, Hojjatoleslam val muslimin Sayed Hossein Fali and several others: There have been several more arrests of followers of Grand Ayatollah Shirazi in January 1997. They include Hojjatoleslam val muslimin Sheikh Mohammad Amin Ghafoori, a well-known religious figure and writer, together with his wife, and Hojjatoleslam val muslimin Sayed Hossein Fali who is said to have been arrested and tortured on several previous occasions. Their whereabouts are unknown and there are fears that they may be at risk of torture or ill-treatment and that they may be prisoners of conscience.
