 Indonesia: Fear for safety / fear of torture or ill-treatment | Amnesty International 
 Two human rights activists, Indra P Keumala (alias Iin), and Happy (alias Lalok), have been detained by the Police Mobile Brigade (Brimob) in Southeast Aceh District, Aceh Province. Amnesty International believes they are at serious risk of torture or ill-treatment.
