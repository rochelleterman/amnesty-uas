 Egypt: Protests force regime change in Egypt: Further Information | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Following unprecedented protests by Egyptians protesting against poverty, corruption and police abuse, President Hosni Mubarak’s 30-year rule was brought to an end on 11 February 2011.The country is now governed by a council of military officers, the Higher Council of the Armed Forces (majlis al a’la lil quwwat al musallaha), who have dissolved the parliament and suspended the constitution.
