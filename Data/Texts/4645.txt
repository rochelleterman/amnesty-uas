"UA 134/93 - Colombia: fear of ""disappearance"": Reinaldo Amorocho 
 UA 134/93 - Colombia: fear of ""disappearance"": Reinaldo Amorocho | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Reinaldo Amorocho, a travelling salesman, has not been seen since he was reportedly detained by soldiers at about 6.30 pm on 7 April 1993. At the time he was travelling in the department of Santander. His detention has not been acknowledged and there is serious concern for his safety. "
