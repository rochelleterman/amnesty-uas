"Venezuela: Further information on fear of torture / fear of further arbitrary arrests 
 Venezuela: Further information on fear of torture / fear of further arbitrary arrests | Amnesty International 
 Wilma Rauseo (f), trade unionist, and new names: Graciela Aguirre Garcia (f), Marisela Baoda Medina (f), teacher: Two more members of the Movimiento Bolivariano Revolucionario 200 were arrested on 25 April 1997 and are being held by the Military Intelligence Directorate (DIM) in Boleita Norte, Caracas. There are fears that they may face torture or ill-treatment in military custody.
