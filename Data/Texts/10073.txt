 USA (Texas): Further information on Death penalty/Legal concern John Paul Penry | Amnesty International 
 On 16 November, the US Supreme Court blocked the          execution of John Paul Penry less than four hours before  it was due to be carried out. However, the Court has not  yet announced whether it will consider the merits of John Penry's appeal.
