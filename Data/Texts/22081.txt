 Iran: Further information on fear of execution | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Mohammad Reza Haddadi has been scheduled for execution on 27 May 2009. He has been sentenced to death after being convicted of a crime he was alleged to have committed while still under 18. There is no further information on Naser Qasemi, who was sentenced to death for a crime he was alleged to have committed when he was 15."
