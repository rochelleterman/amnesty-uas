"Colombia: Fear for safety 
 Colombia: Fear for safety | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is seriously concerned for the safety of ASPROVIVIR members, following the appearance of their names on a ""death list"" circulated in the city of Villavicencio, Meta Department, on 6 February by unknown individuals. According to reports, since then many of them have received death threats by telephone and Luis Castro whose name was also on the list has been killed.
