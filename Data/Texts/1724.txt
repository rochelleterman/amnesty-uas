"Nigeria: Fear of ill-treatment / possible prisoners of conscience: Chief Olabiyi Durojaiye 
 Nigeria: Fear of ill-treatment / possible prisoners of conscience: Chief Olabiyi Durojaiye | Amnesty International 
 Chief Olabiyi Durojaiye, aged 63, is a lawyer, an economist and a founding member of the National Democratic Coalition (NADECO). Since 3 December 1996 he has been detained in military custody, incommunicado and without charge, and he is believed to be a possible prisoner of conscience.
