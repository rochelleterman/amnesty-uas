"Colombia: Further information on fear for safety / possible extrajudicial executions / ""disappearance"" 
 Colombia: Further information on fear for safety / possible extrajudicial executions / ""disappearance"" | Amnesty International 
 Eliseo Narvaez Corrales, Eder Narvaez Corrales; Miriam Contreras (f), Wilmer Amaris: Eliseo and Eder Narvaez Corrales, two peasant farmer leaders involved with families forcibly displaced from the Bellacruz Ranch, department of Cesaar, by a paramilitary group, have been killed in circumstances suggesting they were extrajudicially executed. Paramilitaries forced Wilmer Amaris to take them to the brothers' farm and shot them dead as they came out of the house. Miriam Contreras, Eliseo Narvaez's wife, was injured.
