"USA: Death penalty / Legal concern, James Blake Colburn 
 USA: Death penalty / Legal concern, James Blake Colburn | Amnesty International 
 James Colburn is scheduled to be executed in Texas on 6   November 2002.  He was sentenced to death in October 1995 for the murder of 55-year-old Peggy Murphy in June 1994.  James Colburn has an extensive history of paranoid        schizophrenia, a serious mental illness whose symptoms    include delusions and hallucinations.
