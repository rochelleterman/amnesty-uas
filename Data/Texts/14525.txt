"Turkey: Further information on fear of torture: Riza Dinc 
 Turkey: Further information on fear of torture: Riza Dinc | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Lawyer Riza Dinc has now been formally charged with membership of an illegal organization (Rizgari) and committed to prison. He has reported being extensively tortured in police custody and entered a formal complaint at his hearing on 9 February 1995. No further action appears to have been taken by the authorities regarding his allegations."
