"USA (Ohio): Further Information on Death penalty / Legal concern, Gregory Lott. 
 USA (Ohio): Further Information on Death penalty / Legal concern, Gregory Lott. | Amnesty International 
 The Ohio Supreme Court has granted an indefinite stay of  execution to Gregory Lott because of his claim of mental  retardation. He was sentenced to death in 1987 for the    murder of John McGrath in 1986.
