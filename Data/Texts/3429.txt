 Further Information on Democratic Republic of Congo: Detention without charge/prisoner of conscience, N'sii Luanda Shandwe (m),  human rights defender. | Amnesty International 
 Human rights activist N'sii Luanda Shandwe is currently   convalescing in a Kinshasa clinic after undergoing an     operation for appendicitis. He appears to be recovering   well from the operation, but may soon be returned to      Kinshasa's main prison.
