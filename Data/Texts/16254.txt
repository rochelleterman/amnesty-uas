"Uzbekistan: Further information on Fear of imminent execution 
 Uzbekistan: Further information on  Fear of imminent execution | Amnesty International 
 Nikolay Ganiyev's death sentence was commuted by the Board of the Supreme Court on 14 February. He has nine years and six months left to serve in prison. Amnesty International has just received reports that Nigmatullo Fayzullayev was executed at the end of April. It is feared that his co-accused Maksim Strakhov could be executed at any time.
