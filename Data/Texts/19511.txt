 Guinea-Bissau: Torture/lack of medical care for several detainees: Medical Action. | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Amnesty International is seriously concerned for the      health of four army officers, who reportedly have been    tortured and ill-treated on several occasions. Fode       Mandjam, Queita Mané and Almane Alam Camará have been     held incommunicado without charge. The fourth officer, is in a coma, reportedly as a result of torture. Another     officer, Mussá Cassamá died in detention, reportedly as a result of torture. Furthermore,Ernesto Carvalho,          vice-president of the political party Unidade Nacional is being held incommunicado without charge. Following        abdominal surgery, several months ago, he has missed one  check-up and is reportedly weak.
