 Iran: Iranian-Canadian facing execution in Iran: Hamid Ghassemi-Shall | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 A man with dual Iranian-Canadian nationality, Hamid Ghassemi-Shall, appears to be at imminent risk of execution. His family were told on 15 April that his death sentence had been passed to the body within the Judiciary that carries out executions. "
