"Iran: Fear of torture and ill-treatment/Incommunicado detention 
 Iran: Fear of torture and ill-treatment/Incommunicado detention | Amnesty International 
 Four men (Firouz Abou Farhan, also known as Nasr Daraji, Eshaghi, Abdulali Douraghi and Karim Douraghi), all members of Iran's Arab minority, were reportedly arrested on 6 November, and are now believed to be held incommunicado at an unknown location. They are at risk of torture or ill-treatment.
