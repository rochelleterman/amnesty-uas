 Democratic Republic of Congo: Further information on Detention without charge/prisoner of conscience, N'sii Luanda Shandwe (m),  human rights defender. | Amnesty International 
 Human rights defender N'sii Luanda Shandwe is facing charges of treason, following his arrest at home on 19 April. Amnesty International considers the charges to be unfounded and an attempt to censure his human rights work.
