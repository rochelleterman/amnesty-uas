"USA (Oklahoma): Further Information on Death penalty / Legal concern - Hung Thanh Le (m), Vietnamese national 
 USA (Oklahoma): Further Information on Death penalty / Legal concern - Hung Thanh Le (m), Vietnamese national | Amnesty International 
 On 6 February, the Oklahoma Court of Criminal Appeals set Hung Thanh Le's execution date as 26 February. Governor Brad Henry of Oklahoma had rejected a unanimous recommendation by the state Pardon and Parole Board that this former Vietnamese refugee's death sentence should be commuted to life imprisonment.
