 Kazakhstan: Ethnic Uzbeks at risk of torture if returned: Further information | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Kazakhstan extradited 28 ethnic Uzbeks to Uzbekistan on 9 June 2011, where they are at real risk of torture. Four more ethnic Uzbek men are also at risk of forcible return. The wives and children of all 32 men are also at risk of extradition."
