"Indonesia/East Timor: Fear for safety / arbitrary arrests / unlawful killings 
 Indonesia/East Timor: Fear for safety / arbitrary arrests / unlawful killings | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is concerned for the safety of two women, Etelvina and Atina, and possibly other East Timorese from the District of Same, who have allegedly been taken into custody by the Indonesian Armed Forces (ABRI) following an attack on the ABRI headquarters. Vincente de Andrade is believed to have been killed in an ABRI attack on Taitudak village.
