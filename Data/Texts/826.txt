"Ethiopia: Health concern: Asrat Woldeyes 
 Ethiopia: Health concern: Asrat Woldeyes | Amnesty International 
 Dr Asrat Woldeyes, one of Ethiopia's most prominent medical doctors,and leader of an officially recognised opposition party, in prison since 1994, is gravely ill. There are fears that he is not receiving adequate medical care and treatment.
