 EXTRA 18/93 - Turkey: fear of torture: Abdullah Avsar | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Abdullah Avsar was detained on 20 February 1993 and is being held incommunicado by the police in Izmir. His lawyer has applied for permission to see him but has reportedly not been successful. AI fears that he in being interrogated under torture. "
