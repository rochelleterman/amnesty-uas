"Guatemala: Further information. Fear for safety: 22 Street children, aged 13-17 
 Guatemala: Further information. Fear for safety: 22 Street children, aged 13-17 | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International has learned that  the 22 street     children detained on 2 February 1999 and accused of       'public nuisance', were released after some days in       juvenile detention centres. Apparently no formal          accusation was ever made against them.
