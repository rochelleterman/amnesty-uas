 Mexico: Lawyer at risk after partner killed | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Lawyer Ernesto Rábago Martínez was shot and killed in his office in Chihuahua City, northern Mexico on 2 March 2010. He and his female partner, Estela Ángeles Mondragón, had previously been threatened and attacked because of their legal work for an Indigenous community involved in a land dispute. Estela Ángeles Mondragón and her daughter are in grave danger."
