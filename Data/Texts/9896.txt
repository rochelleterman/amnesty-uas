"USA (North Carolina): Further information on death penalty / legal concern: Johnnie Lee McKnight 
 USA (North Carolina): Further information on death penalty / legal concern: Johnnie Lee McKnight | Amnesty International 
 A hearing into Johnnie McKnight's competency to stand     trial will resume on 18 September 2000. The prosecution   is continuing to seek both to try this learning disabled, mentally ill young man, and to obtain a death sentence    against him for a crime he allegedly committed when he    was 17 years old.
