 Burundi: Further information on fear of ill-treatment / torture / medical concern / death in custody / fear of ""disappearance"" | Amnesty International 
 Commandant Ndabacekure, army officer, Captain Nziyimana, army officer, Isaac Nitereka, member of the executive board of PARENA and PARENA representative for urban Bujumbura, Alexis Simbavimbere, PARENA member, Laurent Bimenyumuremyi, PARENA deputy provincial representative in Gitega, Emmanuel Manzi, a Rwandese national, also known as Paul Mugambage, ex-member of the Rwandese Patriotic Army, Isidore Rufyikiri, senior PARENA official, Army Lt-Col (retd.) Pascal Ntako, senior PARENA official; Jean Paul Bizimana, Leonidas Ntakaye, member of the executive board of PARENA: Amnesty International has received confirmation that Laurent Bimenyumuremyi is currently being held without charge in Muramvya, and has not reportedly been ill-treated since his transfer. There had been fears that he had ""disappeared"".
