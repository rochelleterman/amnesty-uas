"UA 107/93 - Iran: Death Penalty: Salim Saberniah, Mustafa Ghaderi 
 UA 107/93 - Iran: Death Penalty: Salim Saberniah, Mustafa Ghaderi | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Salim Saberniah and Mustafa Ghaderi were reportedly sentenced to death by the Islamic Revolutionary Court of Tabriz after being arrested attempting to cross the border into Turkey. The sentences were confirmed by the Supreme Court on 21 March 1993."
