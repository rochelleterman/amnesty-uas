"USA (Texas): Further information: Death penalty, James Vernon Allridge 
 USA (Texas): Further information: Death penalty, James Vernon Allridge | Amnesty International 
 James Allridge was executed in Texas on 26 August 2004. He was sentenced to death in 1987 for the murder of a convenience store worker, Brian Clendennen, two years earlier.
