"Bangladesh: Further information on Prisoner of Conscience/Fear of torture, Shahriar Kabir 
 Bangladesh: Further information on Prisoner of Conscience/Fear of torture, Shahriar Kabir | Amnesty International 
 Prisoner of conscience Shahriar Kabir was released on 20 January, on six months' interim bail. The police are reportedly seeking to appeal against the bail order, which was granted by the High Court Division of the Supreme Court on 19 January.
