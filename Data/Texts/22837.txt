 Syria: Further information: Death in custody of Syrian activist: Ghayath Mattar | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Syrian activist Ghayath Mattar, aged 26, is reported to have been killed in custody following his arrest by Syrian security forces on 6 September. His dead body was returned to his family on 10 September 2011. Four other activists arrested at the same time are in grave danger. "
