"Cameroon: Detention without charge/Fear of torture or     ill-treatment, seven human rights activists 
 Cameroon: Detention without charge/Fear of torture or     ill-treatment, seven human rights activists | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is concerned for the safety of      Albert Mukong, a human rights activist and former         executive director of the Human Rights Defence Group      (HRDG), and six members of Southern Cameroon National     Council (SCNC). They are detained without charge at Mamfe Gendarmerie station, South West province and at risk of   being tortured or ill-treated.
