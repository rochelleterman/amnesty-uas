"UA 265/94 - Colombia: ""disappearances"": Luis Alirio Rodriguez Pineda 
 UA 265/94 - Colombia: ""disappearances"": Luis Alirio Rodriguez Pineda | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Luis Alirio Rodriguez Pineda, a peasant farmer, is reported to have ""disappeared"" after being abducted on 13 June 1994 from his home in San Pablo, Municipio de Teorama, Norte de Santander department. Reports indicate that early that morning a group of men raided his house and beat him under interrogation. They also threatened his wife and their baby. According to a witness at Barranquilla Model Prison, he and Luis Rodriguez were tortured in the base of the Army Second Brigade in Barranquilla until 24 June."
