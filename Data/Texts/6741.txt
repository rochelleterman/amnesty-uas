 Haiti: Fear for safety/Possible excessive use of force: Demonstrators in Port-au-Prince

 | Amnesty International 
 The alleged fatal shooting on 13 February of a demonstrator during protests against the results of recent elections has heightened tensions in the capital, Port-au-Prince. The man's death has been attributed by protestors to members of the United Nations Stabilisation Force in Haiti.
