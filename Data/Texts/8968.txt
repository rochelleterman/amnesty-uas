 Further information on EXTRA 26/94 (AMR 51/28/94, 27 April) - USA (Arkansas): death penalty: Jonas Whitmore, Edward Pickens | Amnesty International

 Jonas Whitmore was executed as scheduled in Arkansas on 11 May 1994. His execution was followed an hour later by the execution of Edward Pickens, black, who had been sentenced to death in 1976 for murder."
