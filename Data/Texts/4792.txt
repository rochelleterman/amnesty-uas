"Further information on UA 124/93 (AMR 23/25/93, 22 April) - Colombia: fear of ""disappearance"": Delio Vargas Herrera 
 Further information on UA 124/93 (AMR 23/25/93, 22 April) - Colombia: fear of ""disappearance"": Delio Vargas Herrera | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 The office of the Colombian Presidential Adviser on Human Rights has informed Amnesty International that following an investigation by the Special Investigations Unit of the Procurator General's Office and the Technical Investigations Unit (CTI) of Villavicencio, the vehicle that was used in the abduction and subsequent ""disappearance"" of Delio Vargas, and its driver have been identified. The driver, a former army officer and member of the army intelligence force B2 attached to the army's VII Brigade based in Villavicencio, is now in detention. However the whereabouts of Delio Vargas, the 28-year-old president of a human rights group who was last seen on 19 April 1993, remain unknown. "
