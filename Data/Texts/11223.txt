"India: Further information on Fear for safety 
 India: Further information on Fear for safety | Amnesty International 
 Human rights defenders Teesta Setalvad, Rais Khan Azeezkhan Pathan and Suhel Tirmizi have received a number of threats. The three are involved in protecting witnesses, including Zahira Sheikh and Sehruneesa Sheikh, who have provided testimony against those accused of taking part in communal violence in the state of Gujarat in February and March 2002.
