 Iraq: Further information on Fear of torture or ill-treatment		 | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Ryadh al-Qaysi and Fakhri ‘Abd Fatlawi, professors at Baghdad University’s Faculty of Dentistry, and four other Baghdad University staff members who were arrested on 17 February 2008, have been released. However, one professor, Ussama al-Mulla, remains in the custody of the Iraqi security forces and is at risk of torture and ill-treatment."
