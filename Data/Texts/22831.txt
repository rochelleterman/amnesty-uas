 Syria: Further information: Activist continues to be held incommunicado | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Syrian political activist George Sabra has been detained incommunicado at an undisclosed location since security forces arrested him on 20 July 2011 in Qatana, Syria. He appears to be a victim of enforced disappearance and is at serious risk of torture and other ill-treatment. "
