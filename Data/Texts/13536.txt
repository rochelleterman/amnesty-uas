 Sri Lanka: ""Disappearance""
 | Amnesty International 
 The whereabouts of Velupillai Tharmalingham and Ponnusamy Sivakumar (known as ""Raja"") are unknown since they were   arrested by the Sri Lanka army in separate incidents      earlier this month in    the Vavuniya area. Amnesty       International is concerned for their safety.
