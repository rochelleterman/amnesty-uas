"USA (Iowa): Further information on death penalty: Possible reintroduction of the death penalty 
 USA (Iowa): Further information on death penalty: Possible reintroduction of the death penalty | Amnesty International 
 The Iowa legislature will not debate or vote upon reinstatement of the death penalty.  Proponents of the death penalty decided on 12 February 1998 to drop their plans for a full debate because of lack of support. The issue will not be raised again during 1998.
