 Turkey: Fear of ""disappearance"" / fear of torture: Suleyman Seyhan | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Suleyman Seyhan, a Kurd, has been held in unacknowledged detention since 30 October 1995. It is feared he is being held under torture. He and his sons and daughters have been detained and allegedly tortured on several occasions."
