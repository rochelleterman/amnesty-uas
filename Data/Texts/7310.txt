"Mexico: fear for safety: Bishop Arturo Lona Reyes, human rights activist 
 Mexico: fear for safety: Bishop Arturo Lona Reyes, human rights activist | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 On 29 June 1995, the car in which Bishop Arturo Lona Reyes was travelling was shot at by two masked men. During the past 10 years Arturo Lona Reyes, who is Bishop of Tehuantepec and a well-known human rights activist, has reportedly received death threats on six occasions before this latest attack. All the threats were reportedly as a result of his activities on behalf of the region's poor and dispossessed, particularly the indigenous."
