"Thailand:Health concern / Fear of forcible repatriation / Prisoner of conscience - Sok Yoeun 
 Thailand:Health concern / Fear of forcible repatriation / Prisoner of conscience  - Sok Yoeun | Amnesty International 
 Recognised refugee and prisoner of conscience Sok Yoeun is being denied medical attention, although his health has deteriorated drastically in the last few weeks. There are also fears that he may be forcibly returned to Cambodia, where he is wanted for political reasons.
