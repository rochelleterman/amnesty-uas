 Indonesia (East Timor): Fear for safety: The population   of East Timor

 | Amnesty International 
  
 Amnesty International fears for the safety of the entire  population of East Timor. Armed pro-Indonesian militias   and Indonesian troops have complete control of the        territory and are committing grave human rights           violations with impunity."
