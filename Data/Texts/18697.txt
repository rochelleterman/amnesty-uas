 Syria: Incommunicado detention / fear of torture or ill-treatment: 'Ali Sayed al-Shihabi

 | Amnesty International 
 'Ali Sayed al-Shihabi has not been seen or heard of since he was summoned to a meeting with State Security agents in the capital Damascus at 10am on 10 August 2006. He is at risk of torture and other ill-treatment.
