 Syria: Further information: Detainee faces secret military trial in Syria: Bassel Khartabil   | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Bassel Khartabil has been detained incommunicado since his arrest on 15 March. He is believed to have been referred to a Military Field Court, which does not allow for the right to defence or for appeals to verdicts and whose proceedings are conducted in secret."
