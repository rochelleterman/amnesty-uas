 Mexico: Death threats / Fear for safety | Amnesty International 
 Four policemen convicted of shooting dead Eduardo Torres Garcicrespo may shortly be released, and it is feared that they will try to take revenge on his family, whose testimony helped to convict them. The family have received death threats.
