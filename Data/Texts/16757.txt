"Egypt: Further information on Fear of torture or ill-treatment/ Incommunicado detention. 
 Egypt: Further information on Fear of torture or ill-treatment/ Incommunicado detention. | Amnesty International 
 Mahmud Hassan Hassan, Ramiz Gihad and Wa'el Tawfiq remain in detention and are reportedly held at Borg al-'Arab Prison, near Alexandria. They have been denied access to their lawyers or relatives and were reportedly tortured or ill-treated whilst being held at the headquarters of the State Security Intelligence (SSI) in Lazoghly Square.
