"Colombia: Further information on fear for safety: members of the Asociacion de Familiares de Detenidos y Desaparecidos (ASFADDES), human rights organization 
 Colombia: Further information on fear for safety: members of the Asociacion de Familiares de Detenidos y Desaparecidos (ASFADDES), human rights organization | Amnesty International 
 New names: Jose Daniel Alvarez Ruiz, ASFADDES General Coordinator, Javier Alvarez Ruiz, project worker with CICAN (Infant Centre-House of the Child and other CICAN workers: Javier Alvarez Ruiz is reported to have received a written death threat on 2 August 1997. He works for CICAN which is an ASFADDES project. As a result of these death threats AI is not only seriously concerned for the safety of Javier and Jose Daniel Alvarez but also of other members of ASFADDES and CICAN.
