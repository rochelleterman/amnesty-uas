"USA (Texas): Further information on Death penalty / Legal concern, Napoleon Beazley 
 USA (Texas): Further information  on Death    penalty / Legal concern, Napoleon Beazley | Amnesty International 
 Napoleon Beazley remains scheduled to be executed in Texas on 15 August. He was sentenced to death in 1995 for a murder in Tyler, Smith County, committed when he was 17 years old. International law prohibits the execution of those who were under 18 at the time of the crime.
