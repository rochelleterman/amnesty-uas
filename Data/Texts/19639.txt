 Sudan: Incommunicado detention/fear of torture, Beshara Mugaw Tagibbo (m) | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Shopkeeper Beshara Mugaw Tagibbo was arrested on 14 February 2008 by the National Intelligence and Security Services (NISS). His family has not been allowed to visit him. He is at risk of torture or ill-treatment."
