 Russian Federation: Further information: Tajikistani asylum seeker faces extradition | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Moscow City Court has upheld the order of the Prosecutor General to extradite Nizomhon Juraev to Tajikistan, despite Nizomhon Juraev having applied for asylum in the Russian Federation. If returned to Tajikistan, Nizomhon Juraev is at risk of torture and ill-treatment.  "
