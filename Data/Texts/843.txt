 Ethiopia: Further information on fear for safety / legal concern | Amnesty International 
 Abebe Abashu, Yadesa Bedassa, Alemayehu Dirro, Ademe Gebre-Senbet, Sori Kitila, Dawit Mekonnen, Isayas Negatu, Mohamed Sheka, Alemayehu Umatta, Muktar Usman and Hundesa Wakwaya have been released from detention. It is possible that  Alemu Kanaa,  Amsalu Taye, Bekele Tefera, Tejitu Tucho (f) and many others of the Oromo ethnic group who were arrested with them have also been released.
