"Israel and the Occupied Territories: Further information on torture / legal concern: Khaled Farraj 
 Israel and the Occupied Territories: Further information on torture / legal concern: Khaled Farraj | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Khaled Farraj was released without charge on the evening of 16 May 1995. He had been detained and interrogated for a total of 56 days since his arrest on 21 March. Following his release he stated in an affidavit that he had been subjected to torture and ill-treatment in custody."
