"Egypt: Further information Death Penalty/Unfair trial/Risk of imminent execution: Ahmed Ibrahim al-Sayyid al-Naggar 
 Egypt: Further information Death Penalty/Unfair trial/Risk of imminent execution: Ahmed Ibrahim al-Sayyid al-Naggar | Amnesty International 
 Ahmed Ibrahim al-Sayyid al-Naggar was executed by hanging at al-Isti'naf prison in Cairo on 23 February 2000. He    was sentenced to death in absentia in an unfair trial     before the Supreme Military Court in Cairo in October     1997.
