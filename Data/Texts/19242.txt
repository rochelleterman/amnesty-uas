"Tunisia: Further information on legal concern / prisoner of conscience / health concern: Khemais Chammari 
 Tunisia: Further information on legal concern / prisoner of conscience / health concern: Khemais Chammari | Amnesty International 
 Prisoner of conscience Khemais Chammari, a human rights activist and parliamentarian, stopped his hunger-strike on 19 September 1996 due to health problems. Amnesty International remains concerned that he is not receiving adequate medical treatment.
