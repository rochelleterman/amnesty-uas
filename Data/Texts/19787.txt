 Argentina: Further information: Teenage rape survivor gets legal abortion | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 A 15-year-old girl from the southern Argentinian province of Chubut was given a legal abortion on 20 March. She was pregnant after being raped by her step-father. An appeal judge ruled that the abortion could go ahead after she had previously been denied the abortion by a family court judge."
