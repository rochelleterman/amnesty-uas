"Argentina: Further information on fear for safety / death threats 
 Argentina: Further information on fear for safety / death threats | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International has received no further reports of  threats or intimidation of human rights defenders Sonia   Torres de Parodi, Elvio Zanotti and his family, and Maria Teresa Sanchez. Earlier this year all three, who work for the human rights organisation Abuelas de Plaza de Mayo,   Grandmothers of the Plaza de Mayo, received threatening   letters and were followed and spied on.
