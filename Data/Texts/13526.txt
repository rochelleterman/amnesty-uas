"Sri Lanka: Fear for safety: Somaratne Rajapakse 
 Sri Lanka: Fear for safety: Somaratne Rajapakse | Amnesty International 
 There are fears for the safety of Somaratne Rajapakse who is in Welikade prison hospital following an attack on him by prison guards on 23 August 1998.  One of the attackers subsequently visited him in the hospital and allegedly threatened him.
