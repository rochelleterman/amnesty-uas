"Venezuela: Fear for safety/use of excessive force 
 Venezuela: Fear for safety/use of excessive force | Amnesty International 
 The security forces have reportedly used excessive force, and detained large numbers of opposition supporters, during nationwide street protests against the government of President Hugo Chavez. At least nine people have died and scores of others have been injured, including a number of police officers.
