"Tunisia: Further information on fear of torture / legal concern / prisoner of conscience: Radhia Aouididi (f) 
 Tunisia: Further information on fear of torture / legal concern / prisoner of conscience: Radhia Aouididi (f) | Amnesty International 
 Radhia Aouididi (f), aged 27: Information has just been received that the pre-trial detention of prisoner of conscience Radhia Aouididi has been renewed for a further four months. She was arrested on 9 November 1996, and was held for a month illegally in incommunicado detention, during which time she was beaten, ill-treated and threatened with rape. Amnesty International is continuing to call for her immediate and unconditional release as a prisoner of conscience.
