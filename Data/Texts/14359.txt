"Turkey: Further information on fear of torture or ill-treatment: Semdin Saklk and Arif Saklk (not Hasan, as originally stated) 
 Turkey: Further information on fear of torture or ill-treatment: Semdin Saklk and Arif Saklk (not Hasan, as originally stated) | Amnesty International 
 On 24 April 1998 Semdin and Arif Saklk were committed to prison reportedly on charges of treason, which carries the dealth penalty. While in detention they were given no access to a lawyer and Semdin Saklk was reportedly hospitalized for a kidney complaint.
