"Guatemala: Further information on fear for safety 
 Guatemala: Further information on fear for safety | Amnesty International 
 There continues to be concern for trade unionists Debora Guzman Chupen, Felix Gonzalez, her husband, and their four-month old baby. They have received yet more death threats and have had to move several times to secret locations.
