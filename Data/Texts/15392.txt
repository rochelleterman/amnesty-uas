"Turkey: Fear of ""disappearance"" / fear of torture: Masallah Yalcin 
 Turkey: Fear of ""disappearance"" / fear of torture: Masallah Yalcin | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 On 13 December 1995, police officers detained Masallah Yalcin, a Kurdish worker, aged 24. His parents have been unable to trace his whereabouts since and it is feared that he is being interrogated under torture and may ""disappear"" in unacknowledged police custody."
