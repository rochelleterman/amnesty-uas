 Rwanda: Further information: Politician charged, was not ill-treated: Deogratias Mushayidi | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Rwandan politician Deogratias Mushayidi has now been granted access to legal counsel, and was brought before a court on 18 March.  His lawyer has confirmed that he is in good health and has been treated well by the authorities."
