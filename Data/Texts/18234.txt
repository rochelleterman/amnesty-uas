"Palestinian Authority: Further information on prisoner of conscience / torture or  ill-treatment: Maher al-Dasuqi 
 Palestinian Authority: Further information on prisoner of conscience / torture or  ill-treatment: Maher al-Dasuqi | Amnesty International 
 Maher al-Dasuqi was released without charge on 4 October  1999. He is reported to be in good health. He had been    arrested on 15 September, shortly after a Palestinian     prisoner's mother criticized the Palestinian Authority on the television programme that he hosts.
