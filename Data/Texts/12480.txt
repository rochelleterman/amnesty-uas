 NEPAL: Medical concern: Yagya Raj Pant (m), student activist

 | Amnesty International 
 Student activist Yagya Raj Pant was reportedly severely beaten by police when he was arrested on 26 January 2006. According to the non-governmental organization, the Forum for Protection of People's Rights - Nepal (PPR-Nepal), he has not received adequate medical treatment for his injuries.
