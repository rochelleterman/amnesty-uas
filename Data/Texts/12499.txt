"Nepal: Further Information on Fear for safety/possible ""disappearance""/medical concern 
 Nepal: Further Information on Fear for safety/possible ""disappearance""/medical concern | Amnesty International 
 Bhagarith Kharel was released on bail on 30 December 2003, but immediately rearrested by police. He is reportedly now held at the Central Jail in Kathmandu. Bhagirath Karel's family is concerned that he is not receiving proper medical treatment for a heart condition. Amnesty International has no further information on the whereabouts of Maha Prasad Angai.
