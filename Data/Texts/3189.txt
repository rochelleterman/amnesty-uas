 Uganda/Rwanda: Fear of torture or ill-treatment/Fear of refoulement | Amnesty International 
 Three Rwandese men were detained by Ugandan security forces on 9 October. Amnesty International is concerned that they are at risk of torture or ill-treatment and that two of the men may be forcibly repatriated (""refouled"") to Rwanda.
