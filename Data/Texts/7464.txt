"Mexico: Further information on fear for safety/excessive use of force 
 Mexico: Further information on fear for safety/excessive use of force | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International has received further information about the violent confrontations that took place on 14 June, when State police entered the central square in Oaxaca City with the intention of evicting striking teachers. While strikers and police were reportedly injured in the confrontation, Amnesty International has been able to confirm that no deaths occurred.
