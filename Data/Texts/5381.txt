"Colombia: Fear for Safety/Possible ""Disappearance"", Aury Sara Marrugo 
 Colombia: Fear for Safety/Possible ""Disappearance"", Aury Sara Marrugo | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is seriously concerned for the      safety of Trade Union leader Aury Sara Marrugo, who has   ""disappeared"" after reportedly being abducted by armed    gunmen on 30 November.  The kidnapping, follows some      twenty days of threatening phone calls to Union Sindical  Obrera (USO), Oil Workers' Trade union members around     Colombia.
