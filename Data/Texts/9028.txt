"Further information on EXTRA 17/94 (AMR 51/17/94, 25 March; and follow-up AMR 51/24/94, 22 April) - USA (Maryland): death penalty: John Frederick Thanos 
 Further information on EXTRA 17/94 (AMR 51/17/94, 25 March; and follow-up AMR 51/24/94, 22 April) - USA (Maryland): death penalty: John Frederick Thanos | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 John Frederick Thanos was executed by lethal injection in Maryland on 17 May 1994. He was notified of his execution only one hour before it took place. This was the first execution in Maryland in nearly 33 years."
