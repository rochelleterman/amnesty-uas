 Nigeria: Execution / fear of further executions: 32 prisoners convicted of armed robbery | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Eighteen prisoners convicted of armed robbery offences were executed by firing squad on or around 8 September 1995.  The executions took place in a sports stadium in Warri, Delta State, southern Nigeria.  They are reported to have been carried out as part of a crackdown on crime in the region.  AI fears that more executions may be carried out in the near future."
