"Ukraine: Fear of imminent execution: Konstantin Aleksandrovich Venik 
 Ukraine: Fear of imminent execution: Konstantin Aleksandrovich Venik | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Konstantin Aleksandrovich Venik, aged 23, is facing imminent execution unless his sentence is commuted by the President of Ukraine. He was sentenced to death for murder by the Nikolayev Regional Court on 21 December 1994."
