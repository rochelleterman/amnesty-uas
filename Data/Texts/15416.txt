 Turkey: Fear of ""disappearance"" / fear of torture | Amnesty International 
 Fatma Turunc (f), mother of three, Kanber Turunc, husband of Fatma, aged 40+: Amnesty International is concerned for the safety of the above, a Kurdish street seller and his wife. On 15 October 1996, Kanber Turunc failed to return to his home in Gaziantep. Ten days later, police officers reportedly detained his wife who was at home with her three children.
