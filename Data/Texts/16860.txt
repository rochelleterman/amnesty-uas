 Egypt: Further information on prisoners of conscience / fear of further prisoners of conscience / fear of torture | Amnesty International 
 Akram Alfy Mikha'il, student, Mostafa Mohammad Mostafa, civil servant, and Kamal Khalil Khalil, engineer: Akram Alfy Mikha'il and Mostafa Mohammad Mostafa were released from detention on 16 October 1997. Kamal Khalil Khalil remains in detention and is currently being held in Mazra'at Tora Prison, near Cairo. Amnesty International considers him to be a prisoner of conscience.
