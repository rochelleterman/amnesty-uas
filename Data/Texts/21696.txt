 Serbia (Kosovo): Further information: Families at renewed risk of eviction | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Thirty-three families, including Roma displaced from Kosovo, could be forcibly evicted from their homes in Belgrade, Serbia's capital, at any time from 1 March 2012. Some of the families have been offered accommodation that does not meet international standards on adequate housing, while others will be left homeless. "
