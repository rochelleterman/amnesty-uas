"Rwanda: ""Disappearance"": Emmanuel Munyemanzi 
 Rwanda: ""Disappearance"": Emmanuel Munyemanzi | Amnesty International 
 Emmanuel Munyemanzi, a journalist who worked as head of production for Rwanda's national television station, ""disappeared"" in the capital Kigali on 5 May 1998. He went to work as usual in the morning and never returned.  His fate remains unknown.
