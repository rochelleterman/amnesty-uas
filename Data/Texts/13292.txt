"UA 71/94 - Singapore: death penalty: Chin Seow Noi, Chin Yau Kim, Ng Kim Heng 
 UA 71/94 - Singapore: death penalty: Chin Seow Noi, Chin Yau Kim, Ng Kim Heng | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Chin Seow Noi, her brother Chin Yau Kim and Ng Kim Heng were sentenced to death in October 1992 for the murder of a woman. Their appeal to the Court of Criminal Appeal was rejected in May 1993. A final appeal will now be made to the President for clemency."
