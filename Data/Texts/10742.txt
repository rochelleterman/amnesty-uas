"People's Republic of China: death penalty: Wang Yougen 
 People's Republic of China: death penalty: Wang Yougen | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 On 22 March 1995, the newspaper Zhejiang Gongren Ribao reported that Wang Yougen had been sentenced to death by Hangzhou Municipal Intermediate People's Court in Zhejiang Province. He had been convicted of stealing a car. It is not known whether he has appealed against the sentence."
