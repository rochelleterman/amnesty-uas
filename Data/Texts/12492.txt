"Nepal: Fear of torture/fear for safety, Pushpa Raj Kadariya 
 Nepal: Fear of torture/fear for safety,  Pushpa Raj Kadariya | Amnesty International 
 Farmer, Pushpa Raj Kadariya was reportedly arrested by army personnel while on his way home in Ramechhap district on 30 January. Amnesty International is concerned that he may be subjected to torture and ill-treatment in detention.
