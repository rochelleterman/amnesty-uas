 EXTRA 26/94 - USA (Arkansas): death penalty: Jonas Whitmore | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Jonas Whitmore, white, is scheduled to be executed in Arkansas on 11 May 1994. He was sentenced to death for murder in 1987. An affidavit by his aunt claimed that he was severely physically abused as a child by his father. His aun was not asked to testify at his trial."
