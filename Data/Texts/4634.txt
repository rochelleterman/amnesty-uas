"Colombia: Fear of summary execution 
 Colombia: Fear of summary execution | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is concern that the Fuerzas Armadas Revolucionarias de Colombia (FARC), the largest armed     opposition group in the country, are intending to         summarily try and possibly execute a local FARC commander 'Gildardo' reportedly responsible for the kidnapping and  killing of  three US indigenous rights activists.
