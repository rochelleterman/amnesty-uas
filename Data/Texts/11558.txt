"Indonesia: Further information on Fear for safety / Fear of torture or ill-treatment 
 Indonesia: Further information on Fear for safety / Fear of torture or ill-treatment | Amnesty International 
 Sixteen people are still held in Manokwari police station (Police Resort, Polres), where they are at serious risk of torture or ill-treatment. Some are thought to have been charged. Following their initial 10-minute meeting with lawyers on 23 May, their access to legal representation continues to be restricted.
