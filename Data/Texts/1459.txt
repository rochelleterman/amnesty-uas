"Mauritania: Further information on  Fear for safety / ill-treatment 
 NA 
 Eight female opposition members, Marième Mint Saiga,      UFD/EN women coordinator in Almina, Benina Mint Chedad,   UFD/EN women coordinator in Riadh, and six other female   UFD/EN members, who had been arrested by the police, have been released. However, five male opposition leaders are  still detained incommunicado, and another opposition      supporter, Mohamed Mounir, has been arrested. Amnesty     International is concerned for their safety.
