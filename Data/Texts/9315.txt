"Further information on EXTRA 50/93 (AMR 51/44/93, 9 June) - USA (Texas): death penalty: John Selvage 
 Further information on EXTRA 50/93 (AMR 51/44/93, 9 June) - USA (Texas): death penalty: John Selvage | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 John Selvage, who was scheduled to be executed on 20 July 1993, has been granted a stay of execution. According to reports, the stay was granted by a District Court judge because Selvage, a paranoid schizophrenic, is incompetent to be executed. "
