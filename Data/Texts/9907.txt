"USA (Virginia):  Further information on death penalty:  Derek Rocco Barnabei 
 USA (Virginia):  Further information on death penalty:  Derek Rocco Barnabei | Amnesty International 
 Derek Barnabei was executed on 14 September 2000 in       Virginia. In his final statement he maintained his        innocence of the 1993 rape and murder of Sarah Wisnosky,  for which he was sentenced to death. He had been on death row for five years.
