 Mexico: Further information on fear for safety / Intimidation 

 | Amnesty International 
 Leading human rights lawyer Leonel Guadalupe Rivero narrowly escaped being attacked on 6 April, by three men who beat up his bodyguards. Amnesty International is seriously concerned for his safety, as he was a colleague of human rights lawyer Digna Ochoa, who was shot dead in her office in October 2001.
