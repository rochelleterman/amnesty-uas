"Further information on EXTRA 66/93 (EUR 70/15/93, 9 July) - Federal Republic of Yugoslavia (Kosovo province): ill-treatment / legal concern: Sami Kurteshi 
 Further information on EXTRA 66/93 (EUR 70/15/93, 9 July) - Federal Republic of Yugoslavia (Kosovo province): ill-treatment / legal concern: Sami Kurteshi | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Sami Kurteshi has been released from police custody following his arrest at the offices of the Council for the Defence of Human Rights and Freedoms in Pristina. He reportedly needed medical treatment for injuries inflicted on him by the police at the time of his arrest. "
