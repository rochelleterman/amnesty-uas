"Malaysia: Further information of fear of refoulement 
 Malaysia: Further information of fear of refoulement | Amnesty International 
 Concerns have intensified that the Malaysian authorities are about to return 27 Acehnese asylum-seekers to Indonesia, where they would be at risk of serious human rights violations. The Indonesian authorities suspect them of being sympathetic to the Acehnese separatist movement.
