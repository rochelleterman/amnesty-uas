 Mexico: Farmer disappeared, Michoacán State, Mexico | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 The Orozco Medina family, peasant farmers in Nuevo Zirosto, Municipio de Uruapan, Michoacán State, Mexico, have been subject to a pattern of harassment involving municipal police since 2007. At least three family members have been abducted, with the most recent one feared to be the victim of enforced disappearance in May."
