 India: Campaigners against mine abducted by gunmen | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Two leaders of the Dongria Kondh indigenous communities, campaigning against a proposed bauxite mine in the Niyamgiri Hills in the eastern Indian state of Orissa, were abducted by unidentified gunmen on 9 August 2010. The authorities have not yet begun an investigation."
