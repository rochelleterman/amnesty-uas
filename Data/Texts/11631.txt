"Indonesia: Fear of torture: Two men (names unknown) 
 Indonesia: Fear of torture: Two men (names unknown) | Amnesty International 
 AI is concerned for the safety of two men, thought to be members of the Free Papua Movement (OMP), arrested by the Indonesian Armed Forces during a military operation to free 11 hostages held for three months by the OMP in the Indonesian province of Irian Jaya. The names of the two were not given and there has been no information on their whereabouts since their arrest. AI fears that they may be subjected to torture during interrogation.
