 Belarus: Torture and other ill-treatment/health concern, Emanuel Zeltser (m) | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 US national Emanuel Zeltser was detained when he flew in to Belarus on 12 March 2008. He is held in a state security services detention facility in the capital, Minsk, where according to his lawyer he has been interrogated and beaten, and has been denied the medicine he needs urgently to treat his diabetes and arthritis."
