 Nigeria: Health concern | Amnesty International 
 Ben Charles Obi, George Mbah, journalists: There is concern for the above two prisoners of conscience, following reports that their health has seriously deteriorated since their imprisonment in May 1995. Both men are being held incommunicado in harsh conditions. Amnesty International is calling for them to receive immediate medical care and is also calling for their immediate and unconditional release
