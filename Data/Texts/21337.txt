 Spain: Further information: Migrant family face new eviction threat | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Moroccan migrants Abdelilah Ghailan, Fatima Zhora Alami Wahabi and their two children were to have been forcibly evicted from their house in Madrid on 24 June. The eviction was prevented by the presence of Amnesty International members, other social activists and journalists, but the family now face eviction as early as 22 August."
