 Sri Lanka: Further information: Remains of Sri Lankan defender exhumed | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Remains thought to be of Sri Lankan human rights defender Pattani Razeek were exhumed in Valaichchenai, Eastern Sri Lanka on 28 July. His family have tentatively identified his remains, pending a post mortem due to be carried out on 2 August 2011."
