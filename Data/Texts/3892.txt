 Brazil: Further information on Fear for safety / Fear of torture | Amnesty International 
 Following the riot in Franco da Rocha juvenile detention centre on 11 March, many of the detainees were reportedly beaten by prison guards and police. The monitores (guards) had threatened to take revenge on the detainees for the riot and the killing of their colleague.
