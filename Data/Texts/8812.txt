 USA: Death Penalty / Legal concern: Juan Raul Garza | Amnesty International 
 Juan Raul Garza is set to become the first prisoner put   to death in the USA under federal law since 1963. The US  Supreme Court denied his final appeal in November 1999,   and the government could now set a date for his execution at any time.
