 Nepal: Further information on Incommunicado detention/fear of torture, Sharadha  Shrestha
   


 | Amnesty International 
  
  Amnesty International has been informed that Sharadha    Shresthaa Central Committee member of the      Women's    Association (Revolutionary), was arrested, along with     Sudhip Sapkota, on 25 July She has transferred to Chitwan district jail in November 2002. She is being held under   preventive detention.  Sudhip Sapkota was later released."
