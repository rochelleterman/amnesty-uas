"EXTRA 107/92 - Turkey: fear of torture: Hulya Ozcan and three other girls whose names are not known to Amnesty International 
 EXTRA 107/92 - Turkey: fear of torture: Hulya Ozcan and three other girls whose names are not known to Amnesty International | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 AI concerned about the four girls referred to above. They were detained on 17 December 1992, after police raided their communal home in Adana. They are held in incommunicado detention, presumably at the Anti-Terror Branch of Adana Police Headquarters, suspected of links with an illegal organization. It is feared that they are being interrogated under torture. "
