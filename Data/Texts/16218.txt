"Uzbekistan: Further Information on death penalty, Farid Nasibullin 
 Uzbekistan: Further Information on death penalty, Farid Nasibullin | Amnesty International 
 Farid Nasibullin could be executed at any time: the Uzbek authorities are feared to have recently executed a man who had not been the subject of mass international appeals. Farid Nasibullin could meet the same fate without continued international pressure on the Uzbek President to commute his death sentence.
