 USA (Oklahoma): Death penalty, Kevin Young (m) | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Kevin Young is scheduled to be executed in Oklahoma on 22 July 2008. He was sentenced to death in Oklahoma County in 1998 for the murder of Joseph Sutton during an armed robbery in May 1996. He has been on death row for a decade. He has a clemency hearing in front of the state Pardon on Parole Board on 8 July."
