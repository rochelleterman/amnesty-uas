 Nepal: Fear for safety/fear of ""disappearance"" / detention without charge | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is seriously concerned for the safety of the four named people. They were reportedly rearrested by security forces personnel in plain clothes at the Morang District Court, eastern Nepal on 14 July and their current whereabouts are unknown.
