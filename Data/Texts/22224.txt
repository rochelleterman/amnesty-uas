 Iran: Further Information on Arbitrary arrest/fear of torture or ill-treatment: Mahboubeh Karami (f) | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Mahboubeh Karami has been held in Evin Prison in Tehran since 13 June 2008. Bail of 1,000 billion rials (approximately US$110,000) was set on 12 July, but her family have been unable to raise such a large amount."
