"Ecuador: Further information on Fear for safety/arbitrary detention/legal concern, Fernando Villavicencio 
 Ecuador: Further information on Fear for safety/arbitrary detention/legal concern, Fernando Villavicencio | Amnesty International 
 Fernando Villavicencio was released without charges in the late afternoon of 2 February. He had been arrested by the security forces on 31 January, apparently for his legitimate involvement in organizing demonstrations and protests against the government's economic policy.
