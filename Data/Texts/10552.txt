"Myanmar: Health concern / prisoners of conscience 
 Myanmar: Health concern / prisoners of conscience | Amnesty International 
 U Cho Aung Than, Dr Aung Khin Sint and U Win Tin: The individuals above-named, all prisoners of conscience in their 60s, have been transferred to hospital. Amnesty International is concerned that the three be given all the medical attention they need, and that they not be returned to prison under any circumstances.
