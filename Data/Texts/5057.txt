"UA 291/94 - Colombia: fear for safety: Julio Ramon Olivera Gracia, Marina Salas 
 UA 291/94 - Colombia: fear for safety: Julio Ramon Olivera Gracia, Marina Salas | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 There is concern for the lives of Julio Ramon Olivera Gracia and Marina Salas after Julio Ramon Olivera recently survived an attempt on his life and Marina Salas received a written threat. They are President and Secretary respectively of the Popular Civic Movement for the Defence of Sampues. Julio Ramon Olivera is also a candidate for the Departmental Assembly of Sucre for the Civic Convergence Movement, and has been involved for 35 years in peasant farmer land struggles."
