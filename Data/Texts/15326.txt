 Further information on UA 338/94 (EUR 44/98/94, 14 September; and follow-up EUR 44/134/94, 7 November) - Turkey: ""disappearance"" / fear of extrajudicial execution: Sakir Oner | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Amnesty International has learned that Sakir Oner, from Angule village, near Lice, is now in Diyarbakir prison. There had been concern for his safety when he ""disappeared"" after being detained by gendarmerie on 24 July."
