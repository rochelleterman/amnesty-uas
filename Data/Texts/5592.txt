"Cuba: Further information on arbitrary detention / harassment / possible prisoners of conscience 
 Cuba: Further information on arbitrary detention / harassment / possible prisoners of conscience
 | Amnesty International 
 Bernardo Fuentes Camblor, journalist, Magaly Pino Garcia, journalist, Jorge Enrique Rives, journalist (all three are members of Patria and the Cuban Concilium): Amnesty International has learned that the above, all members of the independent press agency, Patria, have been released. They had been held incommunicado since 12 August 1996.
