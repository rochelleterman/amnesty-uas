"USA: Death Penalty/Legal Concern, Lawrence Jacobs. 
 USA: Death Penalty/Legal Concern, Lawrence Jacobs. | Amnesty International 
 At a retrial scheduled to begin on 16 September in        Jefferson Parish, Louisiana, the prosecution is intending to seek a death sentence against Lawrence Jacobs for a    crime he is accused of committing when he was 16 years    old. International law, respected in almost every country in the world, prohibits the use of the death penalty      against people who were under 18 at the time of the       crime.
