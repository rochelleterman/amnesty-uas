"Further information on EXTRA 28/92 (EUR 11/03/92, 17 March) - Albania: death penalty: Hajdar Niman Gjuriqi 
 Further information on EXTRA 28/92 (EUR 11/03/92, 17 March) - Albania: death penalty: Hajdar Niman Gjuriqi | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Amnesty International has recently learned of the execution of Hajdar Gjuriqi, which was probably carried out a few days after 1 June 1992, when his death sentence for murder was confirmed by the President of the Republic. "
