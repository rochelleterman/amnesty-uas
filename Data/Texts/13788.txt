"UA 388/94 - Socialist Republic of Viet Nam: death penalty: Nguyen Tung Duong 
 UA 388/94 - Socialist Republic of Viet Nam: death penalty: Nguyen Tung Duong | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Nguyen Tung Duong, a policeman, was sentenced to death for murder on 21 October by the People's Court of Ha Noi after a three-day trial. He had been charged with the murder of a young unemployed man, Nguyen Viet Phuong, the motive apparently being robbery."
