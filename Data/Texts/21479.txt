 Russian Federation: Tajik man faces extradition, risks torture | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 The Tajikistani authorities have asked the Russian authorities to extradite Ismon Azimov from Russia for ""organization of a criminal group"", suspecting that he is a member of the banned Islamic Movement of Uzbekistan (IMU). Ismon Azimov would be at serious risk of torture or other ill-treatment if returned to Tajikistan."
