"Mexico: Further information on: fear for safety: Bishop Arturo Lona Reyes 
 Mexico: Further information on: fear for safety: Bishop Arturo Lona Reyes | Amnesty International 
 Following accusations made against the above in the local media by members of the ruling Institutional Revolutionary Party, the human rights activist and bishop has began to receive anonymous telephone calls. Amnesty International believes that he is being targeted because of his work on behalf of indigenous and peasant communities.
