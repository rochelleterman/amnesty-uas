"USA: Death penalty / legal concern: Joseph (Joe) Ernest Atkins 
 USA: Death penalty / legal concern: Joseph (Joe) Ernest Atkins | Amnesty International 
 Joseph Ernest Atkins is scheduled for execution in South Carolina on 22 January 1998. He was made eligible for the death penalty by having a previous conviction for murder, possibly due to an inadequate defence at the time.
