"UA 135/94 - Peru: death threats / fear for safety: Mariano Paliza Mendoza, and staff of the Asociacion Pro Derechos Humanos (APRODEH), Association for Human Rights 
 UA 135/94 - Peru: death threats / fear for safety: Mariano Paliza Mendoza, and staff of the Asociacion Pro Derechos Humanos (APRODEH), Association for Human Rights | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 There is concern for the safety of Mariano Paliza Mendoza, a journalists, who is reported to have recently received several death threats. He is the director and presenter of the APRODEH human rights radio programme ""Accion Urgente"" which is broadcast on Radio Cadena. There is also concern for the safety of the rest of the staff who work for APRODEH."
