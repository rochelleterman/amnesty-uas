 USA (Federal): Death penalty: Bruce Carneil Webster (m)

 | Amnesty International 
 Bruce Webster, black, aged 33, is due to be executed at the Federal Correctional Complex, Terre Haute, Indiana, on 16 April. He was sentenced to death in federal court in Texas in 1996 for the murder of a 16-year-old girl, Lisa Rene.
