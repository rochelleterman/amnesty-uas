 Bahrain: Further information: Health professionals’ sentences upheld | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 On 1 October 2012, Bahrain’s Court of Cassation upheld the sentences against nine health professionals who challenged a Court of Appeals ruling. Six of them are at risk of being sent to prison at any time to serve their sentences. If jailed, they would be considered prisoners of conscience."
