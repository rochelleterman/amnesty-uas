 Bahraini human rights activist arrested: Sayed Yousif Almuhafdah  | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Bahraini human rights activist Sayed Yousif Almuhafdah was arrested on 2 November 2012 for participating in an “illegal gathering” and “taking part in an unauthorised march”. Amnesty International considers him to be a prisoner of conscience. "
