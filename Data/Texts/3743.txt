"Bolivia: Further information on fear for safety 
 Bolivia: Further information on fear for safety | Amnesty International 
 Dr Waldo Albarracin, a human rights defender, and his family have been forced to abandon their home because of fears for their safety. The authorities do not appear to have taken any steps to provide protection for him and his family and there is no indication that the death threats against him have been investigated.
