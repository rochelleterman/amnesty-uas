 Russian Federation: Man ‘disappeared’ in North Caucasus   | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 A man from Ingushetia, the North Caucasus, Russia, has been forcibly disappeared. Amir Chaniev was last seen on 4 September 2011 when 30-40 unidentified officers of law-enforcement agencies searched his family’s flat and drove him away. His sister Karina Chanieva was unlawfully detained the next day. "
