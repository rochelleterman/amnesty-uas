"Peru: Further information on death threats / fear for safety: Tito Guido Gallegos Gallegos, human rights lawyer 
 Peru: Further information on death threats / fear for safety: Tito Guido Gallegos Gallegos, human rights lawyer | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Dr Tito Guido Gallegos Gallegos has received more death threats. Further to the letter he received on 23 June 1995, he has had several anonymous telephone calls in which he has been warned to stop his work on behalf of victims of human rights violations. AI is gravely concerned for his safety."
