"Further information: Somalia: Prisoners of conscience/Fear of torture/ Incommunicado detention 
 Further information: Somalia: Prisoners of conscience/Fear of torture/ Incommunicado detention | Amnesty International 
 Up to 100 detained demonstrators from the Gaboye minority group who had reportedly protested peacefully at the shooting of Khadar Osman Dhabar have been released. They were held incommunicado for a short period and then freed without charge.
