"EXTRA 104/92 - USA (State of Washington): death penalty: Westley Allan Dodd 
 EXTRA 104/92 - USA (State of Washington): death penalty: Westley Allan Dodd | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Westley Allan Dodd is scheduled to be executed in the State of Washington on 5 January 1993. If carried out, it will be the first execution in Washington State in 29 years. Dodd, white, was sentenced to death in 1990 for the murders of three children. "
