"Gambia: Fear for safety/Incommunicado detention/possible prisoner of conscience, Guy-Patrick Massoloka. 
 Gambia: Fear for safety/Incommunicado detention/possible  prisoner of conscience, Guy-Patrick Massoloka.

 | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is concerned for the safety of Guy-Patrick Massoloka, a journalist for the Pan African News Agency (PANA). He was arrested in Banjul, the capital, on 19 July by agents of the National Intelligence Agency (NIA), and has been detained incommunicado since then.
