"People's Republic of China: Further information on legal/health concern: Xin Hong 
 People's Republic of China: Further information on legal/health concern: Xin Hong | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Xin Hong, the 67 year old mother of an exiled Chinese police official, Gao Peiqi, who was arrested in September 1994 in Tianjin City, accused of ""leaking state secrets"", apparently in connection with her son's activities abroad, was released in May this year. According to sources, Xin Hong, was released in May 1995 after being detained for about eight months. Gao Peiqi, was not informed of his mother's release until September."
