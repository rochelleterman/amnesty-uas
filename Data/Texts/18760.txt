 United Arab Emirates (UAE): Further information on fear of torture or ill-treatment/ incommunicado detention/health concern: Humeid Salem al-Ghawas al-Za'abi (m) | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International has learned that Humeid Salem al-Ghawas al-Za'abi, aged 39, was released without charge by the authorities in Abu Dhabi on 3 January. He is in good health and has returned to his family.
