"USA (Maryland): Further information on Wesley Eugene Baker (m) 
 USA (Maryland): Further information on Wesley Eugene Baker (m) | Amnesty International 
 Wesley Baker was executed on 5 December 2005 for the murder of Jane Frances Tyson, who was white, in 1991. There had been doubts about whether Baker fired the shots which killed Tyson. In Maryland, only the actual murderer can be sentenced to death.
