"Indonesia: Fear for safety / possible ""disappearance"": M Yahya Daud 
 Indonesia: Fear for safety / possible ""disappearance"": M Yahya Daud | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International fears for the safety of M Yahya     Daud whose whereabouts are unknown since he was arrested  by a riot control police unit, the PPRM (Petugas Penindak Rusuh Massa), on 4 August 1999.Those detained by the PPRM are at risk of being tortured, ill-treated, ""disappeared"" or unlawfully killed.
