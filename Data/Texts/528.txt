"Chad: Fear for safety: Ngarlegy Yorongar le Moiban 
 Chad: Fear for safety: Ngarlegy Yorongar le Moiban | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is concerned for the safety of Ngarlegy Yorongar le Moiban, a prominent political opponent, since the Agence Nationale de Securite (ANS), National Security Agency started keeping a continual presence outside his home. It is feared that he may be at risk of ill-treatment by the ANS.
