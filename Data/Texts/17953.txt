"Jordan: Possible prisoner of conscience/Legal concern, Fawaz Zurayqat. 
 Jordan: Possible prisoner of conscience/Legal concern,    Fawaz Zurayqat. | Amnesty International 
 Fawaz Zurayqat, a businessman and station manager of Arab Television (ATV), a new English-language satellite channel with offices in London and Baghdad was detained on 3 March. He is being held without charge at the headquarters of the General Intelligence Department in the capital Amman. Amnesty International considers him to be a possible prisoner of conscience, detained solely for the peaceful expression of his political beliefs.
