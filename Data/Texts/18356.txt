"Saudi Arabia: Imminent Execution 
 Saudi Arabia: Imminent Execution | Amnesty International 
 Three named Sri Lankan men have reportedly had their death sentences referred to the King, which is the final appeal stage of Saudi Arabia's secretive judicial system. The King may grant clemency, but if he chooses to ratify their sentences, they could be executed at any time.
