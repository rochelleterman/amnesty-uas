"USA (Ohio): Death penalty, Adremy Dennis 
 USA (Ohio): Death penalty, Adremy Dennis
 | Amnesty International 
 Adremy Dennis is due to be executed in Ohio on 13 October 2004. He was sentenced to death for the murder of Kurt Kyle who was shot in the head in the early hours of 5 June 1994 during a street robbery. Adremy Dennis and Leroy Anderson were arrested and charged in the murder. Adremy Dennis was sentenced to death on 29 December 1994. Leroy Anderson was sentenced to 40 years' to life imprisonment as an accomplice.
