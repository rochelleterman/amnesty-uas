 Bahrain: Further information: Human rights activist released on bail: Nabeel Rajab | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Prominent human rights activist Nabeel Rajab was released on bail on 28 May 2012. However, he still has three cases against him relating to his activism and has to go back to court for hearings on 17 and 24 June. He is also banned from travelling abroad."
