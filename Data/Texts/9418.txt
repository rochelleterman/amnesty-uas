 USA (Ohio): Further information on Death penalty / Legal concern: James J. Filiaggi (m) 

 | Amnesty International 
 James Filiaggi, aged 41, was executed in Ohio on the morning of 24 April. He was sentenced to death in 1995 for the murder of his former wife, Lisa Huff Filiaggi, who was shot on 24 January 1994.
