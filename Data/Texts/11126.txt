 India: Police brutality / use of excessive force / Fear of ill-treatment | Amnesty International 
 Over 150 women and 50 men were reportedly arrested when police broke up their peaceful protest at the site of the Maheshwar Dam project in the Indian state of Madhya Pradesh on 20 May 1998. Some were reportedly beaten.
