 Colombia: Family and human rights NGO threatened | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 On 24 March 2011, the family of a girl who was allegedly raped by an army officer and subsequently killed along with her two brothers received a death threat. An NGO supporting them, Humanidad Vigente, was also threatened. On 22 March the judge working on the case was killed in eastern Colombia. "
