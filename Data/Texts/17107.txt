"Iran: Fear for safety/Incommunicado detention/Risk of torture and ill-treatment 
 Iran: Fear for safety/Incommunicado detention/Risk of torture and ill-treatment | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is concerned for the safety of Reza Ameri Nassab, Ali Moghtadari and Arash Hashemi, who were violently arrested on 9 July. They may be at risk of torture or ill-treatment and could be forced into making ""confessions"" under duress.
