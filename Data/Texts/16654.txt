"Egypt: Death penalty/Fear of Imminent Execution/Unfair trial 
 Egypt: Death penalty/Fear of Imminent Execution/Unfair trial | Amnesty International 
 Brothers Ezzat and Hamdi Ali Hanafi have been sentenced to death after an unfair trial, and could be executed at any time. The sentence has been sent to the supreme religious authority (Mufti) for approval. President Hosni Mubarak still has the power to commute the sentence.
