"Bolivia: fear of ill-treatment / legal concern: 45 peasant women and three children 
 Bolivia: fear of ill-treatment / legal concern: 45 peasant women and three children | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 AI fears that 45 peasant women and three young children, under five years old, detained on 27 December 1995 during a peaceful march by women from various coca-leaf growing communities in Bolivia, may be facing ill-treatment in custody.  It is alleged that those currently detained have not been given any food since arrest."
