 EXTRA 105/92 - Turkey: fear of torture / fear of ""disappearance"": Ekrem Kas | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Ekrem Kas was arrested on 10 December in Diyarbakir. He has not been seen since and his detention has not been acknowledged by the police. His relatives have made enquiries to the police and prosecutor's office in Diyarbakir but have been told that he is not registered as being in detention. There are grave fears for his safety. "
