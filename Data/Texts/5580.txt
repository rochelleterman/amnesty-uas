"Cuba : Further information on arrest of human rights defender 
 Cuba : Further information on arrest of human rights defender | Amnesty International 
 Nestor Rodriguez Lobaina, who was detained on 7 December 1998 was released on 15th December. He was arrested after protesting against the Cuban government's refusal to allow him to attend the Paris conference marking the 50th anniversary of the UDHR.
