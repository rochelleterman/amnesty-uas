 Bahrain: Further information: Prominent Bahraini activist imprisoned: Nabeel Rajab | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Prominent Bahraini human rights activist, Nabeel Rajab, was arrested on 9 July at his home to serve the three-month prison sentence imposed on him on that day by the Lower Criminal Court which convicted him of libel. He is a prisoner of conscience."
