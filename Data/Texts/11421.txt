"Indonesia: Fear of torture or ill-treatment/Death in custody 
 Indonesia: Fear of torture or ill-treatment/Death in custody | Amnesty International 
 One detainee, Philipus Murib, has died after reportedly being beaten by the police in Papua (formerly known as Irian Jaya). Thirteen others are currently being detained at the police headquarters (Police Resort, Polres) in Jayapura, and are at grave risk of being tortured or ill-treated.
