"Further information on UA 05/93 (ASA 20/03/93, 8 January) - India: Extrajudicial execution / legal concern: at least 53 people in Sopore, Kashmir 
 Further information on UA 05/93 (ASA 20/03/93, 8 January) - India: Extrajudicial execution / legal concern: at least 53 people in Sopore, Kashmir | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 The Jammu and Kashmir governor has instituted a judicial inquiry into the killings in Sopore. The inquiry will be held by a sitting judge. One inspector and two sub-inspectors of the Border Security Force (BSF) were suspended immediately after the incident; five other BSF personnel have also been suspended. "
