"Turkey: Torture: Cevat Soysal 
 Turkey: Torture: Cevat Soysal | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is gravely concerned at reports     that Cevat Soysal, a Turkish citizen who had been granted political asylum in Germany in 1995 was severely tortured while held at the National Intelligence Agency in Ankara  and later at Ankara Police Headquarters. He was           reportedly apprehended in Moldova on 13 July 1999 and     flown to Turkey later that day.
