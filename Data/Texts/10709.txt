 People's Republic of China: Further information on fear of torture and ill-treatment/medical concern | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International has learnt from reliable sources that Ablikim Abdiriyim has been tried in secret. He is    very ill as a result of beatings suffered in detention    and continues to be denied access to the medical          treatment he needs. Amnesty International is concerned    about his health and fears that his life may be at risk.
