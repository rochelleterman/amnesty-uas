 Malaysia: Fear of ill-treatment / possible prisoner of conscience:Tian Chua | Amnesty International 
 Tian Chua, a prominent human rights activist was arrested on 30 November 1998.  After reports that he was ill-treated during previous periods of detention, Amnesty International is concerned that he may be at risk of further ill-treatment in detention.
