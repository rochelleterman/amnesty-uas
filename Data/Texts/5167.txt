"Colombia: Further information on fear for safety: Pedro Julio Mahecha Avila 
 Colombia: Further information on fear for safety: Pedro Julio Mahecha Avila | Amnesty International 
 Since October 1996, Pedro Julio Mahecha Avila and his family have been subjected to intimidation and harassment by persons believed to be members of the Colombian security forces. AI is seriously concerned for their safety.
