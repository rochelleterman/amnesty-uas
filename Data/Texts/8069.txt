"Peru: Further information on threat of withdrawal from the jurisdiction of the Inter-American Court of Human Rights 
 Peru: Further information on threat of withdrawal from the jurisdiction of the Inter-American Court of Human Rights | Amnesty International 
 On 7 July 1999 Peru's Congress voted to withdraw from the jurisdiction of the Inter-American Court of Human Rights. The decision to withdraw is the latest in a series of     measures taken over past years by the two governments of  President Alberto Fujimori which have had the effect of   undermining the rule of law and the protection of human   rights.
