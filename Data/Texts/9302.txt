"USA (Oklahoma): Death penalty, Garry Thomas Allen 
 USA (Oklahoma): Death penalty, Garry Thomas Allen | Amnesty International 
 Garry Allen is scheduled to be executed in Oklahoma on 19 May 2005. He was sentenced to death for the 1986 murder of Gail Titsworth, with whom he had had two children. The Oklahoma Pardon and Parole Board has recommended that Governor Brad Henry commute Garry Allen's death sentence.
