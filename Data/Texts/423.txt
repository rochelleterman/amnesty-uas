"Cameroon: Further information on fear for safety: Abdoulaye Math, President of the Movement for the Defence of Human Rights and Liberties 
 Cameroon: Further information on fear for safety: Abdoulaye Math, President of the Movement for the Defence of Human Rights and Liberties
 | Amnesty International 
 Human rights defenders Abdoulaye Math and Semdi Soulaye   returned to Maroua on 13 June 1999. Since their return    they are not known to have been threatened or harassed by the security forces. Amnesty International continues to   monitor their situation closely.
