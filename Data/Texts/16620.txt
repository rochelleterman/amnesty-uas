 Egypt: Risk of torture/ill-treatment | Amnesty International 
 At least five men were detained earlier this month because of their actual or perceived homosexuality, in Buhaira province, southeast of Alexandria. Amnesty International fears that they are at risk of torture and ill-treatment.
