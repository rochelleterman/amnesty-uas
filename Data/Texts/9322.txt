"USA (Nebraska): Death penalty / Legal concern: Carey Dean Moore (m) 
 USA (Nebraska): Death penalty / Legal concern: Carey Dean Moore (m) | Amnesty International 
 Carey Moore is due to be executed in Nebraska's electric chair on 8 May. He was sentenced to death in 1980 for the murder of two men in Omaha in August 1979. Carey Moore was aged 21 at the time of the crimes. He is now 49. He has given up his appeals. Nebraska has not carried out an execution for 10 years.
