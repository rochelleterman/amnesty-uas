"Sri Lanka: Fear for safety: Sivam Ashokumar 
 Sri Lanka: Fear for safety: Sivam Ashokumar | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is gravely concerned for the safety of Sivam Ashokumar who may be at risk from members of the People's Liberation Organization of Tamil Eelam (PLOTE),  an armed Tamil group fighting alongside the Sri Lankan    security forces, attempting to force him to retract       information about his detention and torture at Kovilkulam PLOTE camp earlier this year. This information was        provided to Amnesty International via several reliable    sources in Vavuniya.
