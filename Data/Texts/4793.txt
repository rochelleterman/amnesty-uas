 Colombia: Further information on apparent extrajudicial executions / fear for safety and new concern: ""disappearance"" | Amnesty International 
 Guillermo Leon Barrera Henao, Francisco Javier Taborda Taborda, Alvaro Vasquez, Elsa Maria Rueda (f); new names: Susana Bravo (f) human rights activist, Anndres Taborda: AI is increasingly concerned for the safety of the civilian population of the municipality of El Carmen de Atrato, department of Choco, after a paramilitary group operating in the region delivered a written communique threatening 25 members of the town. There is particular concern for the safety of Andres Taborda, who was reportedly abducted by paramilitiaries on 27 June 1996 and whose whereabouts remain unknown.
