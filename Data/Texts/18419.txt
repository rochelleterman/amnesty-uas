"Saudi Arabia: Further information on Death Penalty/Fear of imminent execution 
 Saudi Arabia: Further information on Death Penalty/Fear of imminent execution | Amnesty International 
 Juvenile offender Dhahian Rakan al-Sibai'I was executed on 21 July in Taif, according to a Ministry of Interior statement. There is no new information about Mohamed Kohail, who has been sentenced to death, or his brother Sultan Kohail, who may also be at risk of execution.
