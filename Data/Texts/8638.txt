 USA (South Carolina):Further information death penalty / legal concern: Brett Blair Hollis | Amnesty International 
 The trial of Brett Hollis in Lexington, South Carolina,   has been rescheduled to begin on 21 February 2000. The    prosecution still intend to seek a death sentence against the defendant for crimes he is accused of committing when he was 17 years old.
