 Nepal: Further informationon fear of torture/ medical concern: Bablu Rai (m) 

 | Amnesty International 
 Bablu Rai, aged 34,  was reportedly transferred from the Banke District Police Station to the Banke District Prison on 3 June, and is believed to be no longer at risk of torture. However he has not yet received adequate medical treatment for injuries reportedly caused by previous torture.
