"Colombia: ""Disappearance""/Extrajudicial Executions 
 Colombia: ""Disappearance""/Extrajudicial Executions | Amnesty International 
 Paramilitary gunmen raided the hamlet of Puerto Lajas on 4 October and abducted nearly 50 people, including a number of small children. They have reportedly executed several of them, and the rest are in grave danger. Amnesty International is concerned for the safety of the people of Puerto Lajas, municipality of El Tarra.
