 Sri Lanka: Further information on fear for safety / fear of torture / ""disappearance"" | Amnesty International 
 It has been reported by the Sri Lankan embassy in Belgium that Subramaniam Tharmabalasingham has been released. The whereabouts of Kathirgamathamby Sentilkumar, Ponnampalam Thavarajah and Arumugam Mylvaganam who were also taken into custody in January and February 1998 remains unknown.
