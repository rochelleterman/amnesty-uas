 Kenya: Fear of arrest of prisoner of conscience: Amos Kiprotich Kandie: | Amnesty International 
 An arrest warrant has been issued against Amos Kiprotich Kandie in an apparent attempt to prevent him standing as a parliamentary candidate on 29 December 1997. The police are searching for Amos Kiprotich Kandie who is believed to be in hiding.
