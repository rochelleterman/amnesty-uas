"USA (Delaware): Further information on death penalty / legal concern: Justin L. Burrell 
 USA (Delaware): Further information on death penalty / legal concern: Justin L. Burrell | Amnesty International 
 On 31 August 1999 the jury at Justin Burrell's trial      decided that he should not be sentenced to death. Unless  the judge overturns the jury's recommendation of a life   sentence, Justin Burrell will be sentenced to life        imprisonment without parole.
