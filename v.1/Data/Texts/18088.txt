 Lebanon: Further information on ""disappearance"" / fear of torture / health concern | Amnesty International 
 Gabi 'Aql Karam; and new name: Magi 'Aql Karam (f): There is concern at reports that Magi 'Aql Karam, the sister of Gabi 'Aql Karam, was detained on 1 March 1997 and is being held at an unknown location. She was reportedly detained by members of the Syrian intelligence forces in Chtoura, Beqaa' Valley.
