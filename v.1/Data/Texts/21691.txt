 Bosnia-Herzegovina: medical action: further information on doctors and nurses arrested in Sarajevo (EUR 63/02/94, 4 February) | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 AI has now learned that the four doctors and two nurses (not three, as stated in EUR 63/02/94) were released in a prisoner exchange on 15 June 1994. They are Drs Dejan Kafka, Ranko Medan, Nemanja Velijkov and Marko Vukovic. The nurses have been named as Verica Vujicic and Jagoda Delic. Igor Sablak was released soon after his arrest."
