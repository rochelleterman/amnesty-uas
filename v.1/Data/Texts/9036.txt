"United States of America : Further information on Death penalty / Legal concern, Louis Jones. 
 United States of America : Further information on Death penalty / Legal concern, Louis Jones. | Amnesty International 
 Former soldier Louis Jones was executed by the US         Government on 18 March in the US Penitentiary in Terre    Haute, Indiana. He was sentenced to death in October 1995 for the murder of a white woman, US Army Private Tracie   Joy McBride, eight months earlier.
