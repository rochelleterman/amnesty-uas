 Mexico: Further Information on Fear for safety | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Gunmen fired at Melesio Melchor Ángeles’ house on the night of 27 July 2008, and also at a house that Jorge Aragón Martínez has just moved from. Both men are political activists and appear on community radio station Zaachila Radio, which is based in the Oaxaca town of Zaachila, where they have criticised Zaachila’s local government."
