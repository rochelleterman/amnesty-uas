"UA 250/94 - Turkey: fear of ""disappearance"": Ahmet Tekin 
 UA 250/94 - Turkey: fear of ""disappearance"": Ahmet Tekin | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Ahmet Tekin was detained by soldiers while travelling from Diyarbakir to the Yildiz (Bamusi) district of Darakol (Kiya) village on 7 May 1994. His relatives reportedly saw him in Lice state hospital 10 days later when he was in a very bad condition. His present whereabouts are unknown and there are fears he may have ""disappeared""."
