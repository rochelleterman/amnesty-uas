"Further information on UA 406/93 (MDE 11/05/93, 18 November) - Bahrain: fear of forcible exile: Dr 'Abd al-'Aziz Rashid al-Rashid 
 Further information on UA 406/93 (MDE 11/05/93, 18 November) - Bahrain: fear of forcible exile: Dr 'Abd al-'Aziz Rashid al-Rashid | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Dr 'Abd al-'Aziz Rashid al-Rashid was refused entry to Syria on 19 November. On his return to Bahrain, he was given a passport valid for one year and forcibly exiled to the United Arab Emirates on 20 November."
