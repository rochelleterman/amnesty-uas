"Turkey: further information on: fear for safety 
 Turkey: further information on: fear for safety | Amnesty International 
 Esref Yasa, Kurd, aged 33; new names: M.Siddik Yasa, Hikmet Yasa, Seymus Yasa, Faik Yasa, and another: on 24 March M.Siddik Yasa, Hikmet Yasa, Seymus Yasa and the driver of the car were detained at a security checkpoint.  On the same day Faik Yasa was detained at his home and later taken away by security forces.  All are relatives of Esref Yasa.  AI fears for their safety.
