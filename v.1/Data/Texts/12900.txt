"Pakistan: Death penalty / legal concern: Arshad Jamil 
 Pakistan: Death penalty / legal concern: Arshad Jamil | Amnesty International 
 Former army major Arshad Jamil is to be hanged in Hyderabad Central Jail on 28 October 1996. He was sentenced to death in October 1992 after being found guilty of killing nine villagers in Tando Bahawal, Sindh province, in the context of a land dispute.
