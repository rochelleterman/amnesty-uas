"UA 135/93 - Colombia: possible ""disappearance"": Eduardo Avila Fonseca 
 UA 135/93 - Colombia: possible ""disappearance"": Eduardo Avila Fonseca | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Eduardo Avila Fonseca, an activist with the opposition party M-19 Democratic Alliance, was last seen at 6pm on 20 April 1993, when he was reportedly seized by armed men in Bogota. His family have not been able to find out anything about his whereabouts and there is serious concern for his safety. Eduardo Avila had been a bodyguard of the leader of M-19 Democratic Alliance and presidential candidate Carlos Pizarro, who was assassinated in 1990. "
