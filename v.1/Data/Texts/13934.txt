 Andreas Ionathan Charalambous and Andreas Panayioti Georgiou have been sentenced to five and eight months' imprisonment respectively by Nicosia Military Court. They are now in Nicosia Central Prison. Theodoros Antoni Charalambous was fined; he is not facing imprisonment. The trials of the others have been postponed."
