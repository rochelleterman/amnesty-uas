 USA (Florida): Further information Death penalty / Legal concern, Amos Lee King. | Amnesty International 
 Amos King was executed in Florida on the evening of 26 February 2003 for the murder of Natalie Brady, white, in March 1977. He had been on death row for most of the past quarter of a century. He maintained his innocence to the end.
