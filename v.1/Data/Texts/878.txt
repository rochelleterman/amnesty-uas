"Ethiopia: Further information on health concern: Asrat Woldeyes 
 Ethiopia: Further information on health concern: Asrat Woldeyes | Amnesty International 
 Dr Asrat Woldeyes is still being held in custody in a hospital in Addis Ababa where he is receiving medical treatment. He has been allowed  visits from some foreign visitors and diplomats, and also from his family and lawyer. His treatment is better now and his health is gradually improving.
