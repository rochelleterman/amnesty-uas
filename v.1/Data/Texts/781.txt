"Ethiopia: Further information on death penalty 
 Ethiopia: Further information on death penalty | Amnesty International 
 Abdulkarim al-Naji Abdelradi, al-Arab Sadiq Hafiz, Safwat Hassan Abdelghani: On 26 March 1997 the Supreme Court rejected the appeals of the above named Egyptian nationals. They had been sentenced to death on 20 September 1996 after being convicted of involvement in an attempt to assassinate Egyptian President Hosni Mubarak in Addis Abbaba in 1995.
