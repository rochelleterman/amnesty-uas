"Sri Lanka: Further information on torture: Thambirajah Kamalathasan 
 Sri Lanka: Further information on torture: Thambirajah Kamalathasan | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International has learned that Thambirajah Kamalathasan, who was tortured for several days following his arrest on 15 July 1998 was remanded into judicial custody at Colombo Remand Prison on 6 August. Relatives have been able to visit him there.
