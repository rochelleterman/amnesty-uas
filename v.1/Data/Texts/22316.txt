 Iran: Further Information on Incommunicado detention/Fear of torture or ill-treatment: Jamila Nabgan  | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Jamila Nabgan, sister of a prominent member of an outlawed Arab political party, Habib Nabgan, was released on bail on 24 October, four days after she was arrested. She now has to report every morning to the Ministry of Intelligence detention facility in Ahvaz, Khuzestan province, where she is then detained from 8am until 10pm"
