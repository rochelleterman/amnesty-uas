"Rwanda: Further information on ""disappearance"" / fear for safety: Sylvere Kanani 
 Rwanda: Further information on ""disappearance"" / fear for safety: Sylvere Kanani | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International has learned that Sylvere Kanani was released on bail on 27 November 1996. The reasons for his arrest and pending charges remain unclear. He was detained incommunicado by soldiers of the Rwandese Patriotic Army (RPA) for 22 days, during which time he was beaten and suffered death threats. There continues to be concern for his safety.
