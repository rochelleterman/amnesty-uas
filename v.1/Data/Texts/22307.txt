 Iran: Further Information on Imminent execution: Raheleh Zamani | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 The execution of Raheleh Zamani has reportedly been postponed until around 2 January 2008. She had been due to be hanged in Evin Prison, in the capital, Tehran, on 19 December 2007 for the killing of her husband. She is a mother of two children aged five and three."
