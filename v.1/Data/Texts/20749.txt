 Venezuelan activist received death threats: Humberto Prado Sifontes | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 The safety of Venezuelan human rights defender Humberto Prado Sifontes, director of the Venezuelan Observatory of Prisons (Observatorio Venezolano de Prisiones) and his family are seriously at risk following a campaign of intimidation and death threats against him that began in the wake of violent disturbances at El Rodeo Prison in the state of Miranda on 12 June 2011."
