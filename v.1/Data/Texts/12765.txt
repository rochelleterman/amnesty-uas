"Nepal: Further information on Fear for safety/ possible ""disappearance"" 
 Nepal: Further information on Fear for safety/ possible ""disappearance"" | Amnesty International 
 Bishnu Thapa was reportedly released on 13 April, from Bhaireni barracks, Dhading district. According to his relatives he was first held at Bhaireni barracks, then transferred to Gajuri army camp and finally released from Bhaireni. Om Prakash Timalsena's whereabouts remain unknown.
