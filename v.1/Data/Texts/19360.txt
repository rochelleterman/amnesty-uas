 Yemen: Further information on incommunicado detention / fear of torture or ill-treatment: Omar Ibrahim Dagah | Amnesty International 
 Omar Ibrahim Dagah has now been in incommunicado          detention for nearly two months. He has already been      seriously ill-treated, and Amnesty International is       increasingly concerned that he is at risk of torture and  further ill-treatment. His family are still being denied  access to him.
