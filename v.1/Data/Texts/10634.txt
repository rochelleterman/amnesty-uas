 People's Republic of China: death sentences / fear of execution: Lin Huixiong, Lin Youkuan, Lin Yi, Qiu Guibiao | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Four people in Shenzehen, Guangdong province, have been sentenced to death for the crime of ""speculation and profiteering"". This is reported to be the first death sentence passed for such a crime in Shenzen. It is not known whether they have appealed against their death sentences."
