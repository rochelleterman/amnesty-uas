"UA 432/94 - Colombia: possible extrajudicial execution / fear for safety: Ciro Reyes 
 UA 432/94 - Colombia: possible extrajudicial execution / fear for safety: Ciro Reyes | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 According to witnesses, Ciro Reyes was shot dead at his home in Ocana's Santa Clara district on 27 November 1994 by two men on a motorcycle. Ciro Reyes was Secretary General of the management committee of a community housing project in Ocana. His death has heightened fears for the safety of all popular leaders in the area. A ""black list"" of 60 local community and popular leaders is known to have appeared in Ocana. It is not known at present if Ciro Reyes' name was one of those on the list."
