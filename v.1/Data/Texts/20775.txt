 Bangladesh: Fear for safety: Dr M A Hasan | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Civil society organization leader Dr M A Hasan has received death threats after his organisation, the War Crimes Fact Finding Committee, published on 3 April 2008 a list of people they alleged had committed war crimes during the war of independence that led to the creation of Bangladesh in 1971. He and his family are believed to be in serious danger of attack."
