"Colombia: Further information on extrajudicial executions / fear for safety 
 Colombia: Further information on extrajudicial executions / fear for safety | Amnesty International 
 200 displaced inhabitants of la Balsita village: The members of the community of La Balsita were forced to flee their village after paramilitary groups killed several community members in November 1997. 200 of them have been threatened by paramilitary gunmen after seeking refuge in the municipal capital, Dabeiba, department of Antioquia.
