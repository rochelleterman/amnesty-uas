 Saudi Arabia: Fear of torture/Incommunicado detention | Amnesty International 
 George Thomas, an Indian national working in Saudi        Arabia, was reportedly arrested, as he returned from a    Catholic worship service.   Amnesty International is      concerned that he may have been  arrested solely for his  religious beliefs, and if so  would consider him a        prisoner of conscience.
