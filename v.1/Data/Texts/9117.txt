"USA (Ohio): Further information on: Death penalty / Legal concern: William Henry Smith 
 USA (Ohio): Further information on: Death penalty / Legal concern: William Henry Smith | Amnesty International 
 William Smith was executed in Ohio on 8 March 2005. He was sentenced to death in April 1988 for the rape, robbery and murder of a 47-year-old woman, Mary Bradford, in her home in Cincinnati in September 1987.
