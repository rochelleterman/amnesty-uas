 Further information on EXTRA 28/94 (AMR 51/36/94, 11 May) - USA (Florida): death penalty: Marvin Johnson | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Marvin Johnson, who was scheduled to be executed in Florida on 19 May 1994, has been granted an indefinite stay of execution. The reason for the stay is not known at present."
