"Nigeria: Further information on fear of ill-treatment / possible prisoner of conscience: Godwin Agbroko 
 Nigeria: Further information on fear of ill-treatment / possible prisoner of conscience: Godwin Agbroko | Amnesty International 
 Godwin Agbroko, aged about 40, editor of The Week news magazine and former prisoner of conscience, was released on 6 May 1997. He had been detained without charge or trial since 18 December 1996 and was reportedly chained throughout.
