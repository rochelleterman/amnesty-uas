 Bangladesh: Further information: Bangladeshi man released | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 A 20 year-old man, Manik, was released from detention on 29 March 2011. He had been held without charge in Bangladesh since 8 March. The Urgent Action issued on 15 March may have helped put pressure on the Bangladeshi authorities to release Manik."
