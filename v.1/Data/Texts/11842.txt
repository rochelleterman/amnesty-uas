 Indonesia/East Timor: Possible ""disappearance"" / fear of torture: Lucas da Costa | Amnesty International 
 There are serious concerns for the safety of Lucas da Costa, who has not been seen since leaving his home to go to work on 23 December 1997. There are fears he may have been abducted by the Indonesian security forces.
