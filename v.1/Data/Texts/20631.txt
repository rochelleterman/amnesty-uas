 USA: Execution scheduled in Washington State: Cal Brown | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Cal Brown, a 52-year-old man, is scheduled to be executed in Washington State, USA, on 10 September after over 16 years on death row. This would be the state’s first execution for nine years, its first “non-consensual” execution since 1994, and only its fifth execution since 1963. "
