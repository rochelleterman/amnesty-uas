 Indonesia  Fear for safety/Fear of torture or             ill-treatment | Amnesty International 
 At least 15 people are in custody at Manokwari police station (Police Resort, Polres) in Papua (formerly known as Irian Jaya). Amnesty International believes they are at serious risk of torture or ill-treatment.
