 Ukraine/Uzbekistan: Further information on fear of forcible deportation / fear for safety | Amnesty International 
 Muhammad Bekzhon and Yusif Ruzimuradov have been          sentenced to 15 years' imprisonment, Kobil Diyarov to 12  years and Negmat Sharipov to eight years. The verdict was finally announced on 18 August. All six defendants        reiterated earlier allegations that they were tortured    and ill-treated in pre-trial detention.
