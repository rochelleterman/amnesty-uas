 Turkmenistan: Fear for safety / death in custody / health concern | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is seriously concerned for the      safety of possible prisoner of conscience Mukhametkuli    Aymuradov, following the sudden death in suspicious       circumstances of his co-defendant and fellow prisoner     Khoshali Garayev. Both were serving long sentences in the maximum security prison of Turkmenbashi.
