"Indonesia: Further information on possible ""disappearance"" 
 Indonesia: Further information on possible ""disappearance"" | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International has learnt that Eurico Pereira,     Zelia Fernandes and Rogerio Soares are safe. They were    reported missing on 11 August 1999 after a pro-Indonesian militia group opened fire on the office of the Dewan      Solidaritas Mahasiswa Timor Timur (DSMTT), Student        Solidarity Council of East Timor.
