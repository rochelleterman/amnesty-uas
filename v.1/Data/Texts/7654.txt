"Mexico: Fear for safety: Maria Estela Garcia Ramirez and her family 
 Mexico: Fear for safety: Maria Estela Garcia Ramirez and her family | Amnesty International 
 Maria Estela Garcia Ramirez has been forced to flee her home in Oaxaca State in the face of recent threatening incidents linked to the murder of her husband, allegedly by members of the police. She fears that her family or her husband's family may become the target of reprisals in her absence.
