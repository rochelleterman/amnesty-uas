 Iran: Further Information on Arbitrary arrests/Fear of Torture or ill-treatment/Possible prisoners of conscience | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Behrouz Karimizadeh and Peyman Piran have been released on bail, but Ali Kantouri is still detained, and is not receiving the medication he needs. All three student activists were detained for their alleged role in demonstrations around the time of Iran's National University Students' Day, on 7 December 2007."
