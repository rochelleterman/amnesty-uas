"Syria : Further information on fear of torture/incommunicado detention: 'Abd al-Rahman al-Musa 
 Syria : Further information on fear of torture/incommunicado detention: 'Abd al-Rahman al-Musa | Amnesty International 
 'Abd al-Rahman al-Musa was convicted by the Supreme State Security Court (SSSC) on or around 27 June 2006, under Law 49 of 1980, which states that membership of or affiliation to the Muslim Brotherhood (MB) is punishable by execution. He was sentenced to death, immediately commuted to 12 years' imprisonment.
