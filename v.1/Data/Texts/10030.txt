"USA (Kentucky): Further information on: Death penalty/Legal concern, Thomas Clyde Bowling 
 USA (Kentucky): Further information on: Death penalty/Legal concern, Thomas Clyde Bowling | Amnesty International 
 On 23 November, the Kentucky Supreme Court issued a stay of execution for Thomas Bowling, who had been due to be put to death on 30 November. The state did not appeal against the stay, and the execution warrant against Thomas Bowling expired at midnight on 30 November.
