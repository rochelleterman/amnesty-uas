 Nepal: Torture / fear for safety | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is concerned for the safety of Hari Bahadur Lama and Dinesh Thapa who were arrested on 4      August 1999. Suk Bahadar Lama, who was arrested with them has reportedly died in custody, possibly as a result of   torture.
