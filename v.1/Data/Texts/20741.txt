 Venezuela: Demand protection for man and family: Néstor Caudi Barrios  | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Two men tried to kill Néstor Caudi Barrios on 2 January. He and his family have been subjected to a campaign of intimidation by the police in northern Venezuela since 2003, during which five male members of the family have been killed by the police. "
