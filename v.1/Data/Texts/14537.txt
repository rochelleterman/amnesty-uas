"Turkey: Further information on torture / death after torture: Abdulkerim Bagdur, Yalcin Kilic 
 Turkey: Further information on torture / death after torture: Abdulkerim Bagdur, Yalcin Kilic | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Abdulkerim Bagdur was released from police custody approximately one week after being detained. He was reportedly tortured in detention but no further details are available at present. No official investigation into the death of Yalcin Kilic is known to have taken place."
