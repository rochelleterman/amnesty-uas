"USA (Oklahoma): Further information on death Penalty / prison conditions 
 USA (Oklahoma): Further information on death Penalty / prison conditions | Amnesty International 
 Tuan Anh Nguyen, former refugee from Vietnam; John Wayne Duvall and John Walter Castro, Native American: Tuan Anh Nguyen was executed by lethal injection on 10 December. John Duvall and John Castro remain scheduled for execution on 17 December and 7 January respectively.
