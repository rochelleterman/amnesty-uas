"Further information on UA 379/92 (ASA 16/15/92, 1 December) - Myanmar (Burma): prisoner of conscience / health concern: Daw Aung San Suu Kyi 
 Further information on UA 379/92 (ASA 16/15/92, 1 December) - Myanmar (Burma): prisoner of conscience / health concern: Daw Aung San Suu Kyi | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 The husband and son of Daw Aunt San Suu Kyi were allowed to visit her for two weeks in April 1993. According to a statement made by her husband, her health is no longer threatened. Amnesty International is continuing to call for her immediate and unconditional release. "
