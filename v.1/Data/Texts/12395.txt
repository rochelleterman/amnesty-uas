"Maldives: Further information Fear for Safety/ Fear of Torture or ill-treatment 
 Maldives: Further information  Fear for Safety/ Fear of Torture   or ill-treatment | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International has learned that Mohamed Zaki, Ibrahim Luthfee and Ahmad Didi, also known as Ahammadhee, were taken to Jinnaee court in Male' on 7 July and sentenced to life imprisonment. Naushad Waheed, who was believed to have been released, is reportedly still under house arrest in Male', waiting for his trial to begin. The charges against him are not known.
