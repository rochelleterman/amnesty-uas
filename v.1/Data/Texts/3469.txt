"Democratic Republic of Congo: Incommunicado detention / fear of torture 
 Democratic Republic of Congo: Incommunicado detention / fear of torture	 | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is extremely concerned that Kala    Bongamba and Feu d'Or Bonsange, who work on the           newspaper, l'Alarme, are being subjected to torture.They  were arrested on 27 September 1999. The director of       l'Alarme, Clovis Kadda, was arrested on 22 September 1999 and was severely tortured.He has since been released but  is in hiding, fearing rearrest.
