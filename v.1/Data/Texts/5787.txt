"Further information on UA 385/93 (AMR 29/18/93, 1 November and follow-up 29/20/93, 1 December) - El Salvador: apparent political killings / fear for safety: Eleno Hernan Castro 
 Further information on UA 385/93 (AMR 29/18/93, 1 November and follow-up 29/20/93, 1 December) - El Salvador: apparent political killings / fear for safety: Eleno Hernan Castro | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Investigations by the National Police have concluded that the murder of Eleno Hernan Castro, a former military leader of the FMLN, was not politically motivated. This finding has reportedly been accepted by ONUSAL and the FMLN."
