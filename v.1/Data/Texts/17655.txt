"Israel / Occupied Territories: Fear of torture: Ayman Mahmud Hassan Kafisha 
 Israel / Occupied Territories: Fear of torture: Ayman Mahmud Hassan Kafisha | Amnesty International 
 Ayman Kafisha (ID no: 9-2505838-9) was arrested on 5 April 1997 on suspicion of involvement in a Hamas bombing in Tel Aviv in March. He is being held incommunicado and there are fears that he is being subjected to torture during interrogation.
