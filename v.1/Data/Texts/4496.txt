"Colombia: fear for safety: Diego Alberto Ortiz Prieto 
 Colombia: fear for safety: Diego Alberto Ortiz Prieto | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Diego Alberto Ortiz Prieto has not been seen since 27 April 1995 when he was abducted by armed men believed to be members of a paramilitary organization. Reportedly he had previously been threatened due to his participation with the Cauca Teachers Association, ASOINCA."
