 Mexico: Fears that detainee has been disappeared: José Fortino Martínez Martínez | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 José Fortino Martínez Martínez was detained on 5 June 2011 in Nuevo Laredo, Tamaulipas state, Mexico, by members of the Mexican Navy. Neither his family nor a local human rights organization know where he is and are concerned that he is a victim of enforced disappearance. "
