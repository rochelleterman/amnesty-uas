 China: Human rights activist under house arrest: Chen Guangcheng  | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Human rights activist Chen Guangcheng and his family are currently under house arrest imposed by village leaders in Dongshigu village, Shandong province, China. Chen Guangcheng is in urgent need of medical attention. He and his family are running out of food but the authorities have refused to let them leave their home."
