"UA 426/93 - Israel and the Occupied Territories: deliberate and arbitrary killings / fear of further killings: Shalva Uzana, Yitzhak Weinstock 
 UA 426/93 - Israel and the Occupied Territories: deliberate and arbitrary killings / fear of further killings: Shalva Uzana, Yitzhak Weinstock | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Shalva Uzana and Yitzhak Weinstock, two Israeli civilians, were shot and killed on 1 December 1993 near al-Bireh in the West Bank. The Islamic Resistance Movement (HAMAS) is reported to have claimed responsibility for this most recent attack, and to have threatened further similar attacks."
