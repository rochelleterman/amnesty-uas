 Nepal: Fear for safety/ possible ""disappearance"" | Amnesty International 
 Three named men were reportedly arrested in Kathmandu district between 11 January and 1 March. Their whereabouts are unknown, and Amnesty International is concerned that they may have ""disappeared"".
