"Russian Federation (Chechnia): Fear for Safety: Civilians in Grozny 
 Russian Federation (Chechnia): Fear for Safety: Civilians in Grozny | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is concerned for the safety of      civilians remaining in Grozny, capital of the Chechen     Republic. On 6 December the Russian Federal authorities   gave them an ultimatum to leave the capital by 11         December or risk their lives.
