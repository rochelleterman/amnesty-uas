 China: Chinese artist beaten by police  | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 The trial of Chinese artist Wu Yuren, who was beaten by police officers while in detention, is expected to take place in late October 2010. He has been held since 31 May, and the authorities have refused him medical attention. His family and lawyer were told he is facing charges of “obstructing public service”. "
