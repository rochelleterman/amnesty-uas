"India: Fear for safety 
 India: Fear for safety | Amnesty International 
 Five Nepalis were taken from their home in New Delhi by men believed to be members of the Indian security forces. They have not been seen since, and lawyers and friends have been unable to find them, despite visiting the local police, police headquarters and the offices of the Investigation Bureau. Amnesty International fears that they may be tortured or possibly even killed.
