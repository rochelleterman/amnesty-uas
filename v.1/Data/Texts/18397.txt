 Saudi Arabia: Fear of flogging 

 | Amnesty International 
 According to press reports, a court in the northern region of Hail has sentenced 16 secondary and middle school pupils, believed to be aged between 12 and 18, to a total of 5,800 lashes, in addition to prison terms. The 16 pupils, all of whom may be children (under the age of 18), are at risk of imminent flogging.
