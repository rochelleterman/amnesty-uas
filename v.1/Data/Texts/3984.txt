"Brazil: Further information on fear of unfair trial / potential prisoner of conscience: Jose Rainha Junior 
 Brazil: Further information on fear of unfair trial / potential prisoner of conscience: Jose Rainha Junior | Amnesty International 
 Jose Rainha Junior, peasant leader: On 23 September 1997 state Supreme Court judges in Esperito Santo voted to transfer the second trial of Jose Rainha Junior to the state capital, Vitoria. No date has yet been set for the second trial.
