"Iran: Further information on death penalty: Sheyda Khoramzadeh Esfahani (f) 
 Iran: Further information on death penalty: Sheyda Khoramzadeh Esfahani (f) | Amnesty International 
 Sheyda Khoramzadeh Esfahani was executed in August 1997. She was found guilty of ""organising 'corrupt gatherings' "". Her husband was executed in late December 1996 or early January 1997.
