"Nepal: Disappearance / fear for safety 
 Nepal: Disappearance / fear for safety | Amnesty International 
 Suresh Ale Magar and Pawan Shrestha, both members of the  All Nepal Nationalities Organization were released from   jail on 23 December 1999, but reportedly rearrested       immediately. The police deny having them in custody, and  their whereabouts are unknown.      Amnesty International is concerned for their safety.
