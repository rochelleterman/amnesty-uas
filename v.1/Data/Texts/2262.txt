"Further information on EXTRA 35/93 (AFR 53/12/93, 5 April and follow-up AFR 53/17/93, 14 May) - South Africa: death penalty: fear of resumption of executions 
 Further information on EXTRA 35/93 (AFR 53/12/93, 5 April and follow-up AFR 53/17/93, 14 May) - South Africa: death penalty: fear of resumption of executions | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 A government spokesperson announced on 11 June 1993 that Parliament will debate the issue of the death penalty on Thursday 17 June. Parliament will vote on whether to end the moratorium on executions."
