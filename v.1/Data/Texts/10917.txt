 China: Fear of torture or ill-treatment/ prisoner of conscience: Zheng Enchong (m)
 | Amnesty International 
 Prisoner of conscience Zheng Enchong, aged 55, lawyer and human rights defender, has allegedly been beaten at Tilanqiao Prison in Shanghai, where he is currently serving a three-year sentence. Amnesty International fears he is at risk of further torture and ill-treatment.
