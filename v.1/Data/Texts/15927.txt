"Ukraine: Further information on death penalty: Sergey Tekuchev 
 Ukraine: Further information on death penalty: Sergey Tekuchev | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International has learned with deep regret that 27-year-old Sergey Tekuchev was executed on 17 October 1996. Amnesty International is appalled at this latest example of Ukraine violating the commitment to impose an immediate moratorium on executions after joining the Council of Europe.
