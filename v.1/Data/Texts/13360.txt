"Singapore: imminent execution: Jahabar Bagurudeen, Indian national, shop assistant; Ismail Bin Sumali 
 Singapore: imminent execution: Jahabar Bagurudeen, Indian national, shop assistant; Ismail Bin Sumali | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Jahabar Bagurudeen, an Indian national, is scheduled to hang on 2 June 1995. Ismail Bin Sumali is believed to face execution on the same day. Jahabar Bagurudeen was convicted of murder, and has lost his appeal and had his petition for clemency rejected. Ismail Bin Sumali was convicted of drug trafficking."
