"UA 12/94 - Uzbekistan: fear of imminent execution: Shukhrat Yuldashev 
 UA 12/94 - Uzbekistan: fear of imminent execution: Shukhrat Yuldashev | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Shukhrat Yuldashev, aged 23, was sentenced to death for murder by the City Court of Tashkent in November 1991. An appeal to the Supreme Court was turned down in June 1992. There are fears that a decision on his petition for clemency may be taken soon and that he faces imminent execution."
