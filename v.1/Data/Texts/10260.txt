"Antigua and Barbuda: Further information on the death penalty 
 Antigua and Barbuda: Further information on the death penalty | Amnesty International 
 On 26 January 2000 Mellanson HARRIS, Marvin JOSEPH,       Michael Lorriston CORNWALL, Confessor Valldez FRANCO and  Michael MASON received last-minute stays of execution     from a court in Antigua and Barbuda. This is to allow     them to appeal to the Judicial Committee of the Privy     Council in the United Kingdom.
