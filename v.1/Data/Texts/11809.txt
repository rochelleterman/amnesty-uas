"Indonesia: Possible ""disappearance"" 
 Indonesia: Possible ""disappearance"" | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is concerned for the safety of M    Nurdin Husein and Ir. Muslim who have been missing,       feared ""disappeared"", since they were arrested on around  20 May 1999. Both men had been involved in organizing a   community demonstration. A third man also involved in     organizing the demonstration, Iskandar, alias Raja Kanda, was shot dead in Pulo Tinggi Village, Teunom, on 3 June.
