"Guatemala: Death threats/fear for safety 
 Guatemala: Death threats/fear for safety | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is concerned for the safety of Bishop Alvaro Ramazinni and Father Juan Jos_ Aldaz Donamar’a. According to church sources on 3 March an unidentified individual phoned into a church radio station in San Marcos Department, and threatened the Church. Church figures have interpreted this as a direct threat against Bishop Alvaro Ramazinni and other San Marcos clergy, such as Father Aldaz.
