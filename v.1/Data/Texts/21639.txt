 Kyrgyzstan: Further information: Prisoner of conscience denied medical care: Ulugbek Abdusalamov  | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 The authorities are not providing Ulugbek Abdusalamov with adequate medical assistance. In 2009 he had a cerebral haemorrhage; he suffers from high blood pressure, stomach ailments and a heart condition, and requires constant medical care. He should be released, or at least be hospitalised."
