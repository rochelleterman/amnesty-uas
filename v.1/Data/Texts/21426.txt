 Russian Federation: Activist pronounced ""sane"", trial continues  | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Russian civil society activist Yulia Privedennaia, who was detained by order of a court in a Moscow psychiatric hospital for three weeks, has now been ruled to be ""sane"". Her trial before the Moscow Regional Court will therefore continue, but Amnesty International fears that her trial may not be fair."
