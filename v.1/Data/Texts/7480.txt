 Mexico: Fear for safety | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is concerned for the safety of the family of Griselda Tirado Evangelio, a lawyer and indigenous activist who was murdered on 6 August. On the night of 11 August, a group of unidentified people reportedly tried to enter the family's house in the municipality of Huehuetla, Puebla State.
