"Trinidad and Tobago: Further information on death penalty 
 Trinidad and Tobago: Further information on death penalty | Amnesty International 
 Dole Chadee, also known as Nankissoon Boodram, Joey       Ramiah, Joel Ramsingh, Ramkalawan Singh, Russell          Sankeralli, Bhagwandeen Singh, Clive Thomas, Robin Gopaul and Stephen Eversley have been granted stays of execution until 26 May 1999 by the Judicial Committee of the Privy  Council in London. They are scheduled to be hanged on 18, 19 and 20 May 1999.
