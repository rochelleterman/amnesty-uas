 Mexico: Migrants in danger in Mexico - further information | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Migrants staying at a shelter in the northern Mexican city of Saltillo, Coahuila state, are at imminent risk of being abducted by people-traffickers. In addition, migrants and staff working at the shelter are at risk of harassment and attack. The authorities have done little to protect them.  "
