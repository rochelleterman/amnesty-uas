 Syria: Syrian writer brought to trial: Raghdah Sa’id Hassan: Further information | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Raghdah Sa’id Hassan, a 39-year-old female Syrian writer arrested in February 2010, is now being brought to trial. Amnesty International has now seen the charges against her, which show that she is being targeted for researching human rights, corruption and democracy in Syria.  "
