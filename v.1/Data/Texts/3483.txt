 Democratic Republic of Congo: Arbitrary arrest / Fear of  torture, N'sii Luanda Shandwe | Amnesty International 
 Human rights defender N'sii Luanda Shandwe was finally freed without charge on 6 September 2001 after spending three months as a prisoner of conscience at Kinshasa's main prison. He is reported to be in good health.
