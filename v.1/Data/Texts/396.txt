 Cameroon: Further information on torture and ill-treatment / health concern | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International remains seriously concerned about the health and safety of Ebenezer Akwanga, Fon Peter Fonyam, (f), Bika Iserisu, Grace Yaya Kwei (f), Wilson Che Neba, Philip Tete, Patrick Yimbu, Ndifet Zacharia Khan, Fidelis Nyankwe, Ndum Anoh Robertson, Salifu Tanko  and Ndifon Joseph Tangu.They are among 51 prisoners held  without trial in connection with attacks by armed groups  in March 1997.
