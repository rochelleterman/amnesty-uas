"Burundi: Further information on legal concern / death penalty: Jean Minani 
 Burundi: Further information on legal concern / death penalty: Jean Minani | Amnesty International 
 On 28 October 1998 Jean Minani was acquitted of all charges relating to the murder of Lieutenant Colonel Lucien Sakubu. Amnesty International had expressed concern that he might be convicted solely on the basis of a statement extracted under torture.
