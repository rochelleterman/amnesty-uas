"Colombia: Fear for safety / possible extrajudicial executions: Residents of the Municipality of Granada, Antioquia 
 Colombia: Fear for safety / possible extrajudicial executions: Residents of the Municipality of Granada, Antioquia | Amnesty International 
 Fabian Suarez Garcia, carpenter, Gerardo Alzate, aged 65, peasant farmer; a 20-year-old man, name unknown: Recent paramilitary activities in the Municipality of Granada - including the possible extrajudicial executions of two men - have raised concern for the safety of local residents. Fabian Suarez Garcia was killed at his home by a group of armed civilians who then went to a petrol station where they killed Gerardo Alzate and seriously injured a 20-year-old man.
