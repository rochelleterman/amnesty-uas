 Guatemala: Fear for safety | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Members of Grupo de Apoyo Mutuo (GAM), Mutual Support     Group, a prominent Guatemalan human rights organization,  have been threatened and intimidated, and a vehicle       belonging to the organization has been stolen by heavily  armed men. 
On 9 December unidentified men driving a Toyota car with  no registration number kept GAM member Mario Polanco's    home under vigilance all day and part of the night.       Polanco's family called the police, but did not get any   assistance."
