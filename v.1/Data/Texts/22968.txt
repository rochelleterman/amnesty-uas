 Yemen: Death Penalty/fear of imminent execution: 'Aisha Ghalib (f) | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 'Aisha Ghalib is at risk of imminent execution. She has exhausted all of the appeals available to her and the death sentence against her has now been ratified by the President. Sources in Yemen have informed Amnesty International that she could be executed as early as 19 April 2009."
