 Switzerland: Further information on fear of forcible return: Anatole Zali  | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 On 19 March 2008, the Swiss Migration Office permitted a request from Cameroonian national Anatole Zali to re-examine his asylum claim. He was released from detention at Zurich airport, and is staying in Switzerland until the authorities decide on his asylum claim. "
