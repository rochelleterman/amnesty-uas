"USA (Missouri): Further information on: Death penalty / Legal concern, Raymond E. Wood 
 USA (Missouri): Further information on: Death penalty / Legal concern, Raymond E. Wood | Amnesty International 
 Raymond Wood is no longer facing the possibility of a death sentence. On 30 July, the prosecutor withdrew her notice of intent to seek the death penalty against him. She gave no reason for her decision.
