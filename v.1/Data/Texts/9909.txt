 | Amnesty International 
 Edward Jerome (""E.J."") Harbison, aged 52,  is due to be executed in Tennessee on 26 September 2007.  He was sentenced to death in 1985 for the murder of Edith Russell in 1983. He has been on death row for more than 20 years.
