"Turkey: Fear of disappearance / fear of torture: Hamza Guneri 
 Turkey: Fear of disappearance / fear of torture: Hamza Guneri | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 AI is concerned for the safety of Hamza Guneri who was taken from his home in Agri by plainclothes policemen on 11 September 1995. His family have had no news of him since then and fear that he has ""disappeared"" in police custody."
