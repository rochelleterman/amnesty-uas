 Pakistan: Further information on incommunicado detention / torture/ill-treatment / unfair trial: Akhtar Mengal | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Akhtar Mengal was released on 9 May 2008, after the Sindh government withdrew the charges against him. On 21 April the Balochistan government had also withdrawn all charges against Mengal, who is the leader of the Balochistan National Party and former Chief Minister of Balochistan."
