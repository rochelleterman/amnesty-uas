"Belarus: Further information on fear of execution / death penalty 
 Belarus: Further information on fear of execution / death penalty | Amnesty International 
 The Supreme Court of Belarus has stated that on 20 January 1998 it commuted the death sentence passed on F. Verega to 15 years' imprisonment.  The Supreme Court did not uphold Igor Ganya's appeal, and therefore he remains under sentence of death.
