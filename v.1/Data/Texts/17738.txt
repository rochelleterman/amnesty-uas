"Israel: Torture / fear of torture: Asam Halman 
 Israel: Torture / fear of torture: Asam Halman | Amnesty International 
 Asam Halman, a resident of Bethlehem, was detained on 25 July 1997 and is being held in the interrogation wing of the Detention Centre in Jerusalem. He has been subjected to torture during interrogation and Amnesty International fears this may continue.
