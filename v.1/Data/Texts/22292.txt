 Iran: Fear of torture and other ill-treatment/ possible prisoner of conscience: Ramtin Soodmand | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Ramtin Soodmand, a Christian who works for a church in Tehran and with youth groups, was arrested on 20 August 2008. He is at risk of torture or other ill-treatment and is being held in an unknown location. He is a prisoner of conscience and should be released immediately as he has been detained solely for his religious beliefs."
