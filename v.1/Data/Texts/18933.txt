"Algeria: Further information on Incommunicado detention/ fear of torture or ill-treatment: Fethi Hamaddouche (m) 
 Algeria: Further information on Incommunicado detention/ fear of torture or ill-treatment: Fethi Hamaddouche (m) | Amnesty International 
 Fethi Hamaddouche, aged 24 or 25,  was released on 12 October 2007 on the eve of the Eid ul-Fitr feast. His brother has confirmed that he was released without charge and is in good health.
