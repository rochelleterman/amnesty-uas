 Rwanda: Further information on fear for safety / death penalty / unfair trial | Amnesty International 
 The trial of Bonaventure Ubalijoro has been postponed.    The trial is now apparently due to take place on or       around 9 September. The details of the formal charges     against him  remain unconfirmed. Eustache Nkerinka        remains under house arrest.
