"People's Republic of China: death penalty: Tan Bingyuan, bank teller, Qin Zeyu, manager 
 People's Republic of China: death penalty: Tan Bingyuan, bank teller, Qin Zeyu, manager | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 The Hunan Daily of 26 August 1995 has reported that 18 people were convicted of economic crimes in Hunan Province on 25 August 1995. Twelve of them reportedly received death sentences. Only two names were given for those sentenced to death: Qin Zeyu and Tan Bingyuan. AI opposes the death penalty in all cases and is calling on the authorities to commute these sentences."
