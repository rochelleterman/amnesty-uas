"Guatemala: Further information on Fear for safety/Threats 
 Guatemala: Further information on Fear for safety/Threats | Amnesty International 
 On 3 September, Galindo Alvarado Mendoza, a cousin of Otoniel de la Roca Mendoza, was shot and killed by two men in the town of Nueva Concepcion, Escuintla Department. He was killed in front of his 11-year-old niece. Amnesty International is concerned for her safety and that of other members of Galindo Alvarado Mendoza's family.
