 Mauritania: Further Information on Fear of ill-treatment or torture/ Incommunicado detention/ Medical concern and  new concern: Unfair trial | Amnesty International 
 Sid'Ahmed Ould Haidalla, the son of former presidential candidate Mohamed Khouna Ould Haidalla, is being subjected to cruel, inhuman or degrading treatment amounting to torture. He has been charged with ""conspiracy and rebellion against public order"" in connection with a case which began in 2002.
