"Nepal: Further information on Fear for safety/possible ""disappearance"" 
 Nepal: Further information on Fear for safety/possible ""disappearance"" | Amnesty International 
 Both Akash Lama and his friend (name unknown), were released on 29 September 2003. They were reportedly arrested on 21 September by security personnel the reasons for their arrest are unknown, but may have been because they were considered to be breaking the curfew declared by the District Administration Office.
