 Venezuela: Hunger striking farmer is missing in Venezuela: Franklin Brito Rodríguez | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Franklin Brito Rodríguez, a farmer, went missing from a military hospital in the Venezuelan capital, Caracas, on 9 January 2010. Staff at the hospital, where he is being treated for the effects of a hunger strike, are said to have sedated him and taken him from his bed. There has been no news of his whereabouts since then."
