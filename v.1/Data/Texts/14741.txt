"Turkey/Greece: Further information on Fear for safety, torture, ill-treatment 
 Turkey/Greece: Further information on  Fear for safety, torture, ill-treatment | Amnesty International 
 All the African nationals stranded on the border between Greece and Turkey have now left the area: most have returned to Istanbul, while some are believed to have entered Greece. More than 150 African nationals have filed complaints at the Human Rights Association office in Istanbul about their treatment by police officers and gendarmes.
