"Nepal: Possible ""disappearance"" / fear of torture 
 Nepal: Possible ""disappearance"" / fear of torture | Amnesty International 
 Four named men were reportedly detained on 3 April, and are now believed to be held incommunicado at the Charali Army Barracks, in the eastern district of Jhapa. They are at risk of torture. All four are political activists. It is not clear why they were taken into custody.
