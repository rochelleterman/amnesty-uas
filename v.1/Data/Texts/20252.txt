 Mexico: Further information: Indigenous activist freed but still at risk: Maximino García Catarino | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Maximino García Catarino, a leading member of the Organization for the Future of the Mixteco People, was released from prison in Ayutla de Los Libres, Guerrero state, Mexico on 20 March 2012. The charges against him were dropped. He is still at risk of reprisals. "
