 Turkey: Fear of ""disappearance"" / fear of torture: Serif Yilmaz, aged 15, Sibli Yilmaz, his brother, aged 35 | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 AI is concerned for the safety of the above two brothers who are being held in unacknowledged detention. According to the mother of Serif Yilmaz, a team of gendarmes came to her house and took the boy away on 1 September. When she made inquiries about him, his detention was denied. His brother, Sibli, was detained a week later and his detention was again denied."
