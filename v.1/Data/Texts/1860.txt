"Rwanda: Further information on fear of executions 
 Rwanda: Further information on fear of executions | Amnesty International 
 Gaspard Mutabazi, sergeant in the Rwandese Patriotic Army (RPA) (executed), Mfizi Muhirwa, Isidore Bishogo, Ernest Gakire, Kami Mutangana, all RPA soldiers and new name: Emmanuel Rutaysire: There are fears of further executions following reports of a second summary execution of a soldier of the Rwandese Patriotic Army.Emmanuel Rutaysire was executed in public on 29 January 1998.
