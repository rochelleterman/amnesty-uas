"China: Further information on Fear of Torture/Arbitrary Arrest. 
 China: Further information on Fear of Torture/Arbitrary Arrest. | Amnesty International 
 On 9 May, workers' representatives Yao Fuxin and Xiao Yunliang were sentenced to prison for seven and four years respectively by the Liaoyang City Intermediate People's Court in Liaoning Province, north east China. Both Yao Fuxin and Xiao Yunliang will appeal against the sentences to the Liaoning Provincial Supreme Court.
