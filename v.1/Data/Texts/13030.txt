"Papua New Guinea: death penalty: Charles Ombusu 
 Papua New Guinea: death penalty: Charles Ombusu | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Charles Ombusu was sentenced to death for murder on 20 February 1995. He was found guilty of wilful murder and rape. He is the first person to be sentenced to death since the reintroduction of the death penalty in 1991."
