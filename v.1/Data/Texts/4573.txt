"Colombia: Further information on ""disappearance"" / apparent extrajudicial executions / fear for safety: Civilian population of the communities of northeastern Choco 
 Colombia: Further information on ""disappearance"" / apparent extrajudicial executions / fear for safety: Civilian population of the communities of northeastern Choco | Amnesty International 
 New names: Rosendo Benitez, Nelson Benitez: Amnesty International remains seriously concerned for the safety of members of the civilian communities in the northeast of the Choco department where paramilitary groups allied to the security forces continue to operate and commit serious human rights violations. On 21 March 1997 members of a paramilitary group interrogated Rosendo Benitez about his son, Nelson Benitez, and then later abducted him. He is feared dead. Nelson Benitez has fled the region.
