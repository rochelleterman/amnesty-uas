 Iran: Possible ""disappearance"" / fear for safety | Amnesty International 
 Student activists Manuchehr Mohammadi, Gholamreza         Mohajeri-Nezhad and Maryam Shansi (f) were arrested in    Tehran on 13 July 1999. They are now reportedly detained  incommunicado, and may be at risk of torture. Amnesty     International believes all three may be prisoners of      conscience.
