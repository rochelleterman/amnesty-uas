"Bangladesh : Possible prisoners of conscience/Fear of     torture/Incommunicado detention 
 Bangladesh : Possible prisoners of conscience/Fear of     torture/Incommunicado detention | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is concerned for the safety of some 15 opposition politicians and two journalists held        incommunicado since 8 December. They were all arrested    without a warrant, and are at risk of torture. They may   be prisoners of conscience, detained for propagating      views critical of government policies.
