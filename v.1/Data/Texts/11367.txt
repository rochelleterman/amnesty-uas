 Indonesia: Torture/ ill-treatment | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is concerned for the safety of human rights defenders Husni Abdullah and Mahyeddin who are being detained by police in Nanggroe Aceh Darussalam Province (NAD). Husni Abdullah has not been given access to his family or lawyers, while Mahyeddin has been given restricted access to lawyers but not to his family. They have reportedly been beaten, and are at risk of further torture or ill-treatment
