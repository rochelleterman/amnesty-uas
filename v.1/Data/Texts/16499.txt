 Syria/Lebanon: ""Disappearance"" / fear of torture or ill-treatment: Tony Michael Zakkour | Amnesty International 
 The above-named individual was reportedly abducted by Syrian military intelligence officers in Lebanon on 20 February 1998. There is grave concern that he may be at risk of torture or ill-treatment whilst held in incommunicado detention and that he could be held for some time without charge or trial.
