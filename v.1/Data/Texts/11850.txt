"Indonesia: Fear for safety / possible ""disappearance"": Ramli 
 Indonesia: Fear for safety / possible ""disappearance"": Ramli | Amnesty International 
 There is concern for the safety of Ramli, a 45-year-old   resident of Panton Rayeuk Village, Alue Bu in East Aceh.  He has not been seen since his arrest by security forces  on 13 August 1999. The reason for his arrest is not       clear, but he may be at risk of torture, ill-treatment or ""disappearance"".
