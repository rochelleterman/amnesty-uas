"Uzbekistan: Further information on border closure impeding safe return of refugees 
 Uzbekistan: Further information on border closure impeding safe return of refugees | Amnesty International 
 Around 7,300 Tajik refugees in Sakhi camp, northern Afghanistan: The authorities of Uzbekistan have agreed to comply with UN's requests to facilitate the safe repatriation of Tajik refugees in Afghanistan by reopening the Termez border crossing between the two countries.
