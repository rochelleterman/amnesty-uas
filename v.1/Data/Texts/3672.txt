"Bahamas: further information on: death penalty: Ricardo Farrington 
 Bahamas: further information on: death penalty: Ricardo Farrington | Amnesty International 
 The above, who was originally scheduled to be executed on 9 April 1996, has been granted leave to appeal to the Judicial Committee of the Privy Council in London, the final court of appeal for the Bahamas. The full appeal hearing is expected to be held in July.
