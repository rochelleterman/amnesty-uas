 Jordan:  Further Information on Fear of deportation, detention and ill-treatment   Ibrahim Hamdi 'Atia Mursi 

 | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International has learned that Ibrahim Hamdi 'Atia Mursi is no longer at risk of deportation to Egypt. According to reports, he continues to be detained in Jordan pending re-settlement but is not in danger of ill-treatment.
