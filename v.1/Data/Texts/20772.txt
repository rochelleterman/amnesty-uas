 Bangladesh: Further information: Authorities told to respond about detainees | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Two student opposition activists in Bangladesh, Al Mukaddas and Mohammad Waliullah, are still missing after they were allegedly detained by security forces on 4 February. On 15 February a court ordered the government to respond by 7 March to allegations that the two men had been illegally detained. "
