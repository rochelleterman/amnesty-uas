 USA: Further information on: Incommunicado detention/detention without charge/legal concern, Ali-Saleh Kahlah al-Marri | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International remains seriously concerned for the physical and psychological welfare of Ali Saleh Kahlah al-Marri, aged 40, who has been held in US custody without charge or trial for over four-and-a-half years. He is held in a military detention facility in Charleston, South Carolina.
