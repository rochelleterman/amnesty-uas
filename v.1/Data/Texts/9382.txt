"EXTRA 53/94 - USA (Texas): death penalty: George Lott, Warren Bridge, Herman Clark, Walter Williams 
 EXTRA 53/94 - USA (Texas): death penalty: George Lott, Warren Bridge, Herman Clark, Walter Williams | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 George Lott, white, who was sentenced to death in February 1993, is scheduled to be executed on 20 September 1994. Warren Bridge, white, who was sentenced to death in 1980, is scheduled to be executed on 21 September. He reportedly received poor legal representation at trial. Herman Clark , black, who was sentenced to death in May 1982, is scheduled to be executed on 27 September. Walter Williams, also black, is scheduled to be executed on 5 October."
