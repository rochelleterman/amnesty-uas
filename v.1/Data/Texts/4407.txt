"Colombia: Fear for safety / possible extrajudicial execution: Victor Julio Garzon 
 Colombia: Fear for safety / possible extrajudicial execution: Victor Julio Garzon | Amnesty International 
 There is grave concern for the safety of popular activists supporting peasant farmers involved in demonstrations in coca-producing regions in Colombia, following the killing of trade union leader Victor Julio Garzon, the secretary general of the National United Agricultural Federation (FENSUAGRO) on 7 March 1997.
