"China: Further information on: Fear for safety/ death threats: Gao Zhisheng (m) 
 China: Further information on: Fear for safety/ death threats: Gao Zhisheng (m) | Amnesty International 
 Human rights lawyer Gao Zhisheng, aged 42, was detained in Shandong Province on 15 August by police officers from Beijing. He is believed to be held incommunicado at an unknown location and is at risk of torture or ill-treatment.
