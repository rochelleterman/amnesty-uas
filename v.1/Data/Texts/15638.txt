 Russian Federation: Freedom of Expression, ""School of Peace"", non-governmental minority rights organization | Amnesty International 
 A prominent Russian non-governmental organization may face closure as a result of its activities in defence of ethnic minority rights. The closure of the organization, ""School of Peace"", is due to be decided at a court hearing scheduled to take place on 22 August.
