 Nepal: Further information on Fear for safety / possible ""disappearance"" | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International has received information that Ramesh Dhungana was released on 26 May. He had reportedly been arrested by security force personnel in civilian clothes on 12 March in Balaju, Kathmandu. Amnesty International is increasingly concerned about the safety of Indra Raj Pathak who was reportedly arrested by security force personnel in civilian clothes on 8 March in the Bhimsenthan area of Kathmandu.
