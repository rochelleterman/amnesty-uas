"Mexico: Further information on ""disappearance"" / fear for safety / ill-treatment: Ignacio Garcia Muniz 
 Mexico: Further information on ""disappearance"" / fear for safety / ill-treatment: Ignacio Garcia Muniz | Amnesty International 
 Ignacio Garcia Munoz has ""reappeared"" and is being held in the Social Readaptation Centre of Chilpacingo, Guerrero State. He has been accused of possession of seeds from the poppy plant. AI believes that he has been targeted for his involvement with the Party of the Democratic Revolution (PRD). AI has received no further information on Alejandro Samano Zapata and Martha de Jesus Caballero.
