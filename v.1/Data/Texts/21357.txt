 Turkey: Prison transfer for human rights defender: Halil Savda  | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Human rights defender Halil Savda was transferred to a new prison on 15 March. He has now served 28 days of a 100-day prison sentence, for speaking publicly about his support for conscientious objectors. He is a prisoner of conscience, detained solely for the peaceful exercise of his right to freedom of expression. "
