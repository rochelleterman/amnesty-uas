"USA (Texas): Further Information on Death penalty / Legal concern  William Hodges. 
 USA (Texas): Further Information on Death penalty / Legal concern  William Hodges. | Amnesty International 
 William Hodges, facing trial in Texas on charges of rape  and murder, is no longer facing a death sentence.         Instead, he has been sentenced to life imprisonment. He   will not be eligible for parole until he has spent at     least 60 years in prison.
