"USA (Missouri): Further information on death penalty: Roosevelt Pollard 
 USA (Missouri): Further information on death penalty: Roosevelt Pollard | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Roosevelt Pollard was granted a state of execution, hours before he was due to be executed on 11 January 1995. His case has been sent back for review to the Federal District Court. The main issue to be considered is whether he is competent to be executed."
