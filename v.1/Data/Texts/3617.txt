"Argentina: Further Information on Threats/Fear for Safety. 
 Argentina: Further Information on Threats/Fear for Safety. | Amnesty International 
 Gustavo Melmann, members of his family and family friends were subjected to harassment and intimidation following the conviction of three policemen for the murder of Gustavo and Laura Melmann's daughter Natalia. They are continuing their legal struggle for justice, but have reportedly not received any new death threats.
