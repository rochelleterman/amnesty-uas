"Jordan: Death penalty: Kayed Ahmad 
 Jordan: Death penalty: Kayed Ahmad | Amnesty International 
 Kayed Ahmad, aged 29, was sentenced to death by the Amman Criminal Court on 25 July 1996, having been convicted of murder. His case will be reviewed by the Court of Cassation by 24 August.
