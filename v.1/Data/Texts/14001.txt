 Greece: Ill-treatment/ fear of further ill-treatment: Genc Dauti | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 AI is concerned for the well-being of 16-year-old Albanian, Genc Dauti, detained on or around 2 November by Greek police, who has allegedly been beaten in Corfu police station.  AI fears he is at risk of further ill-treatment, particularly if transferred to Korydallos prsion in Athens."
