"Further information on UA 216/93 (ASA 13/07/93, 2 July 1993) - Bangladesh: fear of imminent execution: Munir Hussain 
 Further information on UA 216/93 (ASA 13/07/93, 2 July 1993) - Bangladesh: fear of imminent execution: Munir Hussain | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Munir Hussain's appeal for clemency to the President was rejected on 3 July 1993. No date for the execution has been announced, but his family have been unofficially informed that the order has been sent to Dhaka Central Jail, and his execution could take place anytime. "
