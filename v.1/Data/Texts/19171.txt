"Tunisia: Further information on prisoner of conscience and health concern: Abdel Moumen Belanes 
 Tunisia: Further information on prisoner of conscience and health concern: Abdel Moumen Belanes | Amnesty International 
 There is increasing concern about the health of Abdel Moumen Belanes, a student and prisoner of conscience, who has been on hunger-strike since 17 March 1997. He has reportedly still not received a medical examination, in spite of requests made by his lawyers.
