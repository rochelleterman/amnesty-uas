 China: Death penalty: Luo Guohong | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 According to a Reuters report from Beijing dated 30 October 1995, the above, a 35-year-old former bank manager, has been sentenced to death for embezzlement by a court in Guangdong province. It is not known whether Luo Guohong has appealed against the death sentence. Successful appeals are rare."
