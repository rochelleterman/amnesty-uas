"USA (Missouri): death penalty: Tony Murray 
 USA (Missouri): death penalty: Tony Murray | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Tony Murray, black, is scheduled to be executed in Missouri on 26 July 1995. He was sentenced to death for the murders of two men during a robbery. Murray was reportedly one of three people who took part in the crime: one was acquitted and the other received a life sentence."
