 Burundi: Further information on - Disappearance / Fear of torture /         Incommunicado detention 

 | Amnesty International 
 Paul Nsanzurwimo and Emile Masabo were released from Cibitoke military camp, where they had been illegally held, on 27 or 28 March. However, Emile Masabo has since been detained again, and is now in military custody. It is not clear why Emile Masabo is still in custody.
