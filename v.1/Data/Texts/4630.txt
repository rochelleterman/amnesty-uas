 Colombia: prisoner of conscience / fear for safety: Luis Gabriel Caldas Leon, 18, conscientious objector | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Luis Gabriel Caldas Leon, aged 18, from Bogota, has been arrested by police after declaring himself unwilling to perform military service. AI considers him to be a prisoner of conscience who should be released immediately and unconditionally."
