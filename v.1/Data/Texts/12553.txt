"Nepal: Further information on - Fear of Torture/Fear for safety 
 Nepal: Further information on  - Fear of Torture/Fear for safety | Amnesty International 
 Newspaper editor, Gopal Budhathoki was released on 26 March. He had reportedly been held at the army barracks at Chhauni in Kathmandu. Gopal Budhathoki is the editor and publisher of the weekly newspaper Sanghu (Bridge). He had been arrested by the security forces on 3 March, on his way home from the newspaper's offices in Kathmandu. There is no abstract for this document.
