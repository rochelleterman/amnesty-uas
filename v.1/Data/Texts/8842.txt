 USA (California): Further information on insane man due to be executed: Horace Kelly, black | Amnesty International 
 Horace Kelly was not executed as scheduled on 14 April 1998 as the hearing to determine his sanity, initiated by tthe San Quentin prison warden after psychiatric reports, had not yet taken place. His federal appeal had been refused after missing the one-year deadline.
