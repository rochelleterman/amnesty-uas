 Sri Lanka: Further Information on Fear for safety  Nandini Herat (f) | Amnesty International 
 Five police officers implicated in the torture of Nandini Herat were recently transferred out of Wariyapola police  station, reportedly on the orders of the Sri Lankan       Inspector General of Police. It is not known whether they have been suspended from duty.
