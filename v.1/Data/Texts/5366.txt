"Colombia: Fear for Safety, Members of peasant communities in Cauca department 
 Colombia: Fear for Safety, Members of peasant communities in Cauca department | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is concerned for the safety of      peasant farmer communities and social activists in the    southwestern department of Cauca, where protesters have   been participating in a strike and demonstrations since   16 September. Security forces in the area have reportedly restricted access to food, water and medical attention    for peasant farmers participating in the strike.
