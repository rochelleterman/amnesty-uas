"Bangladesh: Death threats 
 Bangladesh: Death threats	 | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is concerned for the safety of the family of Ahsanullah Master, an Awami League Member of Parliament who was killed by up to ten gunmen at about 12.25 pm on 7 May. His family have reportedly been receiving death threats following a case against 18 people, filed by his younger brother Motiur Rahman Moti.
