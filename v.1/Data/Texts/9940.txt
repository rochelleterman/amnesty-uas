 USA (Alabama): Further information on Death penalty / Legal concern: Thomas Douglas Arthur (m) 
 | Amnesty International 
 Thomas Arthur, white, aged 65, received a 45-day stay of execution from Governor Bob Riley of Alabama on 27 September 2007, the day he was scheduled to be executed. The state is moving to set a new date ""as soon as possible"".
