 Bangladesh: Fear for Safety | Amnesty International 
 Leaders of Islamist groups have threatened to attack members of the Ahmadiyya Muslim Jamaat religious community, on Friday 11 March, in the city of Bogra. This attack is intended to be a strong statement by Islamist groups to pressure the government to declare Bangladesh's Ahmadis ""non-Muslims"".
