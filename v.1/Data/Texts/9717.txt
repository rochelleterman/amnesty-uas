 USA (Texas): Further information on death penalty: Patrick Bryan Knight (m)

 | Amnesty International 
 Patrick Knight, white, aged 39, was executed in Texas on 26 June. He was sentenced to death in Randall County in 1993 for the murder of Mary Ann and Walter Werner in 1991.
