"Indonesia: Fear for safety/Possible 'disappearance' 
 Indonesia:   Fear for safety / Possible disappearance | Amnesty International 
 Human rights activist Koes Sofyan was taken from his parents home in Aceh on 14 March, by two men in plain clothes believed to be from the military. He has not been seen since, and there are serious concerns for his safety.
