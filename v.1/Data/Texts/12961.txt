"Pakistan: Further information on: death penalty: Mutabar Khan 
 Pakistan: Further information on: death penalty: Mutabar Khan | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International has learnt that Mutabar Khan was executed in Peshawar Central Prison on 13 June 2006. He was sentenced to death for murder in 1998 and all his appeals to overturn the sentence were consequently dismissed by superior courts.
