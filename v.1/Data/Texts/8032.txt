"Peru:  Fear for safety/Possible Prisoners of conscience - Forty five people 
 Peru:  Fear for safety/Possible Prisoners of conscience - Forty five people	detained under anti-terrorism         legislation | Amnesty International 
 Forty five people were arrested when they went to vote in the second round of Peru's presidential elections, under  sweeping anti-terrorism legislation. Such prisoners can   be held without charge for 15 days, during which they are frequently tortured and ill-treated.
