"USA (Texas): Death penalty / legal concern: Shaka Sankofa 
 USA (Texas): Death penalty / legal concern: Shaka Sankofa | Amnesty International 
 Shaka Sankofa, formerly Gary Graham, is scheduled for execution in Texas on 11 January 1999 for a murder committed when he was 17.  He has spent half his life on death row and came within hours of execution in 1993. International law bans the death penalty for crimes committed by people under 18.
