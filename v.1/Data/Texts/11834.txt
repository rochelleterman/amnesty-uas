 Inodnesia: Fear for safety / possible ""disappearance"" / fear of torture, ill-treatment or killing | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International fears for the safety of Mulyadi     Ibrahim and Husin Idris who have not been seen since they were arrested by the PPRM (Petugas Penindak Rusuh Massa), a riot control police unit, on 30 July 1999 in the        province of Aceh, northern Sumatra. They could be at risk of torture, ill-treatment, ""disappearance"" or unlawful    killing.
