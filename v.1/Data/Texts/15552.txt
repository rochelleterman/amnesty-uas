"Russian Federation: Fear of forcible deportation / torture / unfair trial: Dodojon Atovulloyev 
 Russian Federation: Fear of forcible deportation / torture / unfair trial: Dodojon Atovulloyev | Amnesty International 
 Journalist Dodojon Atovulloyev was detained by Russian police on 5 July. He was reportedly arrested at the request of the Tajik authorities, and may be forcibly returned to Tajikistan, where he would be at grave risk of torture.
