 Morocco: Unwell prisoner still in solitary confinement: Further information: Kaddour Terhaz | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Kaddour Terhaz, a 73-year-old Moroccan with chronic medical conditions, remains in solitary confinement, sleeping on a mattress on the floor. He has a 12-year prison sentence for exercising his right to freedom of expression and must be immediately and unconditionally released."
