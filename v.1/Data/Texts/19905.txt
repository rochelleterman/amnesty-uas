 Colombia: Human rights group threatened in Colombia | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Members of the Colombian human rights NGO the Inter-Church Justice and Peace Commission (Comisión Intereclesial Justicia y Paz), including priest Father Javier Giraldo, are in danger following the appearance of threatening graffiti in Bogotá, Colombia's capital. The Inter-Church Justice and Peace Commission has been receiving death threats for several years."
