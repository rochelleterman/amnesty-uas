"EXTRA 76/94 - Singapore: imminent execution: Elke Tsang Kai Mong 
 EXTRA 76/94 - Singapore: imminent execution: Elke Tsang Kai Mong | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Elke Tsang Kai Mong, was was arrested on 26 July 1992 and convicted of trafficking over 4kg of heroin, is scheduled to be executed on 16 December 1994. Her appeal was dismissed on 26 January 1994."
