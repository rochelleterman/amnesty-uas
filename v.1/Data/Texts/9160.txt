 USA (Virginia): Death penalty, Dennis Mitchell Orbe 
 | Amnesty International 
 Dennis Orbe is scheduled to be executed in Virginia on 31 March 2004. He was sentenced to death in October 1998 for the murder of Richard Burnett, a 39-year-old white man who was shot and killed in January 1998 during a robbery of the convenience store where he worked.
