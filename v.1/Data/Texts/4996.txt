 Colombia: Further information on fear for safety and new concern: possible extrajudicial execution: members of the Union Sindical Obrera (USO), Worker's Trade Union: Rafael Gonzalez Barraza | Amnesty International 
 On 10 October 1996, Rafael Gonzalez Barraza, aged 44, Secretary General of the Workers' Trade Union, was killed by two gunmen in the El Cerro district of Barrancabermeja, department of Santander.
