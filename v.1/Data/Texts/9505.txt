 USA (Tennessee): Death penalty / Legal concern: Paul Dennis Reid (m) | Amnesty International 
 Paul Reid, who has a history of mental illness, is scheduled to be executed in Tennessee on 28 June. He was sentenced to death in 1999 for the 1997 murder of two young women. He has given up appeals against his conviction and death sentence.
