 USA(Texas): Further information on death penalty / Legal concern   Thomas Joe Miller-El, black, aged 50 | Amnesty International 
 On 15 February, the US Supreme Court announced that it would hear the appeal of Thomas Miller-El. His execution, scheduled for 21 February, is stayed pending the Court's ruling on the case.
