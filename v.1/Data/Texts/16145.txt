 Turkmenistan: Fear of imminent execution: Andrey Voronin and Kamal Nepesov | Amnesty International 
 Andrey Voronin and Kamal Nepesov were sentenced to death for murder on 1 April 1998 and could be imminently executed. They allege they were forced to sign confessions under torture and their families were threatened.
