"UA 394/94 - Iran: fear of torture: Hengameh Amini 
 UA 394/94 - Iran: fear of torture: Hengameh Amini | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Hengameh Amini, whose mother is the well-known singer Marzieh, was reportedly arrested about three weeks ago, apparently in connection with her mother's decision to join the National Council of Resistance, an opposition group. AI fears that she is being tortured in incommunicado detention in Evin Prison. Hengameh Amini is reportedly not involved in politics."
