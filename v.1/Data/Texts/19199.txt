"Tunisia: Further information on: legal concern / prisoner of conscience: Khemais Chammari 
 NA 
 The above, a human rights activist and parliamentarian, was tried and sentenced to five years' imprisonment on 17 July 1996. An AI observer attended the trial in which he was accused of ""disclosing a national secret to a foreign power"", a charge which he denies. AI considers Khemais Chammari to be a prisoner of conscience and calls for his immediate and unconditional release.
