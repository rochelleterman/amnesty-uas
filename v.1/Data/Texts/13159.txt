 Philipines: ""Disappearance""/fear for safety, Victor ""Jun"" Saducos | Amnesty International 
 Victor ""Jun"" Saducos was abducted on 23 November by armed men believed to be members of the 204th Brigade of the    Armed Forces of the Philippines (AFP). His whereabouts    remain unknown, and Amnesty International is concerned    for his safety.
