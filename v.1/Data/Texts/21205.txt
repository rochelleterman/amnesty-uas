 Singapore: Further information on death penalty: Tan Chor Jin (m) | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Tan Chor Jin was hanged in the morning of 9 January 2009. The president had rejected his appeal for clemency in the last week of December. Tan Chor Jin had been sentenced to death in May 2007 for the murder of a nightclub owner he said owed him money."
