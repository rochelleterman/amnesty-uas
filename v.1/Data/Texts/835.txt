 Ethiopia: Further information on Fear for safety / Possible prisoners of conscience / Harassment of human rights defenders | Amnesty International 
 Professor Mesfin Wolde Mariam and Dr Berhanu Nega were released on bail on 5 June. However, they are still facing charges that appear to be entirely without foundation, and Amnesty International would consider both men prisoners of conscience if convicted.
