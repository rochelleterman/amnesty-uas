 Iran: Further information: Disappearance / Possible extrajudicial execution, Siamak Pourzand	 | Amnesty International 
 It appears that Siamak Pourzand was arrested after he left his sister's house in central Tehran on 24 November. There has not been, as far as Amnesty International is aware, official acknowledgement that he is in custody.
