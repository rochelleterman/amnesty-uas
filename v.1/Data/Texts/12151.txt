 Laos: Further information on Possible prisoners of conscience / Fear of ill-treatment: Thongpaseuth Keuakoun | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International has obtained the names of five more men arrested following an attempted pro-democracy         demonstration in Vientiane on 26 October 1999. They are   Kamphouvieng Sisaath, Seng-Aloun Phengphanh, Bouavanh     Chanhmanivong, Keochay and Khamnoy Khamphilavong (aged    70).
