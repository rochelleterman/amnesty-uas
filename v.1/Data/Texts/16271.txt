 Uzbekistan: Imminent execution/torture and ill-treatment/impunity	 | Amnesty International 
 Three men have exhausted their appeals against their death sentences, and could be executed at any time. Mass appeals now could save their lives. All three are alleged to have been tortured or ill-treated in custody, and two of them allegedly confessed under duress.
