"Further information on UA 371/93 (AMR 36/28/93, 21 October) - Haiti: fear for safety: Samuel Milord 
 Further information on UA 371/93 (AMR 36/28/93, 21 October) - Haiti: fear for safety: Samuel Milord | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 According to family members, Samuel Milord was abducted on the evening of 20 October 1993 in the Debussy area of Port-au-Prince, not in Desdunes, as first thought. He has not been seen since, and there are serious concerns for his safety. "
