"Jordan: Further Information on Possible prisoner of conscience/Legal concern, Fawaz Zurayqat. 
 Jordan: Further Information on Possible prisoner of conscience/Legal concern, Fawaz Zurayqat. | Amnesty International 
 Fawaz Zurayqat was released from the General Intelligence Department in Amman sometime between the evening of 30 March 2003 and the morning of 31 March 2003. According to initial reports, Fawaz Zurayqat was not ill-treated whilst in detention, nor has he been charged with a criminal offence
