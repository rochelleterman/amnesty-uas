"Egypt:Possible prisoner of conscience/health concern, Farid Zahran 
 Egypt:Possible prisoner of conscience/health concern, Farid Zahran | Amnesty International 
 NGO activist Farid Zahran was arrested by plainclothes security officers in Cairo on 20 September. Amnesty International believes that he may be a prisoner of conscience, and is concerned that although he has a serious heart condition he is not receiving adequate medical care.
