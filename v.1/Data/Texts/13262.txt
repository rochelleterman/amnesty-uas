"Philippines: Further information on imminent execution: Josefina Esparas 
 Philippines: Further information on imminent execution: Josefina Esparas | Amnesty International 
 On 27 September President Estrada granted Josefina        Esparas, scheduled for execution by lethal     injection  on 30 September 1999, a 20-day reprieve to allow the      newly created ""conscience committee"" to study her case.   The committee will recommend to the President whether or  not to grant her clemency.
