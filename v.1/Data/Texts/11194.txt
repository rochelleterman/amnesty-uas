"UA 261/94 - India: fear of torture / fear of ""disappearance"": Ajit Kumar Bhuyan 
 UA 261/94 - India: fear of torture / fear of ""disappearance"": Ajit Kumar Bhuyan | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 In the early hours of 5 July 1994 police reportedly arbitrarily arrested Ajit Kumar Bhuyan at his home in Guwahati, Assam. His current whereabouts are unknown. He is the editor of the mass circulation Assamese language weekly ""Saadin"". He is also a coordinator of the Human Rights Struggle Committee and a founder member of the Assam Union of Working Journalists."
