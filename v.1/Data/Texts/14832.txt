"Turkey: Further information on prisoner of conscience / fear of torture / fear for safety: Eren Keskin (f) 
 Turkey: Further information on prisoner of conscience / fear of torture / fear for safety: Eren Keskin (f) | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 On 2 June 1995, Eren Keskin, arrested on 1 June, was brought to Bayrampasa prison in Istanbul to serve a two years and six months' sentence. AI considers her to be a prisoner of conscience and is calling for her immediate and unconditional release."
