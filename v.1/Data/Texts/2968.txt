"Sudan: Fear for safety/torture and ill-treatment/incommunicado detention, Adel Abdallah Nasr al-Din (m), lawyer and human rights defender 
 Sudan: Fear for safety/torture and ill-treatment/incommunicado detention, Adel Abdallah Nasr al-Din (m), lawyer and human rights defender | Amnesty International 
 Human rights activist Adel Abdallah Nasr al-Din was arrested by the National Security and Intelligence Agency on 16 June 2004, and taken to the agency's centre in the town of Nyala where he is at risk of torture and ill-treatment. He is held incommunicado.
