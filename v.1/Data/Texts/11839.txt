 Indonesia/East Timor: Death penalty: | Amnesty International 
 Luis Maria da Silva and Francisco da Costa: On 11 December 1997, these two Timorese men were sentenced to death by the Bacau District Court. The two men are reported to have lodged an appeal to the High Court.
