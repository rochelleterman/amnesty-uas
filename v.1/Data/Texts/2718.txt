"UA 159/94 - Sudan: prisoner of conscience / fear of torture: Sid Ahmad al-Hussein 
 UA 159/94 - Sudan: prisoner of conscience / fear of torture: Sid Ahmad al-Hussein | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Sid Ahmad al-Hussein, the most senior member of the banned Democratic Unionist Party remaining in Sudan, was arrested in Khartoum on 20 March 1994. AI believes him to be a prisoner of conscience and fears that he may be being tortured in secret detention."
