"Mexico: Further information on ""disappearance"" / fear for safety and new concern: torture 
 Mexico: Further information on ""disappearance"" / fear for safety and new concern: torture | Amnesty International 
 Manuel Ramirez Santiago, civil rights activist, Fermin Oseguera Santiago, trade union activist: The above were released, after suffering torture and being interogated about the activities of  the Popular Revolutionary Army (EPR), on 1 November 1996. They had been abducted on 22 October at gunpoint by unidentified individuals, believed to be members of the security forces.
