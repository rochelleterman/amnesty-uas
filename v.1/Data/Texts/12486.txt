"Nepal: Further information on Fear of torture/possible ""disappearance"", Manoj Rai (m) aged 15, student - Released 
 Nepal: Further information on Fear of torture/possible ""disappearance"",  Manoj Rai (m) aged 15, student - Released | Amnesty International 
 On 1 January 2004, Manoj Rai was released by police on condition that he report to the Hanuman Dhoka District Police Office (DPO) on a daily basis. He was arrested on 27 September 2003 and initially held at the DPO, where he was reportedly severely beaten.
