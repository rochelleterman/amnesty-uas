"Colombia: fear for safety: Omar Garcia 
 Colombia: fear for safety: Omar Garcia | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 There are fears for the safety of Omar Garcia, witness to the arrest in 1993 of two men who subsequently ""disappeared"". One of the two police officers implicated in the ""disappearances"" is about to go on trial; Omar Garcia has bee receiving death threats. Another witness to the arrests was killed in 1994, heightening fears for Omar Garcia's safety."
