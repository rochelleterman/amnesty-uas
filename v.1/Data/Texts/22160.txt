 Iran: Fear of torture: Farshad Dousipour (also known as Doustipour)  | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Kurdish student Farshad Dousipour was arrested on 15 May 2008 by plainclothes Revolutionary Guards officers. He had been walking in the street at the time, in the city of Ivan-e Gharb, in the western province of Ilam. It is not known where he was taken, and he is at risk of torture."
