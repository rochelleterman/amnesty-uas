"Turkey/Greece: Further information on Fear for safety, torture, ill-treatment 
 Turkey/Greece: Further information on  Fear for safety, torture, ill-treatment | Amnesty International 
 Fears for the safety of the group of African nationals stranded in the Turkish border area with Greece continue. They are without access to food, clean water, shelter and medical attention. Attempts to reach them from the Turkish side have been refused. It is believed that they may be close to mine-fields.
