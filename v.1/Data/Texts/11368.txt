"Indonesia:Further information on fear of imminent execution 
 Indonesia:Further information on fear of imminent execution | Amnesty International 
 The authorities are reportedly preparing for the execution of Fabianus Tibo, Dominggus da Silva and Marinus Riwu. The intended date and time have not been announced. President Yudhoyono rejected the three men's appeal for clemency in November 2005, but the authorities still have the power to delay the execution while the case is reviewed.
