"Jamaica: Death penalty 
 Jamaica: Death penalty | Amnesty International 
 Samuel Lindsay, Henry McKoy, Lansford James: According to reports the above three prisoners on death row at St Catherine District Prison have been moved to the condemned cell, and may face imminent execution. If carried out, these will be the first executions in Jamaica since 1988.
