 USA: Further information: Martin Grossman executed in Florida  | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Martin Grossman, aged 45, was executed in the US state of Florida shortly after 6pm on 16 February 2010 for a crime committed when he was 19 years old. He was convicted of murder in 1985, and had been on death row for nearly a quarter of a century."
