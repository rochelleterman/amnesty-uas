"Iran: Further information on Fear of imminent execution 
 Iran: Further information on Fear of imminent execution | Amnesty International 
 Alireza Asakreh, Malek Banitamim and Ali Matouri Zadeh were reportedly executed on 19 December in Sepidar prison in Khuzestan province. Nine other men remain at grave risk of imminent execution. They are: Abdullah Suleymani, Abdulreza Sanawati Zergani, Qasem Salamat, Mohammad Jaab Pour, Abdulamir Farjallah Jaab, Majed Alboghubaish, Khalaf Derhab Khudayrawi, Abdul Husain Haribi, Husain Maramazi and Husain Asakreh.
