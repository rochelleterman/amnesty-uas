 Sudan:  Further information on fear  of torture or ill-treatment/Detentions  without  charge/Possible prisoners of conscience - Yousef Hussein and Abbas Mohamed al Tahir
 | Amnesty International 
 Communist Party members, Yousef Hussein and Abbas Mohamed al Tahir were released without charge on 10 January 2002. They had been held in incommunicado detention since 3 November 2001.
