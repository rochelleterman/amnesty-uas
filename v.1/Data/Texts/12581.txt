 Nepal: Further information on Fear for safety/possible ""disappearance/New concern: torture/ ill-treatment | Amnesty International 
 Shyam Raj Acharya was released after 15 days in detention at Chhauni army barracks, Kathmandu. He was freed on condition that he reports to the security forces every week. Amnesty International continues to be concerned about the safety of Gyanendra Prasad Bidari, Manoj Kumar Shah and Kamal KC who were reportedly arrested on 30 October 2003, 7 November 2003 and 13 November 2003 respectively.
