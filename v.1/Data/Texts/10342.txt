"Bangladesh: Fear of safety/Threats, Rabindra Ghosh. 
 Bangladesh: Fear of safety/Threats, Rabindra Ghosh. | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is concerned for the safety of Rabindra Ghosh, an advocate of the Supreme Court of Bangladesh and a leading member of the Hindu community. He has been threatened in recent months and was reportedly attacked on 1 July.
