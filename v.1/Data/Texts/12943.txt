 Pakistan: Prisoner of conscience / fear of torture: Humayun Far, journalist | Amnesty International 
 The whereabouts of journalist Humayn Far, arrested on 28 June 1997 in Islamabad, remain unknown, and there is also concern that on the evidence so far received, he may be a prisoner of conscience.
