"Russian Federation (Chechen Republic): Further information on fear of further executions: 27 people, names unknown 
 Russian Federation (Chechen Republic): Further information on fear of further executions: 27 people, names unknown | Amnesty International 
 There is concern that the 27 people arrested on suspicion of kidnapping may face the death penalty, after the President urged the Shari'a courts to give them severe punishments. He recently issued a decree which allows courts to sentence kidnappers to death.
