"USA (California): Further information on death penalty / legal concern: Thomas Thompson 
 USA (California): Further information on death penalty / legal concern: Thomas Thompson | Amnesty International 
 Thomas Thompson has been scheduled for execution on 14 July 1998. His death sentence was originally annulled by the Ninth US Circuit Court of Appeal but this decision was then overturned by the US Supreme Court on 29 April 1998.
