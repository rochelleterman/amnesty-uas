"Jordan: Death penalty: Firas Issam 
 Jordan: Death penalty: Firas Issam | Amnesty International 
 Firas Issam was sentenced to death by the Amman Criminal Court on 28 December 1997. The death sentenced must now be ratified by the Court of Cassation and Kin Hussein bin Talal before it can be carried out. AI appeals for the commutation of the death sentence.
