"Venezuela: Death threats: Luis Jesus Bello, lawyer and human rights defender 
 Venezuela: Death threats: Luis Jesus Bello, lawyer and human rights defender | Amnesty International 
 There is concern for the safety of Luis Jesus Bello after he reportedly received a death threat on 11 July 1997. The threat came during an interview on a local radio station from the director of a company accused of serious abuses against local Indian communities perpetrated with the acquiescence of local authorities.
