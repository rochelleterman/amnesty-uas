 Ecuador: Fear for safety/Incommunicado detention/possible prisoners of conscience	   	    	  	

 | Amnesty International 
  
 Luis Muñoz, David Conrado, Juan Pablo Barragán, Jose Luis Proaño, Alberto Saltos. Stalin Saltos, Ivon Ramos and     Julia Butterfly. The eight environmentalists named above  were detained     without charge on 16 July 2002 in       Quito, the capital.
