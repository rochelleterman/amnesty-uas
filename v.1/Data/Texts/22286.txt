 Iran: Further information: Freed Iranian Christians still at risk of trial | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Maryam Rostampour and Marzieh Amirizadeh Esmaeilabad, two Iranian female converts to Christianity, were released from Evin Prison on 18 November 2009.  Both have been acquitted of “acting against state security” but continue to face other charges relating to their conversion. "
