 Indonesia: Further information on Fear of torture / Possible prisoners of  conscience.

 | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International has learned that six named people were each sentenced to four and a half months' imprisonment for ""insulting the President or Vice-president"" on 4 June. All six were released five days later, having already served the sentence in pre-trial detention.
