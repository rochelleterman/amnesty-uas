 India: Fear for safety / fear of ""disappearance"": Zahoor Ahmed Khanday | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is concerned for the safety of      15-year-old Zahoor Ahmed Khanday, after he was detained   by state police. His whereabouts are unknown. He is       apparently being held hostage, and is at risk of torture  or death in custody.
