 Sudan: Further information: Activist detained again after release order | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Sudanese human rights activist, Bushra Gamar Hussein Rahma, was re-arrested on 14 August 2011 by agents of the National Intelligence and Security Services (NISS), immediately after a judge had ordered his release. He is being held in an unknown location, and is at risk of torture and other ill-treatment.  "
