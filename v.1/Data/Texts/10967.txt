"People's Republic of China: Further information on arbitrary arrest / fear for safety: Hongda Harry Wu 
 People's Republic of China: Further information on arbitrary arrest / fear for safety: Hongda Harry Wu | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Harry Wu, a human rights activist and prisoner of conscience, was sentenced on 24 August 1995 to 15 years' imprisonment by the Wuhan city Intermediate People's Court for ""stealing state secrets"" and ""posing as a state functionary"". The court also sentenced him to expulsion from China."
