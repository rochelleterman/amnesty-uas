"Russian Federation (Chechya):  Incommunicado detention / Fear of torture and ill-treatment - Adam Abubakarov 
 Russian Federation (Chechya):  Incommunicado detention / Fear of torture and ill-treatment - Adam Abubakarov | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is concerned that 16-year-old       Chechen Adam Abubakarov is detained incommunicado by the  Russian authorities, reportedly at a prison hospital in   the Russian town of Pyatigorsk. He has reportedly been    held in several secret ""filtration camps"", where torture  and ill-treatment have been routine and systematic.
