"Indonesia: Fear for safety / possible ""disappearance"" / fear of torture, ill-treatment or unlawful killing: M Husin 
 Indonesia: Fear for safety / possible ""disappearance"" / fear of torture, ill-treatment or unlawful killing: M Husin | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International fears for the safety of M Husin who is missing apparently after being arrested by the PPRM    (Petugas Penindak Rusuh Massa), a riot control police     unit, on 1 August 1999 in the province of Aceh, northern  Sumatra. Those detained by the PPRM are at risk of        torture, ill-treatment, ""disappearance"" or unlawful       killing.
