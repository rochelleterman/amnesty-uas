"Further information on UA 281/94 (AMR 36/30/94, 20 July) - Haiti: ""disappearance"" / extrajudicial execution / fear for safety: Moise Paul, Emile Georges 
 Further information on UA 281/94 (AMR 36/30/94, 20 July) - Haiti: ""disappearance"" / extrajudicial execution / fear for safety: Moise Paul, Emile Georges | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Moise Paul, Coordinator of the Union of Young Patriots of Drouillard, has been released. He had been detained on 16 July 1994 in Cite Soleil and was reportedly ill-treated by attaches in detention. Emile Georges remains ""disappeared"". It is believed that he may also hae been detained in Cite Soleil on 18 July 1994 by the security forces."
