"Cuba: Arrest of human rights defender Nestor Rodriguez Lobaina 
 Cuba: Arrest of human rights defender Nestor Rodriguez Lobaina | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is calling for the immediate and unconditional release of Nestor Rodriguez Lobaina, President of the Cuban Youth Movement for Democracy, who was arrested on 7 December 1998 after trying to organize a protest against the refusal to grant him permission to leave Cuba in order to attend the Paris Conference marking the 50th anniversary of the Universal Declaration of Human Rights.
