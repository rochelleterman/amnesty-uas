"Burundi: Fear of torture and ill-treatment: Domitien Ndayizeye 
 NA 
 Domitien Ndayizeye, the Executive Secretary of the Front pour la Democratie au Burundi (FRODEBU), Front for Democracy in Burundi, was arrested without a warrant at his home on 22 February 1997 by members of the Public Security Police (PSP). He is currently detained at Kigobe PSP detention centre in Bujumbura, and there is concern he may be subjected to torture and ill-treatment.
