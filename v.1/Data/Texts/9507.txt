"EXTRA 61/94 - USA (Nebraska): Robert Williams 
 EXTRA 61/94 - USA (Nebraska): Robert Williams | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Robert Williams, black, is scheduled to be executed in Nebraska on 16 November 1994. He was sentenced to death in 1978 for the rape and murder of a white woman and the murder of another white woman. His sentence for the murder of Patricia McGarry was overturned but he is still scheduled to be executed for the murder of Catherine Brooks."
