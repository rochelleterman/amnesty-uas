"Guatemala: Further information on death penalty 
 Guatemala: Further information on death penalty | Amnesty International 
 Pedro Castillo Mendoza, Roberto Giron: On 13 September 1996, the above were legally executed by firing squad for the rape and murder of a four-year-old. The execution was shown on national television.
