 Colombia: Environmentalist feared disappeared: Sandra Viviana Cuellar Gallego | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 On 17 February Colombian environmentalist Sandra Viviana Cuellar Gallego may have been subjected to enforced disappearance in Cali, western Colombia, on her way to give a lecture at the National University in nearby Palmira. Her identity card, mobile phone and other personal belongings have been found, but her whereabouts remain unknown. "
