 Tunisia: Ex-prisoners detained incommunicado  | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Former prisoners Ayman Gharib and Anis el-Krifi were arrested early in the morning of 19 July, at their homes in the town of Menzel Bourguiba, in the north of Tunisia. Their families have not heard from them and do not know where they are or why they were arrested."
