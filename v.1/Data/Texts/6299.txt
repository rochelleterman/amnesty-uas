 Guatemala: Fear for safety | Amnesty International 
 Security guards have evicted protesting workers from Finca Nueva Linda, a plantation in southwest Guatemala. Amnesty International fears the evicted people may be in grave danger, as the security guards are reportedly searching the area for community leaders.
