 Indonesia: Further information on Fear for safety/ Intimidation, human rights, Alberth Rumbekwan | Amnesty International 
 On 11 October two men forcibly entered the Jakarta office of the Institute for Human Rights Study and Advocacy      (ELS-HAM). It is believed that they were looking for      Alberth Rumbekwan, the head of ELS-HAM's Jakarta office.
