"Indonesia: Fear for safety/Fear of torture or ill-treatment 
 Indonesia: Fear for safety/Fear of torture or ill-treatment | Amnesty International 
 Idawati binti Hanafiah and M. Jafar Ibrahim were arrested on 26 June. Both are now believed to be held at Pidie police station (Police Resort, Polres). The police are reportedly refusing to let their relatives see them, and Amnesty International is concerned that they are at serious risk of torture or ill-treatment.
