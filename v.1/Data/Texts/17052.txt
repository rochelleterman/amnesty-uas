 Iran: Fear for safety | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is concerned for the safety of      Heshmatollah Tabarzadi, editor-in-chief of the weekly     newsletter Hoveyat-e-Khish (Our Identity), and Hossein    Kashani, its director, who have been arrested after       questioning about publishing information contrary to      ""public order and the public interest"".
