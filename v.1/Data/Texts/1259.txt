"Lesotho: Fear for safety: Candi Ramainoane 
 Lesotho: Fear for safety: Candi Ramainoane | Amnesty International 
 There is concern for the safety of Candi Ramainoane, the editor-in-chief of the Sesotho language newspaper MoAfrika, after a series of suspicious incidents at his home and office, following criticisms of his journalism by members of the government. He has previously been threatened for his negative reporting of the government.
