"Further information on UA 179/93 (ASA 41/04/93, 3 June) - Viet Nam: death penalty: Wong Chi Shing 
 Further information on UA 179/93 (ASA 41/04/93, 3 June) - Viet Nam: death penalty: Wong Chi Shing | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Wong Chi Shing may soon be executed following President Le Duc Anh's recent rejection of his appeal. Wong Chi Shing is a Hong Kong resident with a British passport who was convicted of smuggling heroin and sentenced to death on 28 May 1993 by the People's Supreme Court in Ho Chi Minh City."
