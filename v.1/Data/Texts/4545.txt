"Colombia Fear for Safety Viaina Maria Villamil - wife of Julio Galeano and their three children 
 Colombia  Fear for Safety Viaina  Maria Villamil  - wife  of  Julio Galeano and their three children | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is seriously concerned for the safety of Viaina Maria Villamil, her three children and members of the Sindicato de Trabajadores de las Empresas Municipales de Cali (SINTRAEMCALI), Cali Trade Union of Municipal Service Workers, following the murder of Julio Galeano in Cali, Valle del Cauca Department.
