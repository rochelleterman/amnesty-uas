 Nepal: Fear for safety/ possible ""disappearance"" | Amnesty International 
 Deepak Thakuri and Yag Prasad Dulal were reportedly arrested by the security forces on 27 January and 6 February respectively. Their whereabouts are now unknown. Efforts to locate them, including informing the National Human Rights Commission (NHRC), have so far failed, and Amnesty International fears they may have ""disappeared"".
