 Syria: Fear of torture or ill-treatment: Mahmoud al-Najjar (m) | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Teacher Mahmoud al-Najjar was arrested on 16 June 2008 at his house in Aleppo city by State Security agents who also confiscated political publications. His whereabouts are unknown, and he is in grave danger of torture."
