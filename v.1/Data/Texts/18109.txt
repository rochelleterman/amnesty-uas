 Lebanon: Fear of torture/Incommunicado detention/Possible prisoner of conscience, Hassan Ahmad al-Nahas | Amnesty International 
 Hassan Ahmad Al-Nahas, married and father of 10 children, has been held at an interrogation centre of the Internal  Security in Saida since 9 December. It is feared that he  might be at risk of torture or ill-treatment.
