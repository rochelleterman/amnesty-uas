"Tanzania: Further information on Fear of Torture 
 Tanzania: Further information on Fear of Torture | Amnesty International 
 Most of those charged with the murder of a police officer during a demonstration on Pemba Island have been released. However, prisoners of conscience Juma Duni Haji and Machano Khamis Ali remain in detention, despite Zanzibar's Attorney General having ordered their release.
