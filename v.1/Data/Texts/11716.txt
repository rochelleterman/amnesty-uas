"Indonesia: Fear of ill-treatment / possible prisoner of conscience: Oka Dwi Chandra 
 Indonesia: Fear of ill-treatment / possible prisoner of conscience: Oka Dwi Chandra | Amnesty International 
 The above, a 23-year-old student activist who was taken into police custody during a demonstration in support of Megawati Sukarnoputri, the leader of Indonesia's Democratic Party, has been transferred to miliitary detention. AI fears that this move places him at greater risk of torture or ill-treatment and the fact that lawyers attempting to act on his behalf have been denied access to him heightens this fear.
