"Sudan: Further information on incommunicado detention/fear of torture or other ill-treatment/medical concern/possible prisoners of conscience 
 Sudan: Further information on incommunicado detention/fear of torture or other ill-treatment/medical concern/possible prisoners of conscience | Amnesty International 
 Eight men, (Nazmi Mohammed Hamed, Nayef Mohammed Hamed,   Al-Khatib Mohammed Salem, Maysara Izzeddin Mohammed       Mansur, Farouq Nouri, Hisham Abbas, Da'ud Suleima and     Isam Mohammed Faqir) were released on 13 September 2007.  It is not yet known how they were treated in custody.     They had been arrested on 28 August 2007, apparently as a result of their opposition to the construction of the     Kajbar Dam.
