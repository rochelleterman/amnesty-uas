 Iran: Further information: Opposition leaders continue to be held | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Iranian opposition leaders Mehdi Karroubi, Mir Hossein Mousavi and Zahra Rahnavard remain under house arrest without an arrest warrant, charge or trial. All three continue to have limited access to family members and no legal representation. Recently, Mehdi Karroubi was allowed to visit his family for only one hour."
