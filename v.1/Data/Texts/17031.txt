 Iran: Fear of imminent execution, Abbas Hosseini | Amnesty International 
 Afghan refugee Abbas Hosseini is reportedly scheduled for execution on 1 May for a murder committed when he was 17. Iran is a state party to international treaties that expressly prohibit the use of the death penalty for crimes committed below the age of 18.
