 Trinidad and Tobago: ill-treatment / fear of ill-treatment: Dr Robert Naidike | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 The whereabouts of Dr Robert Naidike, a Nigerian national, detained on 28 November 1995, are unknown. This, coupled with allegations that he has already been subjected to beatings, have raised fears that he may be facing further ill-treatment in custody."
