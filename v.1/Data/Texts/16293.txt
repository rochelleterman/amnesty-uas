"Uzbekistan: Further information on Fear of torture or ill-treatment/Prisoner of conscience/Incommunicado detention 
 Uzbekistan: Further information on Fear of torture or ill-treatment/Prisoner of conscience/Incommunicado detention | Amnesty International 
 Prisoner of conscience and journalism student, Erkin Yakubzhanov, was released on 29 July, by Uzbekistani border guards. He reportedly does not face any criminal charges and has been sent back to his village in Kyrgyzstan.
