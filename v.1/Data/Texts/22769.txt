 Syrian brothers held by intelligence agency: Further information | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Syrian brothers ‘Abd al-Rahman Hammada and Wa’el Hammada, who have been detained since 30 April and 12 May 2011 respectively, are now believed to be held incommunicado at one of the three Air Force Security branches in the capital Damascus. Reports suggest that they have been tortured or otherwise ill-treated. "
