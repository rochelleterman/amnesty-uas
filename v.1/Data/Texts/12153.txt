"Laos: Further information on health concern / prisoners of conscience: 
 Laos: Further information on health concern / prisoners of conscience: | Amnesty International 
 Thongsouk Saysangkhi, Latsami Khamphoui and Feng Sakchittaphong: AI has learned of the sad news of the death, in prison, of Thongsouk Saysangkhi in mid-February 1998.  He had suffered months of ill-health.His death heightens concerns regarding the chances of survival of Latsami Khamphoui and Feng Sakchittaphong, both of whom are seriously ill.
