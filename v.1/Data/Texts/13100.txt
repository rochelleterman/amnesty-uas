 Philippines: Imminent Execution, Rolando Pagdayawon | Amnesty International 
 Rolando Pagdayawon is scheduled to be executed on 30      August. He was convicted of the rape of his common-law    wife's nine-year-old daughter. His is the third execution date to be set in the Philippines in the last month.
