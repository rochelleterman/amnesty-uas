 USA: Further information: Stay of execution for Cuban national | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Manuel Valle, a Cuban national who was due to be executed in Florida on 2 August 2011, has received a stay of execution until 1 September. He was convicted of the murder of a police officer in 1978. He was 27 years old when first sent to death row. He is now 61. "
