"Bangladesh: Fear for safety/Death Threats 
 Bangladesh: Fear for safety/Death Threats | Amnesty International 
 On 24 August, deaths threats were made against Hasan Azizul Huq, a retired Professor from Rajshahi University. Amnesty International is concerned for his safety and that of his family, who are at risk of further threats and harassment following his criticism of religious based politics in Bangladesh.
