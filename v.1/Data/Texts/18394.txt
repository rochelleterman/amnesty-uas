 Saudi Arabia: Further information on fear of imminent execution | Amnesty International 
 According to the Saudi Arabian Human Rights Commission, the death sentence against Suliamon Olyfemi has been upheld by the Court of Cassation and ratified by the Supreme Judicial Council. Amnesty International fears that Suliamon Olyfemi could be executed within days.
