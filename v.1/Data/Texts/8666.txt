 EXTRA 24/93 - USA (Virginia): death penalty: Syvasky Poyner | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Syvasky Poyner, black, aged 36, is scheduled to be executed by the state of Virginia on 18 March 1993. He was sentenced to death in 1984 for the murders of four white women and one black woman. "
