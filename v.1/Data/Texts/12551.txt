 Nepal: Possible ""disappearance"" / Fear for safety, Dilli  Ram Apagain

 | Amnesty International 
 At around midnight on 27 February, Dilli Ram Apagain was taken from his home in the town of Biratnagar, in southeastern Nepal, by 15 people dressed in civilian clothes, who identified themselves as security personnel. His whereabouts are unknown and there are concerns for his safety.
