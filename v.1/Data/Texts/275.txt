 Burundi: Further information on - Disappearance / Fear of torture /         Incommunicado detention

 | Amnesty International 
 Paul Nsanzurwimo may need urgent medical treatment. Whilst in custody he was beaten on the head and face whilst wearing glasses and some glass is feared to have entered his eye. He has not yet received any medical attention. Amnesty International fears that unless he receives medical treatment, his condition could worsen.
