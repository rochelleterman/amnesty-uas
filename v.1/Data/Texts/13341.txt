"UA 256/94 - Singapore: death penalty: Jasbir Singh, Charanjit Singh 
 UA 256/94 - Singapore: death penalty: Jasbir Singh, Charanjit Singh | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 On 14 February 1994 the Court of Criminal Appeal rejected the appeal of Jasbir Singh and Charanjit Singh against their sentence of death for drug-trafficking. They had both been tried and sentenced on 5 January 1993."
