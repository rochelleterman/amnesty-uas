 Rwanda/Burundi: Fear of refoulement / fear for safety | Amnesty International 
 More than 300 Burundi refugees are at imminent risk of being forcibly returned to Burundi from Rwanda. Should they return to Burundi they could be at risk of serious human rights abuse.
