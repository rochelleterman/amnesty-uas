"USA (North Carolina): Death penalty / legal concern: Dorthia Bynum (f) 
 USA (North Carolina): Death penalty / legal concern: Dorthia Bynum (f) | Amnesty International 
 The prosecution intends to seek a death sentence against  Dorthia Bynum for a crime she is accused of committing    when she was 17 years and three months old, at a          forthcoming trial in Alamance County Superior Court, in   Graham, North Carolina.  International law forbids the    use of the death penalty for crimes committed by          under-18-year-olds.
