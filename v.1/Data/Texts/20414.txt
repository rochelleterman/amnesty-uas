 Mexico: Further information: Staff and migrants at a shelter threatened | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 On 7 December the Belén Migrants shelter (Belén, Posada del Migrante) in Saltillo, Coahuila state, was broken into. This follows a series of acts of intimidation which have escalated in the past months. The migrants staying at the shelter and the staff working there are in danger."
