 Equatorial Guinea: Detention with charge or trial/ Harsh detention conditions: Brígida Asongsua Elo  | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Brígida Asongsua Elo was arrested on 16 December 2007 at Malabo Central Police Station, where she continues to be held without charge or trial, in harsh conditions which amount to degrading and cruel treatment. She is being held in a common cell together with 70 to 100 male detainees. "
