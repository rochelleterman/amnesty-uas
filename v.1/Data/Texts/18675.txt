"Syria: Incommunicado detention/fear of torture and ill-treatment 
 Syria: Incommunicado detention/fear of torture and ill-treatment | Amnesty International 
 Hasan Mustafa, a Syrian Kurd, is reportedly being held incommunicado at an unknown location by the Syrian authorities. He is at risk of torture or ill-treatment. His arrest came two months after his brother, Khalil Mustafa, was reportedly tortured to death in detention.
