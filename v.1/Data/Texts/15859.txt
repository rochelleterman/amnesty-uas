"Belarus: Fear of execution / death penalty 
 Belarus: Fear of execution / death penalty | Amnesty International 
 Igor Ganya and F Verega: There is grave concern that Igor Ganya and F Verega, both citizens of Moldava, may face execution if their appeals are not upheld by the Supreme Court or President Lukashenka. They were sentenced to death on 30 June 1997 for murder.
