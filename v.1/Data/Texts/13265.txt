"Philippines: Further information on imminent execution: Romeo Gallo 
 Philippines: Further information on imminent execution: Romeo Gallo | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International has just learned that the Supreme   Court has commuted Romeo Gallo's death sentence to life   imprisonment.  His second stay of execution, announced by President Estrada on 27 September, was due to expire on   23 October 1999.
