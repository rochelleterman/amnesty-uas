 Iran: Incommunicado detention/fear of torture or other ill-treatment: Jamila Nabgan (f) | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Jamila Nabgan, a member of the Arab minority in Iran was arrested at her home on 19 October 2008 by officers from the Ministry of Intelligence. Her whereabouts are unknown and she is at risk of torture and other ill-treatment. "
