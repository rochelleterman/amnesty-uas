"Tunisia: Prisoner of conscience / fear of ill-treatment: Abdel Moumen Belanes 
 Tunisia: Prisoner of conscience / fear of ill-treatment: Abdel Moumen Belanes | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is concerned at reports that prisoner of conscience Abdel Moumen Belanes has been denied visits by his lawyers since 26 April and by his family since 29 April 1997. There are fears that he may be being subjected to ill-treatment or torture in prison.
