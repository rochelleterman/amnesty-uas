"Syria: Further information on: Prisoners of conscience/incommunicado detention/fear of torture 
 Syria: Further information on: Prisoners of conscience/incommunicado detention/fear of torture | Amnesty International 
 The eight directors, named above, of the unauthorised Jamal al-Atassi Forum were released without charge on 31 May, after one week in detention. They were not ill-treated, according to newspapers quoting Hussein al-'Awadat and Suhayr Jamal al-Atassi. 'Ali al-'Abdullah is apparently still detained incommunicado in Adra Prison, and he is at serious risk of torture. He has spent 19 days in detention.
