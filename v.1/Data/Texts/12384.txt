"Republic of Maldives: Further information on ""Disappearance"" / Prisoner of Conscience, Abdulla Shakir 
 Republic of Maldives: Further information on  ""Disappearance"" / Prisoner of Conscience, Abdulla Shakir | Amnesty International 
 Member of parliament Abdulla Shakir, who was arrested on 18 July, is now known to have been held for several days at Male' police station on ""unspecified civil grounds"". He was eventually released unconditionally.
