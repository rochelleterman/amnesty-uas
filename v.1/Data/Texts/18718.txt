 Syria: Further information on fear of torture or ill-treatment: Sheikh Murad al-Khiznawi (m) | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International has received information that the Syrian Kurdish activist, Sheikh Murad al-Khiznawi, has been released. He was briefly detained by the Syrian authorities on Syria's border with Jordan as he attempted to flee the country.
