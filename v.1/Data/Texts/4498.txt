"Colombia: Possible extrajudicial execution / fear for safety: Ermilda Araque (f) 
 Colombia: Possible extrajudicial execution / fear for safety: Ermilda Araque (f) | Amnesty International 
 There is grave concern for the safety of Ermilda Araque, a community activist in the municipality of Salgar, department of Antioquia, who has been subject to harassment, and whose name reportedly appears on a paramilitary ""death list"". Fears for her safety have been heightened by the killing by unidentified gunmen of Diego Marquez Zapata, a community leader in Salgar, on 18 March 1997.
