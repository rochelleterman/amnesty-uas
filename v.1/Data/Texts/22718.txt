 Syria: Syrian blogger held incommunicado: Ahmad Mohammad Hudaifa  | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Ahmad Mohammad Hudaifa (pen name Ahmad Abu al-Khair), a male Syrian blogger, is currently held incommunicado at the Palestine Branch, a Military Security interrogation and detention centre in Damascus, notorious for torture. Amnesty International believes that he may be a prisoner of conscience, detained solely for the peaceful exercise of his right to freedom of expression. He is at risk of torture or other ill-treatment. "
