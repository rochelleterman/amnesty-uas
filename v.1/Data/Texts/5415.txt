 Cuba: Further information on fear of death penalty / fear of ill-treatment: Ramon Fidel Basulto Garcia | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 The trial of Ramon Fidel Basulto Garcia, accused of murder and piracy, is due to take place on 21 April 1995. The prosecution is requesting that he be sentenced to death and to 30 years' imprisonment for piracy."
