"Russian Federation (Chechen Republic): Fear for safety / Fear of torture / Prisoner of conscience, Dik Altemirov, aged 67. 
 Russian Federation (Chechen Republic): Fear for safety /  Fear of  torture / Prisoner of conscience, Dik Altemirov, aged 67. | Amnesty International 
 Dik Altemirov, a respected Chechen human rights activist and former government minister and vice president, has been detained and is believed to be held incommunicado at a secret detention facility, where he is at grave risk of torture and ill-treatment. Amnesty International considers him a prisoner of conscience.
