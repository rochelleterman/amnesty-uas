"UA 178/94 - Socialist Republic of Viet Nam: death penalty: Vu Van Vuong 
 UA 178/94 - Socialist Republic of Viet Nam: death penalty: Vu Van Vuong | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 On 26 April 1994 the Quang Ninh People's Court sentenced Vu Van Vuong to death for the destruction of high-voltage power line supports, a criminal offence under Article 94 of the Criminal Procedure Code."
