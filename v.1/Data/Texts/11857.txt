"Indonesia: Fear for safety / incommunicado detention 
 Indonesia: Fear for safety / incommunicado detention | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is concerned for the safety of      Syaiful Bahri (alias Apa Dom) and Jalil bin Meutini, who  are being detained by the Indonesian National Army. They  have not had access to independent legal counsel or to    thier families and there are grave fears that they are at risk of torture, ill-treatment or unlawful killing.
