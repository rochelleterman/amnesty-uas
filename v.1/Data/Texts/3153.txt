"Uganda: Death penalty: Joel Lubangakene (m), soldier 
 Uganda: Death penalty: Joel Lubangakene (m), soldier

 | Amnesty International 
 Private Joel Lubangakene was sentenced to death by hanging by a military court on 2 January, for reportedly shooting dead an 18-year-old student on 26 December in northern Uganda. Some reports indicate that Lubangakene could be executed on 8 January.
