 Sudan: Protestors at risk of torture in Sudan | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 On 30 January 2911, protests spread across North Africa to Sudan's capital Khartoum. Following a crackdown by the security service and police, more than 70 people were arrested, with 20 still held in detention. They are at risk of torture or other ill-treatment. "
