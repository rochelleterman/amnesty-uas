"Tunisia: Prisoner of conscience / health concern, Zouheir Yahiaoui 
 Tunisia: Prisoner of conscience / health concern, Zouheir Yahiaoui | Amnesty International 
 Prisoner of conscience Zouheir Yahiaoui has been on hunger strike since 15 May in protest at his continued detention. After 42 days of hunger strike there is a serious risk to his health, and possibly to his life. Amnesty International is calling for his immediate and unconditional release.
