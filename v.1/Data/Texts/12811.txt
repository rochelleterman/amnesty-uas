 Nepal: Further Information on: Incommunicado detention / fear of ""disappearance""  / Fear of torture

 | Amnesty International 
 Raju Sharma Dhakal was reportedly released from prison on 3 November, but was immediately re-arrested by security forces at the prison gates. His whereabouts are currently unknown and he stands at very high risk of torture or ""disappearance"".
