"Bahamas: further information on: death penalty 
 Bahamas: further information on: death penalty | Amnesty International 
 Thomas Reckley; new names: Dwayne McKinney, Cyril Darville: the above are due to be hanged on 13 March 1996.  If the hangings are carried out they will be the first to take place in the Bahamas for 12 years.
