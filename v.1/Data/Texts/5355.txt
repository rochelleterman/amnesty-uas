 Colombia: Further information on Fear for Safety, members of the Sindicato de Trabajadores de las Empresas Municipales de  Cali (SINTRAEMCALI). | Amnesty International 
 Serious concerns remain for the safety of the members of  Sindicato de Trabajadores de las Empresas Municipales de  Cali (SINTRAEMCALI), the Trade Union of Municipal Service Workers of Cali, following a bomb attack on one of the    trade union's meeting places in the city of Cali in Valle del Cauca Department.
