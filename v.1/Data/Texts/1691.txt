 Nigeria: Further information on fear of ill-treatment / legal concern and new concerns: prisoners of conscience / health concern | Amnesty International 
 Mohammed Adamu, Soji Omotunde, Adetokunbo Fakeye, Onome Osifo-Whiskey, Babafemi Ojudu, Rafiu Salau. Released: Ben Adaji, Jenkins Alumona and Akinwumi Adesokan: Six of the journalists named above remain in incommunicado detention without charge or trial since their arrests in 1997. AI is concerned by their conditions of detention. Three journalists have been released. Ben Adaji was charged and released on bail.
