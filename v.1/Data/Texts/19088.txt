 Further information on UA 277/93 (MDE 30/08/93, 18 August) - Tunisia: fear of torture: Samir Moussa, Mohamed Tahar Badi | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Mohamed Tahar Badi was released without charge on 25 August after 29 days in incommunicado detention. Samir Moussa continues to be held in illegally prolonged incommunicado detention since 25 July, despite repeated attempts by his family to determine his place of detention. "
