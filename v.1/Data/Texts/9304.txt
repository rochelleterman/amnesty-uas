"EXTRA 67/93 - USA (Texas): death penalty: Danny Ray Harris 
 EXTRA 67/93 - USA (Texas): death penalty: Danny Ray Harris | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Danny Harris is scheduled to be executed on 30 July 1993. He was convicted along with three others, including his younger brother, Curtis Harris. Curtis Harris, who was only 17 years old at the time of the murder, was executed on 1 July 1993. Danny Harris, black, was first sentenced to death in February 1980. His first death sentence was overturned on appeal, but he was resentenced to death at a second trial in July 1984. Danny Harris was 18 years old at the time of the crime. "
