 USA (Texas): Further information on Death penalty, Windell Broussard, black,     aged 41 | Amnesty International 
 Windell Broussard was executed in Texas by lethal injection on 30 January. He had been sentenced to death in 1993 for the murder of his ex-wife, Dianna Fay Harris Broussard, and her 10-year-old son, Corey Harris.
