 Syria: Further information: Syrian man released: Wa’el Hammada | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Wa’el Hammada, a Syrian man who had been held incommunicado detention and reportedly subjected to torture and other ill-treatment, was released on bail on 1 August. He and his brother ‘Abd al-Rahman Hammada have been charged and are currently awaiting trial."
