"Nigeria: Fear of torture/Incommunicado detention, Buba Galadima 
 Nigeria: Fear of torture/Incommunicado detention, Buba Galadima | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is concerned for the safety of Buba Galadima, who is a member of the Conference of Nigerian Political Parties (CNPP) and chairman of the mobilisation committee of the CNPP. He was arrested at his office on the evening of 29 April, by intelligence officers from the State Security Services in the capital Abuja. He is being held incommunicado detention without charge and is at risk of torture or ill-treatment.
