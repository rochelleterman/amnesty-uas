 Spain: Further information: Man returned to Morocco from Spain: Ali Aarrass | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Ali Aarrass, a dual Belgian-Moroccan national, was extradited from Spain to Morocco on 15 December. Neither Ali Aarrass's lawyers nor his relatives were informed by the authorities of his extradition and only learnt about it through the media. They do not know where in Morocco he was sent to."
