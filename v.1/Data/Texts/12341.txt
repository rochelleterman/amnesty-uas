"Malaysia: Fear of forcible return/Fear of ill-treatment 
 Malaysia: Fear of forcible return/Fear of ill-treatment | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is concerned for the safety of 239 asylum seekers from Myanmar and Indonesia arrested on 19 August by police in the capital Kuala Lumpur. They are at risk of being forcibly returned to the Indonesian province of Nanggroe Aceh Darussalam (NAD), where they would be at serious risk of human rights violations.
