 Singapore: Further information on Possible imminent execution / Unfair trial, Vignes s/o Mourthi (m) aged 23, Malaysian national | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International was saddened to learn that Vignes s/o Mourthi and Moorthy A/L Angappan were hanged at 6am on 26 September at Changi Prison. Both men had been sentenced to death in August 2002 for drug trafficking.
