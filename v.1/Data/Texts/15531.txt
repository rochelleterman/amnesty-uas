 Russian Federation: Fear for safety/death threats

 | Amnesty International 
 Leaflets inciting violence against human rights defender Boris Maksovich Kreindl, his daughter Elena aged 16 and other mebers of his family have been distributed in the city of Tomsk, including at the building where he has his office. Those responsible are believed to be people who object to his campaigning on behalf of Roma.
