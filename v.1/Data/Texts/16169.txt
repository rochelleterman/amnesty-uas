"Turkmenistan: Fear of imminent execution 
 Turkmenistan: Fear of imminent execution | Amnesty International 
 Irina Maisuradze, Gulshirin Shykhyeva and Tylla Garadzhayeva are at risk of imminent execution. They were sentenced to daeth on drugs charges in September 1998. Their last hope lies with a petition for clemency to the President of Turkmenistan.
