 Bahamas: Death penalty | Amnesty International 
 The Bahamian government has scheduled John Higgs and      David Mitchell to be hanged on 10 August 1999. Their      executions have been scheduled despite the fact that the  Inter-American Commission on Human Rights (IACHR) is      still considering petitions filed by both men.
