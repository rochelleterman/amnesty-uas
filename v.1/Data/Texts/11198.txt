 India: Fear for safety / fear of torture and ill-treatment / medical concern | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is concerned for the safety of Abdul Sattar, Sita Ram and Satya Narain, three social workers who have been tortured while illegally detained in police custody. None are thought to have received medical attention for injuries sustained.
