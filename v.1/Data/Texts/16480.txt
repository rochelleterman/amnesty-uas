"Federal Republic of Yugoslavia: Death after custody / fear of ill-treatment: Feriz Blakcori 
 Federal Republic of Yugoslavia: Death after custody / fear of ill-treatment: Feriz Blakcori | Amnesty International 
 On 10 December 1996, Feriz Blakcori, an ethnic Albanian primary school teacher and political activist from Kosovo province, died in Pristina hospital, reportedly after being arrested and ill-treated by police.
