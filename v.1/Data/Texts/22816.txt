 Syria: Further information: Doctors held incommunicado in Syria | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Syrian doctor Mohamad Osama Abdulsalam Al-Baroudi has been held incommunicado since he was arrested from his Damascus clinic on 18 February. He is believed to have been tortured and there are grave fears for his health. Fellow doctor Mahmoud Al Refaai, arrested on 16 February, also continues to be held incommunicado."
