"Rwanda: Forcible return / Fear for safety 
 Rwanda: Forcible return / Fear for safety | Amnesty International 
 Rwandese nationals Bertin Murera and Benjamin Rutabana    were forcibly returned from Tanzania to Rwanda on 5       February 2000, and their whereabouts are now unknown.     There are unconfirmed reports that they are in military   custody, where they could be at risk of torture or death.
