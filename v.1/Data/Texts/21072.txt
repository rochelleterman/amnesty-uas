 Indonesia: Imminent Execution | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Jurit bin Abdullah and Ona Denis are in imminent danger of execution. The Deputy Attorney for General Crimes told the Indonesian news agency Antara on 5 December that the executions would be carried out before the end of the year. According to Antara, both men have exhausted all the legal avenues to avoid execution, including an appeal for presidential clemency.  However, the president still has the power to commute their sentences"
