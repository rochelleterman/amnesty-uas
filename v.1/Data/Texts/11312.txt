"India: Possible forcible return of asylum-seekers 
 India: Possible forcible return of asylum-seekers | Amnesty International 
 Scores of ethnic Chin are reported to have been forcibly  returned to Myanmar from the northeastern Indian state of Mizoram, and handed over to the Myanmar armed forces.     Amnesty International is concerned that many of those who have been detained may have well-founded fears of         persecution and possible torture in Myanmar.
