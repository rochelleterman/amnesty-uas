 Pakistan: Further information: Abducted students found in detention | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Pakistani brothers Abdullah and Ibrahim Mohamed El-Sharkawi have now been found in detention, after allegedly being abducted by Pakistani intelligence agencies in Pakistan on 25 and 29 May 2011 respectively. Ibrahim was released on bail on 27 June; Abdullah was located on 23 July. "
