 Libya: Further information: Woman detained after rape claims is released: Eman al-Obaidi | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 A Libyan woman, Eman al-Obaidi, who was detained after alleging that she was raped by members of Colonel Mu’ammar al-Gaddafi’s forces, has been released. She is still concerned for her own safety, however, and has said that she has been prevented from leaving Tripoli."
