"Syria: Fear of Torture/Legal Concern, Sulayman Hissu 
 Syria: Fear of Torture/Legal Concern, Sulayman Hissu | Amnesty International 
 Sulayman Hissu is being held in incommunicado detention after he was arrested at Damascus airport by Syrian security forces. There are fears that he may be tortured or ill-treated while he is held without access to his family or a lawyer.
