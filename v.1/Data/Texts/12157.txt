 Laos: Fear of Torture - one person, name unknown,arrested 
 | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International fears for the safety of one person  reportedly arrested today in the Laotian capital,         Vientiane, on suspicion of involvement in a bomb attack   on a bus on 6 June 2000, in which two people died.        Amnesty International has reliable reports of widespread  torture  and ill-treatment in police custody and prisons  in Laos   and believes this detainee is at serious risk.
