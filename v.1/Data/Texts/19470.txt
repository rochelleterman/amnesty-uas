 Equatorial Guinea: Prominent human rights defender missing: Fabián Nsue Nguema | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Lawyer and human rights defender Fabián Nsue Nguema has not been seen or heard from since 22 October, when he visited a client in Black Beach Prison in the Equatorial Guinea capital of Malabo. There are fears for his safety."
