"Egypt: Death penalty: Nadi Abu-Zeid Mohammad 
 Egypt: Death penalty: Nadi Abu-Zeid Mohammad | Amnesty International 
 Nadi Abu-Zeid Mohammad,  30-year-old farmer, was sentenced to death by the Fayoum Criminal Court on 12 June 1997 after being found guilty of murder. His sentence has been passed on for approval by the Mufti.
