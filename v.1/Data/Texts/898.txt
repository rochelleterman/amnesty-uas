 Somaliland / Ethiopia: Fear of torture / Incommunicado detention / forcible return | Amnesty International 
 According to reports the Somaliland authorities forcibly returned five Ethiopian men (Mohamed Sheikh Abdi Sheikh   Mahamud, Mohamed Adan Omar, Jowhar Sheikh Bashir, Khadar  Abdi Tarsan and Mohamed Abdi Aar) to the Ethiopian        security forces on 13 October 2007. They had been         arrested allegedly for political reasons at the request   of the Ethiopian authorities in the Somaliland capital,   Hargeisa. The men are believed to be held in military     custody in Jijiga, the capital of the Ogaden region.
