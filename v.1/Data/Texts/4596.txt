"UA 124/93 - Colombia: fear of ""disappearance"": Delio Vargas 
 UA 124/93 - Colombia: fear of ""disappearance"": Delio Vargas | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Delio Vargas, president of the regional office of ASCODAS (Colombian Association for Social Assistance), which works with displaced people from Meta department, has not been seen since he was abducted from a street in Villavicencio on 19 April 1993 by armed men. Delio Vargas is a member of the opposition party Union Patriotica and has been involved in the organization of a forum entitled ""Peaceful Alternatives in Meta Department"", due to take place on 23 April.
