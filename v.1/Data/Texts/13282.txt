"Singapore: Death Penalty - Julaiha Begum 
 Singapore: Death Penalty -  Julaiha Begum | Amnesty International 
 Julaiha Begum (f), aged 52, an ethnic Indian born in Singapore, was sentenced to death in March 2000 for the murder of her husband. She faces imminent execution after her appeal for presidential clemency was denied.
