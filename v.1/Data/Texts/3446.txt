"Democratic Republic of Congo: Further information on Arbitrary/fear of torture: N'sii Luanda Shandwe 
 Democratic Republic of Congo: Further information on Arbitrary/fear of  torture: N'sii Luanda Shandwe | Amnesty International 
 Human rights defender N'sii Luanda Shandwe continues to be detained without charge at Kinshasa's central prison, along with at least 50 other people, including Anne-Marie Kamwanya Masumbuko, who are apparently detained in connection with the assassination of President Laurent-D_sir_ Kabila on 16 January 2001.
