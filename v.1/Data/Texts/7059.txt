"Honduras: Fear for safety / death threats: Dora Emperatriz Oliva Guifarro 
 Honduras: Fear for safety / death threats: Dora Emperatriz Oliva Guifarro | Amnesty International 
 There is concern for the safety of 33-year-old human rights defender, Dora Emperatriz Oliva Guifarro. On 5     July 1999 she was abducted by gunpoint and detained for   two hours by two men, reportedly paramilitaries, who      threatened to kill her. Dora Oliva works for the Comite   de Familiares de Detenidos-Desaparecidos en Honduras      (COFADEH).
