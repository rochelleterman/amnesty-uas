 Israel: Further information on torture and ill-treatment / judicial authorization of torture and ill-treatment | Amnesty International 
 Suspected members of Islamist groups; Muhammad 'Abd al-'Aziz Hamdan, student; and new name: Khader Mubarak, teacher, aged 32: On 17 November 1996 the Israeli Supreme Court rejected the petition of detainee Khader Mubarak to bar the General Security Service from using ""physical pressure"" against him. He is a Palestinian suspected member of Hamas who has been accused of having information about violent attacks against Israeli targets.
