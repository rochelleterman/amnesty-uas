 Tajikistan: Further information: Journalist fined and banned from journalism | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 On 14 October 2011, a court in the northern city of Khujand in the Soghd region, Tajikistan, found journalist, Makhmadyusuf Ismoilov, guilty of the charges brought against him of libel, insult and inciting hatred. He will be appealing against the verdict."
