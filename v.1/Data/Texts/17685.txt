"Palestinian Authority: Further information on prisoner of conscience / legal concern: Daoud Kuttab 
 Palestinian Authority: Further information on prisoner of conscience / legal concern: Daoud Kuttab | Amnesty International 
 Daoud Kuttab, a well-known Palestinian journalist and broadcaster, was released on 27 May 1997. During his week in detention in Ramallah Police Station he had not been interrogated or charged with any offence.
