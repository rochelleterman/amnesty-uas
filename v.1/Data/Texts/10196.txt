"UA 308/94 - Venezuela: ill-treatment / torture: Jose Manuel Flores, Jose Gregorio Guedez, Ruben Sanchez, Jose Luis Sanchez 
 UA 308/94 - Venezuela: ill-treatment / torture: Jose Manuel Flores, Jose Gregorio Guedez, Ruben Sanchez, Jose Luis Sanchez | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 The four men named above are being held incommunicado by military intelligence in Caracas. They were arrested by the state police on 17 August 1994 in Guacara, Carbobo state. They were reportedly beaten at the time of arrest. They have been accused of transporting weapons and belonging to Bandera Roja, an armed opposition group. AI fears that they may be torture in incommunicado detention."
