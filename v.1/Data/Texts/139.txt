"Burundi: Further information on fear of torture and ill-treatment: Domitien Ndayizeye 
 NA 
 Domitien Ndayizeye, Executive Secretary of the Front for Democracy in Burundi (FRODEBU), was released without charge on 27 February. He was detained for questioning at the Kigobe PSP detention centre where he was tortured by members of the Documentation nationale, intelligence unit.
