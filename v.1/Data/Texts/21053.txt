 Indonesia: Further information on fear of imminent execution: Achmad Suradji (m)  | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 According to Indonesian media reports, prosecutors are planning to execute five death row inmates in July 2008, and the first of these is Achmad Suradji. He was sentenced to death on 27 April 1998 for the murder of 42 women and girls found buried in a field next to his house in the village of Semayang, North Sumatra province."
