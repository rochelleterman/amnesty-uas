"United States of America (Georgia): Death penalty/Legal concern, Curtis Malcolm Woodyard 
 United States of America (Georgia): Death penalty/Legal concern, Curtis Malcolm Woodyard | Amnesty International 
 Curtis Woodyard is facing trial in Augusta, Georgia, at   which the Richmond County prosecution intends to seek a   death sentence. Curtis Woodyard was 17 at the time of the crime with which he has been charged. International law,  respected in almost every country in the world apart from the USA, prohibits the use of the death penalty against   people who were under 18 years old at the time of the     crime.
