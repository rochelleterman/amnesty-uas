 EXTRA 29/93 - Zimbabwe: death penalty: Timothy Mhlanga, Martin Bechani Bakaka, Luke Kingsize Chiliko, John Chakara Zacharia Marichi | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 The four men named above are to be executed on 16 March 1993. There were all sentenced to death for murder and have had their appeals against the death sentence rejected in the Supreme Court. AI is urgently appealing to President Mugabe of Zimbabwe to commute the death sentences. "
