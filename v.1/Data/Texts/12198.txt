"UA 258/94 - Malaysia: death penalty: Abdul Rahman Ibrahim 
 UA 258/94 - Malaysia: death penalty: Abdul Rahman Ibrahim | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 On 27 June 1994 the Supreme Court dismissed Abdul Rahman Ibrahim's appeal against his death sentence imposed on 30 March 1991 for drug trafficking. His only hope for a pardon or commutation now resides with the Head of State for Penang."
