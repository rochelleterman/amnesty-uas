"Further information on EXTRA 23/94 (AMR 51/23/94) - USA (Kansas): possible reinstatement of the death penalty 
 Further information on EXTRA 23/94 (AMR 51/23/94) - USA (Kansas): possible reinstatement of the death penalty | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 On 23 April 1994, Governor Finney allowed a bill reinstating the death penalty in Kansas to become law. Governor Finney herself opposes the death penalty but allowed the bill to pass without her signature as she believes that it is the will of the people in Kansas."
