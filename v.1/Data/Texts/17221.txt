 Iran: Death penalty / flogging | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is gravely concerned at reports     that Morteza Amini Moqaddam, who was sentenced to death   on 16 December 1999 after being found guilty of murder,   will be executed, in public, in the next few days.        Amnesty International is also concerned at reports that   Hamed Nazari, who was charged in connection with the      murder, has been sentenced to 74 lashes. He also          reportedly received a 15 year prison sentence.
