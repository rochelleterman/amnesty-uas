 Tunisia: Further information: Three men released; one still detained | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Three of the four men arrested on 19 July in the northern Tunisian town of Menzel Bourguiba have been released. The fourth man, Haitham el-Mejri, remains in detention and is facing an unfair trial before a military court. He could face the death penalty if convicted."
