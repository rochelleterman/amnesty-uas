"Iran: Further information on fear of death penalty / prisoners of conscience / fear of torture / health concerns 
 Iran: Further information on fear of death penalty / prisoners of conscience / fear of torture / health concerns | Amnesty International 
 Hamid Reza Jalaipour one of four employees of the Iranian daily newspaper Tous arrested on 16 September 1998 was released on 13 October pending further investigations. Mashallah Shamsolva'ezin,  Mohammad Javadi Hessar and Rahim Nabavi remain in detention and are reportedly suffering from poor health due to the conditions of their detention.
