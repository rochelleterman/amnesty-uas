 Sri Lanka: Further information on fear of torture | Amnesty International 
 Thirugnanam Sambandan, Kasinathan Naguleswaran and Rajendran Sudarsan and Rajaratnam Rajavel who were taken prisoner by the Liberation Tigers of Tamil Eelam (LTTE) have been released. E Nayavel (Amudan) was not taken prisoner, as reported earlier.
