"UA 418/93 - Singapore: death penalty: Johannes van Damme 
 UA 418/93 - Singapore: death penalty: Johannes van Damme | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Johannes van Damme, a Dutch national aged 58, was arrested on 27 September 1991 and convicted of trafficking in heroin. he was sentenced to death on 26 April 1993 and his appeal against the sentence was rejected on 23 November 1993. His only hope of clemency now lies with President Ong Teng Cheong."
