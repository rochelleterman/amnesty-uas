 Nepal: Fear for safety/possible ""disappearance"" | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is concerned for the safety of farmers Gyanendra Prasad Bidari, Manoj Kumar Shah, Kamal KC, and Shyam Raj Acharya, who were reportedly arrested by members of the security forces between 30 October and 18 November. Their whereabouts are unknown.
