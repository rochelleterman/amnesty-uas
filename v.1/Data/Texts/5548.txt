 Cuba: Further information on legal concern / ill-treatment / harassment / prisoner of conscience / possible POC | Amnesty International 
 Nestor Rodriguez Lobaina, President of Jovenes por la Democracia, Radames Garcia de la Vega, Vice-president of Jovenes por la Democracia; Ramon Rodriguez, Rafael Fonseca Ochoa; and new name: Heriberto Leyva Rodriguez: Amnesty International is calling for the immediate and unconditional release of the above prisoners of conscience. It is believed they have been detained solely for peacefully exercising their rights to freedom of expression, association and assembly.
