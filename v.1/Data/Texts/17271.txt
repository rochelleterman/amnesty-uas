 Iran: Death Penalty: Feyz Mohammad, an Afghan national

 | Amnesty International 
 Feyz Mohammad, a 16 year old Afghan national, has reportedly been sentenced to death by a juvenile court in Karaz. He was accused of distributing approximately 7 kilos of morphine, and being a member of a drug trafficking gang.
