"Democratic Republic of Congo: Further information on Arbitrary/fear of torture: N'sii Luanda Shandwe 
 Democratic Republic of Congo: Further information on Arbitrary/fear of  torture: N'sii Luanda Shandwe | Amnesty International 
 Human rights activist N'sii Luanda Shandwe has now been held without charge for over 72 hours. Although it is now known where he is held, and it appears that he has not been ill-treated or subjected to further interrogation, he is still at risk of ill-treatment or torture while he remains incommunicado.
