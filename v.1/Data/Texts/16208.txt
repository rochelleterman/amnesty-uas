 Uzkebistan : Further information on Death penalty/Fear of imminent              execution/Torture/Ill-treatment 
 | Amnesty International 
 After more than a year and a half on death row, Vazgen Arutyunyants and Armen Garushyants have had their death sentences commuted. According to unofficial sources, their death sentences have been replaced by 10-year prison terms. .
