"Colombia: Fear for Safety - Further information on Forcibly displaced civilian communities 
 Colombia:   Fear for Safety - Further information on Forcibly displaced civilian communities | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is seriously concerned for the safety of the Afro-Colombian civilian communities of the Cacarica River Basin (Cuenca del Cacarica) in the department of Choc— and the indigenous communities living in the same area, following threats received after the reported arrival of 500 army-backed paramilitaries to the area.
