"Yemen: Further information on Death penalty/imminent execution: Adib Mohamed 'Ali al-Magtari (m) 
 Yemen: Further information on Death penalty/imminent execution: Adib Mohamed 'Ali al-Magtari (m) | Amnesty International 
 Adib Mohamed 'Ali al-Maqtari is now said to be scheduled for execution on 7 November. He was sentenced to death in 2003, for murder. His appeals were rejected by the Court  of Appeal in Ta'iz in  August 2005 and the Supreme Court  in 2007, and his death  sentence was ratified by the      President.
