 Syria: Further information: Syrian prisoner released, brother still held | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 ‘Abd al-Rahman Hammada was released on bail on 27 June 2011 after spending a month and a half in detention. He was reportedly tortured during the first two weeks of his detention. His brother Wa’el Hammada has been held incommunicado since his arrest on 12 May. Amnesty International is gravely concerned for his safety. "
