 USA (Texas): Further information on death penalty/legal concern: Jaime Elizalde (m), Hispanic | Amnesty International 
 The execution of Jaime Elizalde, which was scheduled for 2 November, has been postponed. This is to allow time for him to be questioned about his recent statements that he committed the murder of another man for which someone else is serving a life sentence.
