"UA 10/93 - Peru: possible reintroduction of the death penalty 
 UA 10/93 - Peru: possible reintroduction of the death penalty | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 The newly established Congreso Constituyente Democratico (CCD) is to debate a bill on the death penalty which is reported to make provision for the execution of persons found guilty of treason, as defined in a counter- insurgency decree law passed in August 1992. The bill is presented by the Nueva Mayoria-Cambio 90 political alliance, which is backed by President Fujimoro. "
