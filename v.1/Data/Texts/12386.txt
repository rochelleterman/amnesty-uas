"Maldives: Medical Concern/ Prisoners of Conscience 
 Maldives: Medical Concern/ Prisoners of Conscience

 | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is seriously concerned for the health of the two prisoners of conscience, Ahmed Ibrahim Didi and Mohamed Zaki. Their health is reportedly deteriorating and they require urgent medical treatment at a hospital with adequate medical facilities.
