 UA 95/93 - Zaire: fear of torture / incommunicado detention: Mukendi Placide, Lukulungu Martin, Mbayi, Mutombo Anaclet, Mukuna Georges Anderson, Mbuyi Kalombo, Tshisimbi, Madame Kajinga Tambwe | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 The eight people named above were arrested by members of the security services in Kinshasa between 25 and 27 March 1993 and are believed to be held incommunicado. They are all known members of the largest opposition party in Zaire, the Union pour la Democratie et le Progres Social (UDPS). There are fears for their safety in incommunicado detention. "
