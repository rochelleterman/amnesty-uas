"Nicaragua: Further information on Fear for safety- Dorothy Virginia Granada 
 Nicaragua: Further information on Fear for safety- Dorothy Virginia Granada | Amnesty International 
 The order for the deportation of Dorothy Granada was lifted by a civil court on 6 February. The government has not appealed against the ruling. Dorothy has come out of hiding, and has returned to her work in Mulukuku, in the North Atlantic Autonomous Region.
