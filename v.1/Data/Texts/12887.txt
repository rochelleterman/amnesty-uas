 Pakistan: Fear for safety / possible extrajudicial execution | Amnesty International 
 Following the assassination of Nazar Mohammad in Pakistan there are fears for the safety of Din Mohammad, Abdul Ashna and other members of the Afghan political party Da Sohl Ghorzan. The party was established after members fled to Pakistan after an attempted military coup.
