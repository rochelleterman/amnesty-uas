"EXTRA 20/93 - Turkey: fear of torture: Mehmet Gokalp and others 
 EXTRA 20/93 - Turkey: fear of torture: Mehmet Gokalp and others | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Mehmet Gokalp, representative of the Turkish Human Rights Association in Derik, southeast Turkey, was detained along with others on 25 February 1993 following the funeral of villagers killed by the security forces. Disturbances followed the funeral and some 400 people were detained. Most have now been released but they reported that those still in detention may be being tortured. "
