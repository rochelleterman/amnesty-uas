"Indonesia (East Timor): Fear for safety: Antonio and Francisco 
 Indonesia (East Timor): Fear for safety: Antonio and Francisco | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International has grave fears for the safety of   refugees Antonio and Francisco, who were detained by      members of  a militia group on 21 October 1999 near       Nenuk, Belu       District, West Timor. They are at risk  of torture,        ill-treatment or death in custody.
