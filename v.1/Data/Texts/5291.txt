"Colombia: Fear for Safety for Human Right defenders 
 Colombia: Fear for Safety for Human Right defenders | Amnesty International 
 Five human rights activists are in immediate danger after they were declared ""military targets"" by a paramilitary group in telephone threats. The threats were made only days after two other human rights defenders were abducted in the same area.
