 Mexico: ""Disappearance"" / fear for safety: Gregorio Alfonso Alvarado Lopez | Amnesty International 
 The above, a teacher, member of a teacher's union and an Indian rights non-governmental organization, has not been seen since 26 September 1996. It is feared that he has been ""disappeared"" by paramilitary groups reportedly operating in the State of Guerrero.
