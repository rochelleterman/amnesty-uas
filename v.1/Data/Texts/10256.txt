"St Lucia: Further information on death penalty: Stephen Mongroo 
 St Lucia: Further information on death penalty: Stephen Mongroo | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Stephen Mongroo, who was scheduled to be executed in St Lucia on 27 April 1995, has been granted a stay of execution. Two other prisoners scheduled to be executed this week have also been granted stays."
