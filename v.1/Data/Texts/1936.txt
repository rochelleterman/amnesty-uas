"Rwanda: Fear for safety: Emmanuel Hitimana 
 Rwanda: Fear for safety: Emmanuel Hitimana | Amnesty International 
 Emmanuel Hitimana, an investigator at the Rwandese Association for the Defence of Human Rights (ARDHO), was arrested in Butamwa, Rural Kigali, on 30 May 1997. The reasons for his arrest are unknown and there is concern for his safety.
