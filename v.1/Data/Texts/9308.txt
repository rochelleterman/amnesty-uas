 USA: Futher information on death penalty / legal concern: Anthony Porter | Amnesty International 
 On 21 September 1998, the Illinois Supreme Court granted Anthony Porter a stay of execution in order for it to be established whether he is mentally fit to be executed.  The Court has ordered that a competency hearing be conducted and its findings reported by 2 November.
