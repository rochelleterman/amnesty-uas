 USA (Connecticut): Further information: Death penalty: Michael Bruce Ross

 | Amnesty International 
 Connecticut Governor Jodi Rell has announced that she will not grant Michael Ross a reprieve. Michael Ross is scheduled to be executed by lethal injection in the early hours of 26 January 2005. He has dropped his appeals against his death sentence. The State of Connecticut has not carried out an execution for almost 45 years.
