"Iran: Further information on Prisoners of Conscience 
 Iran: Further information  on  Prisoners of Conscience

 | Amnesty International 
 On 13 January, the Tehran Revolutionary Court sentenced prisoners of conscience Mehrangiz Kar and Shahla Lahiji to four years' imprisonment, and Ali Afshari to five years' imprisonment. They reportedly have 20 days in which to appeal against their sentences.
