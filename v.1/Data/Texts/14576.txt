"Turkey: Torture and ill-treatment: Baki Ya 
 Turkey: Torture and ill-treatment: Baki Ya | Amnesty International 
 Baki Ya has been tortured and ill-treated in prison. According to reports, he has been beaten and is being held in isolation in a cell with no window and thus no ventilation or daylight. Amnesty International believes these conditions amount to ill-treatment.
