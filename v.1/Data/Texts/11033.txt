"China: Further information on hunger strike / health concern / prisoners of conscience: Chen Ziming and Wang Zhihong 
 China: Further information on hunger strike / health concern / prisoners of conscience: Chen Ziming and Wang Zhihong | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 On 26 October, Wang Zhihong, was released from police custody by the Chinese authorities. Chen Ziming, who is suffering from cancer, is being denied proper medical care. There has been no further news as to whether or not he remains on hunger-strike."
