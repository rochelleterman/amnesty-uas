 Syria: Further information: Syrian reporter's brother held | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Amnesty International has learnt that ‘Imad Walid Kharsa, the brother of detained Syrian reporter ‘Adel Walid Kharsa, is also being detained and has been held incommunicado since August. It is gravely concerned that both men are at risk of torture and other ill-treatment."
