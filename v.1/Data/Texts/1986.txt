"UA 335/94 - Sierra Leone: death penalty: Amara Conteh 
 UA 335/94 - Sierra Leone: death penalty: Amara Conteh | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Amara Conteh, a 77-year-old warrant officer, was sentenced to death by a court martial in Freetown on 7 September 1994. He was sentenced by a civilian judge after being convicted of collaborating with rebel forces. It is not known whether he has been allowed any right of appeal."
