"USA (Missouri): Further Information, Death penalty: John Clayton Smith 
 USA (Missouri): Further Information, Death penalty: John Clayton Smith | Amnesty International 
 John Clayton Smith was executed in Missouri on 29 October. He was sentenced to death in 1999 for the murder of his former girlfriend Brandie Kearnes and her stepfather, Wayne Hoewing, in 1997.
