"Yemen: Incommunicado detention / fear of torture or ill-treatment: Omar Ibrahim Dagah 
 Yemen: Incommunicado detention / fear of torture or ill-treatment: Omar Ibrahim Dagah | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is concerned that Omar Ibrahim      Dagah has been ill-treated and may be at risk of torture  or ill-treatment in incommunicado detention. He was       arrested on 27 August 1999, reportedly by the Political       Security branch of the security forces after an       explosion in the Tuwahi area of Aden.
