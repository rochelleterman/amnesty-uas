"Mexico: Further information on: fear for safety: Bishop Arturo Lona Reyes 
 Mexico: Further information on: fear for safety: Bishop Arturo Lona Reyes | Amnesty International 
 AI is once again concerned for the safety of the above, bishop of the Diocese of Tehuantepec, Oaxaca, a renowned human rights activist and founder of the local Tepeyac Human Rights Centre. On 19 June 1996 he was falsely accused in the local media of supporting armed groups in the region. He has already been subjected to death threats and attacks.
