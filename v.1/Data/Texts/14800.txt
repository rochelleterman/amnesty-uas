"Turkey: Fear of torture/ ill-treatment/ police brutality 
 Turkey: Fear of torture/ ill-treatment/ police brutality | Amnesty International 
 At least two members of the legal pro-Kurdish political party HADEP have died and hundreds of others have been beaten or arrested as they tried to take part in World Peace Day on 1 September. According to official figures, 877 people were arrested throughout the country. The true figure is likely to be much higher.
