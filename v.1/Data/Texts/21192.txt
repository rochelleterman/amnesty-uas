 Pakistan: Imminent Execution: Aurangzeb (m) | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Aurangzeb (alias Rangoo, son of Karam Ali) is to be executed by hanging on January 2nd 2008 in the district jail of Gujranwala. Aurangzeb's age could not be confirmed but he is believed to be in his late twenties. He was convicted of murdering a man called Imtiaz Ahmed in a village near the town of Mandi Bahuddin in central Punjab province more than 10 years ago."
