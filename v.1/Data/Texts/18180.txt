"Palestinian Authority: Further information on prisoner of conscience / legal concern: Fathi Subuh 
 Palestinian Authority: Further information on prisoner of conscience / legal concern: Fathi Subuh | Amnesty International 
 Fathi Subuh, a 43-year-old university assistant professor of education, arrested on 2 July 1997, has now been held illegally for 25 days incommunicado. Amnesty International is continuing to call for his immediate and unconditional release as a prisoner of conscience.
