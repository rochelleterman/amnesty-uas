"Albania: Legal concern / prisoner of conscience: Idajet Beqiri 
 Albania: Legal concern / prisoner of conscience: Idajet Beqiri | Amnesty International 
 On 5 November 1996 the Albanian Court of Appeal will hear Idajet Beqiri's appeal against his 15-year prison sentence for ""crimes against humanity"". Amnesty International believes that the charges against the above, a leader of the opposition Party of National Unity, were politically motivated and considers him to be a prisoner of conscience.
