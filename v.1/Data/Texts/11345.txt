 Further information on EXTRA 80/92 (ASA 20/51/92, 8 October) - India: death penalty: Sukhdev Singh, alias Sukha, Harjinder Singh, alias Jinda | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 The two men named above were executed by hanging at 4am (local time) on 9 October 1992 at Pune Jail, Maharashtra. They had been sentenced to death for murdering General A Vaidya in August 1986, to which they had pleaded guilty. "
