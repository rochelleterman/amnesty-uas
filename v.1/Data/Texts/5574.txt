"Cuba: Prisoners of conscience / legal concern 
 Cuba: Prisoners of conscience / legal concern | Amnesty International 
 Raul Rivero Castaneda, journalist and founder of Cuba Press; William Cortes, Efren Martinez Purgaron, correspondents for Cuba Press: The three journalists named above are reported to have been detained in recent days and are thought to be held incommunicado. All three are believed to be prisoners of conscience detained solely for peacefully exercising their right to freedom of expression.
