"Egypt: Further information on Prisoner of conscience / health concern, Ashraf Ibrahim 
 Egypt: Further information on Prisoner of conscience / health concern, Ashraf Ibrahim | Amnesty International 
 On 7 August, the State Security Prosecution officially charged Ashraf Ibrahim with leadership of an illegal organization and referred his case for trial to the (Emergency) Supreme State Security Court, which denies defendants the right to an appeal before a higher court.
