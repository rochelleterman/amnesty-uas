"People's Republic of China: death penalty: Guo Xiaoping, Li Hua, Li Lixin 
 People's Republic of China: death penalty: Guo Xiaoping, Li Hua, Li Lixin | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 The official Chinese Xinhua news agency reports that on 22 January 1995 three people were sentenced to death in Hubei province. They were reportedly involved in a ""major case of robbing ancient graves"" in Guodian village, Sifang township. Their sentences are reported to be part of a ""crackdown on the trend of robbing ancient graves""."
