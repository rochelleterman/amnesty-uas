 Sudan: Fear of torture or ill-treatment/ detention without charge. | Amnesty International 
 Nine men were detained without charge some time between 1 and 12 April. They are being held incommunicado at Shala prison near El Fasher town, North Darfur province, and may be at risk of torture or other ill-treatment.
