 Murat Fani, a Kurdish asylum seeker returned from Germany in March 1994, was reportedly arrested by police on or around 9 November 1994. They reportedly beat him and his wife before taking him away. His wife was told that he was been held in Mersin but this was not confirmed when she inquired about his whereabouts. He had been detained for nine days on his return from Germany.
