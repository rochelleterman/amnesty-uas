"UA 257/94 - Colombia: ""disappearance"" / fear for safety: Martha Leticia Garcia, Arnulfo Atehortua 
 UA 257/94 - Colombia: ""disappearance"" / fear for safety: Martha Leticia Garcia, Arnulfo Atehortua | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Martha Leticia Garcia was abducted on 16 April 1994 from her restaurant in La Veta, Cocorna municipality, Antioquia department, by armed men. They were reportedly also looking for her son, Arnulfo Atehortua. Later the same men are reported to have ransacked his house. There is serious concern for their safety."
