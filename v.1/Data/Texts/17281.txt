 Iran: Further information on Medical negligence/prisoner of conscience, Abbas Amir Entezam | Amnesty International 
 Prisoner of conscience Abbas Amir Entezam has been released for one month's ""obligatory leave"" on medical grounds, reportedly at the request of his doctor. He was released from Evin prison last week, and will receive treatment at home.
