"Iran: Further information on Imminent Execution/flogging: Hamid Heydari 
 Iran: Further information on Imminent Execution/flogging: Hamid Heydari | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International has learned with regret that Hamid Heydari was executed on 10 January. He was hanged in public in Shahr-e Rey, a suburb of the capital, Tehran.
