 USA: Further information: Utah Board denies clemency; lawsuit filed: Ronnie Lee Gardner | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 On 14 June, the Board of Pardons and Parole in Utah, USA, denied clemency to Ronnie Lee Gardner. His lawyers have filed a civil rights lawsuit in federal court challenging the fairness of the clemency process. Ronnie Gardner is scheduled to be executed by firing squad on 18 June.   "
