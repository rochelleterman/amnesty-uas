"Guatemala: Fear for safety 
 Guatemala: Fear for safety | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is seriously concerned for the safety of the peasant population of Livingston municipality, including peasant activist Ramiro Choc, in light of recent armed attacks and threats made against them. There are fears that similar incidents could occur in the area in the near future.
