"Further information on EXTRA 32/94 (AMR 51/47/94, 27 June) - United States of America: Imminent move to extend the Federal Death Penalty 
 Further information on EXTRA 32/94 (AMR 51/47/94, 27 June) - United States of America: Imminent move to extend the Federal Death Penalty | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 A disagreement between the House and Senate Judiciary Committees, currently in the process of reconciling their crime bills, has delayed final passage of the Federal Crime Bill. The disagreement centres around a crucial provision in the House version that would allow state defendants to challenge their death sentences on grounds of racial discrimination."
