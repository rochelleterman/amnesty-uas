"Cambodia: Fear for safety / possible prisoner of conscience: Kim San and Meas Minear 
 Cambodia: Fear for safety / possible prisoner of conscience: Kim San and Meas Minear | Amnesty International 
 Kim San and Meas Minear, both human rights workers, are being detained since 21 December 1998 after observing riotous demonstrations in Sihanoukville. AI fears they may be at risk of torture and ill-treatment and are likely to be charged with organizing riots.
