 Nepal: Further information on Fear for Safety/Fear of Torture | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International has learned that the seven named men have now returned to their homes. They had reportedly been harassed by security forces personnel who threatened to rearrest them, forcing them to seek refuge in the offices of a local human rights organization for two days.
