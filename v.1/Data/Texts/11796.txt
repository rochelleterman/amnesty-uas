"Indonesia/East Timor: Arbitrary arrest / fear for safety 
 Indonesia/East Timor: Arbitrary arrest / fear for safety | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is concerned for the safety of      seven East Timorese people who were taken from the        village of Faulara on 16 and 18 July 1999, and are now    being held at local military command  in the town of      Maubara. There are serious fears that they could be at    risk of torture or ill-treatment. Another person is       reported to have been killed during the militia           operations in which the seven were detained.
