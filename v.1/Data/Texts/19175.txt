"Tunisia: Fear of torture/Incommunicado detention, Mohammed ben Hedi Naouar 
 Tunisia: Fear of torture/Incommunicado detention, Mohammed ben Hedi Naouar | Amnesty International 
 There has been no news of Mohammed ben Hedi Naouar since his reported arrest by State Security Department officers on the island of Djerba, Tunisia, on 5 July. Amnesty International fears that he may be being held in secret detention and is at risk of torture.
