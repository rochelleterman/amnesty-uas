 Egypt: Egyptian protester denied medical care: Mahmoud Mohamed Amin | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Mahmoud Mohamed Amin is currently in detention and after taking part in a protest in Cairo on 4 May. During the 2011 uprising he received an injury to the face from shotgun pellets, resulting in nerve damage which causes him severe pain in the face. He has not received adequate medical care while in detention. A possible prisoner of conscience, he may face an unfair trial before a military court."
