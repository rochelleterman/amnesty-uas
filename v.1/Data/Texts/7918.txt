"Further information on UA 394/93 (AMR 46/40/93, 5 November) - Peru: fear of ""disappearance"": Raul Yupanqui Barboza 
 Further information on UA 394/93 (AMR 46/40/93, 5 November) - Peru: fear of ""disappearance"": Raul Yupanqui Barboza | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Raul Yupanqui Barboza, who ""disappeared"" in Huancayo following his detention on 22 September 1993 was released the following day. Due to the threats he received he only reported his ""reappearance"" several months after his release."
