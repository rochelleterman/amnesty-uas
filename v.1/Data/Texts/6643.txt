"UA 359/93 - Guatemala: death threats: Gustavo Monzon Escobar 
 UA 359/93 - Guatemala: death threats: Gustavo Monzon Escobar | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Gustavo Monzon Escobar, the Coordinator of Casa Nazareth, an organization that provides assistance to indigenous peasants and students, has reportedly been subjected to harassment. For several months he has reportedly been receiving death threats by mail and at his workplace. "
