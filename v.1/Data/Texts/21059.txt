 Indonesia: Further Information on Imminent Execution: Achmad Suradji (m) | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Achmad Suradji was executed by a Brimob firing squad on 10 July 2008 at 9:58 pm, at a plantation in Deli Serdang, North Sumatra. His was granted his last request to see his wife Tumini, also on death row convicted of the same offence."
