 Venezuela: Further Information on Death threats/ harassment/ intimidation | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Human rights defender Jose Luis Urbano has been threatened as a result of his work as president of the non-governmental organization Fundación Pro-Defensa del Derecho a la Educación (Foundation for the Defense of the Right to Education). His life, and the lives of his family and other members of the Foundation, may be at risk."
