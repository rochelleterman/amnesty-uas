 Sudan: Fear for safety/ fear of torture or ill-treatment | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is concerned for the safety of those named who were arrested on 30 and 31 March in the capital Khartoum by members of the security forces. They are reportedly accused of supporting an attempted military coup and are being held in incommunicado detention at Kober prison, where some of them may be are at risk of torture or other ill-treatment.
