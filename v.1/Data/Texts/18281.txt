"Saudi Arabia: Fear of Flogging 
 Saudi Arabia: Fear of Flogging | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is concerned that two Filipino      women may face imminent flogging. The women were reported on 10 May 1999 to have been sentenced to 700 lashes each  and two years imprisonment for carrying out illegal       abortions. AI is concerned that a further 13 people may   also be at risk of flogging.
