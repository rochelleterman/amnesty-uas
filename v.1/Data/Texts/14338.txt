"Turkey: Possible ""disappearance""/ fear of torture or ill-treatment, Abdulkadir Bartan 
 Turkey: Possible ""disappearance""/ fear of torture or ill-treatment, Abdulkadir Bartan | Amnesty International 
 Abdulkadir Bartan was reportedly detained by the Turkish army on 15 April, during a military operation against the armed group the Kurdistan Worker's Party (the PKK). It is not known where he is being held and he may be at risk of torture or ""disappearance"".
