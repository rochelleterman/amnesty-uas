"Palestinian Authority: Further information on Torture/ill-treatment/legal concern, Mohammed Lahloh 
 Palestinian Authority: Further information on Torture/ill-treatment/legal concern, Mohammed Lahloh | Amnesty International 
 Mohammed Lahloh was released without charge on 7 November. He had been held in connection with the killing of Islamic Jihad member Iyad Hardan, who was extrajudicially executed in April, apparently by Israeli security forces.
