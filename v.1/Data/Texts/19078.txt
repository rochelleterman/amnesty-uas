"Further information on UA 260/93 (MDE 30/07/93, 6 August) - Tunisia: fear of torture: Tawfik Rajhi 
 Further information on UA 260/93 (MDE 30/07/93, 6 August) - Tunisia: fear of torture: Tawfik Rajhi | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Tawfik Rajhi, arrested on 26 July, was brought before the public prosecutor on 18 August 1993, after being held incommunicado for 23 days, well beyond the 10-day maximum period allowed under Tunisian law. "
