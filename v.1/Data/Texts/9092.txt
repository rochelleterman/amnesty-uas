 USA (Delaware): Further information on death penalty, David F Dawson
 | Amnesty International 
 The federal US Court of Appeals for the Third Circuit granted a stay of execution to David Dawson, who was scheduled to be put to death on 9 March 2001, pending a decision in the case from the US Supreme Court. The state did not appeal against the stay of execution.
