 Trinidad & Tobago: Further information on: Imminent execution: Lester Pitman 

 | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International has learned that Lester Pitman was granted a stay of execution by the High Court on 10 June pending further legal hearings. He is no longer in danger of imminent execution. However, a death warrant could be re-issued once his appeal has been heard.
