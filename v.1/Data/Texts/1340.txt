"Further information on UA 26/93 (AFR 36/12/93, 3 February) - Malawi: fear of ill-treatment / legal concern: The Reverend Peter Kaleso 
 Further information on UA 26/93 (AFR 36/12/93, 3 February) - Malawi: fear of ill-treatment / legal concern: The Reverend Peter Kaleso | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Reverend Peter Kaleso, Presbyterian minister and opposition activist, was released on bail on 2 February 1993 after being charged with ""uttering words abusive to the president"" at an opposition rally. If convicted, he could be imprisoned for up to three years on this charge. He was not ill- treated. "
