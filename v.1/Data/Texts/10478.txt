"UA 293/94 - Myanmar (Burma): fear of torture: Khin Maung Swe, Daw San San Nwe, U Sein Hla Oo 
 UA 293/94 - Myanmar (Burma): fear of torture: Khin Maung Swe, Daw San San Nwe, U Sein Hla Oo | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Khin Maung Swe, a prominent member of the NLD and MP-elect, Saw San San Nwe, a writer and her daughter, U Sein Kla Oo, a journalist, were arrested in Yangon (Rangoon) on 4 0r 5 August 1994. There whereabouts in detention are not known. AI fears for their safety in view of reports of torture and ill-treatment of government opponents in detention."
