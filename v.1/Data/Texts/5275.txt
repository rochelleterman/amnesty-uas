"UA 379/94 - Colombia: death threats / fear for safety: Gustavo Suarez Nino 
 UA 379/94 - Colombia: death threats / fear for safety: Gustavo Suarez Nino | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Father Gustavo Suarez Nino, a mayoral candidate in the forthcoming elections, has received death threats from Colsingue, a paramilitary group believed to have links with the security forces. Father Gustavo Suarez is a priest and teacher in the town of Sogamoso, Boyaca department, and is standing for the position of Mayor of Sogamoso as a representative of the civic movement ""United for Sogamoso""."
