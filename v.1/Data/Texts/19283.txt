"Tunisia: Further information on fear of ill-treatment / health concern / prisoner of conscience: Mohamed Hedi Sassi 
 Tunisia: Further information on fear of ill-treatment / health concern / prisoner of conscience: Mohamed Hedi Sassi | Amnesty International 
 Mohamed Hedi Sassi, a member of the illegal Parti communiste des ouvriers tunisiens (PCOT) was released conditionally by the authorities on 14 December. While Amnesty International welcomes his release, there remains concern that the charges against him have not been dropped.
