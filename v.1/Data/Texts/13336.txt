 Singapore: Further information on Death penalty / imminent execution   Anthony Ler | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International has learned that Anthony Ler was    hanged at dawn on 13 December in Changi Prison. He had    been sentenced to death in December 2001 after being      found guilty of hiring a 15-year-old boy to kill his      wife.
