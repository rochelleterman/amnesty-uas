 Pakistan: Further information on possible ""disappearance""/fear for safety: Hayatullah Khan (m), journalist

 | Amnesty International 
 Journalist Hayatullah Khan was found shot dead near the town of Mir Ali in North Waziristan tribal district, on 16 June 2006. He was abducted by five armed men on 5 December 2005, as he was on his way to report on a protest against a missile attack in the region.
