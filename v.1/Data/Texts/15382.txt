 Turkey: Further information on fear of ""disappearance"" / fear for safety | Amnesty International 
 Abdurrahman Temel, headman of Kocatepe village, Semsettin Temel, Abdulkadir Ektiren, Ali Ektiren, Ata Ektiren, Hami Ektiren, Sukru Ektiren, Zeki Ektiren, Bube Efe, Seydo Efe, Sinan Efe, Yusuf Efe, Abdurrahman Efe, Esra Efe, Ali Akyol, Vedat Akyol, Musa Dursun, Mehmet Dursun, Sehmus Soylemez, Hasan Bozkan, plus three other persons, names not known: The above-named villagers from Kocatepe village in the southeastern province of Mardin, who were detained on 27 June 1996, were taken to court on 12 July. All were released except for Ishak Ektiren who was formally arrested.
