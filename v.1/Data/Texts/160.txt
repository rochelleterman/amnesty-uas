"Burundi: Fear of Torture/incommunicado detention 
 Burundi: Fear of Torture/incommunicado detention | Amnesty International 
 At least six people, including a local government official, were arrested on or around 4 January. They are reportedly held incommunicado at the Gakumbu army camp, near the airport, where they are at severe risk of torture.
