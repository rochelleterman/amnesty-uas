"Israel/South Lebanon: Further information on fear for safety: Civilians in parts of Lebanon and Israel 
 Israel/South Lebanon: Further information on fear for safety: Civilians in parts of Lebanon and Israel | Amnesty International 
 On 23 November 1997 at least seven Lebanese civilians were killed and others wounded when mortar shells were fired at the small village of Beit Lif in South Lebanon. AI fears that more civilians may be killed or wounded.
