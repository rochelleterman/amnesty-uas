"UA 155/94 - India: death penalty: Dhananjoy Chatterjee 
 UA 155/94 - India: death penalty: Dhananjoy Chatterjee | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Dhananjoy Chatterjee was sentenced to death in August 1991 for rape and murder. The High Court and the Supreme Court have both upheld his sentence, but the date of his execution has been postponed several times. He is in imminent danger of execution."
