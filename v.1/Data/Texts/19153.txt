"Tunisia: Fear of Attack/ Ill-treatment 
 Tunisia: Fear of Attack/ Ill-treatment | Amnesty International 
 On 28 August at 10.35am Lasad Jouhri, a former prisoner of conscience and victim of torture, was assaulted by five plainclothes policemen. Lasad Jouhri has recently been the target of repeated incidents of harassment and intimidation by members of the security forces, including death threats.
