 China: Further information on Fear of torture or ill-treatment/ prisoner of conscience: Zheng Enchong (m) 
 | Amnesty International 
 Lawyer and human rights defender Zheng Enchong was released on 5 June 2006, after serving a three-year prison sentence. However, since he was freed, he and his family have been harassed and intimidated by the authorities, and Zheng Enchong has been detained four times since his release. Amnesty International fears they are at risk of further abuse.
