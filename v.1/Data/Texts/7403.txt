 Mexico: Fear for safety | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is concerned for the safety of Juan Anzaldo Meneses, member of the indigenous rights defender group Ce-Acatl. He was reportedly stopped by State Judicial Police, Policia Judicial del Estado (PJE) in Mexico State on 12 June and taken to an unidentified location where he was threatened and interrogated about his work, before being released.
