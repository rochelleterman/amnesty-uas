"Guatemala: Further information on Fear for Safety, Marta Elena Pichol Guarcax 
 Guatemala: Further information on Fear for Safety, Marta  Elena Pichol Guarcax | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International has been informed that Marta Elena Pichol Guarcax reappeared on 7 March 2001. No further details are yet available. She had been missing since 19 February and was believed to have been abducted.
