"UA 449/94 - Venezuela: fear for safety: fear for safety of prisoners as National Guard deployed in seven prisons 
 UA 449/94 - Venezuela: fear for safety: fear for safety of prisoners as National Guard deployed in seven prisons | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 The Venezuelan authorities have announced that they are sending military personnel into seven prisons throughout the country following violent incidents in recent weeks. AI is calling for those so deployed to abide by the United Nations Code of Conduct for Law Enforcement Officials, in order to avoid the repetition of recent human rights violations in the context of prison unrest."
