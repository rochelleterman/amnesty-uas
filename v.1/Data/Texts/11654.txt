"Indonesia: Fear of torture or ill treatment 
 Indonesia: Fear of torture or ill treatment | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is concerned for the safety of 36   people who were reportedly arrested after clashes at a    pro-independence demonstration. They are currently being  held in Sorong ""Police Resort"" (Polres), where they are   at risk of torture or ill-treatment.
