 Haiti: The re-trial of Louis Jodel Chamblain, a test of the judicial system in Haiti | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 On 22 April 2004 convicted human rights abuser and rebel  leader Louis Jodel Chamblain gave himself up to the       Haitian authorities and is currently in custody in        Port-au-Prince. Another convicted human rights abuser,    Jean Pierre Baptiste, known as ""Jean Tatoune"", has        announced to the press that he will follow Chamblain's    example on 1 May."
