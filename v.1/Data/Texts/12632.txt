"Nepal: Further information on Fear of 'disappearance'/Fear for safety/fear of torture 
 Nepal: Further information on Fear of 'disappearance'/Fear for safety/fear of torture | Amnesty International 
 Khim Lal Devkota is currently detained in Central Jail, Kathmandu. His relatives were informed of his whereabouts in early July and have been allowed to visit him. A habeas corpus petition, lodged on his behalf, is pending in the Supreme Court and the final hearing is scheduled 31 July.
