 Saudi Arabia: Shi'a cleric released: Sheikh Tawfiq Jaber Ibrahim al-‘Amr: Further information | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Saudi Arabian Shi’a cleric Sheikh Tawfiq Jaber Ibrahim al-‘Amr was released on 6 March 2011 without charge after being detained for a week incommunicado. He was arrested following a sermon he gave calling for reforms in Saudi Arabia. "
