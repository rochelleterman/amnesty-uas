"USA: Further Information on Death penalty / Legal concern, Gregory Lott 
 USA: Further Information on Death penalty / Legal concern, Gregory Lott | Amnesty International 
 Gregory Lott has received a stay of execution from a federal court. He was scheduled to be executed in Ohio on 27 April 2004. He was sentenced to death in 1987 for the murder of 84-year-old John McGrath in 1986.
