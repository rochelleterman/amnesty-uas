"Further information on EXTRA 66/94 (ASA 36/13/94, 22 November) - Singapore: imminent execution: Daniel Chan Chi-pun 
 Further information on EXTRA 66/94 (ASA 36/13/94, 22 November) - Singapore: imminent execution: Daniel Chan Chi-pun | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Daniel Chan Chi-pun has been granted a last minute stay of execution by the Singaporean government. His lawyer submitted a personal mercy plea to President Ong Teng Cheong on 24 November and he was granted a respite of sentence so that the plea may be considered."
