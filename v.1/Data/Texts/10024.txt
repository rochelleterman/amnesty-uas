"USA (Texas): Death Penalty/legal concern: Jaime Elizalde 
 USA (Texas): Death Penalty/legal concern: Jaime Elizalde | Amnesty International 
 Jaime Elizalde is scheduled to be executed in Texas on 2 November. He was sentenced to death in 1994 for the murders of two men. No physical evidence links him to the crimes, and key evidence against him has been called into question.
