"Philippines: Death penalty: Supreme Court confirms first death sentence: Leo Pilo Echegaray 
 Philippines: Death penalty: Supreme Court confirms first death sentence: Leo Pilo Echegaray | Amnesty International 
 On 25 June the Philippine Supreme Court confirmed the death sentence passed on the above, a house painter, sentenced to death in September 1994. This is the first sentence to be confirmed by the Supreme Court since the Philippines re-introduced the death penalty at the end of 1993.
