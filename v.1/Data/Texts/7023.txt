"Honduras:Further information on  Law reform: Office of the National Commissioner for the Protection of Human Rights 
 Honduras:Further information on  Law reform: Office of the National Commissioner for the Protection of Human Rights | Amnesty International 
 On 30 April 1999, the government announced that           amendments to the Law of the National Commissioner for    the Protection of Human Rights, which would limit both    the Office's scope and reduce the Commissioner's period   of office, would be withdrawn.
