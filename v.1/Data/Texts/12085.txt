 Republic of Korea (South Korea): Further information on health concern / fear of ill-treatment / prisoner of conscience: Park Yong-gil (f) | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 76-year-old prisoner of conscience, Park Yong-gil, has been sentenced by Seoul District Criminal Court to one year's imprisonment, suspended for three years. She was charged under the National Security Law for making an unauthorized visit to North Korea in July 1995. She was released on 1 December 1995."
