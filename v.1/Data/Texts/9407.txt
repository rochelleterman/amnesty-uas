"USA (Oklahoma): Further information on death penalty: Robert Wesley Knighton  
 USA (Oklahoma): Further information on death penalty: Robert Wesley Knighton 

 | Amnesty International 
 Robert Knighton was executed in Oklahoma on 27 May. He was sentenced to death in December 1990 for the murders of Virginia Denney and her husband Richard Denney, who were shot dead in their home on 8 January 1990.
