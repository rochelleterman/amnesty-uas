 Further information on UA 288/92 (MDE 15/22/92, 11 September and follow-ups MDE 15/24/92, 25 September, MDE 15/25/92, 9 October) - Israel and the Occupied Territories: torture / ill-treatment: Ahmad Sulayman Musa Qatamesh | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Ahmad Qatamesh, arrested on 1 September 1992, remains under interrogation and is reported to have suffered loss of vision. He was brought before a military judge on 15 October 1992 and his detention was extended for a further 25 days. His lawyer has asked the authorities to allow a specialist in ophthalmology to visit and treat him. AI has had as yet no response from the Israeli authorities about the allegations of torture or ill-treatment in this case or about Ahmad Qatamesh's legal situation. "
