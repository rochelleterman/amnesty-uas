"India: Fear for safety: Y. Mani, human rights defender 
 India: Fear for safety: Y. Mani, human rights defender | Amnesty International 
 On 16 April 1999, human rights defender Y. Mani was taken into army custody where he was badly beaten and           threatened with death. Although released the next day,    Amnesty International is concerned for his continued      safety.
