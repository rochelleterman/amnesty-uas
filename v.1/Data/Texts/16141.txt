"Turkmenistan: Further information on fear for safety / incommunicado detention / possible prisoner of conscience: Nurberdi NURMAMEDOV (Claire) 
 Turkmenistan: Further information on fear for safety / incommunicado detention / possible prisoner of conscience: Nurberdi NURMAMEDOV (Claire) | Amnesty International 
 On 25 February 2000, Nurberdi Nurmamedov was sentenced to five years in prison on charges of ""hooliganism"". Amnesty International believes that the systematic ill-treatment  of prisoners in Turkmenistan and the harsh prison         conditions might place Nurberdi Nurmamedov's life in      danger.
