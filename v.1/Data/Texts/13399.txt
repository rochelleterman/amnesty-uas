 Sri Lanka: Fear of Torture: Chitravel Manivannan | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is concerned for the safety of      Chitravel Manivannan, who has reportedly been repeatedly  tortured during interrogation by the police Counter       Subversive Unit (CSU) in Vavuniya. He is reported to be   in need of medical attention.
