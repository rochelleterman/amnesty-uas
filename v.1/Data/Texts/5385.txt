"Colombia: Further information on fear for Safety 
 Colombia: Further information on fear for Safety | Amnesty International 
 Army-backed paramilitaries have arrived in the Cacarica River Basin (Cuenca del Cacarica) area in force, and have begun attacking communities there and in the surrounding area. The inhabitants had been forced to flee their homes in 1997, but recently returned to resettlement camps.
