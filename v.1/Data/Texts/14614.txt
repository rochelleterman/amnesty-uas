 Turkey: Further information on ""disappearance"": Veysi Karagul | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Veysi Karagul, former President of pro-Kurdish Democracy Party (DEP), detained in Mersin on 24 December 1994, was formally arrested by Derik Public Prosecutor in late January 1995. He is now in Mardin Closed Prison. It is not known what charges he faces."
