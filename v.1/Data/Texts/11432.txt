 Indonesia: Fear for safety/possible ""disappearance"" | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is concerned for the safety of Mukhlis Ishak and Zulfikar, who are believed to be detained by the Indonesian Military, Tentara Nasional Indonesia, (TNI) in Nanggroe Aceh Darussalam (NAD) Province. Their current whereabouts is unconfirmed, and they are at risk of ill-treatment, torture or ?disappearance?.
