 Ethiopia: Further information on  Fear for safety / Use of excessive force by   Security forces | Amnesty International 
 Four members of the opposition Ethiopian Democratic Party (EDP), who had been released, were re-arrested on 24 June.Amnesty International is concerned for their health, already weakened by their earlier arrest. They have not had access to their families or lawyers.
