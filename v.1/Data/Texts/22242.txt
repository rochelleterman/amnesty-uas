 Iran: Kurd’s death sentence to be carried out: Hossein Khezri: Further information | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Hossein Khezri, a member of Iran’s Kurdish minority, is at imminent risk of execution. His death sentence was sent ""for implementation"" on around 17 November 2010, after the publication of a letter that he wrote from prison describing how he has been tortured."
