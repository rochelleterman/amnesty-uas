"Colombia: Fear for safety 
 Colombia: Fear for safety | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is seriously concerned for the safety of six mayors and human rights adviser Pedro Chica. They were all kidnapped in the municipality of San Francisco, department of Antioquia by members of the army-backed paramilitary group Bloque Metro Autodefensas Unidas de Colombia (AUC), Metro Bloc of the Self-Defence Groups of Colombia.
