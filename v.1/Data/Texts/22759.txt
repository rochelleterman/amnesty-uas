 Syria: Further information: Syrian Kurd tortured in custody: ‘Abdelbaqi Khalaf | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 ‘Abdelbaqi Khalaf is being tortured or otherwise ill-treated, according to reliable sources. Every few weeks, they say, he is taken from Damascus Central prison in ‘Adra, northern Damascus, to a branch of State Security, where officers are trying to force him to “confess” to something which he maintains is not true.
