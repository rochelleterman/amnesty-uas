"Ethiopia: Death penalty 
 Ethiopia: Death penalty | Amnesty International 
 Abdulkarim al-Naji Abdelradi, al-Arab Sadiq Hafiz, Safwat Hassan Abdelghani: On 20 September 1996, the three Egyptian nationals mentioned above were sentenced to death. They were convicted of involvement in an attempt to assassinate Egyptian President Hosni Mubarak in Addis Abbaba in 1995.
