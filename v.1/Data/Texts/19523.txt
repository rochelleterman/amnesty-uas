 Kenya: Peaceful protestor shot dead by police: Jackson Maina Kihato | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Jackson Maina Kihato, an unarmed 74-year old man was shot by police on 19 July, in Kabete NITD, Nairobi. He had been caught up in protests against forced evictions that had happened on 10 July, where an estimated 1,000 people were forcibly evicted from their homes and market stalls destroyed. "
