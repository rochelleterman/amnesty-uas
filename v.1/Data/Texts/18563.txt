"Syria: Further information on: Prisoners of conscience/torture/unfair trial 
 Syria: Further information on: Prisoners of conscience/torture/unfair trial | Amnesty International 
 On 31 March, prisoners of conscience Muhannad al-Dabas and Muhammad 'Arab were released from Adra Prison, just outside the capital Damascus. It has been reported that their release followed President Bashar al-Asad's refusal to ratify their sentences.
