"Further information on EXTRA 18/93 (EUR 44/14/93, 24 February) - Turkey: fear of torture: Abdullah Avsar 
 Further information on EXTRA 18/93 (EUR 44/14/93, 24 February) - Turkey: fear of torture: Abdullah Avsar | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Abdullah Avsar has been transferred to Diyarbakir Gendarmerie Headquarters, where he is being held incommunicado. His health is said to be poor as a result of torture. Under emergency regulations, he may be held in Diyarbakir for up to 30 days before appearing before a judge. "
