 Iran:  Further information on possible ""disappearance"" / fear for safety | Amnesty International

 Amnesty International has received reports that Manuchehr Mohammadi, an Iranian student leader, was released on 30  May 1999. He reportedly claimed that while detained he    was beaten. 
According to reports received by Amnesty International,   Gholamreza Mohajeri-Nezhad, another student activist, was detained on 30 May 1999. There were no further details as to his status or whereabouts."
