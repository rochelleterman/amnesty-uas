 Zimbabwe: Further information on torture and ill-treatment/ harassment | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Jenni Williams and Magodonga Mahlangu, the leaders of the activist organisation Women of Zimbabwe Arise (WOZA) were granted bail on 3 July 2008 by the High Court. They had spent nearly six weeks in detention at Chikurubi Maximum Prison in Harare. They were arrested after engaging in peaceful protest against state-sponsored violence.
