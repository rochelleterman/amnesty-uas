 Israel/OPT: Further information: Israel allows Palestinian patient out of Gaza: Ahmed Abu Mteer (m) | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 The Israeli authorities have agreed to grant Palestinian man Ahmed Abu Mteer permission to leave Gaza in order to travel to a Palestinian hospital in East Jerusalem, where he can receive life-saving treatment.  In May 2009, he was referred for life-saving treatment to Augusta Victoria Hospital in East Jerusalem. The authorities have now confirmed that he will be permitted to travel on 12 July 2009."
