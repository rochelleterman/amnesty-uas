 USA: Further information: USA to appeal release of Guantánamo detainee: Adnan Farhan Abdul Latif  | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 The US Department of Justice said on 17 September that the federal government will appeal a district court order to release Yemeni national Adnan Farhan Abdul Latif from Guantánamo. He has been held there since January 2002. "
