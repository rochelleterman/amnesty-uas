"UA 36/94 - Turkey: fear of ""disappearance"": Fethi Yildirim 
 UA 36/94 - Turkey: fear of ""disappearance"": Fethi Yildirim | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Fethi Yildirim, local board member and treasurer of the Democracy Party (DEP) in Viransehir, province of Urfa, has been held in unacknowledged detention since 5 January 1994. He was detained by Anti-Terror Branch police officers at the home of Ahmet Oner, who together with Ali Kuran, another board member, Lamih Kapancilar and Abdo Mutlu were also detained, but later released. There is grave concern for his safety."
