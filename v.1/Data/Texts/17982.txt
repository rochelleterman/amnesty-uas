"Jordan: Death penalty: 
 Jordan: Death penalty: | Amnesty International 
 Ahmad Muhammad, Ahmad Salim, Yasser Muhammad, Sa'id Mahmud, 'Eid Muhammad, Muhammad Khalil, Mahmud Khalil and Mifdi Khalil: The eight above-named family members were sentenced to death by the Amman Criminal Court at the end of October 1997. The death sentences must now be ratified by the Court of Cassation and King Hussein bin Talal.
