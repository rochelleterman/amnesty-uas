 Indonesia (East Timor): Fear for safety / possible ""disappearance"" / fear of torture or ill-treatment | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is seriously concerned for the      safety of Deomentino de Fatima, Francisco do Nascimento   and one unknown person who are believed to be in the      custody of   the Dili-based militia group Aitarak.        Reports indicate    that two of them have been badly      beaten and that one may  have been killed.
