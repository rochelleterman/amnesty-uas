 USA: Further information: Idaho carries out first execution since 1994: Paul Rhoades | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 On the morning of 18 November 2011, the US state of Idaho carried out its first execution in 17 years, and only its second since 1957. Paul Rhoades, aged 54, was put to death by lethal injection after more than two decades on death row. "
