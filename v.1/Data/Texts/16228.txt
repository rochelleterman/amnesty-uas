"Uzbekistan: Further information on Fear for safety/incommunicado detention/ fear of torture, Babir Makhmudov 
 Uzbekistan: Further information on Fear for safety/incommunicado detention/ fear of torture, Babir Makhmudov | Amnesty International 
 Babir Makhmudov was released without charge on 9 April. He was detained following a series of explosions, as well as attacks on police checkpoints, in the capital, Tashkent, and the city of Bukhara from 28 to 31 March 2004.
