"UA 383/94 - Uzbekistan: fear of imminent execution: Valizhon Akhmedzhanovich Akhmedzhanov 
 UA 383/94 - Uzbekistan: fear of imminent execution: Valizhon Akhmedzhanovich Akhmedzhanov | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Valizhon Akhmedzhanov, who worked as the deputy director of a brick factory in Toytyuba in Tashkent, was sentenced to death by Tashkent Regional Court on 17 February 1994 for premeditated, aggravated murder. The Supreme Court turned down his appeal on 18 October and he faces imminent execution. A petition for clemency has been lodged with the President."
