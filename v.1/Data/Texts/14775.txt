"Further information on EXTRA 37/94 (EUR 44/57/94, 13 July) - Turkey: fear of torture: Mehmet Fida Alin 
 Further information on EXTRA 37/94 (EUR 44/57/94, 13 July) - Turkey: fear of torture: Mehmet Fida Alin | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 On 13 July Mehmet Fida Alin was taken to court, formally arrested and charged. He is now in prison in Adana awaiting trial. No further information is available about his treatment while in unacknowledged detention."
