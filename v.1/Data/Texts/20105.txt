 Guatemala: Fear for safety/death threats | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 José Suasnavar, deputy director of the Guatemalan Forensic Anthropology Foundation (Fundación de Antropología Forense de Guatemala, FAFG) received four threatening SMS messages on and between 27/29 July 2008. FAFG carries out exhumations of mass graves created during Guatemala’s internal armed conflict (1960-1996)."
