 Turkey: Further information on fear of torture / prisoners of conscience: Ahmet Killi, Cagatay Isik | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Ahmet Killi and Cagatay Isik were released without charge after five days in police custody. They have reported being severely beaten in police custody but have not entered a formal complaint of torture. No further details are available."
