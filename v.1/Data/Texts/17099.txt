"Iran: Fear of torture and ill-treatment, Asghar Mazaheri Kalahroudi 
 Iran: Fear of torture and ill-treatment, Asghar Mazaheri Kalahroudi | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is concerned for the safety of Asghar Mazaheri Kalahroudi, who is reportedly being held in incommunicado detention at an unknown location, where he may be at risk of torture or ill-treatment. Asghar Mazaheri Kalahroudi left his home in Tehran on 13 June between 5pm and 6pm and did not return.
