"UA 254/94 - Honduras: death threats / fear for safety: Members of the Comite de Familiares de Detenidos Desaparecidos en Honduras (COFADEH), Jorge Valladares Valladares 
 UA 254/94 - Honduras: death threats / fear for safety: Members of the Comite de Familiares de Detenidos Desaparecidos en Honduras (COFADEH), Jorge Valladares Valladares | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 On 7 June Jorge Valladares Valladares, Assistant to the National Commissioner for the Protection of Human Rights, was stopped and threatened by two unidentified gunmen. COFADEH has reported that on 27 June a death threat against its members was found recorded on their answering machine. These threats follow recent efforts by human rights organizations to press for investigations into past human rights abuses."
