"Further information on EXTRA 13/94 (AMR 29/03/94, 28 February) - El Salvador: fear for safety: Nidia Diaz, Elmer Cruz Pineda 
 Further information on EXTRA 13/94 (AMR 29/03/94, 28 February) - El Salvador: fear for safety: Nidia Diaz, Elmer Cruz Pineda | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 There has been another attempt on the life of Nidia Diaz. On 19 May 1994 armed men attacked her car outside her house. She was in her house at the time, but her bodyguard, Elmer Cruz Pineda, was injured."
