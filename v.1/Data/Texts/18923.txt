"Algeria: Possible ""disappearance""/Secret detention/Fear of torture or ill-treatment: Mohammed Yahi 
 Algeria: Possible ""disappearance""/Secret detention/Fear   of torture or ill-treatment: Mohammed Yahi | Amnesty International 
 Mohammed Yahi was arrested by agents of the military security on 25 June at his home in Lassouaf, a district in the town of Dellys, north-eastern Algeria. He is being held in secret detention at an unknown location and is at risk of torture.
