"Burundi: Further information on fear for safety / fear of ill-treatment: Mr Pascal Birari 
 Burundi: Further information on fear for safety / fear of ill-treatment: Mr Pascal Birari | Amnesty International 
 Fears continue for the safety of Pascal Birari who has been transferred to Mpimba Prison where he remains at risk of ill-treatment. Concerns also remain for the safety of 15 other detainees at Kiyange Military position.
