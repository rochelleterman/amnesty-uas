 Nepal: Fear for safety/possible ""disappearance"" | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is concerned for the safety of nine named people, who were reportedly arrested in Kathmandu between 1 September and 21 November. Their whereabouts are unknown and they may have ""disappeared"".
