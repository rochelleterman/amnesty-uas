"Lebanon: Further Information on Death penalty/imminent execution 
 Lebanon: Further Information on Death penalty/imminent execution | Amnesty International 
 Ahmad 'Ali Mansour, Badea' Waleed Hamada and Remi Antoan Zaatar were executed at Rumieh prison in the capital Beirut on 17 January 2004. These were the first executions to take place in Lebanon since President Lahoud took office on 24 November 1998.
