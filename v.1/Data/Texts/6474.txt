 Guatemala: Further information on Fear for Safety/Extrajudicial execution, Waldemar Barrera | Amnesty International 
 The former mayor of Puerto Barrios has been accused of the killing of radio presenter Jorge Mynor Alegr’a, and a warrant has been issued for his arrest. Jorge Mynor Alegr’a had regularly denounced alleged corruption by municipal and port authorities in Puerto Barrios, Izabal Department, on the phone-in show that he presented.
