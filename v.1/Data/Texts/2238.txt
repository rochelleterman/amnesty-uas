"Further information on EXTRA 51/93 (AFR 53/19/93, 24 May) - South Africa (Venda): death penalty: Frans Netshirombeni, Wilson Nelukalo 
 Further information on EXTRA 51/93 (AFR 53/19/93, 24 May) - South Africa (Venda): death penalty: Frans Netshirombeni, Wilson Nelukalo | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 The scheduled executions of Frans Netshirombeni and Wilson Nelukalo were halted on 28 May, as a result of a confidential agreement reached between the officials of the nominally independent ""homeland"" of Venda, and the lawyers acting on behalf of the two men. No further details are available. "
