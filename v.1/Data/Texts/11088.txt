"India: Further information on ""disappearance"" / fear of torture or ill-treatment: Hidiyat-ul-Rashid 
 India: Further information on ""disappearance"" / fear of torture or ill-treatment: Hidiyat-ul-Rashid | Amnesty International 
 Hidyiat-ul-Rashid was released on 4 April 1998. Reports indicate that he was tortured while in detention and that he is now receiving medical treatment in a civil hospital in Pulwama, near Srinagar.
