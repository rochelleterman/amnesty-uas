"USA (Texas): Further information on Death penalty/Legal concern John Paul Penry 
 USA (Texas): Further information on Death penalty/Legal concern John Paul Penry | Amnesty International 
 On 27 November, the US Supreme Court announced that it    would consider the merits of John Paul Penry's appeal.    This means that he is no longer at threat of imminent     execution. The Court will hear oral arguments in the case some time next year.
