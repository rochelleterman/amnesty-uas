"Turkey: Further information on: Fear of ""disappearance"": Ozcan Elci 
 Turkey: Further information on: Fear of ""disappearance"": Ozcan Elci | Amnesty International 
 The above, a shopkeeper and a father of five, was released on 23 May 1996. Reportedly, he had been held by the gendarmerie in Bitlis and was severely tortured. No charges were brought against him.
