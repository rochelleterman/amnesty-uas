 USA: Further information: Last-minute stay of execution for Texas man: Anthony Haynes | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 On 18 October, the US Supreme Court granted a stay of execution to Anthony Haynes about two-and-a-half hours before he was scheduled to be put to death in Texas, for the murder of an off-duty police officer committed when he was 19 years old."
