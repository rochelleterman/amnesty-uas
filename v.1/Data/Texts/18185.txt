"Palestinian Authority: Further information on Fear of torture and ill-treatment / Incommunicado detention / Legal concern 
 Palestinian Authority: Further information on Fear of torture and ill-treatment / Incommunicado detention / Legal concern | Amnesty International 
 About 30 students and one staff member from Birzeit       University in the West Bank were released unconditionally by Sunday 5 March 2000. However it has been reported that during their detention some of the students were beaten   and one student was threatened with rape.
