"USA (Oklahoma): Further information on Death penalty / Legal concern - Hung Thanh Le (m), Vietnamese national 
 USA (Oklahoma): Further information on Death penalty / Legal concern - Hung Thanh Le (m), Vietnamese national

 | Amnesty International 
 Following a clemency hearing on 9 December, the Oklahoma Pardon and Parole Board recommended by four votes to nil that the governor should commute Hung Thanh Le's death sentence to life imprisonment. The Board's decision is not binding on the governor. It is not known when the governor will make his decision on the case.
