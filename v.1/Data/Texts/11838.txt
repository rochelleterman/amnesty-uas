 Indonesia: Fear for safety / possible ""disappearance"": Mustafa Sulaiman | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is concerned for the safety of      Mustafa Sulaiman, who has not been seen since he was      arrested by members of the Indonesian National Army (TNI) on 25 June 1999. Those detained by the TNI are at risk of torture, ill-treatment,""disappearance"" or unlawful        killing.
