 Saudi Arabia: Fear of imminent execution | Amnesty International 
 The three prisoners (Suliamon Olyfemi (m), Siti Zainab Binti Duhri Rupa (f), Hadi Sa'eed Al-Muteef (m)), and an unknown number of others, may be at risk of imminent execution. There has been an increase in the number of executions recently: at least 23 people have been executed in Saudi Arabia this year, and eight people, mostly foreign nationals, have been executed since 29 October.
