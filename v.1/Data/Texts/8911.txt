"USA: Impunity: Victims of human rights violations in all countries 
 USA: Impunity: Victims of human rights violations in all countries | Amnesty International 
 The USA is jeopardising the establishment of the proposed UN Human Rights Council, which promises to be more effective at protecting human rights than the existing Commission on Human Rights. The President of the UN General Assembly has put forward a draft resolution to establish the Council, but the US ambassador the UN, John Bolton, has threatened to put the draft to a vote.
