"Turkmenistan: Imminent danger of death in custody/Torture, ill-treatment Shagildy Atakov 
 Turkmenistan:  Imminent danger of death in custody/Torture,   ill-treatment Shagildy Atakov | Amnesty International 
 Baptist Christian Shagildy Atakov is believed to have been treated so harshly in prison is in imminent danger of dying. There are reports that he was imprisoned solely because of his religious beliefs, in which case Amnesty International would consider him a prisoner of conscience.
