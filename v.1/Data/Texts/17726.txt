 Israel/Occupied Territories: incommunicado detention/fear of torture/medical concern: Amin Muhammad Ghazi al-aghbar, Palestinian | Amnesty International 
 Amin Muhammad Ghazi al-Aghbar has been held incommunicado and forbidden to meet with a lawyer since his arrest on   16 March 1999. Amnesty International is concerned that he is at risk of torture or other ill-treatment under        interrogation.
