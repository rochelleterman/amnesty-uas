"EXTRA 94/92 - South Africa (Bophuthatswana): fear of execution: Stephen Mashinini, Samuel Mnisi, Jonathan Molema 
 EXTRA 94/92 - South Africa (Bophuthatswana): fear of execution: Stephen Mashinini, Samuel Mnisi, Jonathan Molema | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 The above-named are scheduled to be executed on 17 November 1992 in the nominally-independent homeland of Bophuthatswana. All had been sentenced to death for murder. Lawyers representing the three will be submitting an urgent application to the Mafikeng Supreme Court for a stay of execution and challenging the constitutionality of the death penalty in Bophuthatswana.
