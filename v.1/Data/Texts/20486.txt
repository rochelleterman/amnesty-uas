 USA (Louisiana): Further information: No clemency in life without parole case: Christi Cheramie | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Christi Cheramie was sentenced at the age of 16 to life in prison without the possibility of parole. She has been denied clemency by the Louisiana Board of Pardons and must wait seven years before she is permitted to submit a further application for clemency."
