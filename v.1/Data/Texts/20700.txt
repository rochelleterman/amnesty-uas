 USA: Further information on Death penalty/Legal concern: Richard Wade Cooey (m) | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Richard Cooey was executed in Ohio on 14 October 2008. He was sentenced to death on 5 December 1986 for the murders of Wendy Offredo and Dawn McCreery committed three months earlier.  He was 19 years and two months old at the time of the crime. He had spent nearly 22 years on death row."
