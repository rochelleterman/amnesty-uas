 Colombia: Fear for safety / ""Disappearance"" | Amnesty International 
 There is serious concern for the safety of Heladio Penagos Rojas after two unidentified men reportedly abducted him from his house on 10 August 1998 in the village of San Francisco, Tibacuy municipality.  His whereabouts remain unknown.
