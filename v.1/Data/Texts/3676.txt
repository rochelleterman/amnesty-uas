"The Bahamas: Death penalty : Trevor Nathaniel Pennerman Fisher and Richard Woods 
 The Bahamas: Death penalty : Trevor Nathaniel Pennerman Fisher and Richard Woods | Amnesty International 
 Trevor Nathaniel Pennerman Fisher was hanged on 15 October 1998. Richard Woods, another death row inmate, was executed one hour later. These are the first executions to have taken place in The Bahamas since 1996.
