"Cuba: Prisoner of conscience / legal concern: Roberto Gonzalez Tibanear 
 Cuba: Prisoner of conscience / legal concern: Roberto Gonzalez Tibanear | Amnesty International 
 According to reports, Roberto Gonzalez Tibanear is to face trial on 27 August 1997 and could be sentenced to up to 5 years' imprisonment. AI believes that he is a prisoner of conscience, detained solely for the non-violent exercise of his right to freedom of expression, association and assembly.
