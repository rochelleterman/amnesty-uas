 Iranian man at risk of execution: Gholamreza Khosravi Savadjani  | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 An Iranian man, Gholamreza Khosravi Savadjani, is scheduled to be executed on 10 September 2012, on the charge of “enmity against God” (moharebeh) for his alleged support of a banned Iranian opposition group, the People’s Mojahedin Organization of Iran (PMOI)."
