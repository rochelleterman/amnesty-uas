 China: Fear of imminent execution, 50 unnamed people | Amnesty International 
 More than 50 people are reported to have been sentenced to death for ""separatist and terrorist"" activities during the first eight months of this year in the Xinjiang Uighur Autonomous Region (XUAR), northwest China. They might be executed at any time.
