"Taiwan (Republic of China): Further information on: death penalty / legal concern / fear of imminent execution 
 Taiwan (Republic of China): Further information on: death penalty / legal concern / fear of imminent execution | Amnesty International 
 Su Chien-ho, Liu Ping-lang, Chuang Lin-hsiung: The above remain under sentence of death, despite widespread concern that they were convicted unfairly, on the basis of confessions extracted under torture. The three men had been sentenced in February 1995 on charges of robbery, rape and murder.
