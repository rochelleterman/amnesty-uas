 Nepal: Further information on Fear for safety / possible ""disappearance."" Jujubhai Maharjan

 | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty has learnt that Jujubhai Maharjan, who was arrested on 18 February and feared to have been 'disappeared', has been released. Jujubhai Maharjan was arrested from the Bajra hotel in Kathmandu district, where he worked as a waiter, and released at the same location on 27 April.
