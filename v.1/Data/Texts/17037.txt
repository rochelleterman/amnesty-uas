 Iran: Further information on death penalty: Saba 'Abd 'Ali (f), 30, Zaynab Haydari (f), 38 | Amnesty International

 Zaynab Haydari has reportedly had her sentence commuted to 80 lashes and a fine. It is believed that she may already have received the lashes. The decision of Iran's Supreme Court regarding Saba 'Abd 'Ali's appeal against the death sentence by stoning is apparently still pending."
