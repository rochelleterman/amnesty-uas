"EXTRA 15/93 - USA (Georgia): death penalty: Larry Lonchar 
 EXTRA 15/93 - USA (Georgia): death penalty: Larry Lonchar | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Larry Lonchar, white, is scheduled to be executed on 24 February 1993. He has chosen to give up his legal appeals and has consented to his execution. He was sentenced to death for murder in June 1987. According to reports, Larry Lonchar has a history of severe mental problems. "
