"Argentina: Further information on Fear for safety for human right lawyers 
 Argentina: Further information on Fear for safety for human right lawyers | Amnesty International 
 Carlos Varela, Diego Lavado, Alejandro Acosta, human rights lawyers in Mendoza province, who represent the families of victims of human rights abuses, have suffered further intimidation. Despite official reassurances, the authorities have not given them adequate protection..
