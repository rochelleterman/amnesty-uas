"Yugoslavia, Kosovo province: Arbitrary detentions / torture 
 Yugoslavia, Kosovo province: Arbitrary detentions / torture | Amnesty International 
 Since several Serbs, some of them police officers, were shot dead or wounded on 22 April, there have been reports that police have arrested apparently arbitrarily many ethnic Albanians. Most have been released within 24 hours and some have reported that they were tortured in custudy.
