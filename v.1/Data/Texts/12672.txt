 Nepal: Fear for safety/ possible ""disappearance"", Hari Prasad Acharya (m), aged 45, Poultry farmer | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International has recently received reports that Hari Prasad Acharya was arrested on 6 November 2003, at his home in Dhading district. He has not been seen or heard from since, and Amnesty International is concerned that he has ""disappeared"".
