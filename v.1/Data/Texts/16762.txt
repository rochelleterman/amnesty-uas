"Further information on UA 327/93 (MDE 12/14/93, 16 September) - Egypt: death penalty: Mahmoud Salah, Mostafa 'Awni Zaki 
 Further information on UA 327/93 (MDE 12/14/93, 16 September) - Egypt: death penalty: Mahmoud Salah, Mostafa 'Awni Zaki | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 The above-named were sentenced to death by the Supreme Military Court in Cairo on 15 September 1993. All legal procedures have apparently taken place and their execution is expected to be soon. They were among eight defendants charted with membership of an illegal organization, murder and attempted murder and possession of weapons and ammunition."
