"Guatemala: Fear for safety 
 Guatemala: Fear for safety | Amnesty International 
 Victor Hugo Duran Guerra, Felix Hernandez, Jose Maria Ortega, Jaime Manfredo Diaz Celada - all members of the ""22 February"" General Trade Union of Guatel Workers: Amnesty International is concerned for the safety of trade union leader Victor Hugo Duran Guerra, who has suffered two attempts on his life and continuous death threats. The organization is also concerned for the safety of the other trade unionists following death threats against them.
