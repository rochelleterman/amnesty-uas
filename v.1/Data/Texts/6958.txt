"EXTRA 51/94 - Haiti: ""disappearance"": Vixene Thomas, Andre Noresca, Emmanuel Deus, Nicolas Seligny 
 EXTRA 51/94 - Haiti: ""disappearance"": Vixene Thomas, Andre Noresca, Emmanuel Deus, Nicolas Seligny | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 The four men named above, all memebr of the FGPKLS peasant organization, have reportedly ""disappeared"" after being arrested by police in the early hours of 22 July 1994. Relatives have been unable to obtain any information about them. There is grave concern for the safety of the men in view of the current human rights situation in Haiti."
