 Colombia: Human rights lawyer at risk: Jorge Molano | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Human rights lawyer Jorge Molano is involved in a high-profile prosecution of a former army officer. He is being followed, and he and his family are in grave danger. Because of his high-profile work and previous threats to his safety, Jorge Molano receives some protection from the Ministry of Interior's Protection Programme."
