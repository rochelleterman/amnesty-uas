"USA (Indiana): Further information on Death penalty / Legal concern, Darnell Williams 
 USA (Indiana): Further information on Death penalty / Legal concern, Darnell Williams | Amnesty International 
 Darnell Williams, who was scheduled to be executed in Indiana on 1 August, has received a 60-day stay of execution. He was sentenced to death in 1987 for his part in a double murder committed during the course of a robbery in 1986.
