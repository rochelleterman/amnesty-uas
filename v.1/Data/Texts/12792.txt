 Nepal: Further information on Fear for safety/fear of ""disappearance"" / detention without charge | Amnesty International 
 The Nepalese authorities have publicly acknowledged that the four named individuals are currently being held in Morang District Prison. Amnesty International remains concerned for their safety as they have only had limited access to a lawyer and have not been granted access to their families.
