"Laos: Fear of torture/Fear for safety/Unfair trial 
 Laos: Fear of torture/Fear for safety/Unfair trial | Amnesty International 
 Sixteen Lao nationals, who were seeking asylum in Thailand, were forcibly returned to Laos on 4 July 2004. Amnesty International fears that they are at serious risk of torture and ill-treatment, and face unfair trial.
