 Bangladesh: Further Information on Medical concern/Torture/Legal concern: Rang Lai Mro (m) | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Rang Lai Mro was released on bail on 8 January, on the orders of the Bangladesh High Court. The head of a community of indigenous Mro people, he had been detained since 22 February 2008 charged with possessing a pistol without a license."
