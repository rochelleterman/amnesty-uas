 Bahrain: Further information: Women activists released but charges remain | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Zainab al-Khawaja and Ma'suma Sayyid Sharaf were released on 20 December. They had been arrested on 15 December for participating in a protest. However, the charges against them remain and Amnesty International is calling for them to be dropped. "
