 Libya: Detainees stripped and beaten in Libya prison | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 On 14 May 2012, 20 detainees were stripped and beaten in Ain Zara Prison, Libya, then left for two days on a concrete floor without mattresses or blankets. The detainees have not yet been examined by forensic pathologists. One of the guards allegedly responsible may still be working at the prison. "
