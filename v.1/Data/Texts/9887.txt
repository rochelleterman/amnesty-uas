"USA (North Carolina): Further information on death penalty / Legal concern, Robert Bacon 
 USA (North Carolina): Further information on death penalty / Legal concern, Robert Bacon | Amnesty International 
 On 18 September, the Governor of North Carolina postponed Robert Bacon's execution for two weeks to ensure that his lawyers have ""full access to the courts"" following last week's attacks on New York and Washington. Robert Bacon is now scheduled for execution on 5 October.
