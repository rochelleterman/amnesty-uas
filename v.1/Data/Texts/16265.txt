"Uzbekistan: Further information on Fear of imminent execution 
 Uzbekistan: Further information on Fear of imminent execution | Amnesty International 
 The Supreme Court of Uzbekistan has put Maksim Strakhov's execution on hold for three months. On 1 August Nikolay Ganiyev's mother was officially informed that her son's case had been referred to the Supreme Court for reconsideration. Amnesty International has not received any further news about Nigmatullo Fayzullayev.
