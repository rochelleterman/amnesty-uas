 Indonesia: Fear for safety / possible ""disappearance"" | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is seriously concerned for the safety of Nasrullah Ibrahim (m), aged 45, Muhammad (m), aged 22, Riza Pahlevi (m), aged 23. Their current whereabouts are unconfirmed, though they are believed to be held at the regional police headquarters, capital Banda Aceh, Aceh Province. They are at risk of torture or ill-treatment.
