 UAE: Further Information: Somali man released in United Arab Emirates: Boqor Osman Mohamoud  | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Somali national Boqor Osman Mohamoud was released on 5 January and has been reunited with his relatives. He was arrested by security forces on 17 November 2011 in the United Arab Emirates (UAE)."
