"USA (Oklahoma): Further information on Death penalty, Garry Thomas Allen 
 USA (Oklahoma): Further information on Death penalty, Garry Thomas Allen | Amnesty International 
 A county judge has stopped Garry Allen's execution in order that his mental competency for execution can be assessed. He was scheduled to be executed in Oklahoma on 19 May 2005. He was sentenced to death for the 1986 murder of Gail Titsworth, with whom he had had two children.
