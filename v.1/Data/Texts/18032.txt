"Kuwait: Death penalty: Kuwaiti police officer (name unknown) 
 Kuwait: Death penalty: Kuwaiti police officer (name unknown) | Amnesty International 
 On 24 June 1996, the Court of Cassation upheld the death sentence passed on a captain in the Kuwaiti police force convicted of drowning a Syrian woman with whom he had a relationship. The death sentence now passes to the Amir of Kuwait for ratification.
