"Iraq: Further information on Imminent execution/possible death sentence: Taha Yassin Ramadhan (m) 
 Iraq: Further information on Imminent execution/possible death sentence: Taha Yassin Ramadhan (m) | Amnesty International 
 Taha Yassin Ramadhan, the former Iraqi vice-president, was executed by hanging in the early hours of 20 March in Baghdad. He is the fourth former official to be executed since Saddam Hussain was hanged on 30 December 2006.
