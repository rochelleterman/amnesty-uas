"UA 283/94 - Belarus: fear of imminent execution: Sergey Vladimirovich Kutyavin 
 UA 283/94 - Belarus: fear of imminent execution: Sergey Vladimirovich Kutyavin | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Sergey Kutyavin was sentenced to death for murder by Mogilev Regional Court on 19 July 1993. His appeal against his death sentence was rejected by the Supreme Court on 11 March 1994. A petition for clemency is pending with the newly-elected President of Belarus."
