"Saudi Arabia: Preventive death penalty action 
 Saudi Arabia: Preventive death penalty action | Amnesty International 
 Use of the death penalty has increased greatly in Saudi   Arabia in recent months. At least 59 people are detained  on charges which carry the death penalty, and Amnesty     International fears that some or all face imminent        execution. Two people, both Saudi Arabian nationals, have been executed in the past week.
