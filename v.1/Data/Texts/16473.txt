 Federal Republic of Yugoslavia: Further information on ill-treatment / possible refoulement | Amnesty International 
 Xhafer Bardiqi, Florim Hoxha, Amir Drugzani and new name Mustafa Hajrallu: Amnesty International has received further reports that ethnic Albanians who have been forcibly returned from Germany to the Federal Republic of Yugoslavia, or have returned voluntarily after their requests for asylum were refused, have been ill-treated by police.
