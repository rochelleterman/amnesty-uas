"Colombia: Further information on ""Disappearances"" / Fear for safety 
 Colombia: Further information on ""Disappearances"" / Fear for safety | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International has learnt that Milton Bercerra managed to escape from the army-backed paramilitary group that had abducted him. He reportedly sustained gunshot wounds to his shoulder and hand. He apparently witnessed the killing of his seven travelling companions. Both he and his family are now in grave danger.
