"Guinea: death penalty: Sekou Bangoura 
 Guinea: death penalty: Sekou Bangoura | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Sergeant Sekou Bangoura was sentenced to death on 6 June 1995. He was convicted of causing the deaths of three people and injuring others after throwing a grenade into a bar in Conakry on 10 May 1995."
