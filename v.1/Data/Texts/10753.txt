"People's Republic of China: death penalty: Yang Genbiao 
 People's Republic of China: death penalty: Yang Genbiao | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 On 30 March 1995 the newspaper Zheijiang Gongren Ribao reported that Yang Genbiao was sentenced to death and deprived of his political rights for life by the Hangzhou Intermediate People's Court, Zhejiang province, for reportedly embezzling 300,000 yuan in collective funds. It is not known whether he has appealed against his death sentence."
