"Argentina: death threats: Maria Alejandra Bonafini 
 Argentina: death threats: Maria Alejandra Bonafini | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Maria Alejandra Bonafini, daughter of Hebe Bonafini, president of the Mothers of Plaza de Mayo, is reported to have received death threats. She was followed by unidentified people on the evening of 22 March 1995 and later received a telephoned death threat, related to the work of her mother. She has abandoned her home in fear of her life."
