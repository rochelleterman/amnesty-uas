"Further information on UA 316/93 (AMR 34/61/93, 10 September) - Guatemala: fear of ""disappearance"" / fear for safety: Salvador Aguirre Corado 
 Further information on UA 316/93 (AMR 34/61/93, 10 September) - Guatemala: fear of ""disappearance"" / fear for safety: Salvador Aguirre Corado | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 The body of agricultural worker, Salvador Aguirre Corado, was recently found. He had reportedly been shot several times. He had been abducted on 7 September 1993 by four armed men, three of whom were wearing military uniform. "
