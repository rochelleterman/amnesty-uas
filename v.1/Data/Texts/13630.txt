"Thailand: Execution / Fear of further executions: Supoj Pengklai 
 Thailand: Execution / Fear of further executions: Supoj Pengklai | Amnesty International 
 Supoj Pengklai was shot dead by a firing squad on 29 October 1998, in Bangkwang Prison, north of Bangkok. The execution, the fourth in less than three years, has raised serious fears for others on death row in Thailand.
