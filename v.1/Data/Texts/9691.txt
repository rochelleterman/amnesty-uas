"USA (Alabama): Further information on death penalty / legal concern: Pernell Ford 
 USA (Alabama): Further information on death penalty / legal concern: Pernell Ford | Amnesty International 
 On 8 July 1999 the 11th US Circuit Court of Appeals       stayed the execution of Pernell Ford, a few hours before  he was due to be executed. The court will hear the case   to decide his competency to waive appeals on 18 August.
