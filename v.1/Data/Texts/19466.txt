 Equatorial Guinea: Further information: Human rights defender charged: Wenceslao Mansogo Alo | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Dr Wenceslao Mansogo Alo, arrested on what appear to be political grounds, has been charged with offences relating to the death of a patient at his clinic. The charges appear to be linked to his human rights work and political activities."
