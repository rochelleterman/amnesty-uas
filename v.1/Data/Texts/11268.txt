"India: Fear for safety: Sheikh Ali Mohammad (60) 
 India: Fear for safety: Sheikh Ali Mohammad (60)

 | Amnesty International 
 Members of the police Special Operations Group (SOG)    took Sheikh Ali Mohammad from outside his home in         India's northern state of Jammu and Kashmir. The SOG have been implicated in a number of recent ""disappearances""    and deaths in custody. Amnesty International is concerned for his safety.
