 Burundi / Democratic Republic of Congo: Over 1,000 Burundi and Rwandese refugees in the Rusizi region of eastern DRC | Amnesty International 
 Since 9 November 1997 at least 1,043 Burundi and Rwandese refugees have been forcibly expelled from eastern DRC to Burundi. AI is concerned for their safety and well-being.
