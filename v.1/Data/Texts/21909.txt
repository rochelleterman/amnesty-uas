 Egypt: Businessman detained, risks torture: Mohammed Abdulwahab el-Sayed (m) | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Businessman Mohammed Abdulwahab el-Sayed has been held incommunicado since 22 June 2009, putting him at risk of torture or other ill-treatment. He runs a mobile phone business, and was returning from a business trip to the United Arab Emirates when he was detained by Cairo airport security services. His family have not heard from him since. He is believed to be held incommunicado by the State Security Investigations (SSI) at an unknown location. "
