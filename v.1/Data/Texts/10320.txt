"Bangladesh: Fear for safety, Religious minorities 
 Bangladesh: Fear for safety, Religious minorities | Amnesty International 
 Since the Bangladesh Nationalist Party (BNP) won a large majority in the general election on 1 October, its supporters have allegedly been attacking Hindus and other religious minorities because of their perceived support for the rival Awami League party. Police have apparenly taken no action.
