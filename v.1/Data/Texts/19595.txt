 Zimbabwe: Break the silence:  end the human rights violations in Zimbabwe (WA 22/05) | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Human rights violations and humanitarian needs are increasing in Zimbabwe while the world watches in silence.  In May 2005, the government of Zimbabwe initiated a campaign of mass evictions in the poor  urban areas of Harare and other parts of the country. The campaign, Operation Murambatsvina, is widely translated as ""Drive   out the rubbish"", but the police refer to it as ""Operation Restore Order"".
