"Myanmar: Further information on Fear for safety/Incommunicado detention/Fear of torture or ill-treatment 
 Myanmar: Further information on Fear for safety/Incommunicado detention/Fear of torture or ill-treatment | Amnesty International 
 Daw Mae Hnin Kyi and two female organizers of the National League for Democracy (NLD) Women's division have been released from custody. They were reportedly detained incommunicado and interrogated for 24 hours, along with at least four other women from the NLD Women's Division.
