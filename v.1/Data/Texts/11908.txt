"UA 380/94 - Japan: fear of imminent execution: 58 prisoners under finalized sentence of death 
 UA 380/94 - Japan: fear of imminent execution: 58 prisoners under finalized sentence of death | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 There have been disturbing reports that Japanese government officials have started rehearsals for executions in some of the seven detention centres holding prisoners under sentence of death. There are 58 such prisoners whose appeals have been exhausted and there are fears they may be executed at any time."
