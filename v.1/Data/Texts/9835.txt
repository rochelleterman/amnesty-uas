"USA (Texas) : Further information on  Death penalty / Legal concern: T.J. Jones 
 USA (Texas) : Further information on  Death penalty / Legal concern: T.J. Jones | Amnesty International 
 T.J. Jones was executed in Texas on 8 August for a murder committed when he was 17 years old. International law,    respected in almost every country of the world except the USA, prohibits the death penalty against child offenders: people who were under 18 at the time of the crime.
