"Pakistan: Possible ""disappearance""/fear for safety: Hayatullah Khan (m), journalist 
 Pakistan: Possible ""disappearance""/fear for safety: Hayatullah Khan (m), journalist

 | Amnesty International 
 On 5 December journalist Hayatullah Khan was abducted by five armed men, as he was on his way to report on a protest against a missile attack in the North Waziristan region of northwest Pakistan. It is feared that he may have been abducted by members of the security forces in an attempt to suppress reports that US forces were responsible for the attack. He may have ""disappeared"".
