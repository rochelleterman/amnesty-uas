 Sudan: Fear for safety / fear of torture or ill-treatment / incommunicado detention | Amnesty International 
 Four named men have reportedly been detained at the Security Forces premises in Nyala, capital of Southern Darfur province, Western Sudan, since 5 August. They are not being allowed any contact with their relatives or lawyers and Amnesty International fears that they may be tortured or ill-treated in detention.
