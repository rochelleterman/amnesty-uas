 Bolivia: Medical Action - Ill-health of Francisco Cortés, Peasant Leader | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Amnesty International is concerned about the poor health  of Francisco Cortés, a peasant leader of Colombian        nationality, currently being held in a maximum security   prison. Public statements made by Cortés' lawyer indicate that he is suffering from hepatitis, headaches, serious   sight problems and constant nosebleeds."
