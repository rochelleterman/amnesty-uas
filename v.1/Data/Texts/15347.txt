 Turkey: Further information on fear of torture / health concern: Ali Guden | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 On 16 November Ali Guden was formally arrested on charges of membership of an illegal organization and committed to Malatya prison. He has now been granted access to a doctor and fears for his health have subsided."
