"China: Fear of torture or ill-treatment: Yang Chunlin (m) 
 China: Fear of torture or ill-treatment: Yang Chunlin (m) | Amnesty International 
 Human rights activist Yang Chunlin, aged 52, was detained on 6 July 2007 in the north-eastern province of           Heilongjiang, and charged with ""subversion of state       power"" on 13 August. He is now held at Heitong Police     Station in Jiamusi City; he has been held incommunicado   since he was detained, putting him at risk of torture or  other ill-treatment.
