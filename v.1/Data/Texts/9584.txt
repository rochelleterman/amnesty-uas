 USA (Virginia): Death penalty | Amnesty International 
 Gregory Beaver, Larry Stout, Lem Tuggle, Ronald Hoke: The above four men are scheduled to be executed in Virginia on 3, 10 (International Human Rights Day), 12 and 16 December 1996 respectively. They were sentenced to death for murder.
