"Guatemala: Fear for Safety/Intimidation 
 Guatemala: Fear for Safety/Intimidation | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is seriously concerned for the safety of Daniel Pascual, Juan Tiney, Rafael Chanchavac Cux, Gilberto Atz, and Elizabeth Cabrera, following an increase in the harassment of and violence towards members of the peasant movement both in the capital Guatemala City and in the rural areas.
