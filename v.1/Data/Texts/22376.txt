 Iraq: Protester detained, at risk of torture in Iraq: ‘Ala Nabil | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 An Iraqi political activist, ‘Ala Nabil, was detained after a protest in Baghdad on 8 April 2011. His whereabouts are unknown and Amnesty International fears that he and other detained activists are at high risk of torture. Other protestors are known to have recently been tortured in detention."
