"Sudan: Fear for safety/ Fear of torture and ill-treatment 
 Sudan: Fear for safety/ Fear of torture and ill-treatment | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is gravely concerned for the safety of Bahar El Din Shibou and Hamid Adam Hassan Abdelrahman, following their arrest by government soldiers. They were reportedly last seen being transferred by helicopter to an unknown location, where they are at risk of torture or ill-treatment.
