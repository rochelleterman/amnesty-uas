"USA: Further information on death penalty / Legal concern, Ernest Paul McCarver 
 USA: Further information on death penalty / Legal concern, Ernest Paul McCarver

 | Amnesty International 
 On 26 March 2001, in the case of Ernest McCarver, the US Supreme Court agreed to consider whether the execution of people with mental retardation violates the US Constitution's Eighth Amendment ban on cruel and unusual punishment. It last reviewed this issue in 1989, when by five votes to four it ruled that it was not unconstitutional to execute such prisoners.
