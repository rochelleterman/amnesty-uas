"Further information on EXTRA 97/93 (AMR 51/95/93, 8 November) - USA (Tennessee): Wayne Bates 
 Further information on EXTRA 97/93 (AMR 51/95/93, 8 November) - USA (Tennessee): Wayne Bates | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Wayne Bates, who was scheduled to be executed on 1 December 1993 is now due to be executed on 28 February 1994. If it is carried out, this will be the first execution in Tennessee since 1960. Amnesty International is trying to confirm information that the execution was postponed pending a hearing on whether Bates is competent to be executed."
