"Indonesia: Further information on fear for safety 
 Indonesia: Further information on fear for safety | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International has just received confirmation that Haryanto Taslam reappeared in mid-April 1998.  It is strongly believed that he was held in custody since 9 March. N.A. Chairal and Hamandi remain in police custody but have now been permitted legal council.
