"Iran: Death penalty: Ahmed Bakhtari 
 Iran: Death penalty: Ahmed Bakhtari | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Ahmed Bakhtari, aged 40, subject of an urgent action issued in December 1993 (UA 428/93, MDE 13/15/93), is again at risk of execution. He has reportedly been sentenced to death for the second time by a Revolutionary Court in Teheran, on charges including activities for an illegal opposition group, sedition and armed robbery. The sentence has yet to be confirmed by Iran's Supreme Court."
