 Nepal: Fear for safety/ possible ""disappearance"" | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is concerned for the safety of Khadka Singh Tamang, and Madhu Kumar Chaulagain who were reportedly arrested in Kathmandu on 26 November 2003 and 17 December 2003 respectively. Their whereabouts are unknown.
