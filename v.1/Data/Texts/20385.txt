 Mexico: Attacked Indigenous women risk reprisals: Further information | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 On 7 September 2010, armed men attacked two Indigenous women from the San Juan Copala town in the southern Mexican state of Oaxaca. One was raped and the other one was shot at. After reporting the attack to the authorities, they and their families could be at risk of reprisals. "
