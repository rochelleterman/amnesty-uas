 Nepal: Further information on 'Disappearance'/fear for safety/torture | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International has received information that Deepak Kumar Chaudhary was released after spending 15 days in detention at Balaju no. 1 Bahini army barracks, Kathmandu. He was reportedly released on condition that he report to the barracks every 15 days.
