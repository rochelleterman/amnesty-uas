"UA 171/94 - Armenia: fear of imminent execution: two prisoners of war from Azerbaijan 
 UA 171/94 - Armenia: fear of imminent execution: two prisoners of war from Azerbaijan | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Two prisoners of war face imminent execution in Armenia after being convicted of murder. They were reportedly among a group of eight tried in Yerevan in proceedings believed to have finished in April 1994. AI fears that they may be executed, especially in the light of recent deaths in Armenia of other prisoners of war."
