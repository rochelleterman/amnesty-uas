"Israel and the Occupied Territories: Torture / fear of torture: Fu'ad Mahmoud Salim Shamasneh (ID No. 90780482) 
 Israel and the Occupied Territories: Torture / fear of torture: Fu'ad Mahmoud Salim Shamasneh (ID No. 90780482) | Amnesty International 
 The above, currently detained at al-Jelameh/Kishon Prison, is reported to be suffering torture including sleep deprivation and beatings. Fu'ad Shamasneh, a Palestinian student of engineering at Bir Zeit University, was arrested by the Israeli security forces on 9 April 1996 and immediately served with a six-month administrative detention order.
