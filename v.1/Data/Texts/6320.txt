"UA 152/94 - Guatemala: fear for safety: Pedro Antonio Asturias Ruiz 
 UA 152/94 - Guatemala: fear for safety: Pedro Antonio Asturias Ruiz | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Pedro Antonio Asturias Ruiz is currently advisor to the Permanent Commissions of the Respresntatives for the Return of the Guatemalan Refugees in Mexico. He has reportedly been subjected to surveillance and other intimidation including death threats since 7 April 1994. AI is seriously concerned for his safety."
