 Colombia: Possible ""disappearance"" / fear for safety | Amnesty International 
 Francisco Patermina, Luz Marina Negrette (f) and family: On 28 February 1997 Francisco Patermina was reportedly abducted by members of a paramilitary group and there are grave concerns for his safety. There is also concern for the safety of his wife, Luz Marina Negrette, who has denounced his ""disappearance"".
