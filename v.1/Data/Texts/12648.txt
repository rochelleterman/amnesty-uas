"Nepal: Further Information on Fear for safety/fear of ""disappearance"" 
 Nepal: Further Information on Fear for safety/fear of ""disappearance"" | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International has received reports that Sanjiv Kumar Karna was seen in custody at Dharapani army barracks in Dhanusha district in early December 2003. There are unconfirmed reports that he was later held at the Maharajgunj army barracks in Kathmandu.
