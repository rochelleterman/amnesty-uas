 USA (Illinois): Child sentenced to life seeks clemency: Jacqueline Montanez | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 On 11 April 2012 the Illinois Prisoner Review Board will review the clemency application of Jacqueline Montanez, the only woman in that state serving a sentence of life imprisonment without the possibility of parole for a crime committed as a child. She has been in prison for more than half her life."
