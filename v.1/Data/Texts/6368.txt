"Guatemala: Fear for safety: David Herrera (m), journalist 
 Guatemala: Fear for safety: David Herrera (m), journalist

 | Amnesty International 
 Journalist, David Herrera, was abducted by gunmen on 10 April, but managed to escape. The abduction appears to have been linked to his work assisting foreign journalists and film crews visiting the country, many of them to investigate human rights issues. Amnesty International believes his life is in danger.
