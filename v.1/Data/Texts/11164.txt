 India: Further information on Fear for safety - Human rights defenders in Andhra Pradesh 

 | Amnesty International 
 The Andhra Pradesh government has ordered a judicial inquiry into the killing of human rights defender Azam Ali, who was working with the Andhra Pradesh Civil Liberties Committee (APCLC). The Home Minister announced the inquiry in a statement to the Legislative Assembly, in which he condemned the killing. Amnesty International is concerned that human rights defenders in the area are in grave danger.
