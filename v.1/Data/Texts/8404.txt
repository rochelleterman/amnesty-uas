 Trinidad & Tobago: Fear of torture or ill-treatment | Amnesty International 
 Seven named Liberian men have sought asylum in Trinidad and Tobago, but have been detained as 'prohibited immigrants'. Amnesty International is concerned about the conditions in which they are being detained and fears that they will be forcibly returned to Liberia where they could be at risk of torture.
