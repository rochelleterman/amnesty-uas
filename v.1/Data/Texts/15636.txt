"Russia: Further information on Health concern/Denial of medical treatment: Mikhail Ivanovich Trepashkin (m), lawyer 
 Russia: Further information on Health concern/Denial of medical treatment: Mikhail Ivanovich Trepashkin (m), lawyer | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International continues to receive information that Mikhail Trepashkin, who is imprisoned at IK 13, an open prison colony located in Sverdlovsk Region, is being denied access to adequate medical treatment for chronic, life-threatening asthma. His lawyers informed Amnesty International that Mikhail Trepashkin has asthma attacks nearly every day.
