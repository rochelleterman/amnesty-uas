"Burundi: Further information on fear for safety 
 Burundi: Further information on fear for safety | Amnesty International 
 Jean-Baptiste Bigirimana, catechist of the Roman Catholic church, Jovin (family name not known), deacon of the Roman Catholic church: The above were released without charge on 2 November 1996. They had not been seen since their apparent kidnapping by members of the Burundi security forces.
