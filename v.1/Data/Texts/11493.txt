"Indonesia: Further information on Fear of torture or ill-treatment/ Unfair trial 
 Indonesia: Further information on Fear of torture or ill-treatment/ Unfair trial | Amnesty International 
 Seven lawyers and human rights defenders involved in the trials of 23 men arrested following violence in March in Papua province have been intimidated and have received death threats. Amnesty International is concerned that they may be in danger. They are Johnson Panjaitan, Ecoline Situmorang, David Sitorus, Aloysius Renwarin, Adolf Stef Waramory, Frederika Korain and Yustina Haluk.
