"Nepal: Further information on  ""disappearance"" / fear of torture: Rajendra Dhakal 
 Nepal: Further information on  ""disappearance"" / fear of torture: Rajendra Dhakal | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International remains concerned for the safety of      Rajendra Dhakal, a lawyer and human rights defender,      whose whereabouts are unknown following his arrest   by     police on 8 January 1999. There are fears that he  may be  tortured in custody.
