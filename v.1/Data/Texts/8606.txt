 USA (Texas): Death penalty / legal concern: Joseph John Cannon | Amnesty International 
 Joseph John Cannon is scheduled to be executed in Texas on 22 April 1998 for the murder of Anne Walsh in 1977.  He was 17 years old at the time of the crime and a diagnosed schizophrenic with a highly disturbed background. AI is urging the State of Texas to find a more humane response to the severity of his crime.
