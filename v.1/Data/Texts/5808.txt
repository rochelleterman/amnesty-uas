"UA 129/94 - El Salvador: apparent political killing / fear for safety: Heriberto Galicia 
 UA 129/94 - El Salvador: apparent political killing / fear for safety: Heriberto Galicia | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Heriberto Galicia, a trade unionist and an opposition candidate in the recent parliamentary elections, was shot dead by unidentified individuals on 27 March 1994 near his home in San Miguel. An unsuccessful candidate for the National Revolutionary Movement (MNR), he had reportedly received anonymous death threats."
