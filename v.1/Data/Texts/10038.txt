"USA (Texas): Further Information on: Death penalty / Legal concern: Frances Elaine Newton 
 USA (Texas): Further Information on: Death penalty / Legal concern: Frances Elaine Newton
 | Amnesty International 
 Frances Newton was given a 120-day reprieve by Texas Governor Rick Perry on 1 December, a few hours before she was scheduled to be executed. A day earlier, the state Board of Pardons and Paroles had recommended by five votes to one that Governor Perry grant the reprieve.
