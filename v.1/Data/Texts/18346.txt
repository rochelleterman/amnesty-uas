 Saudi Arabia: Preventive death penalty appeal | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is deeply alarmed at the dramatic   increase in executions in Saudi Arabia. Ninety people     have been executed so far this year (seven have been      executed in the last ten days). Fifty-eight of those        executed have been  foreign nationals.
