"Colombia: Further information on fear for safety 
 Colombia: Further information on fear for safety | Amnesty International 
 Carlos Ulloa, Stella Guerra, Human Rights workers with    the Intercongregational Commission for Justice and Peace. Francisco Javier Trujillo and other members of the        Association of relatives of the Victims of Trujillo. 
 Carlos Ulloa, Stella Guerra, Human Rights workers with    the Intercongregational Commission for Justice and Peace. Francisco Javier Trujillo and other members of the        Association of relatives of the Victims of Trujillo.
Recent death threats, reportedly by paramilitary groups,  have forced a non-governmental human rights organization  to close one of its offices. Amnesty International is     concerned that intimidation by paramilitary groups is     threatening the work of human rights organisations in     Colombia.
