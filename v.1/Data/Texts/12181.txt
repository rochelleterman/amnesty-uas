"Malaysia: Fear of ill-treatment/incommunicado detention/prisoner of conscience: Mohamad Ezam Mohamad Nor 
 Malaysia: Fear of ill-treatment/incommunicado detention/prisoner of conscience: Mohamad Ezam Mohamad    Nor | Amnesty International 
 Opposition activist Mohamad Ezam Mohamad Nor was arrested on 5 March, outside the home of prisoner of conscience Anwar Ibrahim, the former Deputy Prime Minister. He is now held incommunicado, and Amnesty International believes he may be severely ill-treated.
