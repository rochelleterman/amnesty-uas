 Guatemala: Fear For Safety: Monsignor Alvaro Ramazzini (m)  | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Monsignor Alvaro Ramazzini, the head of the church in the department of San Marcos, some 250 kms east of Guatemala City has been threatened.  Amnesty International believes this latest threat to his safety is linked to his work in defence of human rights."
