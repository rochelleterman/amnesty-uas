 Syria: Further information on fear of torture | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International has recently received information that Heba al-Khaled, her sister Rola al-Khaled and Nadia al-Satour were released from detention towards the end of 2005. No further information is available about the circumstances of their release, nor the conditions of their detention.
