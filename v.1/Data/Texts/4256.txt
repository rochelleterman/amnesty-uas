"UA 14/93 - Colombia: attempted extrajudicial execution / fear for safety: Rafael Duarte Ortiz 
 UA 14/93 - Colombia: attempted extrajudicial execution / fear for safety: Rafael Duarte Ortiz | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 AI is concerned about an attempt on the life of Father Rafael Duarte Ortiz, parish priest of Carcasi in the Magdalena Medio region. He was reportedly fired at by two members of the national police at 7 pm on 8 January 1993. The shots missed and he managed to escape. Father Rafael Duarte had recently denounced human rights violations in Carcasi by members of the Colombian armed forces. "
