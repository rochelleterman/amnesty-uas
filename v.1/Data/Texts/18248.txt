 Palestinian Authority:  Death penalty:  Muhammad Daoud Khawajeh | Amnesty International 
   Muhammad Daoud Khawajeh, aged 19 has been tried and     sentenced to death, two days after he was arrested.       Amnesty  International believes his trial was unfair.     President    Yasser Arafat must ratify the death sentence before it    can be carried out.
