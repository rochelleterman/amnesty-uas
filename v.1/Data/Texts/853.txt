"Ethiopia: Further information on Fear for safety / Possible prisoners of conscience / Harassment of human rights defenders 
 Ethiopia: Further information on Fear for safety / Possible prisoners of conscience / Harassment of human rights defenders | Amnesty International 
 The trial of Professor Mesfin Wolde Mariam and Dr Berhanu Nega has been adjourned until 4 December 2001. Amnesty International considers that both these men would be prisoners of conscience if convicted and is calling for all charges against them to be dropped.
