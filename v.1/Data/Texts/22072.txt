 Iran: Further information: Hossein Ronaghi Maleki free on medical leave | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Iranian blogger Hossein Ronaghi Maleki was granted leave from Tehran’s Evin Prison after payment of bail on 2 July 2012, approximately three weeks after undergoing an operation he needed on his kidney. Amnesty International is calling for Hossein Ronaghi Maleki’s allegations of torture or other ill-treatment to be investigated immediately and impartially."
