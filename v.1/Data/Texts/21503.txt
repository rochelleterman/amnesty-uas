 Russian Federation: Uzbek man believed to be forcibly disappeared: Azamatzhon Ermakov | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Azamatzhon Ermakov has disappeared after being released from detention on 2 November in Nizhnii Novgorod, Russia. There are serious grounds to believe he has been abducted and forcibly returned to Uzbekistan. He is at risk of torture, unfair trial, and other human rights violations in Uzbekistan."
