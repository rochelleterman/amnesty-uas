"Further information on UA 97/93 (ASA 20/18/93, 2 April) - India: fear of extrajudicial execution: Dr Abdul Ahad Guru, Ashiq Hussain 
 Further information on UA 97/93 (ASA 20/18/93, 2 April) - India: fear of extrajudicial execution: Dr Abdul Ahad Guru, Ashiq Hussain | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Amnesty International has now received photographs of the shooting of Mr Ashiq Hussain during the funeral procession of his brother-in-law Dr Abdul Ahad Guru. The Kashmir police have reportedly said that his killing was an accident, but so far no independent inquiry is known to have been carried out into his death "
