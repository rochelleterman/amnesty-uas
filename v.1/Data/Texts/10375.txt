"Further information on UA 364/93 (ASA 13/14/93, 11 October) - Bangladesh: death threats: Taslima Nasrin 
 Further information on UA 364/93 (ASA 13/14/93, 11 October) - Bangladesh: death threats: Taslima Nasrin | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 It has been reported that two armed police guards have now started a round- the-clock protective vigil outside the home of Taslima Nasrin. She is under a death threat by an Islamist group, the Council of Soldiers of Islam. There is still concern, however, that no action has been taken to bring to justice those issuing the threat. "
