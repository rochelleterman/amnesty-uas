"USA (Texas): Further information on death penalty: Vincent Gutierrez (m), Hispanic 
 USA (Texas): Further information on death penalty: Vincent Gutierrez (m), Hispanic | Amnesty International 
 Vincent Gutierrez, aged 28, was executed in Texas on the evening of 28 March. He had been sentenced to death in 1998 for the murder of Jose Cobo in 1997. Vincent Gutierrez was 18 years old at the time of the murder.
