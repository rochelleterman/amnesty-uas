"Cameroon: Further information on prisoner of conscience / fear of further POCs: Pius Njawe 
 Cameroon: Further information on prisoner of conscience / fear of further POCs: Pius Njawe | Amnesty International 
 Pius Njawe, editor-in-chief of the newspaper Le Messager, was released on bail on 15 November 1996. Amnesty International remains concerned that he was a prisoner of conscience and that freedom of expression remains under attack in Cameroon.
