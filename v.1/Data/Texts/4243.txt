"Colombia: Fear for safety / ""disappearances"" / extrajudicial executions: Civilian population of Ituango, department of Antioquia 
 NA 
 There is a serious concern for the safety of the civilian population of the municipality of Ituango, whose members have been subjected to ""disappearances"", extrajudicial executions and death threats since the arrival of a paramilitary group in the area on 30 October 1996. Latest reports indicate that more than 120 people have been killed and over 30 ""disappeared"" by the group Peasant Farmer Self-Defence of Cordoba and Uraba (ACCU).
