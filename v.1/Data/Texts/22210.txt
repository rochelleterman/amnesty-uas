 Iran: Further information on Arbitrary arrest/fear of torture or other ill-treatment: Nahid Kalhor  | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Student Nahid Kalhor was released on bail on 11 June 2008 from the Ministry of Intelligence detention centre in the south-western city of Qom. After being arrested on 1 June, Nahid Kalhor was held in solitary confinement for the first three days of her custody. "
