"Laos: Further information on Fear of torture/fear for safety 
 Laos: Further information on Fear of torture/fear for safety | Amnesty International 
 On 9 July, Pastor Naw Karl Mua, Thierry Falise and Vincent Reynaud were released from prison, escorted to Vientiane airport, and flown from Laos to neighbouring Thailand. Their release followed negotiations between foreign diplomats from the USA, Belgium and France and the Lao government. All had been serving 15-year prison sentences, imposed after an unfair trial on 30 June.
