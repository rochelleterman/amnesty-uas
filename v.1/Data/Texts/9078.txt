"USA (Missouri): Death penalty 
 USA (Missouri): Death penalty | Amnesty International 
 William T Boliek Jr, Ralph Feltrop, Donald E Reese, Andrew W Six: There is concern that the above men are scheduled to be executed in August 1997 in Missouri after being sentenced to death for murder. There is further concern that mitigating evidence relating to psychiatric disorders was not heard in the cases of two of the defendants.
