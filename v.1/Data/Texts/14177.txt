 Turkey: Further information on fear for safety/ risk of torture   and ill-treatment: Fehime Ete | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International remains concerned for the safety of Fehime Ete who was returned to Bitlis Prison on 14 December 2001 from gendarmerie headquarters in Diyarbak r, southeast Turkey, where she had been reportedly tortured and ill-treated during interrogation.
