"Chile: Further information on fear for safety / death threats: Carmen Soria 
 Chile: Further information on fear for safety / death threats: Carmen Soria | Amnesty International 
 Carmen Soria and her family have left Chile for a place of safety following a number of telephone death threats she received in October 1998. The threats are thought to be in connection with her campaign to bring to justice those who murdered her father in July 1976.
