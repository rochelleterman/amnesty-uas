"Mexico: Further information on ""disappearance"" / fear of torture / possible prisoner of conscience: Ignacio Fernando Nino Garcia 
 Mexico: Further information on ""disappearance"" / fear of torture / possible prisoner of conscience: Ignacio Fernando Nino Garcia | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International has learned that Professor Ignacio Fernando Nino Garcia, a human rights defender and teacher, who ""disappeared"" after his reported arrest during a peaceful demonstration on 21 March 1997, has ""reappeared"" and is currently working in Oaxaca.
