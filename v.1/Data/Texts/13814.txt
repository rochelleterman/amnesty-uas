"Russia/Uzbekistan: Further information on fear of forcible repatriation / possible prisoner of conscience: Albert Kayumovich Musin 
 Russia/Uzbekistan: Further information on fear of forcible repatriation / possible prisoner of conscience: Albert Kayumovich Musin | Amnesty International 
 Albert Musin was released from the custody of the Moscow police on 6 March and is currently at liberty. It is not clear if his release was the result of a formal renunciation by the Uzbekistani authorities that they are seeking his extradition.
