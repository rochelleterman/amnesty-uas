"Kazakstan: Further information on fear of imminent execution: Oleg Amiranovich Gorozashvili 
 Kazakstan: Further information on fear of imminent execution: Oleg Amiranovich Gorozashvili | Amnesty International 
 Oleg Gorozashvili, who has been sentenced to death for murder, is still alive, but remains on death row. He awaits a decision by the authorities on whether to commute his death sentence or to begin judicial proceedings which might lead to the sentence being overturned.
