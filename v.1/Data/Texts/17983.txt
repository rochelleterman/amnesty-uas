"Jordan: Further information on death penalty 
 Jordan: Further information on death penalty | Amnesty International 
 'Umar Saleh was executed for murder on 22 October 1998. His sons, Na'im Saleh and Nash'at Saleh had their death sentences commuted to life imprisonment by the Court of Cassation in the Autumn of 1997.
