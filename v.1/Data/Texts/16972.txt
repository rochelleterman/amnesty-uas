 Further information on UA 107/93 (MDE 13/05/93, 8 April) - Iran: death penalty: Salim Saberniah, Mustafa Ghaderi (note correction) | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 There are fears that the above men, who were reportedly sentenced to death by the Islamic Revolutionary Court of Tabriz in March 1993, continue to be at risk of execution. They are apparently still held in Tabriz Prison, but no further information on their current situation is available "
