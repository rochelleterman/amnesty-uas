"USA (California): Death penalty / legal concern: Thomas Thompson 
 USA (California): Death penalty / legal concern: Thomas Thompson | Amnesty International 
 Thomas Thompson is scheduled to be executed in California on 5 August 1997 after being sentenced to death for rape and murder in 1981. There is concern that he may be executed despite grave doubts concerning his guilt.
