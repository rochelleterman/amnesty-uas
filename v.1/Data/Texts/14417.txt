 Turkey:         Risk of torture or ill-treatment | Amnesty International 
 Twenty-one students have been arrested and are feared to have been ill-treated. They are being held incommunicado at the Diyabakir police headquarters in southeastern Turkey, where they are at risk of torture and further ill-treatment
