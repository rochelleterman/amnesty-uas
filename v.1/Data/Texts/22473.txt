 Israel/OPT: Further information: Hunger strikers in grave danger | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Hassan Safadi and Samer al-Barq have been on hunger strike since 21 June and 22 May respectively, in protest at their detention without charge.  They are held in solitary confinement in a prison clinic in Israel which cannot provide the specialist care they need, and have no access to independent doctors."
