 Iran: Human rights defender risks torture: Shadi Sadr (f) | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Human rights defender Shadi Sadr was abducted on 17 July 2009 in Tehran, by men believed to be security officials. She was most probably detained because of her human rights activities after the disputed presidential election. She is at risk of torture; she is a prisoner of conscience."
