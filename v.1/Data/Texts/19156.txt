"Tunisia: Further information on health concern/fear for safety: Mohammed Akrout 
 Tunisia: Further information on health concern/fear for safety: Mohammed Akrout | Amnesty International 
 Political prisoner Mohammed Akrout ended his hunger strike on Friday 28 July after receiving promises from the prison director that he will be allowed to see a doctor. In addition, he has been told that visiting times will be increased and that he will be able to receive books.
