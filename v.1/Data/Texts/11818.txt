"Indonesia / East Timor: Fear of torture / possible prisoners of conscience: Two youths (names unknown) 
 Indonesia / East Timor: Fear of torture / possible prisoners of conscience: Two youths (names unknown) | Amnesty International 
 Two unidentified East Timorese have been arrested following a peaceful pro-independence demonstration in Dili, East Timor, on 25 November 1996. Amnesty International fears that the two, whom it believes may have been arrested solely for the peaceful expression of their views, are at risk of torture or ill-treatment.
