"The Maldives: Further information on denial of medical treatment / deteriorating health conditions and new concern: fear of torture: Ismail Saadiq 
 The Maldives: Further information on denial of medical treatment / deteriorating health conditions and new concern: fear of torture: Ismail Saadiq | Amnesty International 
 Ismail Saadiq, who has reportedly been held in incommunicado detention since early February 1998, could be at serious risk of torture. He is reportedly being denied access to adequate medical treatment although his medical condition is understood to be deteriorating.
