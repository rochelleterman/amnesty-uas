"USA (Alabama): Further information on Death penalty / Legal concern: Geramie Hart 
 USA (Alabama): Further information on Death penalty / Legal concern: Geramie     Hart | Amnesty International 
 On 13 June, a jury in Jefferson County, Alabama, convicted 17-year-old Geramie Hart of capital murder and recommended a sentence of life imprisonment without the possibility of parole. The prosecution had sought a death sentence.
