"Nepal: Further Information on Fear for safety / possible ""disappearance"" 
 Nepal: Further Information on Fear for safety / possible  ""disappearance"" | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International has received information that Bhumi Chamling has been released from detention after spending approximately three months in custody since his detention on 11 October 2003. There is no further information about the whereabouts of Keshav Singh Thakuri, who was arrested by members of the security forces on 4 November 2003.
