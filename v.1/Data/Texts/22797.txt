 Syria: Stop torture of mother of nine (closing text) | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 On 19 February, Mu'eyna Muhammad Yusef Sa'adu was         released without charge from the Military Intelligence    Head Quarters in the capital, Damascus. She had been      detained since 14 January following her arrest by Syrian  military security officers as she re-entered the country, after 24 years as an exile in Jordan. She had apparently  been granted a passport and given permission to return to Syria by its embassy in Amman, Jordan.                    "
