"UA 172/94 - Albania: death penalty: Laver Goga 
 UA 172/94 - Albania: death penalty: Laver Goga | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Laver Goga, a 48-year-old teacher, from the village of Ngjeqar, has been sentenced to death by the district court of Fier, according to a press report of 27 April 1994, after being found guilty of premeditated murder."
