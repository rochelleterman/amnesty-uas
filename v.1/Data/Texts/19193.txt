"Further information on UA 236/94 (MDE 30/18/94, 17 June) - Tunisia: possible prisoner of conscience / fear of unfair trial: Najib Hosni 
 Further information on UA 236/94 (MDE 30/18/94, 17 June) - Tunisia: possible prisoner of conscience / fear of unfair trial: Najib Hosni | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Najib Hosni, a human rights lawyer arrested on 15 June 1994, remains detained without trial and without access to his lawyers. He is detained on charges of falsifying a land contract, but it is believed that he may be a prisoner of conscience detained for his work as a human rights lawyer."
