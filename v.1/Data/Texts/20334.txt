 Mexico: Fear for safety: Cristina Auerbach Benavides (f) | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Cristina Auerbach Benavides, a human rights defender from Mexico City, foiled an attempt to put her life at risk. Through her work with Catholic Church organization, Pastoral Laboral, she has been at the forefront of the campaign to obtain justice for the families of 65 miners who died in the Pasta de Conchos coalmine explosion in the northern state of Coahuila in February 2006.
