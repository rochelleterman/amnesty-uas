"UA 144/94 - Colombia: fear of ""disappearance"": Alvaro Jose Causado Marquez 
 UA 144/94 - Colombia: fear of ""disappearance"": Alvaro Jose Causado Marquez | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Alvaro Jose Causado Marquez, a peasant leader working with the National Peasants' Association and a left-wing political leader, was abducted from his home in El Pinal in Sucre department on 10 April 1994 and has not been seen since. Alvaro Causado had previously reported being the threatened and harassed byu the security forces. The security forces deny that they are holding him."
