"Cuba: Death penalty: Norberto Mejias Cabrera or Norberto Cabrera Mejias 
 Cuba: Death penalty: Norberto Mejias Cabrera or Norberto Cabrera Mejias | Amnesty International 
 Cuban press reports indicate that the above has been sentenced to death after being found guilty of four rapes, robbery and other offences. He is reported to be unemployed and homless and to have been detained in ""re-education centres"" since the age of twelve.
