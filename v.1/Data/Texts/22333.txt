 Iran: Further Information on Fear of Torture/ Prisoner of conscience/ Medical concern: Mohammad Sadiq Kabudvand (m) | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Mohammad Sadiq Kabudvand is believed to have suffered a heart attack on 17 December 2008. He was taken to the medical facility in Evin Prison, but no doctor was available as it was a national holiday in Iran. Amnesty International is concerned that he is not receiving adequate medical care."
