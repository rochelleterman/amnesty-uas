"East Timor: Possible arbitrary arrest / fear of torture or ill-treatment: Rui Campus 
 East Timor: Possible arbitrary arrest / fear of torture or ill-treatment: Rui Campus | Amnesty International 
 Rui Campus, a 20-year-old East Timorese student who was arrested in late January, remains in incommunicado detention in Los Palos.  It is not known if any charges have been brought against him. His family have been refused access to him and he has not yet seen a lawyer heightening concerns that he may be at risk of torture or ill-treatment.
