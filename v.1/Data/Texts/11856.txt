"Indonesia: Fear for safety / incommunicado detention: Muhammad Nasir Ishak 
 Indonesia: Fear for safety / incommunicado detention: Muhammad Nasir Ishak | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is concerned for the safety of      Muhammad Nasir Ishak, who was arrested by the Indonesian  National Army (TNI) on 12 August 1999. He is detained     incommunicado and is therefore at risk of torture or      ill-treatment.
