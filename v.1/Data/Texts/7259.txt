"Further information on UA 336/92 (AMR 41/08/92, 29 October) - Mexico: death threats: Maria Teresa Jardi 
 Further information on UA 336/92 (AMR 41/08/92, 29 October) - Mexico: death threats: Maria Teresa Jardi | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Maria Teresa Jardi, human rights lawyer and director of the Department of Solidarity and Defence of Human Rights of the Archdiocese of Mexico, has continued to receive death threats; the last threat was dated 5 December 1992. It is believed that her investigations into human rights violations which have suggested the involvement of law enforcement agents may be behind the threats. An investigation into the threats has been commissioned. "
