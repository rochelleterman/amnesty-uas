"Further information on UA 54/93 (AFR 32/06/93, 2 March) - Kenya: ill- treatment / legal concern: John Makanga 
 Further information on UA 54/93 (AFR 32/06/93, 2 March) - Kenya: ill- treatment / legal concern: John Makanga | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 John Makanga was relased on bail on about 10 March 1993. He is currently in hospital for treatment for injuries caused by beatings in police custody. He is charged with distributing a seditious document. No date has been set for the trial. "
