"Iran: Arbitrary arrest/possible prisoner of conscience/medical concern: Mansour Ossanlu (m) 
 Iran: Arbitrary arrest/possible prisoner of conscience/medical concern: Mansour Ossanlu (m)

 | Amnesty International 
 Mansour Ossanlu, the Head of the Union of Workers of the Tehran and Suburbs Bus Company (Sherkat-e Vahed) has been detained since 22 December 2005 in Section 209 of Evin Prison in the capital, Tehran. He may be a prisoner of conscience, detained solely on account of his peaceful trade union activities.
