"Bahamas: Further information on fear of imminent execution and new concern: death penalty 
 Bahamas: Further information on fear of imminent execution and new concern: death penalty | Amnesty International 
 Roger Chad Goodman, Anatole Mcquay, Sean Poitier and Alexander Williams: These individuals, scheduled to be hanged on 13 January 1998, have all received reprieves as a result of legal action taken on their behalf. Executions cannot now take place until fresh warrants are issued.
