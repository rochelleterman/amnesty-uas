 Sudan:  Fear for Safety/Fear of Torture/Forced Relocation | Amnesty International 
 Security forces seized 36 people from Otash Camp for internally displaced persons (IDPs) in South Darfur on the evening of 29 October 2007. They are in danger of being tortured or otherwise ill-treated in detention.
