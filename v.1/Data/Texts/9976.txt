"USA (Nevada): Further information on Death penalty: William Patrick Castillo (m) 
 USA (Nevada): Further information on Death penalty: William Patrick Castillo (m) | Amnesty International 
 An hour and a half before William Castillo, aged 34, was to be put to death in Nevada on the evening of 15 October 2007, the state Supreme Court issued a stay, to give it more time to consider questions surrounding Nevada's use of lethal injection.
