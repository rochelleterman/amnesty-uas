 EXTRA 59/94 - USA (Texas): death penalty: Clifton Charles Russell, Mario Marquez | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Clifton Charles Russell, white, is sheduled to be executed in Texas on 18 October 1994. According to reports he comes from a background marked by poverty and violence. Mario Marquez, hispanic, is scheduled to be executed on 26 October. He was allegedly physically abused by his father as a child and was found to be mentally retarded at trial."
