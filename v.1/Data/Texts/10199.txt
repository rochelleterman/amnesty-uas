"Venezuela: Fear for safety / Possible extrajudicial execution 
 Venezuela: Fear for safety / Possible extrajudicial execution | Amnesty International 
 There are fears for the safety of Yolima Rangel (f), Ali Eduardo Sojo, aged 14, Katiuska Sojo (f) and Flor Diaz (f) who are family members of Freddy Diaz who was shot dead, reportedly by a policeman, on 8 July 1998. The family have been threatened and followed by police since reporting the death.
