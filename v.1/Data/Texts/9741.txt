"USA : Further information on fear of indiscriminate mass killing of civilians in Iraq 
 USA : Further information on fear of indiscriminate mass killing of civilians in Iraq | Amnesty International 
 Air strikes against Iraq by UK and US forces were halted on 19 December 1998. Reports vary as to the number of civilian casualties . Houses, hospitals and schools are also reportedly destroyed or damaged.
