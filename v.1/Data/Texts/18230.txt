"Palestinian Authority: Prisoner of conscience / torture or  ill-treatment: Maher al-Dasuqi 
 Palestinian Authority: Prisoner of conscience / torture or  ill-treatment: Maher al-Dasuqi | Amnesty International 
 Maher al-Dasuqi, who hosts a popular television program   broadcast by two local Palestinian stations, was arrested on 15 September 1999, shortly after a Palestinian         prisoner's mother criticized the Palestinian Authority on his program. Amnesty International considers him a        prisoner of conscience.
