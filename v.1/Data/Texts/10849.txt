"China: Further information on: Fear of torture or ill-treatment/medical concern 
 China: Further information on: Fear of torture or ill-treatment/medical concern | Amnesty International 
 Wang Guofeng and Li Suzhi have been released. According to Li Suzhi, the couple were detained for sending children to the school established by their fellow HIV/AIDS activist Li Dan for children whose parents were HIV-positive or who had died of AIDS.
