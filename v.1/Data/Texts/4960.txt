 Colombia: Fear for safety/Death threats | Amnesty International 
 Marqueza Arrieta, whose son is a prominent trade unionist, has received death threats from men believed to be paramilitaries. The real target is believed to be her son, Domingo Tovar Arrieta, a leading trade union activist. His entire family are thought to be in danger.
