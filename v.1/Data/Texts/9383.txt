 USA (Texas): death penalty: Fletcher Mann, Ronald Allridge, James Meanes | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Fletcher Mann, white, Ronald Allridge, black, and James Meanes, black, are scheduled to be executed in Texas on 1 June, 8 June and 20 June 1995 respectively. Ronald Allridge was sentenced to death on 18 November 1985 for murder. There is currently no further information about James Meanes and Fletcher Mann."
