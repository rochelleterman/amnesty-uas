 Belarus: Fear of torture or ill-treatment / possible ""disappearance"" | Amnesty International 
 There is serious concern for the safety of prominent      opposition leader Viktor Gonchar and his companion        Anatoly Krasovsky, who failed to return home on 16        September 1999. Amnesty International fears that they may be in incommunicado detention where they would be at risk of torture, ill-treatment or possible ""disappearance"".
