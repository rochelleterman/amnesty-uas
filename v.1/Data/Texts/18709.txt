 Syria: Further information on Fear of torture/incommunicado detention/prisoner of conscience: Fateh Jamus (m), human rights defender 

 | Amnesty International 
 Prisoner of conscience Fateh Jamus, aged 57, was released from 'Adra prison, near the Syrian capital, Damascus, on 12 October. He was released by the Court of First Instance on bail of 500 Syrian Lira (around $US2).
