"Further information on UA 398/94 (MDE 13/10/94, 11 November) - Iran: fear of torture: Retired General Azizollah Amir Rahimi, Mehrdad Amir Rahimi, his son 
 Further information on UA 398/94 (MDE 13/10/94, 11 November) - Iran: fear of torture: Retired General Azizollah Amir Rahimi, Mehrdad Amir Rahimi, his son | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Fears for the safety of Retired General Azizollah Amir Rahimi have been heightened by the death in custody on 27 November 1994 of another critic of the Iranian government, Ali Akbar Saidi-Sirjani. There is no further information on the detention of his son, Mehrdad Amir Rahimi. His whereabouts in custody remain unknown and there are fears for his safety."
