"Democratic Republic of Congo (DRC): Fear for safety: Hubert Tshiswaka (m) 
 Democratic Republic of Congo (DRC): Fear for safety: Hubert Tshiswaka (m) | Amnesty International 
 Human rights activist Hubert Tshiswaka has been threatened by representatives of a political party led by the Minister of Justice, and on 1 April received an anonymous telephone death threat. Amnesty International believes his life may be in danger.
