 USA (Texas): Death Penalty/ Legal concern, Frances Elaine Newton | Amnesty International 
 Frances Newton is scheduled to be executed in Texas on 14 September 2005. She was convicted in October 1988 of the murder of her husband and two children in April 1987. She was convicted on circumstantial evidence and maintains that she did not commit the murder.
