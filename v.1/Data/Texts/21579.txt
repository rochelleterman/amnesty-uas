 Ukraine: Further Information possible ""disappearance"" - Georgiy Gongadze, journalist | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 A decapitated body believed to be that of missing         journalist Georgiy Gongadze has been found in Ukraine.

Independent journalist Georgiy Gongadze has been missing  since 16 September. Amnesty International believes he may have been secretly detained either by the authorities or  by one of the powerful interests he has offended with his journalism."
