"Guatemala: Death penalty: Manuel Martinez Coronado 
 Guatemala: Death penalty: Manuel Martinez Coronado | Amnesty International 
 On 12 June 1997 the Guatemalan Constitutional Court turned down the appeal of Manuel Martinez Coronado against his death sentence. He was sentenced to death in October 1996 for multiple homicide.
