 Nepal: Fear for safety / Possible ""disappearance"" | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is concerned for the safety of Karsang Dhundrup Lama and Tanka Prasad Tripathi, who were reportedly arrested by members of the security forces in plain clothes on 14 and 17 November respectively. Their whereabouts remain unknown.
