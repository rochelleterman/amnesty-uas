"USA (Texas): Further information on Death penalty / Legal concern, Gerald Lee Mitchell 
 USA (Texas): Further information on Death penalty / Legal concern, Gerald Lee    Mitchell | Amnesty International 
 Gerald Mitchell was executed in Texas on 22 October for a murder committed when he was aged 17. International law, respected in almost every country of the world, prohibits the death penalty against people who were under 18 at the time of the crime.
