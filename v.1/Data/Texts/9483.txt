"USA (Texas): Further information on death penalty / legal concern: Joseph Stanley Faulder 
 USA (Texas): Further information on death penalty / legal concern: Joseph Stanley Faulder | Amnesty International 
 Joseph Stanley Faulder, a Canadian national, is scheduled to be executed on 13 June 1997. Mitigating evidence in Faulder's defence was not presented to the jury that sentenced him to death. Lawyers have filed a ""class action"" faulting the Board of Pardons on behalf of the entire 445 prisoners under sentence of death in Texas.
