 China: Further information: Falun Gong woman risks extended term in RTL | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Falun Gong practitioner Li Shanshan (李珊珊) who is held in a re-education through labour (RTL) camp in northern China, is being threatened with an extended sentence for refusing to renounce her faith. She is at risk of torture and other ill-treatment."
