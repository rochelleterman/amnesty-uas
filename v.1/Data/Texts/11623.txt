 Indonesia: Fear of torture

 | Amnesty International 
 Four named men were arrested during a pro-independence ceremony on 7 July in the town of Wamena, Jayawijaya District, Papua province. One man, Iyut Heselo, was killed whilst Welmus Asso and Elias Asso were shot and are now in the local hospital.
