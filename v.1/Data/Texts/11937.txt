"Cambodia:Further information on Fear of forcible repatriation 
 Cambodia:Further information on Fear of forcible repatriation | Amnesty International 
 The Cambodian authorities have reportedly agreed to give temporary refuge to Vietnamese ethnic minority asylum-seekers. A long-term solution has not yet been decided. There is still no news of Dien Y Lien and his family, and it is feared that they have already been forcibly repatriated to Viet Nam.
