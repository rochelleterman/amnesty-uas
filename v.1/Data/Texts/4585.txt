 Colombia: Further information on: Possible extrajudicial execution / fear for safety: Aida Abella (f) | Amnesty International 
 The above survived an attempt on her life on 7 May, when several gunmen reportedly opened fire with a bazooka on her vehicle on a Bogota highway. She escaped without injury but one person was injured in the attack which is believed to have been carried out by a paramilitary group.
