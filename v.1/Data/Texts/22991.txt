 Yemen: Further information: Journalist detained incommunicado | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 A male Yemeni journalist, Muhammad al-Maqalih, was abducted in the capital, Sana’a, after criticizing the government over armed clashes in Sa’da province, northern Yemen. Muhammad al-Maqalih is now believed to be detained in the Political Security Prison in Sana’a. "
