"Mexico: Further information on fear of imminent deportation of human rights defenders 
 Mexico: Further information on fear of imminent deportation of human rights defenders | Amnesty International 
 Vilma Nunez de Escorcia (f), Nicaraguan national, Benjamin Cuellar, Salvadorean national; George Anne Porter (f) (note corrected name), US national: Amnesty International has learned that George Anne Porter, a member of the Unitarian Universalist Committee, was arbitrarily expelled from Mexico on 25 April 1997. Amnesty International continues to be seriously concerned at the curtailing by the authorities of the peaceful activities of international human rights defenders.
