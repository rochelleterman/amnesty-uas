 USA: Further information: US Supreme Court allows Arizona execution: Jeffrey Landrigan | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Jeffrey Landrigan, a 50-year-old Native American man, was executed in Arizona, USA, on 26 October. The execution went ahead after the Governor of Arizona denied clemency and the US Supreme Court decided by five votes to four to lift a stay imposed by a lower federal court."
