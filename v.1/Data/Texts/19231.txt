"Tunisia: Further information on ""disappearance"" / fear of torture / prisoners of conscience 
 Tunisia: Further information on ""disappearance"" / fear of torture / prisoners of conscience | Amnesty International 
 Ahmed Berramila, Rachid Ennajar, Monji Souab, Jilani Hammami: Ahmed Berramila was released on bail on 12 May 1997. The others remain detained as prisoners of conscience, and Amnesty International continues to call for the unconditional release of all four trade unionists as prisoners of conscience.
