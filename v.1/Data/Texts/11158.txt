 India: Further information on Fear for safety - Human rights defenders in Andhra Pradesh

 | Amnesty International 
 Another human rights defender, working with the Andhra Pradesh Civil Liberties Committee (APCLC), in the southern state of Andhra Pradesh has been killed and others have received threatening phone calls. Amnesty International is concerned that human rights defenders in the area are in grave danger.
