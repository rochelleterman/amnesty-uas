"Sudan: Fear for safety/ Fear of torture or ill-treatment 
 Sudan: Fear for safety/ Fear of torture or ill-treatment | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is concerned for the safety of 4    named students and an unknown number of other students,   who were arrested by the security forces between 19 and   24 October. They are reportedly being held at various     security offices and are at risk of torture or            ill-treatment.
