 Iran: Further information on prisoner of conscience / fear of torture: Amir Yaghoub-Ali (m)

 | Amnesty International 
 Amir Yaghoub-Ali, aged 20, student and advocate of equal  rights for women,  was released on 8 August on bail of 20   million toumans (around US$21,500). He had spent four   weeks in custody.
