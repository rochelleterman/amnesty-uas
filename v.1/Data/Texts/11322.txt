 Further information on UA 366/93 (ASA 20/40/93, 14 October) - India: ""disappearance"" / fear of torture: Mokham Singh, Gurnam Kaur | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Mokham Singh was produced in the Metropolitan magistrates court on 23 November 1993 and authorized to be held in police custody until 25 November. His lawyer has expressed concern that he may be killed in police custody."
