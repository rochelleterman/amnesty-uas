"Indonesia: Further Information on fear of torture or ill-treatment/Incommunicado detention 
 Indonesia: Further Information on fear of torture or ill-treatment/Incommunicado detention | Amnesty International 
 Thirty-two out of the forty people arrested on 26 and 27  November have now been released. This includes twelve of  those arrested on 27 November, in connection with         pro-independence ceremonies and twenty people arrested at a meeting in the Fandini Dalam area of Manokwari town on  26 November.
