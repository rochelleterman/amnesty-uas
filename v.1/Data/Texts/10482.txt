 Myanmar: Further information on Fear for safety/possible ""disappearance"" | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International remains seriously concerned for the safety of at least 90 people who have been missing since the violent attack on supporters of the National League for Democracy (NLD) on 30 May. There are further concerns for the safety of scores of others who are believed to be held incommunicado.
