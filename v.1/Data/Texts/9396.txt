"USA (Arizona): Further information on: death penalty: Luis Mata 
 USA (Arizona): Further information on: death penalty: Luis Mata | Amnesty International 
 Luis Mata, a US citizen of Mexican origin convicted of murder, is scheduled to be executed in Arizona on 22 August 1996. The judge who passed his death sentence has been disqualified for making derogatory racial remarks in some of the cases presented before him.
