"Zimbabwe: Further information on Fear for safety 
 Zimbabwe: Further information on Fear for safety | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International remains concerned for the safety of the Archbishop of Bulawayo, Pius Ncube; and is also       concerned for the safety of members and supporters of the Movement for Democratic Change following reports of       violent attacks and death threats.
