"EXTRA 22/94 - Ukraine: fear of imminent execution: Vasily Mikhaylovich 
 EXTRA 22/94 - Ukraine: fear of imminent execution: Vasily Mikhaylovich | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Vasily Krivonos, aged 19, was sentenced to death for murder on 18 November 1993. His appeal against the death sentence was rejected. A petition for clemency is pending. Vasily Krivonos is said to have learning difficulties and a history of psychiatric problems. He claims his confession was made under duress and denies the charge of premeditated, aggravated murder."
