 Indonesia/East Timor: Further information on possible ""disappearance"" / fear of torture: Lucas da Costa | Amnesty International 
 AI has recently received information that Lucas da Costa, who it feared had been abducted by the Indonesian security forces on 23 December 1997, is now free. He may have been subjected to torture in detention. AI has no information about where he was detained or who detained him.
