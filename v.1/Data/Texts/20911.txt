 China: Medical Action: Ill-health of Dr Wang Bingzhang, imprisoned medical doctor and activist | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 This letter expresses Amnesty International's concern about the deteriorating health of Dr Wang Bingzhang, a    medical doctor and leading activist, who has been kept in solitary confinement in Shaoguan prison since his initial detention on suspicion of ""terrorism"" and other charges,  in July 2002."
