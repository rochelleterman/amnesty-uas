"UA 146/94 - Bosnia-Herzegovina: deliberate and arbitrary killings: Muslim civilians in Gorazde area 
 UA 146/94 - Bosnia-Herzegovina: deliberate and arbitrary killings: Muslim civilians in Gorazde area | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 At least 10 Muslim civilians were reportedly killed by the Bosnian Serb army in Stanici, eastern Bosnia, on 4 April 1994. All the houses in the village were reportedly burnt down and a groups of fleeing villagers, including a handicapped man and two women in their eighties and nineties, were shot dead. AI fears that similar abuses could be taking place against civilians elsewhere in the Gorazde area."
