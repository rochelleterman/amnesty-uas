"Sri Lanka: Fear for safety/Possible ""disappearance"" 
 Sri Lanka: Fear for safety/Possible ""disappearance""	 | Amnesty International 
 Reverend Fr. Thiruchchelvan Nihal Jim Brown and Wenceslaus Vinces Vimalathas went missing after a friend left them at a Sri Lanka Navy (SLN) checkpoint on Kayts Island off the northern peninsula of Jaffna on 20 August at approximately 14:10. There is great concern for their safety, and Amnesty International fears that they may be victims of ""disappearance"".
