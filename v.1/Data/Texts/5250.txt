 Colombia: Fear for safety | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is seriously concerned for the safety of the peasant community of ""La Galleta"" farm worked by members of the political party Corriente de Renovaci—n Socialista (CRS), Socialist Renovation Movement in Montebello Municipality, Antioquia Department, following an increasing number of attacks by army-backed paramilitaries. These attacks have resulted in the killing of 11 peasants from the community in recent months.
