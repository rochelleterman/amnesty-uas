 Iran: Further information on fear of imminent execution | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Four men (Abdulreza Sanawati Zergani, Abdul Husain Haribi, Ahmad Maramazi previously named as Husain Maramazi, and Husain Asakreh) were reportedly executed in a prison in Khuzestan. Abdulreza Sanawati Zergani was executed on 13 November 2007 and the three others were executed on 30 December 2007. They had all been sentenced to death in connection with involvement in bomb explosions which took place in major cities in Khuzestan Province in 2005."
