"Morocco: Further information on death penalty: Mohamed Ben Abdellah 
 Morocco: Further information on death penalty: Mohamed Ben Abdellah | Amnesty International 
 The lawyer of Mohamed Ben Abdellah has appealed for the case to be reviewed by the Supreme Court through the ""cassation"" procedure. If the sentence is upheld by the Supreme Court, the defendant may then present a plea for clemency to King Hassan II.
