"Peru: Fear of ill-treatment: Prisoners belonging to the Movimiento Revolucionario Tupac Amaro (MRTA) 
 Peru: Fear of ill-treatment: Prisoners belonging to the Movimiento Revolucionario Tupac Amaro (MRTA) | Amnesty International 
 There is concern that MRTA prisoners are being held incommunicado and are at risk of ill-treatment. Access to MRTA prisoners, suspended during the hostage crisis, includes visits by family members, priests, lawyers and representatives of the ICRC.
