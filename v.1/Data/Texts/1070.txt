"Kenya: death penalty / legal concern 
 Kenya: death penalty / legal concern | Amnesty International 
 On 26 February 1996 a Kenyan High Court in Nakuru changed the sentences of two men, Joseph Boit Kemei and Samuel Kiptoo, convicted of robbery with violence, from ten years in jail and ten strokes of the cane, to death sentences. They had no legal counsel.
