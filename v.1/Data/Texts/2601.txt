 Sudan: Further information on Fear of torture or ill-treatment/ incommunicado detention: members of the Sudan Development Organization (SUDO) | Amnesty International 
 At 5.30pm on 13 February Alrayah Ibrahim Eldaw, Alfaris Ibrahim, Dawalbeit Kabbur and Osman Ali Ismael, staff members of non-governmental organization the Sudan Development Organization (SUDO), and Sayed Abu Bakr, a volunteer for SUDO were released without charge by security forces.
