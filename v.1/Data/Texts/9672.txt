"USA: Further information on Legal concern / Death penalty 
 USA: Further information on Legal concern / Death penalty | Amnesty International 
 Following discussions between the US authorities and their Australian and UK counterparts, the USA has said that it will not pursue the death penalty against Feroz Abbasi, Moazzam Begg or David Hicks in the event that they are charged and brought to trial by military commission.
