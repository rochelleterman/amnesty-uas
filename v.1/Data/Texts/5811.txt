"El Salvador: Fear for Safety/Death Threats 
 El Salvador: Fear for Safety/Death Threats | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is concerned for the safety of      Beatrice Alamanni de Carrillo, the Procuradora para la    Defensa de los Derechos Humanos (PDDH), Human Rights      Procurator. She has reportedly received anonymous death   threats in her office in the capital, San Salvador. The   threats may be linked to her criticism of the police      following a recent prison riot. Members of the PDDH have  also reported harassment and intimidation from members of the Policia Nacional Civil (PNC) National Civil Police.
