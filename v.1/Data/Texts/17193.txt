"Iran: Further information on Death penalty, Azizullah Shenwari 
 Iran: Further information on Death penalty, Azizullah Shenwari | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International has learned that Azizullah Shenwari, a Pakistani minor sentenced to death in Iran, was reportedly made to assume a false identity by his kidnappers, and may be known to the Iranian authorities as Aziz Afghan, an Afghan national.
