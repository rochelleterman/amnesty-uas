 Iran: Further Information on arbitrary arrest/prisoner of conscience: Maryam Hosseinkhah  | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Journalist and women’s rights defender Maryam Hosseinkhah was released at 7pm on 2 January 2008, from Evin prison where she had been detained since 18 November 2007. She was reportedly accused of “spreading lies” and “propaganda against the system” in connection with articles posted on websites she edits, but she has not yet been formally charged.  "
