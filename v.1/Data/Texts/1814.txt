"Zimbabwe: Health concern - Fletcher Dulini-Ncube (m), aged 61, MP 
 Zimbabwe: Health concern - Fletcher Dulini-Ncube (m), aged 61, MP | Amnesty International 
 An MP belonging to the opposition Movement for Democratic Change (MDC) party was arrested in Bulawayo on 3 August   by Zimbabwe police, despite the fact that he is           recovering from surgery. If he is not permitted to        continue receiving hospital treatment,  his overall       health will deteriorate.
