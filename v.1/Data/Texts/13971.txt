"Germany: Further information on fear of refoulement / fear of torture: Fathelrahman Abdallah 
 Germany: Further information on fear of refoulement / fear of torture: Fathelrahman Abdallah | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International has learned that Sudanese asylum    seeker Fathelrahman Abdallah is due to be deported to     Sudan from Germany on 26 June 1999. In Sudan he would be  at risk of serious human rights violations, including     arbitrary imprisonment and torture. He is due to be       deported despite the fact that the courts have not yet    reached a final decision on his claim for asylum.
