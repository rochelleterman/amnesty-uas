 Mexico: Fear for safety | Amnesty International 
 Norma Mesino Mesino (f), Donalciano Valencia Juarez: There is concern for the safety of the above peasant activists and human rights defenders who will return to Mexico at the end of December 1996. They have been touring several countries in Europe meeting leading human rights organization to explain the dire human rights situation affecting their peasant organizations in the southern states of Guerrero and Oaxaca.
