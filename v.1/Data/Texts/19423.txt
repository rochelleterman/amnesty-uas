 Burundi: Ill-treatment/Legal concern: Pascal Ciza













(Claire) | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Pascal Ciza, thought to be about 12 years old, has been   sexually abused by other male inmates at Mpimba Central   Prison, Bujumbura, where he has been detained for around  10 months. Amnesty International is concerned that this   will continue as long as he remains in detention.
