"USA (Alabama): Further information on Death penalty / Legal concern: Thomas Douglas Arthur 
 USA (Alabama): Further information on Death penalty / Legal concern: Thomas Douglas Arthur | Amnesty International 
 The US Supreme Court has issued a stay of execution for Thomas Arthur, who was scheduled to be put to death by lethal injection on 6 December 2007 in Alabama. The stay was issued in response to a petition filed by Thomas Arthur's lawyers challenging Alabama's lethal injection protocol.
