 Israel: Fear of torture and ill-treatment/detention without charge, 'Abd al-Nasser Quzmar | Amnesty International 
 'Abd al-Nasser Quzmar, from the village of Izbat Salman in the West Bank, was arrested on 16 June. He is being held without charge by the Israeli General Security Services (GSS) in Israel and may be in danger of torture or ill-treatment.
