"Egypt: Further information on Fear of torture or ill-treatment, Sayida Muhammad Gad al-Rab. 
 Egypt: Further information on Fear of torture or ill-treatment, Sayida Muhammad  Gad al-Rab. | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International has learned that Sayida Muhammad Gad al-Rab was released on 20 March 2003 after eight months of detention. She was issued a release order by the State Security Prosecution Office on 8 March. Amnesty International has so far received no information about her treatment in detention.
