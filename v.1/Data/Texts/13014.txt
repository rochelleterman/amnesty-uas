"Pakistan: Further information on Fear of torture/ill-treatment/possible prisoner of conscience 
 Pakistan: Further information on Fear of torture/ill-treatment/possible prisoner of conscience | Amnesty International 
 On 23 November 2007, Munir A. Malik was transferred from Attock Jail to the Pakistan Institute of Medical Sciences in Islamabad, where doctors describe his current condition as 'life-threatening'.  There are also concerns for the health of Tariq Mahmood, who was admitted to the  Services Hospital in Lahore on 25 November for medical    tests. Ali Ahmed Kurd was moved to Rawalpindi on 26       November. Atizaz Ahsan was moved from Adiala jail to his  home in Lahore, where he currently remains under house    arrest.
