 Colombia: Fear for Safety:Pablo Javier Arenales | Amnesty International 
 Human rights activist Pablo Javier Arenales has received  a telephone death threat, apparently from a paramilitary  group. Amnesty International is gravely concerned for his safety and that of other human rights activists in the    region where he works.
