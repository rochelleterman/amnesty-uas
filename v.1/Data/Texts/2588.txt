"Sudan: Fear of Torture 
 Sudan: Fear of Torture | Amnesty International 
 Five men, all from the Fur ethnic group, were arrested on 2 January 2004 on suspicion of belonging to the Sudan Liberation Army (SLA). They are held incommunicado and without charge at the security forces offices in Zalingei, where they are at risk of torture.
