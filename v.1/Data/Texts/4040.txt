 Brazil: Further information on legal concern / possible prisoner of conscience: Frei Anastacio Ribeiro | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Frei Anastacio Ribeiro was released on 1 December 1995. However, he still faces charges in other different judicial districts in the state of Paraiba and Amnesty International will continue to monitor his case."
