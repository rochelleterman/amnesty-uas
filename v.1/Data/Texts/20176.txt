 Honduras: Further information:  Human rights defender tortured: Karla Zelaya   | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 On 23 October Karla Zelaya, a human rights defender working on land rights issues, was abducted in Tegucigalpa, Honduras and held for three hours. The men who abducted her tortured and threatened her while they interrogated her about her work. "
