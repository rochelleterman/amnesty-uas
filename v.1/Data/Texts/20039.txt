 Dominican Republic: Activist incommunicado in Dominican Republic: Juan Almonte Herrera | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 The whereabouts of male community activist Juan Almonte Herrera have not been known since he was abducted on 28 September in Santo Domingo, the capital of the Dominican Republic. He may be held incommunicado by police, and is at risk of enforced disappearance."
