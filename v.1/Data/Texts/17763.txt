"Israel/Occupied Territories: Further information on fear of torture: Ma'mun Omer Mustafa Hamad 
 Israel/Occupied Territories: Further information on fear of torture: Ma'mun Omer Mustafa Hamad | Amnesty International 
 Ma'mun Omer Mustafa Hamad, a construction worker, now has access to his lawyer. His detention has been extended until the end of this month, but the interrogation period has finished and there is no longer any fear of torture.
