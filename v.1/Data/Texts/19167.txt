"Tunisia: Further information on: Forcible return/fear of torture or ill-treatment: Hussein Sumaida (m) 
 Tunisia: Further information on: Forcible return/fear of torture or ill-treatment: Hussein Sumaida (m) | Amnesty International 
 Hussein Sumaida was released on 16 September after 10 days in detention at the headquarters of the Ministry of the Interior's State Security Department in the capital, Tunis. He is still in Tunisia, but has been able to speak to his wife in Canada by telephone.
