 Nepal: Fear for safety / Possible ""disappearance"" | Amnesty International 
 Navraj Thapa was reportedly arrested from his grocery shop in Charkilo, Naubise, Dhading district, at 3am on 12 November by ten security forces personnel in plain clothes. He had been previously arrested on 1 October and held at an unknown location in Kathmandu before being released on 9 October.
