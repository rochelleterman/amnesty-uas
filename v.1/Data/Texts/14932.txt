 Turkey: Fear for safety/ risk of torture and ill-treatment | Amnesty International 
 Gendarmes have reportedly arrested 21 members of a Kurdish family during a raid in southeast Turkey in the early hours of 17 October. It is not known where they are held, and they are at grave risk of torture. Three people were killed during the operation.
