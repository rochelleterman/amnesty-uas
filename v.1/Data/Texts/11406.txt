"UA 173/93 - Indonesia / East Timor: health concern / fear of torture: Xanana Gusmao (includes correction) 
 UA 173/93 - Indonesia / East Timor: health concern / fear of torture: Xanana Gusmao (includes correction) | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 There is concern at reports that Xanana Gusmao, the leader of East Timor's independence movement, may be in poor health after being on hunger-strike for about a week. These reports have been difficult to confirm since neither his family nor representatives of the International Committee of the Red Cross (ICRC) have been permitted to visit him.
