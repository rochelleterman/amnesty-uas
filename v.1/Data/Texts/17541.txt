"Iraq: Further information on Incommunicado detention/fear of torture, Rebwar Arif 
 Iraq: Further information on Incommunicado detention/fear of torture, Rebwar Arif | Amnesty International 
 Rebwar Arif was released on 10 May and has returned to his home in Sulaimaniya, northern Iraq. He had been held incommunicado since 5 May after speaking critically of the Patriotic Union of Kurdistan (PUK), one of the two leading Kurdish political parties controlling an area of northern Iraq.
