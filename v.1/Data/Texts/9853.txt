"USA (Texas): Death penalty / Legal concern, Gerald Lee Mitchell 
 USA (Texas): Death penalty / Legal concern, Gerald Lee    Mitchell | Amnesty International 
 Gerald Mitchell is scheduled to be executed in Texas on 22 October 2001 for a murder committed when he was aged 17. International law prohibits the death penalty against people who were under 18 at the time of the crime. Mitchell was convicted and sentenced by an all-white jury, in a county whose population was about 20 per cent African American.
