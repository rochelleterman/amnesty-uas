"Iran: Death Penalty/ Fear of Imminent Execution: Sattar Sh (m) 
 Iran: Death Penalty/ Fear of Imminent Execution: Sattar Sh (m) | Amnesty International 
 Sattar Sh is at risk of imminent execution for a murder which took place in the town of Islamshahr, south of the capital, Tehran. His death sentence has been upheld by Branch 27 of the Supreme Court in Tehran and could be carried out at any time.
