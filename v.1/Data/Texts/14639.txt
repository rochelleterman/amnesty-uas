"EXTRA 44/93 - Turkey: fear of torture: Teslim Tore, Murat Toprak, Huseyin Bakir, Muazzez Yuce 
 EXTRA 44/93 - Turkey: fear of torture: Teslim Tore, Murat Toprak, Huseyin Bakir, Muazzez Yuce | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 There are fears that the above named who were captured during raids on a number of houses in Istanbul on 5 May 1993, are being interrogated under torture. According to family members, Istanbul police have not registered any of the detentions with the State Security Court Prosecutor and have repeatedly denied their detention. "
