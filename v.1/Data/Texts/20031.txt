 Dominican Republic: Fear for safety/death threats | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Nicolas Disla was unlawfully killed by police officers on 28 March in the capital, Santo Domingo. Two days later, one of the officers allegedly responsible made an attempt on the life of his brother, Juan Carlos Disla, as he attended Nicolas Disla's funeral. Juan Carlos Disla and other members of his family are in grave danger."
