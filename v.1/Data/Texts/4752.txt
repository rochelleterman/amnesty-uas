"Colombia: Further information on fear for safety 
 Colombia: Further information on fear for safety | Amnesty International 
 Army-backed paramilitaries are continuing raids throughout Barrancabermeja city. The women's grassroots organisation OFP is still operating, but has faced further threats and intimidation. CREDHOS, another other human rights organisation still operating in the city, has also faced further paramilitary threats in recent days.
