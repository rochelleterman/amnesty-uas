"Rwanda: Further information on ""disappearance"" / fear for safety / health concern: Ignace Kanyabugoyi 
 Rwanda: Further information on ""disappearance"" / fear for safety / health concern: Ignace Kanyabugoyi | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International has learnt that relatives of Ignace Kanyabugoyi have been able to visit him since early       August 1999. His health is reported to have improved, but precise details of his physical condition and the causes  for his ill-health remain unknown.
