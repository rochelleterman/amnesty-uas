"Colombia: Possible extrajudicial execution / fear for safety: Leonardo Tibaquira and all other members of the Inaia-Sue housing cooperative 
 Colombia: Possible extrajudicial execution / fear for safety: Leonardo Tibaquira and all other members of the Inaia-Sue housing cooperative | Amnesty International 
 On 15 November 1997, members of the paramilitary group Colombia Without Guerrillas (COLSINGUE) attacked the Inaie-Sue housing cooperative in Tenjo and killed Leonardo Tibaquira. AI is seriously concerned for the safety of the families who own properties in the cooperative.
