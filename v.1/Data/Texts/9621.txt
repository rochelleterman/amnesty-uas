 USA: Further information on fear of torture/ill-treatment: Sameh Khouzam (m) 

 | Amnesty International 
 Egyptian national Sameh Khouzam, who faces forcible return to Egypt, has received a 12-day stay of deportation. This is due to expire on 18 June, leaving him at risk of torture or ill-treatment if he is then returned to Egypt.
