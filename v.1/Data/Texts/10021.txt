"USA (Texas): Further information on Death penalty, Emerson Edward Rudd 
 USA (Texas): Further information on  Death penalty, Emerson Edward Rudd | Amnesty International 
 Emerson Rudd was executed in Texas on 15 November 2001. He was sentenced to death in 1989 for the murder of Steve Morgan during a robbery in 1988.
