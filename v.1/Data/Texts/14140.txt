"Further information on UA 183/93 (EUR 42/01/93, 8 June) - Sweden: fear of forcible return (refoulement): Monica Castillo Paez 
 Further information on UA 183/93 (EUR 42/01/93, 8 June) - Sweden: fear of forcible return (refoulement): Monica Castillo Paez | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 The Swedish Immigration Board held a re-hearing on 12 August to examine if there was any new information on her case, and their decision is expected within a few days. When Monica Castillo appeared at the hearing, she was taken into custody by the authorities and is now held in a centre near Stockholm airport. There are fears that she is being held in preperation for her deportation to Peru. "
