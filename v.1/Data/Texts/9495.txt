 United States of America (Tennessee): Further information on Death penalty /     Legal concern, Abu-Ali Abdur'Rahman. | Amnesty International 
 On 6 June, the US Court of Appeals for the Sixth Circuit issued a stay of execution for Abu-Ali Abdur'Rahman. On 10 June, the defence lawyers learned that the state was not going to challenge the order, and the stay of execution was assured.
