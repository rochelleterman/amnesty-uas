"Turkey: Further information on fear of torture: Zeynep Firat (f), Munevver Koz (f) (corrected name) 
 Turkey: Further information on fear of torture: Zeynep Firat (f), Munevver Koz (f) (corrected name) | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 The lawyer Zeynep Firat and her client Munevver Koz were formally arrested on 21 December 1994 and sent to prison. Zeynep Firat was released on 3 January 1995; Munevver Koz remains in prison. This action contains an account of the alleged torture of Zeynep Firat during detention. Munevver Koz was reportedly taken to a forest by police who threatened her by shooting around her."
