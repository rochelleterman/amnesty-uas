"Zimbabwe: Fear of torture/prisoner of conscience 
 Zimbabwe: Fear of torture/prisoner of conscience | Amnesty International 
 Clive Wilson, managing director of The Standard           newspaper, is at risk of torture after his arrest on 22   January 1999, which followed the arrest and torture of    two Standard journalists, Mark Chavunduka and Ray Choto.  Amnesty International considers him a prisoner of         conscience.
