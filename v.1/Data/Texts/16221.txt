"Uzbekistan: Fear for safety/incommunicado detention/ fear of torture, Babir Makhmudov 
 Uzbekistan: Fear for safety/incommunicado detention/ fear of torture, Babir Makhmudov | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is concerned for the safety of Babir Makhmudov, the son of writer and possible prisoner of conscience Mamadali Makhmudov, who was reportedly detained on 30 March 2004. He is being held in incommunicado detention at an unknown location.
