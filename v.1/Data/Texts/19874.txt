 Colombia: Attempted kidnap of female defender: Blanca Nubia Diaz  | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 On 23 January 2012, an attempted kidnapping took place of Blanca Nubia Diaz in Bogotá, Colombia. She is seeking justice for the rape and killing of her daughter Irina del Carmen Villero Díaz by paramilitaries."
