 EXTRA 04/93 - Guatemala: threats / harassment: Bruce Harris, Executive Director of Covenant House for Latin America and other staff members of Covenant House | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 AI is seriously concerned about an incident which took place in Guatemala City on 6 January 1993, in which a member of staff of Covenant House (Casa Alianza) was asked by an unidentified man about the return to Guatemala next week of Bruce Harris, Executive Director of Covenant House. There are indications that the security forces were involved in the incident and that it was an act of intimidation against Bruce Harris. "
