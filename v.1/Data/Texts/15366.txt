 Turkey: Fear of ""disappearance"" / fear of torture: Mazhar Kara and one other man, name unknown | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 On 1 December, Mazhar Kara was detained in the Ofis District of Diyabakir with another man, whose identity is not known. It is feared that the two men are being subjected to torture while held incommunicado."
