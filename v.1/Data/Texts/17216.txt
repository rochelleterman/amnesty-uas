"Iran: Fear of Torture/Medical concern: Vahid (m) 
 Iran: Fear of Torture/Medical concern: Vahid (m) | Amnesty International 
 Vahid, aged 28 (surname unknown), has been sentenced to have his eyes surgically gouged out for a crime committed when he was 16 years old. The Supreme Court rejected an appeal on 9 June, and ordered that the punishment should be carried out. It may now be inflicted at any time.
