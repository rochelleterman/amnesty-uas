 Bahamas: Fear of imminent execution: Trevor Nathaniel Fisher | Amnesty International 
 Trevor Nathaniel Fisher is due to be hanged on 12 September 1996. It is reported that the Committee on the Prerogative of Mercy advised the Governor General that Trevor Fisher's case was not one in which mercy should be exercised.
