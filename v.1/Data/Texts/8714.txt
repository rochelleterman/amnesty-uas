 USA (North Carolina):Further information on Death penalty / Legal concern: Marcus Reymond Robinson (m) 

 | Amnesty International 
 On 25 January 2007 in Raleigh, North Carolina, Wake County Superior Court Judge Donald Stephens blocked the execution of Marcus Robinson, black, aged 333, who was    scheduled to be put to death at 2am local time on 26      January. He was          sentenced to death in 1994 for a murder committed when he had just turned 18 years old.
