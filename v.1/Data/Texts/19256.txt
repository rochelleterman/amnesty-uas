"Tunisia: Further information on Health concern / Ill-treatment / Unfair trial, Abdelatif Bouhajila 
 Tunisia: Further information on  Health concern / Ill-treatment / Unfair trial, Abdelatif      Bouhajila | Amnesty International 
 Abdelatif Bouhajila's health has deteriorated seriously, and his life is in danger. He has been in hospital since 6 October, after a suspected heart attack. He has continued his hunger strike in hospital, and has recently refused to take sugar water or to be fed on a drip.
