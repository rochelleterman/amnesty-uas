"Honduras: Fear for safety/Death threat, Sara Sauceda Flores 
 Honduras: Fear for safety/Death threat, Sara Sauceda Flores | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is concerned for the safety of Sara Sauceda Flores, whose son, Darwin Roberto Flores was reportedly summarily executed in 2002. She received an anonymous death threat on 12 April and has reportedly being intimidated following her complaints against two officers she alleges are responsible for her son's killing.
