 Syria : Further information on: Fear of torture/incommunicado detention: 'Abd al-Rahman al-Musa | Amnesty International 
 'Abd al-Rahman al-Musa has reportedly been moved from the Political Security Detention Centre in Hama, western Syria, and his whereabouts are now unknown. As far as Amnesty International is aware, he has never been allowed access to a lawyer, nor been charged with any offence or brought before a court.
