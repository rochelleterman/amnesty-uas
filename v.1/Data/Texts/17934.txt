"Jordan: Further information on death penalty: Ahmad Ahmad Bani 'Ali: 
 Jordan: Further information on death penalty: Ahmad Ahmad Bani 'Ali: | Amnesty International 
 The Irbid Criminal Court has upheld the death penalty imposed on Ahmad Ahmad Bani 'Ali for the murder of his wife. The death sentence must now be ratified by the Court of Cassation and King Hussein bin Talal before it can be carried out.
