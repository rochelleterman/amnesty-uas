"Indonesia: Fear for safety, East Timorese refugees attempting to return from Indonesia 
 Indonesia: Fear for safety, East Timorese refugees attempting to return from Indonesia | Amnesty International 
 Several hundred East Timorese refugees are thought to be planning to return from West Timor over the next week. They are feared to be at risk of attack from militia groups operating in West Timor, who oppose the repatriation scheme and have previously attacked refugees trying to register join it.
