"Philippines: Further information on fear of ""disappearance"" / fear of ill-treatment: Julius Z Marquez 
 Philippines: Further information on fear of ""disappearance"" / fear of ill-treatment: Julius Z Marquez | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Julius Marquez was released on 13 January 1995, having been held incommunicado since 6 January. AI is concerned about reports that he was threatened with ""disappearance"" in custody and that threats were also made against his wife, also a human rights worker."
