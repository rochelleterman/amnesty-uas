 Colombia: Fear for safety | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is seriously concerned for the      safety of human rights defenders in the city of           Barrancabermeja, department of Santander. This follows    recent death threats, harassment and surveillance of two  human rights organisations that operate in the city.
