 Kyrgyzstan: Further information on: fear of imminent execution: Nikolai Sokolov | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International has learned that the Clemency Commission has reviewed the case of Nikolai Sokolov and has decided against recommending to the President that clemency be granted. However, no decision on the case has yet been taken.
