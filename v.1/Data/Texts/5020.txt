"Further information on EXTRA 34/94 (AMR 23/53/94, 7 July) - Colombia: ""disappearances"": President rejects draft law against ""disappearances"" 
 Further information on EXTRA 34/94 (AMR 23/53/94, 7 July) - Colombia: ""disappearances"": President rejects draft law against ""disappearances"" | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 On 7 July 1994 Colombian President Cesar Gaviria Trujillo rejected a draft law against ""disappearances"", declaring that certain articles of the law are ""unconstitutional"" and ""inconvenient"". The draft law will now return to Congress for further debate."
