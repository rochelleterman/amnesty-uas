"UA 445/95 - Lebanon: death penalty: Husam 'Ali al-Naser 
 UA 445/95 - Lebanon: death penalty: Husam 'Ali al-Naser | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Husam 'Ali al-Naser was sentenced to death by a military court in November 1993 for murder. The sentence was upheld on 13 December 1994. AI is concerned that he is at risk of imminent execution and is calling for this and other death sentences to be commuted."
