"Taiwan (China): further information on: death penalty / legal concern: and new medical concern: Liu Chi-hsun 
 Taiwan (China): further information on: death penalty / legal concern: and new medical concern: Liu Chi-hsun | Amnesty International 
 On 14 March 1996 a further appeal against the death sentence for Liu Chi-hsun was rejected.  AI fears that he now faces imminent execution. He is said to be in poor health and is held in permanent foot shackles.
