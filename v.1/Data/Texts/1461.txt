 Mauritania: Fear of torture or ill treatment / Incommunicado detention/Possible prisoners of conscience | Amnesty International 
 The named men were arrested by members of the security forces on 25 April in the capital Nouakchott. They are being held in incommunicado, without access to lawyers and their families at an unknown location in the capital, where they are at risk of torture or ill-treatment.
