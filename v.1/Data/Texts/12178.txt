 Mohammad Yasir Yusoff, Zainal Abu Bakar, Taufik Afizal Zainal Joned, Siapul Abdullah, Nikli bin Jusut, Zulkisli bin Rasjid, Busri bin Mulid Junus, Munzir bin Jusuf and others: Amnesty International is still concerned for the fate of eight Acehnese asylum seekers who remain in the Dutch and Swiss Embassies in Kuala Lumpur, having sought political asylum on 25 December 1996.
