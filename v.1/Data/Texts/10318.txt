"Further information on UA 408/92 (ASA 13/08/92, 22 December) - Bangladesh: fear of forcible return (refoulement): Burmese Muslim refugees 
 Further information on UA 408/92 (ASA 13/08/92, 22 December) - Bangladesh: fear of forcible return (refoulement): Burmese Muslim refugees | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Following the resumption of repatriations, after they were suspended in mid-January 1993, the UNHCR announced on 30 April, that since 31 January there had been no reports of forced repatriation, and that it is intended that in mid-May, UNHCR and the Bangladesh Government will sign a Memorandum of Understanding on the assistance and protection of refugees from Myanmar.
