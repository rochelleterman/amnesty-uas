"Turkmenistan: Further information on fear for safety / incommunicado detention / possible prisoner of conscience: Nurberdi NURMAMEDOV 
 Turkmenistan: Further information on fear for safety / incommunicado detention / possible prisoner of conscience: Nurberdi NURMAMEDOV | Amnesty International 
 Prisoner of conscience Nurberdi Nurmamedov was released on 23 December under a presidential amnesty to mark the Islamic holy night of Kadir and the end of Ramadan. According to unofficial sources, he returned to his home in the capital, Ashgabat, that day, and is reportedly under strict police surveillance.
