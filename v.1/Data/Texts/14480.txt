"Turkey: Fear for safety - Ali Ekber Palabiyik and his     family 
 Turkey: Fear for safety - Ali Ekber Palabiyik and his     family | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is concerned for the safety of Ali  Ekber Palabiyik and his family. Ali Ekber Palabiyik was   recently harassed by police and has received death        threats. Ali Ekber Palabiyik was one of five eyewitnesses in the trial against police officers accused of killing   the journalist Metin Goktepe in January 1996.
