 Turkey:  Further information on Fear of torture for relatives and supporters of  hunger striking       prisoners

 | Amnesty International 
 Law student Bayla Ba ta was reportedly detained by police on 18 December, after she participated in the daily press conference given by relatives of prisoners on hunger      strike in Taksim Square. Police have since denied having  her in custody, and she is feared to have ""disappeared"".
