"USA (Alabama): Death penalty / legal concern: Pernell Ford 
 USA (Alabama): Death penalty / legal concern: Pernell Ford | Amnesty International 
 Pernell Ford is scheduled to die in Alabama's electric chair on 9 July 1999. He was sentenced to death in 1984 for murder. Pernell Ford has a long history of serious    mental health problems, and has periodically waived his   right to appeal.
