 USA (Arizona): Further information on death penalty / legal concern: Ramon Martinez-Villareal | Amnesty International 
 Ramon Martinez-Villareal, of Mexican origin, who was scheduled to be executed on 21 May 1997, has receieved a 30-day stay of execution from the US Supreme Court to allow for further legal hearings on his mental competence.
