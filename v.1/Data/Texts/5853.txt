 El Salvador: Fear for safety | Amnesty International 
 Group of people working for justice in case of extrajudicial execution victim: Amnesty International is concerned for the safety of this group of people preparing an event to commemorate the first anniversary of an extrajudicial execution. The group seems to be under surveillance and followed, alledgedly by members of the police.
