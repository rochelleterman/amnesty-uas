 Russian Federation: Enforced disappearance/ Fear for safety/ Torture and ill-treatment: Zurab Datkhuevich Ilaev (m),  | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Zurab Datkhuevich Ilaev disappeared on 1 December 2008 and may be held at a law enforcement base near the village of Dolinski. Concerns for his safety are heightened by the fact that his two brothers were allegedly killed after being held at the same place."
