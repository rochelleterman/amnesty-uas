 Uzbekistan: Border closure impeding safe return of refugees | Amnesty International 
 Around 7,300 Tajik refugees in Sakhi camp, northern Afghanistan: Amnesty International is calling on the authorities in Uzbekistan to open immediately the Termez crossing point between Uzbekistan and Afghanistan to expedite the speedy return of refugees to Tajikistan.
