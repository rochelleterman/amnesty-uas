"Lebanon: Further Information on Forcible return: Tareq Souid 
 Lebanon: Further Information on Forcible return: Tareq Souid | Amnesty International 
 Tareq Souid (m), aged 30, may be forcibly returned to Tunisia in the next few days. The Lebanese authorities have set the deadline for his deportation as 27 January if no other country agrees to offer him asylum.
