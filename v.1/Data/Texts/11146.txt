"India: Further information on Fear for safety/Threats. 
 India: Further information on Fear for safety/Threats. | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International remains concerned for the safety of sisters Shazada and Shameema, their father Abdul Rehman Dar and other members of the family following their return to their home on 23 March. The family have not received police protection despite the abduction of the two sisters in October 2002 and subsequent threats against the family.
