 Indonesia: Fear for safety / incommunicado detention | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is concerned for the safety of M    Yusuf Haria and Ridwan who are at risk of torture or      ill-treatment in incommunicado detention. Amnesty         International believes they were arrested on suspicion of having links with the armed separatist group, Gerakan     Aceh Merdeka (GAM), Free Aceh Movement.
