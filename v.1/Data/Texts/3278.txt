"Democratic Republic of Congo:Incommunicado detention/ Fear of torture or ill-treatment- Floribert Chebeya Bahizire 
 Democratic Republic of Congo:Incommunicado detention/ Fear of torture or ill-treatment- Floribert Chebeya Bahizire | Amnesty International 
 On 28 December 2002, Floribert Chebeya Bahizire, president of the Voix des sans voix (VSV), Voice of the   Voiceless, went into hiding after he was twice ordered to report to a military tribunal for questioning. He is      currently in hiding in the capital Kinshasa, amid fears   that he is facing imminent and arbitrary arrest.
