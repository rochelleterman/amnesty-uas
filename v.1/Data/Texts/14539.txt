"Turkey: Fear of torture or ill-treatment 
 Turkey: Fear of torture or ill-treatment | Amnesty International 
 A mutiny by political prisoners in Burdur Prison was put  down on 5 July, and those involved are now reportedly in  solitary confinement. They had barricaded themselves into Burdur Prison in protest at being beaten and ill-treated  on their way to court hearings. It is feared that they    may now be at risk of torture or ill-treatment.
