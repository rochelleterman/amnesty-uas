 Iran: Further Information on Prisoners of Conscience/Fear of Torture or other ill-treatment | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Women's rights activist Ronak Safarzadeh's trial began on or around 13 March 2008, in a session her lawyer attended, where the charges against her were set out. She is accused of being mohareb (at enmity with God), which can carry the death penalty. No further court dates have been announced."
