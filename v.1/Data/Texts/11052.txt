"China: Further information on death penalty / legal concern: Wang Jianye 
 China: Further information on death penalty / legal concern: Wang Jianye | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 The death sentence passed on Wang Jianye on 3 April 1995 has just been upheld by the Supreme People's Court and execution may take place within days. Wang Jianye had started a hunger-strike in an attempt to obtain a meeting with his lawyer and relatives. The hunger-strike reportedly lasted 10 days."
