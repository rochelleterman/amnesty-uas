"Algeria: Further information on ""Disappearance"" / fear for safety: Amine Amrouche 
 Algeria: Further information on ""Disappearance"" / fear for safety: Amine Amrouche
 | Amnesty International 
 Amine Amrouche, aged 22, ""disappeared"" from his home in Baraki on 30 January 1997, is alive and being held in a military security centre in Algiers. There is concern for his safety.
