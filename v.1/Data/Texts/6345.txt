"Further information on UA 31/94 (AMR 34/04/94, 31 January) - Guatemala: death threats / fear for safety: Juan Enrique Quinonez Schwank 
 Further information on UA 31/94 (AMR 34/04/94, 31 January) - Guatemala: death threats / fear for safety: Juan Enrique Quinonez Schwank | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Juan Enrique Quinonez Schwank has reported being followed and threatened in March and April 1994. He has been given a period of 60 days in which to leave the country, otherwise he will be killed."
