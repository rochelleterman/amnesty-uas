 The 26 people referred to above were reportedly arrested between 4 and 6 November 1992. After several days in unacknowledged police custody, 23 of them were registered as detained and authorization was granted to the police to hold them for 15 days. They are currently in incommunicado detention at the Anti-Terror Branch of Istanbul Police Headquarters. AI fears that they are being interrogated under torture. Three detainees have so far not been registered. They are: Celal Meral, Ziya Ulusoy and Mehmet Ustundag. There is grave concern about them, given that five people have apparently ""disappeared"" in police custody since March 1991. Celal Meral was reportedly detained with his wife and child, aged 3, who was kept in police detention for two nights. "
