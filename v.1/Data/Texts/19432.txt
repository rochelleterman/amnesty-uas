 Burundi : Take action: Ratification of the Optional Protocol to Convention on the Rights of the Child: Web action | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 In this action Amnesty International requests the         government of Burundi to ratify the Optional Protocol to  the (UN) Convention on the Rights of the Child on the     involvement of children in armed conflict and the African Charter on the Rights and Welfare of the Child; adopt a   minimum age of at least eighteen for voluntary            recruitment and adopt whatever legislation is necessary   to ensure that the international obligations arising from both treaties are implemented."
