 Mexico: Fear for safety / death threats: Digna Ochoa y    Plácido and members of the Prodh (human rights NGO)

 | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Members of the human rights organization Centro de        Derechos Humanos ""Miguel Agustín Pro Juárez"", AC (PRODH),  have received death threats, sent to their offices on 3  September 1999. According to reports, these threats are   related to the    abduction on 9 August 1999 of Digna     Ochoa y Plácido, a    human right lawyer working with     PRODH, who was later released."
