"Iran: Fear of imminent execution, two named and five unnamed men. 
 Iran: Fear of imminent execution, two named and five unnamed men. | Amnesty International 
 Five unnamed men are reportedly scheduled to be executed  simultaneously at 7.30am on 29 September, in five         different districts across Tehran. Amnesty International  does not know the scheduled execution time of Reza Parsa  and Arash Tajbakhsh, but fears that their execution may   also be imminent.
