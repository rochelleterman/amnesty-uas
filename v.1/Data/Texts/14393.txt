 Turkey: Further information on   Risk of torture and ill-treatment/ police   brutality | Amnesty International 
 172 of the 234 individuals including human rights defeneder Saadet Erdem detained in Mersin, on the eastern Mediterranean coast in connection with banned Newroz celebrations have either been remanded to custody or else released. Meanwhile, all of the 81 individuals detained in Istanbul have also been released. There is no abstract for this document.
