"USA (Arizona): Further information on death penalty: Robert Charles Comer (m) 
 USA (Arizona): Further information on death penalty: Robert Charles Comer (m)

 | Amnesty International 
 Robert Comer was executed on 22 May in Arizona. He had been sentenced to death for murder in 1988. He was also convicted of kidnapping, assault and sexual assault in two separate cases.
