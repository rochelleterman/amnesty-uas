 USA: Further information: Judge orders release of Guantánamo detainee: Adnan Farhan Abdul Latif  | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 On 21 July, a US federal judge ordered the immediate release from Guantánamo of Yemeni national Adnan Farhan Abdul Latif. The US Department of Justice may still appeal this decision."
