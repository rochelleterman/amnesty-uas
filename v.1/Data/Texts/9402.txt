 Further information on EXTRA 53/94 (AMR 51/74/94, 8 September) - USA (Texas): death penalty: George Lott, Warren Bridge, Herman Clark, Walter Williams | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 George Lott was executed as scheduled on 20 September 1994. Warren Bridge has had his execution date changed to 1 December 1994. Herman Clark is scheduled to be executed on 27 September and Walter Williams is scheduled to be executed on 5 October."
