"People's Republic of China: death penalty: Geng Bing, Shu Qiang, Zhang Tongxi 
 People's Republic of China: death penalty: Geng Bing, Shu Qiang, Zhang Tongxi | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 On 15 March 1995, the Zibo Municipal Intermediate People's Court in Shandong province sentenced three men to death, one (Zhang Tongxi), with a two year reprieve. They were convicted on charges including abduction, hooliganism, extortion and rape. It is not known whether they have appealed against their sentences."
