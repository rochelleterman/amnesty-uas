 Syria: Health of arrested Syrian man at serious risk: Mohammed Hasan al-Labwani | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Mohammed Hasan al-Labwani has been held incommunicado since his arrest by General Intelligence, commonly referred to as State Security, on 18 August 2011 in the town of al-Zabadani, near Damascus, Syria. His family fear for his safety as he suffers from a serious medical condition. He is also at grave risk of torture and other ill-treatment. "
