"Mexico: Fear for safety 
 Mexico: Fear for safety | Amnesty International 
 At least 120 Tzotzil Indians in the municipality of Chenalho, Chiapas State: These people have fled their homes as a result of continued harassment, including death threats, by members of armed paramilitary groups for their unwillingness to support the Institutional Revolutionary Party, the ruling party.
