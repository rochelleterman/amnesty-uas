 Chad: Extrajudicial executions / fear for safety: At least 80 people in Moundou | Amnesty International 
 At least 80 people have been killed in southern Chad following a clash between the security forces and the former Armed Forces for the Federal Republic. Others have been arrested on suspicion of collaboration and subsequently tortured or ill-treated.
