"Colombia: Fear for Safety / Abduction / ""Disappearance"" 
 Colombia: Fear for Safety / Abduction / ""Disappearance"" | Amnesty International 
 Army-backed paramilitaries have abducted 10 people, members of peasant farmer families, six of them children, after attacking a village in the north-western department of Sucre, in the early hours of 17 January. They killed 25 villagers picked out from a death list.
