"Yemen: Death penalty / legal concern 
 Yemen: Death penalty / legal concern | Amnesty International 
 Jalal Abdullah al-Rada'i and Abdullah Ali al-Rada'i: The two Yemeni nationals named above have been sentenced to death and crucifixion following court hearings which appear to have breached basic international standards for fair trial. They were tried by the Court of First Instance in al-Mukalla, reportedly without any legal assistance.
