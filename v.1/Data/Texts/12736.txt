"Nepal: Further Information on Fear for safety/possible ""disappearance"" 
 Nepal: Further Information on Fear for safety/possible ""disappearance"" | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International has received information that Lila Dahal was released from detention on 28 December 2003. Amnesty International has received no further information about Yuba Raj Chaulagain, Rebakala Tiwari, Subash Shrestha, Shaha Dev Ghimire and Bhawanath Dhamala, who were reportedly arrested between 3 September and 3 December 2003.
