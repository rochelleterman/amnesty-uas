"Colombia: Fear for safety 
 Colombia: Fear for safety | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is seriously concerned for the safety of Henry Palomo after he was reportedly abducted   on 23rd December and tortured by army backed              paramilitaries. There are also fears for the safety of    members of the CIMITARRA peasant association in the       Magdaleno Medio region who are also at risk.
