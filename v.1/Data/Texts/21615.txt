 Azerbaijan: Further information: Sick prisoner dies in custody, Novruzali Mammadov | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Novruzali Mammadov died in prison on 17 August in Azerbaijan. The 67-year-old language expert's health had suffered badly in Penal Colony No 15, and he had been moved to the central hospital of the penitentiary system on 28 July."
