 Nepal: Fear for safety/ possible ""disappearance"". Sadik Kawadi (m), aged 60, Rastriya Prajatantra Party candidate | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is concerned for the safety of Sadik Kawadi, who was reportedly arrested by plain clothed security forces personnel at 9am on 4 January. Attempts by relatives to locate Sadik Kawadi including informing the National Human Rights Commission (NHRC) have so far failed, and the security forces deny his arrest.
