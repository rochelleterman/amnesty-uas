"Ethiopia: Further information on: Fear of torture/Arbitrary detention/prisoners of conscience 
 Ethiopia: Further information on: Fear of torture/Arbitrary detention/prisoners of conscience | Amnesty International 
 Diribi Demissie, Gemechu Feyera and Sentayehu Workneh and 14 other members of the Oromo ethnic group were arrested on or about 18 May in the capital, Addis Ababa. They are currently being held incommunicado at the 3rd police station, the Central Investigation Department (""Maikelawi""), where some have allegedly been tortured and ill-treated.
