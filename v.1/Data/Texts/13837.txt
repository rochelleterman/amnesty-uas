 Further information on UA 172/94 (EUR 11/03/94, 3 May) - Albania: death penalty: Laver Goga | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Unofficial sources report that Laver Goga is due to be executed on 14 June 1994. If this is correct, it means that his sentence was confirmed on appeal and has been approved by president Berisha."
