"UA 407/94 - Iran: fear of torture: Mohammad Khandjari 
 UA 407/94 - Iran: fear of torture: Mohammad Khandjari | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Mohamad Khandjari was reportedly arrested on 12 November 1994 and is believed to be held in Rasht Prison. He was arrested at dawn by members of the Revolutionary Guard and reportedly beaten up in front of his wife. He was apparently arrested for his alleged participation in the riots in Qazvin in August."
