"Jamaica: Further information on fear for safety: Family of Janice Allen 
 Jamaica: Further information on fear for safety: Family of Janice Allen | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is concerned for the safety of the  family of Janice Allen following continued death threats  and intimidation from the police. The threats have        increased since the Director of Public Prosecutions ruled in May 2001 that a police officer should be charged with  murder in connection with Janice Allen's death
