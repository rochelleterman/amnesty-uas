"USA (Ohio): Further information on death penalty: Darrell Wayne Ferguson (m) 
 USA (Ohio): Further information on death penalty: Darrell Wayne Ferguson (m) | Amnesty International 
 Darrell Ferguson, white, aged 28, was executed in Ohio on 8 August 2006. He was sentenced to death in 2003 for the murder of 61-year-old Thomas King on 25 December 2001 and the murders of Mae Fugate, aged 69, and her 68-year-old husband Arlie Fugate the following evening. Darrell Ferguson had waived his appeals.
