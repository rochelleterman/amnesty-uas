 Liberia: Further Information on Fear for safety/risk of torture or ill-treatment/Incommunicado detention. | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is concerned at reports that Moriah Sando Nyenetue, the mother of Hassan Bility's child, has  not been seen for ten days and may be in detention.       Sheikh K. M. Sackor, Executive Director of Humanist       Watch, a Liberian non-governmental human rights           organization, is also being held in incommunicado         detention without charge, the latest in a pattern of      arbitrary arrests and intimidation of human rights        defenders.
