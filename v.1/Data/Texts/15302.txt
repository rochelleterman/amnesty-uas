 Turkey: Fear of torture / health concern: Ali Guden | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Turkish Human Rights Association member Ali Guden had been in police custody in Mersin since 7 November 1995. AI fears that he may be facing torture during interrogation and is concerned for his health and safety as he is reported to be suffering from a serious heart condition."
