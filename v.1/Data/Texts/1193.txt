"Kenya: Ill-treatment / fear for safety: pro-democracy activists 
 Kenya: Ill-treatment / fear for safety: pro-democracy activists | Amnesty International 
 On 3 May 1997 Kenyan police and security forces violently prevented a peaceful pro-democracy rally from taking place in Nairobi. There are fears for the safety of those preparing and participating in further pro-democracy activities in a pre-election campaign for legal and constitional change.
