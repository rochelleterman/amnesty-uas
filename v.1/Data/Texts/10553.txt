"Myanmar: Mass arbitrary arrests / prisoners of conscience 
 Myanmar: Mass arbitrary arrests / prisoners of conscience | Amnesty International 
 Some 220 National League for Democracy (NLD) activists have been arrested since 6 September 1998.  According to official sources, the activists, including 60 MPs-elect, were detained in order to prevent them from convening parliament, an act which the authorities claim is illegal.
