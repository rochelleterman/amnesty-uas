"Further information on UA 399/93 (AMR 36/32/93, 11 November) - Haiti: fear for safety / threats / harassment / possible extrajudicial execution: Emmanuel Laurent, Venel Remarias, Orelia Joseph 
 Further information on UA 399/93 (AMR 36/32/93, 11 November) - Haiti: fear for safety / threats / harassment / possible extrajudicial execution: Emmanuel Laurent, Venel Remarias, Orelia Joseph | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Amnesty International has been informed by sources in Haiti that, due to certain discrepancies in the testimony of Emmanuel Laurent, all appeals on behalf of him and Venel Remarais should cease. However appeals should continue on behalf of Orelia Joseph, the victim of a possible extrajudicial execution."
