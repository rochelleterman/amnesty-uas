"Further information on UA 386/93 (EUR 44/100/93, 3 November) - Turkey: fear of torture / fear of ""disappearance"": Olcay Kanlibas 
 Further information on UA 386/93 (EUR 44/100/93, 3 November) - Turkey: fear of torture / fear of ""disappearance"": Olcay Kanlibas | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Olcay Kanlibas was released by Malatya State Security Court on 5 November 1993. She was detained in Diyarbakir on 24 October 1993 and was transferred to Malatya Police Headquarters for interrogation. She was reportedly tortured during her detention."
