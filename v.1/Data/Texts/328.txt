"Burundi: Fear of torture or ill-treatment: Desire and up to 19 other men 
 Burundi: Fear of torture or ill-treatment: Desire and up to 19 other men | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is concerned that up to 20 men who were arrested on or around 31 October 1998 could be at risk of torture and ill-treatment in detention. All are believed to be members of a new Tutsi armed opposition group.
