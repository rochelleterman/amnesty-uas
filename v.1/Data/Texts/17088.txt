 Iran: Further information on fear for safety: Student demonstrators | Amnesty International 
 The student demonstrations in Tehran have continued and   there have been similar demonstrations in other cities.   Local residents have joined the students.  One death has  been confirmed in Tabriz, but Amnesty International has   received reports of at least five deaths.
