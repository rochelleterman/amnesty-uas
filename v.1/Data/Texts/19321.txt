 Yemen: Further Information on: Death by stoning and flogging | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International has learnt that Layla Radman 'A'esh was released from prison in March 2004 following a pardon by the Supreme Court on 14 March. Naji Hizam 'Abdullah was also released in the same month after receiving 100 lashes.
