 Morocco/Western Sahara: Health concerns for elderly prisoner: Kaddour Terhzaz | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Relatives of a 72-year-old Moroccan man serving a 12-year prison sentence for exercising his right to freedom of expression are concerned for his wellbeing, given his age and the medical conditions he suffers from. According to his son, he has been held in solitary confinement since the beginning of December 2009.  "
