 Nepal: Fear for safety/ possible ""disappearance"" | Amnesty International 
 Six named people were all reportedly arrested by security forces personnel at Deurali Village Development Committee (VDC) in Nawalparasi district, in two separate incidents on 18 and 23 February. The whereabouts of all six are now unknown, and Amnesty International is concerned that they may have ""disappeared"".
