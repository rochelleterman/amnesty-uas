"Democratic Republic of Congo: Unfair trials / death penalty / fear of imminent execution 
 Democratic Republic of Congo: Unfair trials / death penalty / fear of imminent execution | Amnesty International 
 Tshikombo Mandjika, Ngoie wa Mulango, Beya Mulumba, Mikobi Bope and Kasongo Dibwe were sentenced to death by the Cour d'ordre militaire (Military Order Court) in Lubumbashi, Katanga province, and face imminent execution. Leonard Fuka Unzola and three others are currently on trial.
