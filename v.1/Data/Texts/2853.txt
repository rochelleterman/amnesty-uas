"Sudan: Fear of torture or ill-treatment/death penalty 
 Sudan: Fear of torture or ill-treatment/death penalty | Amnesty International 
 Adel Nasir Ahmad Faraj, a Saudi Arabian national, was     reportedly arrested in Khartoum, Sudan, after a hijacking attempt on 15 October on board a Saudi Arabian airline.   Amnesty International fears that he is at risk of torture and may be sentenced to death after the authorities in    Sudan reportedly agreed to an extradition request from    Saudi Arabia.
