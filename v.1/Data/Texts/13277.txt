 UA 25/93 - Singapore: death penalty: Flor Contemplacion (includes correction) | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Flor Contemplacion, a 39-year-old Filipino maid, was sentenced to death by the High Court in Singapore on 29 January 1993 for the murder of another domestic worker and her employer's four-year-old son. Her case will now come before the Court of Criminal Appeal. "
