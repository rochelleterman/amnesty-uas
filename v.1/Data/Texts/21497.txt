 Russian Federation: Woman disappeared in the North Caucasus | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 On 22 December 2010, Zalina Elhoroeva was taken away by armed camouflaged men wearing balaclavas in the North Caucasus, Russia, according to two witnesses. Her family has filed complaints with law enforcement agencies, but Zalina has not been seen since, and her whereabouts are unknown. "
