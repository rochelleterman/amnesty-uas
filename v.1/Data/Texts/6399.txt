"UA 171/93 - Guatemala: possible extrajudicial execution: Mario Jose Colindres 
 UA 171/93 - Guatemala: possible extrajudicial execution: Mario Jose Colindres | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Mario Jose Colindres, a member of the Association of University Students, leader of the Association of Economic Science, a member of a theatre group from that faculty, and leader of Jornadas Populares, a group organizing medical assistance and consultancies was extrajudicially executed on 23 May 1993 in the department of Santa Rosa. According to information received Mario Jose Colindres was shot dead by two men on his way home from the village of Gabita, Santa Rosa, where he and other members of Jornadas Populares, had given a medical consultancy.
