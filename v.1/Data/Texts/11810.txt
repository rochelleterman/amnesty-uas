"Indonesia: Further information on fear of torture and ill-treatment: Wignyobin Mardi (previously named as Suwigno) 
 Indonesia: Further information on fear of torture and ill-treatment: Wignyobin Mardi (previously named as Suwigno) | Amnesty International 
 Wignyobin Mardi, arrested on 3 September 1996, remains in custody in Jakarta and is facing charges under Indonesia's Anti-Subversion law. Amnesty International is concerned that he may be a prisoner of conscience.
