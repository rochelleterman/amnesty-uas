"Argentina: Fear for safety: Lidia ABINEME 
 Argentina: Fear for safety: Lidia ABINEME | Amnesty International 
 Human rights activist Lidia Abineme was assaulted by      armed men on 19 November 1999. Amnesty International      believes she was attacked because she is pressing for a   thorough investigation into an assault on her son by      police officers, and is concerned for her safety.
