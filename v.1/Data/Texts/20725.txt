 USA (Washington): Further information on Death penalty: Darold J. Stenson (m) | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Darold Stenson has received a stay of execution. He was due to be executed in Washington State on 3 December. He has spent 14 years on death row for two murders committed in 1993 in Clallam County, in the west of the state."
