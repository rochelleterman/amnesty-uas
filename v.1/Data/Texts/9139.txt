"USA (North Carolina) : Further information on Death penalty: Zane Hill 
 USA (North Carolina) : Further information on Death penalty: Zane Hill | Amnesty International 
 Zane Hill was executed in North Carolina on 14 August 1998.  He was sentenced to death in 1991 for killing his 29-year-old son the previous year.
