"Ethiopia: Prisoners of conscience 
 Ethiopia: Prisoners of conscience | Amnesty International 
 Human rights defenders Daniel Bekele and Netsanet Demissie face possible sentences of life imprisonment on 22 November 2007. Amnesty International considers them prisoners of conscience, detained solely for their peaceful human rights activism.
