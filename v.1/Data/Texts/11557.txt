 Indonesia: Arbitrary Detention/Fear of torture or ill-treatment: Alipiu Maya Moniz | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is concerned for the safety of      Alipiu Maya Moniz, who was arrested by a ratih, civilian  militia, on 5 April 1999 in south-western East Timor. Hi  is at risk of torture and ill-treatment.
