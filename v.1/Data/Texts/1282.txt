"Liberia: Torture/Fear for safety, Tiawon Gongloe. 
 Liberia: Torture/Fear for safety, Tiawon Gongloe. | Amnesty International 
 Human rights lawyer Tiawon Gongloe was arrested on 24 April, and has reportedly been tortured. He is detained without charge, and Amnesty International believes he may be a prisoner of conscience, detained solely for his human rights work.
