"India: Fear for safety: Wahengbam Joykumar Singh 
 India: Fear for safety: Wahengbam Joykumar Singh | Amnesty International 
 Wahengbam Joykumar Singh, a community worker with the     United Voluntary Youth Council, has been forced to move   from his home after he was reportedly threatened with     death by a member of the Assam Rifles.   A complaint      concerning the threats was files with the Manipur State   Human Rights Commisison and the Manipur police was asked  to ensure Joykumar's personal safety.
