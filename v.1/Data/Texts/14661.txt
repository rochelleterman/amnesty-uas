 Hasan Ocak has not been seen since 21 March 1995, when he was allegedly detained by police in Istanbul. AI is investigating reports of several ""disappearances"" following the recent disturbances in the Gaziozmanpasa district of the city. There are fears for the safety of Hasan Ocak."
