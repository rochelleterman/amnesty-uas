"USA (Virginia): Further Information on: Death penalty - Bobby Wayne Swisher. 
 USA (Virginia): Further Information on: Death penalty - Bobby Wayne Swisher. | Amnesty International 
 On 1 July, Governor Mark Warner of Virginia granted Bobby Swisher a three-week reprieve from execution. Swisher had been due to be put to death at 9pm local time on 1 July for the murder of Dawn McNees Snyder in February 1997.
