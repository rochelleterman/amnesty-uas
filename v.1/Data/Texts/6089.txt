 Guatemala: Fear for safety | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is concerned for the safety of      members of FAMDEGUA (Asociacion de Familiares de los      Detenidos- Desaparecidos de Guatemala) who have been      harassed by soldiers while exhuming the bodies of those   killed in the 1982 massacre in San Francisco.
