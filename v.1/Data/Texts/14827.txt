"Turkey: Fear for safety / fear of ""disappearance"": A Gani Ucar, Kurd 
 Turkey: Fear for safety / fear of ""disappearance"": A Gani Ucar, Kurd | Amnesty International 
 Shortly after midnight on 12 August masked men and two village guards reportedly attacked houses in Van, eastern Turkey. During the raid they detained A Gani Ucar, at his home, and he has not been seen since. There is grave concern for his safety.
