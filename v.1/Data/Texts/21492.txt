 Russian Federation: Lawyer in Dagestan harassed by authorities: Sapiyat Magomedova | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 A lawyer from Dagestan, Russia, is facing criminal charges brought against her by members of law enforcement agencies, following her allegations that she was severely beaten by police. On 30 September, travel restrictions were issued preventing her from going to Moscow and getting the full legal and medical support she needs."
