"Guatemala: Further information on Fear for Safety/Death Threat -Thelma de Lam 
 Guatemala: Further information on Fear for Safety/Death Threat -Thelma de Lam | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is seriously concerned for the safety of Thelma de Lam, Fiscal Especial para los Defensores de los Derechos Humanos, Special Prosecutor for Human Rights Defenders, who has reportedly been followed and intimidated. Concerns are heightened by the fact that Ms. de Lam is pregnant and has been threatened in the past.
