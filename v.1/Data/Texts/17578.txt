"Iraq: Further information on incommunicado detention / fear of torture or ill-treatment: Mohammed Siyassi Ashkani (m)  
 Iraq: Further information on incommunicado detention / fear of torture or ill-treatment: Mohammed Siyassi Ashkani (m) 

 | Amnesty International 
 Mohammed Siyassi Ashkani, journalist, aged about 35, was released without charge on 19 July. He had been arrested  on 24 January, not 22 January as previously reported. A   Kurdish journalist from the province of Sulaimaniya in    northern Iraq, he is now known to have been accused of    spying for another political party and a foreign country.
