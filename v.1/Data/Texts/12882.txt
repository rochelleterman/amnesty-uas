"Pakistan: Fear of imminent execution/unfair trial: Mohammad Ameen 
 Pakistan: Fear of imminent execution/unfair trial: Mohammad Ameen | Amnesty International 
 Mohammad Ameen may be facing imminent execution. He was sentenced to death by an Anti Terrorism Court in the city of Rawalpindi on 31 January 2001 after being found guilty of involvement in a robbery and killing in Rawalpindi in 1998, when he was 17 years old.
