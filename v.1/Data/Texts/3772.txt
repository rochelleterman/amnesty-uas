"Brazil: Fear for safety 
 Brazil: Fear for safety | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is concerned for the safety of      Macuxi Indian communities in the Raposa/Serra do Sol      indigenous area of Roraima state. Tension is high in the  area following the shooting on 7 February 1999 of a       Macuxi Indian, and the stabbing of a priest working for a church indigenous agency on 10 February.
