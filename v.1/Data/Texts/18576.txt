"Syria: Further information on Torture and ill-treatment/Medical concern: Mu'eyna Muhammad Yusef Sa'adu 
 Syria: Further information on Torture and ill-treatment/Medical concern: Mu'eyna Muhammad Yusef Sa'adu | Amnesty International 
 On 19 February, Mu'eyna Muhammad Yusef Sa'adu was released without charge from the Military Intelligence Head Quarters in the capital, Damascus. She had been detained since 14 January following her arrest by Syrian military security officers as she re-entered the country, after 24 years as an exile in Jordan.
