"Bangladesh: Fear of torture/ill treatment/possible prisoners of conscience/legal concern 
 Bangladesh: Fear of torture/ill treatment/possible prisoners of conscience/legal concern | Amnesty International 
 Two journalists for the British television network,  Channel 4, were arrested on 25 November as they were      leaving Bangladesh for India. Their interpreter and       driver were reportedly arrested on the same day. All four are possible prisoners of conscience, detained for        exercising their right to freedom of expression. They are at risk of torture or ill treatment in detention.
