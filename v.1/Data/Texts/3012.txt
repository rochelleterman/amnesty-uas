"Sudan: Fear for safety/Fear of ""disappearance""/Abductions 
 Sudan: Fear for safety/Fear of ""disappearance""/Abductions | Amnesty International 
 Nineteen men have been abducted by the army and the government-backed Janjawid militia on 28 November, in western Sudan. Their whereabouts are now unknown, and Amnesty International fears that they may be killed or tortured, or may simply ""disappear"".
