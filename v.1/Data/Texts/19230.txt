"Tunisia: Further information on: legal concern / prisoner of conscience and new health concern: Khemais Chammari 
 Tunisia: Further information on: legal concern / prisoner of conscience and new health concern: Khemais Chammari | Amnesty International 
 Prisoner of conscience Khemais Chammari began a hunger-strike on 10 September 1996 to protest at the conditions of his detention. Amnesty International fears that his health may further deteriorate as a result of his hunger-strike.
