"Egypt: Further information on prisoner of conscience: Dr Ahmad al-Ahwany 
 Egypt: Further information on prisoner of conscience: Dr Ahmad al-Ahwany | Amnesty International 
 On 6 May 1997 the Public Prosecutor's Office ordered that Dr Ahmad al-Ahwany remain in detention for a further 15 days pending the outcome of continuing investigations. Amnesty International is continuing to call for his immediate and unconditional release as a prisoner of conscience.
