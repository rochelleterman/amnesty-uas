"Nepal: Further information on Fear for safety/possible ""disappearance"", Tej Narayan Sapkota 
 Nepal: Further information on Fear for safety/possible ""disappearance"", Tej Narayan Sapkota | Amnesty International 
 Tej Narayan Sapkota, who was reportedly arrested on 24 November 2003, is now believed to be held at the District Police Office at Hanuman Dhoka, Kathmandu. He is said to have been held at the Police Academy in Maharajganj, also in Kathmandu, before this.
