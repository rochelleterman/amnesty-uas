"Colombia: Further information on fear for safety: Civilian population of Remedios and Segovia, Antioquia 
 Colombia: Further information on fear for safety: Civilian population of Remedios and Segovia, Antioquia | Amnesty International 
 Paramilitary threats against residents of Segovia and Remedios continue, despite the announcement of new security measures and the increased presence of members of the security forces in the two communities. Segovia and Remedios have been declared Special Public Order Zones by decree, which has resulted in the imposition of an evening curfew and restrictions on freedom of movement.
