 Sudan: Fear of imminent execution: Four women, names unknown: | Amnesty International 
 AI is deeply concerned that four women are to be executed on 10 December 1997 in Khartoum. The women were sentenced to death on 25 Novembre 1997 for a third-time offence of prostitution and were given 15 days to appeal against their sentences. Their sentences were confirmed.
