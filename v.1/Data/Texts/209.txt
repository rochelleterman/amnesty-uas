 Burundi: Fear of torture or ill-treatment | Amnesty International 
 BAYUBAHE, head of Gatwe camp for the displaced, Antoine NZIGAMASABO, driver, Gerard NIYOGENDAKO, William KABURA Gerard BANKINTORE ,traders, were arrested in Bigina zone, Kayogoro commune, Makamba province on 29 June. They are at risk of torture or ill-treatment.
