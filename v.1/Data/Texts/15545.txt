"Russian Federation: Health concern / incommunicado detention: Viktor Fyodorovich Andreyev 
 Russian Federation: Health concern / incommunicado detention: Viktor Fyodorovich Andreyev | Amnesty International 
 Viktor Andreyev, who has been held for three years in detention in Moscow, is being deliberately denied medical treatment although he is reportedly near death. He is being held for the murder of his commanding officer who reportedly tortured him and other conscripts in the Russian army during the conflict in Chechnya.
