"Indonesia: Further information on incommunicado detention / fear of ill-treatment / health concern: Andi Arief 
 Indonesia: Further information on incommunicado detention / fear of ill-treatment / health concern: Andi Arief | Amnesty International 
 Andi Arief who was held in incommunicado detention since 28 March 1998 has been transferred to police headquarters in Jakarta and  has received a visit from a lawyer. There is still grave concern about his state of health.
