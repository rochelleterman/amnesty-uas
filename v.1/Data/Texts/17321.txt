"Iran: Further information: Fear of imminent execution 
 Iran: Further information: Fear of imminent execution | Amnesty International 
 Hajieh Esmailvand has been granted a stay of execution. She was reportedly due to be stoned to death on 21 December, for having an adulterous relationship with a 17-year-old boy, now named as Ruhollah G. He has been sentenced to death by hanging, and there is no further information about him.
