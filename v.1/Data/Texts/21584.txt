 Ukraine: Further information: Ukraine must abide by European Court decision | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 The Ukrainian authorities must abide by the decision of the European Court of Human Rights (ECtHR) taken on 18 March and suspend the deportation of four Afghan citizens from Ukraine.  They must ensure that all four men are provided with an effective opportunity to appeal against the earlier rejection of their asylum applications. "
