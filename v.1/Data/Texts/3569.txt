"Argentina: Fear for safety: Santo Biasatti 
 NA 
 There is grave concern for the safety of Santo Biasatti, a well-known radio and TV journalist, who has received anonymous death threats against himself and members of his family. The threats contained a reference to Jose Luis Cabezas, a press photographer, who was killed in Pinamar, Buenos Aires Province, on 25 January 1997.
