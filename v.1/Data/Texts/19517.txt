 Kenya: Death threats/Fear for safety: Wangari Maathai  | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Human rights defender Professor Wangari Maathai has received three death threats. Two people working for her also received similar threats. The threats were signed ""Mungiki"", the name of an outlawed gang mainly of Kikuyu ethnicity, that has claimed responsibility for beheadings and other murders involving mutilation. "
