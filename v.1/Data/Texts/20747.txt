 Venezuela: Father and daughter beaten by police: Daniel Antonio Núñez, Francis Daniela Núñez Martínez  | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Daniel Antonio Núñez and his daughter Francis Daniela Núñez Martínez were beaten and threatened by police officers in Caracas, the capital of Venezuela, on 30 January. There are concerns for their safety and that of their family."
