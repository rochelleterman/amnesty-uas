 DRC :    Further information on Arbitrary arrest / torture, Golden Misabiko Baholelwa - human rights defender and prisoner of conscience.

 | Amnesty International 
 Contrary to recent government statements that his release was imminent, human rights defender Golden Misabiko Baholelwa remains in detention at Kinshasa's central prison as a result of his human rights activism.
