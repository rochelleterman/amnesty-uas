 Morocco: Further information: Violent crackdown on protests in Morocco | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 In the last week, scores of protesters in Morocco have been physically assaulted by security forces. Nabil Talha, a student who was beaten and arrested on 22 May 2011, has been conditionally released. Another 21 protesters are still detained in Tangiers and face criminal charges. "
