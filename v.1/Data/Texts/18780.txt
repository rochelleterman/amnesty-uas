"EXTRA 103/93 - United Arab Emirates: executions: six men (names unknown) 
 EXTRA 103/93 - United Arab Emirates: executions: six men (names unknown) | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 AI is concerned about reports that six men were executed in the United Arab Emirates on 24 November 1993. No names were published but reports stated that the men were five Indian nationals and a Pakistani national. They had been convicted of murder."
