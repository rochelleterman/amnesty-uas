"Further information on EXTRA 72/93 (AMR 49/04/93, 23 August) - Trinidad and Tobago: death penalty: Michael Bullock, Irvin Phillips 
 Further information on EXTRA 72/93 (AMR 49/04/93, 23 August) - Trinidad and Tobago: death penalty: Michael Bullock, Irvin Phillips | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Michael Bullock and Irvin Phillips, who were scheduled to be executed on 24 August 1993, were granted temporary stays of execution on 23 August, pending the hearing of a constitutional motion filed on their behalf which is expected to be heard in October 1993. "
