 USA (Kentucky): Death penalty/Legal concern, Thomas Clyde Bowling | Amnesty International 
 Kentucky's Attorney General, Greg Stumbo, has asked Governor Ernie Fletcher to set an execution date of 16 November 2004 for Thomas Bowling, who was sentenced to death for a double murder committed in 1990. Governor Fletcher has this week been presented with a clemency petition.
