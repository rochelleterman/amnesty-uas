 Turkey: Further information on Medical Action: Harassment of Human Rights Foundation, Diyarbakir | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Following the raid on the office of the Human Rights      Foundation of Turkey (HRFT) in Diyarbakir on 7 September  2001, a trial has been opened against HRFT lawyer Sezgin  Tanrikulu (38-years-old) for establishing a medical       treatment and rehabilitation centre without seeking       permission from the relevant authorities. The first trial hearing has been scheduled for 19 March 2002."
