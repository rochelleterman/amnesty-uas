 UA 116/94 - Guatemala: death threats / harassment: Gustavo Eduardo Lapola, Victor Hugo Gudiel and other members of the Association of University Students | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 AI is concerned for the safety of the above-named membersof the Asociacion de Estudiantes Universitarios (AEU), based at the University of San Carlos in Guatemala City, following recent incidents of harassment and threats. On 10 March 1994 Gustavo Eduardo lapola was reportedly attacked by unknown men. Various members of the AEU leadership have reportedly received anonymous telephoned threats and have been photographed."
