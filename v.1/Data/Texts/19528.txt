 Liberia: Release: Hassan Bility, journalist and human rights defender: Further information on Medical Action. | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Following months of national and international pressure,  Hassan Bility was released by the Liberian government and handed over to United States (US) embassy officials on 7  December.  He has been flown out of the country to Accra, Ghana, and is expected to go from there to the US.  He is currently receiving some medical treatment in Accra for   injuries sustained as a result of torture and             ill-treatment during his detention."
