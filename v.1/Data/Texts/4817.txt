 Colombia: Further information on: possible extrajudicial execution / fear for safety | Amnesty International 
 Manuel Beltran, Alejandro Teheran, Dagoberto Santero, Julio Santero, Carlos Solano, Milecio de la Cruz, Saul Baltazar - killed, Guillermo Carmona - threatened, Rosember Clemente - threatened; and other Zenu Indigenous leaders: AI deplores the killing of another Zenu indigenous leader, Saul Baltazar, in the indigenous community of San Andres de Sotavento, department of Cordoba, in circumstances suggesting an extrajudicial execution. The killing of Saul Baltazar, regional director of the Colombian Indigenous Movement, heightens concern for the safety of Guillermo Carmona and Rosember Clemente.
