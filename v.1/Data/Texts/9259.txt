"USA (Nevada): Further information on Death penalty / Legal concern: Thomas Nevius 
 USA (Nevada): Further information on Death penalty / Legal concern: Thomas Nevius | Amnesty International 
 Governor Kenny Guinn has decided to seek further assessment of Thomas Nevius's mental abilities. The other eight members of the Pardons Board supported the governor's motion. The assessment is likely to take several months, and no execution date will be sought in the meantime.
