 Mexico: Ill-treatment/Fear for safety | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 On 9 August 2008, Remigio Hernández Arellano and José Gómez were arrested by police officers in the small town of Benemérito de las Américas, Chiapas state, for drinking alcohol in a park. Before being taken to the local police station, four of the arresting officers hit the men repeatedly in their faces and their stomachs. "
