"Pakistan: Further information on imminent Execution / Unfair trial: Mirza Tahir Hussain (m) 
 Pakistan: Further information on imminent Execution / Unfair trial: Mirza Tahir Hussain (m) | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International has received confirmation from Amjad Hussain that his brother Mirza Hussain's death sentence has been commuted by the President of Pakistan. Noting that Mirza had already spent 18 years in detention in Pakistan, which amounts to the duration of the sentence of life imprisonment, he has been released.
