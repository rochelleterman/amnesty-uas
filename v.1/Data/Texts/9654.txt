"USA (Texas): Further information on Death Penalty / Legal Concern - Shaka Sankofa, formerly Gary Graham 
 USA (Texas): Further information on Death Penalty / Legal Concern - Shaka Sankofa, formerly Gary Graham | Amnesty International 
 Shaka Sankofa, formerly Gary Graham, was executed on 22   June 2000 in Texas.  Gary Graham was 17 at the time of    the killing, rendering his execution illegal under        international law prohibiting the use of the death        penalty against those under 18 at the time of the crime.
