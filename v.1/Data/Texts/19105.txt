"Tunisia: Fear of torture or ill-treatment, Tarek Ferchichi. 
 Tunisia: Fear of torture or ill-treatment, Tarek Ferchichi. | Amnesty International 
 Tarek Ferchichi, who is being detained at the 9 avril prison in the capital Tunis, has now been seen by a lawyer. He has also reportedly been charged with spreading false information. At his request no information about his treatment in detention can be made public.
