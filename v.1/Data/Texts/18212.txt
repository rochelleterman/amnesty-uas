"Palestinian Authority: Further information on prisoner of conscience / legal concern: Fathi Subuh 
 Palestinian Authority: Further information on prisoner of conscience / legal concern: Fathi Subuh | Amnesty International 
 On 6 October 1997, the Palestinian High Court of Justice declined to order the release of Fathi Subuh, lecturer in education. He was visited by his lawyer on 9 October, he is in good health.
