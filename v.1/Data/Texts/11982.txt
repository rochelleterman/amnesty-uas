 Cambodia: Further information on fear of torture/fear for safety/fear of disappearance | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International has obtained more information on the number of people arrested during a violent crackdown on opposition protests in Phnom Penh after 7 September 1998. It is thought that possibly over 200 people were arrested and at least 20 killed.
