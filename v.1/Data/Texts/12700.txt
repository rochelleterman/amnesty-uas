 Nepal: Fear for safety / possible ""disappearance"" | Amnesty International 
 Indra Raj Pathak, Ramesh Dhungana and Ravindra Kumar Pudasani were reportedly arrested in and around Kathmandu between 12 and 16 March. The whereabouts of all three men remain unknown, and Amnesty International is concerned that they may have ""disappeared"".
