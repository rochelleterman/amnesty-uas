 Israel: Torture / judicial authorization of torture | Amnesty International 
 Suspected supporters of Islamic groups; Muhammad 'Abd al-'Aziz Hamdan, student: On 14 November 1996 the Israeli Supreme Court gave the General Security Service authorization to use ""increased physical pressure"" on detainee Muhammad 'Abd al-'Aziz Hamdan, a student of Bir Zeit University, said to be a member of Islamic Jihad and to have information about suicide bomb attacks against Israelis.
