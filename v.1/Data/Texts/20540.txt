 USA (Ohio): Further information: Governor grants reprieve from execution, Abdul Hamin Awkal | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 The Governor of Ohio has granted a two-week reprieve to a Lebanese national with mental illness who was facing execution on 6 June. The reprieve is to allow a court more time to examine the question of whether the inmate is “competent” for execution. "
