 Iran: Further Information on Fear of execution | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Juvenile offender Mohammad Reza Haddadi, who was scheduled to be executed on 9 October 2008, has had his execution postponed. Iman Hashemi was pardoned in September by the family of the man he was convicted of killing, and is now free.  There is no further news on Naser Qasemi, who was sentenced to death for a crime committed when he was 15."
