 USA (Virginia): Death penalty / Legal concern: Percy Levar Walton (m)

 | Amnesty International 
 Levar Walton is scheduled for execution in Virginia on 8 December 2006. He was sentenced to death in 1997 for the murders of an elderly white couple, Elizabeth and Jesse Hendrick, aged 81 and 80, and a 33-year-old black man, Archie Moore, in the town of Danville in November 1996.
