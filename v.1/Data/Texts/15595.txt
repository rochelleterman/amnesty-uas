"Russian Federation (Chechya):  Further information on Incommunicado detention / Fear of torture and ill-treatment - Adam Abubakarov 
 Russian Federation (Chechya):  Further information on Incommunicado detention / Fear of torture and ill-treatment - Adam Abubakarov | Amnesty International 
 As of 1 June 2000, sixteen-year-old Adam Abubakarov had   not been seen since his detention by Russian forces on 22 or 23 February 2000. His family believes that he is still being held in incommunicado detention at a prison         hospital, although they have not been   officially        informed of his whereabouts for over three months.
