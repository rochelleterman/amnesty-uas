"Yemen: Fear of imminent execution, Saleh Nasser Ahmed al-Assadi 
 Yemen: Fear of imminent execution, Saleh Nasser Ahmed     al-Assadi | Amnesty International 
 Saleh Nasser Ahmed al-Assadi is facing imminent execution, after his death sentence was approved by President Ali Abdullah Saleh. He is scheduled for execution on 28 April, according to his lawyer. The President still has the power to overturn the death sentence.
