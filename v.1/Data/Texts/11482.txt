"Indonesia: Fear of torture/ill-treatment 
 Indonesia: Fear of torture/ill-treatment | Amnesty International 
 Hassan Hamid, Mohammad Daud Abubakar, Ismail Syahputra, Raman Palu, Nijar Kandang, Tengku Affan, Inur Marlia (f): Six men and one woman, arrested in connection with alleged involvement in armed separatist activity in Aceh, north Sumatra, are currently being held incommunicado in military custody, where there is concern they may be at risk of torture or ill-treatment.
