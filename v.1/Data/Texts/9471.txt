 USA (Georgia): death penalty: Larry Lonchar | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Larry Lonchar is scheduled to be executed in Georgia on 23 June 1995. He was sentenced to death in 1987 for the murders of three people. He is reportedly severely mentally disturbed. An accomplice in the crime was convicted in a separate trials and imprisoned for life."
