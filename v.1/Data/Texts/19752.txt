 Democratic Republic Of Congo (DRC): Death threats/ Fear for safety | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Lawyers representing four men who are appealing against their death sentence and trial observers from local and international NGOs have received a series of anonymous threats in the city of Bukavu, eastern DRC. The threats, which include death threats, began on 17 April 2008 and are mainly being sent as anonymous mobile phone text messages."
