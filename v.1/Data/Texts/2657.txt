"Further information on UA 33/93 (AFR 54/07/93, 10 February) - Sudan: fear of torture / legal concern: Mohamed Abdulsid 
 Further information on UA 33/93 (AFR 54/07/93, 10 February) - Sudan: fear of torture / legal concern: Mohamed Abdulsid | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Mohamed Abdulsid, Khartoum correspondent of the newspaper Asharq al-Awsat, was released on 22 March 1993. It is not known if his release in unconditional: a criminal case has reportedly been filed against him but no further details of any charges have been made public. "
