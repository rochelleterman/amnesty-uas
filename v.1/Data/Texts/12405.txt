"Republic of Maldives: Further information on possible prisoners of conscience / health concern: Ahamed Shafeeq, Ali Moosa Didi, Mohamed Latheef 
 Republic of Maldives: Further information on possible prisoners of conscience / health concern: Ahamed Shafeeq, Ali Moosa Didi, Mohamed Latheef | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 AI has been informed that Mohammed Latheef was transferred from Male police headquarters to house arrest in mid-October. There is continuing grave concern, particularly given his age, over his state of health. AI continues to be concerned about Ahamed Shafeeq and Ali Mossa Didi who remain under house arrest."
