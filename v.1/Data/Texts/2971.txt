"SUDAN: Further information on Fear for safety/fear of torture or ill-treatment / incommunicado detention - Elhadi Tangur (m) 
 SUDAN: Further information on Fear for safety/fear of torture or ill-treatment / incommunicado detention - Elhadi Tangur (m) | Amnesty International 
 Elhadi Tangur was reportedly released from the political section of Kober prison in Khartoum some time between 22 July and 5 August. He had been detained incommunicado for over a month since his arrest on 16 June and had not been charged with any offence.
