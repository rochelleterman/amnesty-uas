 Ecuador: Further information on Fear for safety/Incommunicado detention/possible prisoners of conscience	   	    	  	

 | Amnesty International 
  
 Amnesty International has been informed by INREDH, a      non-governmental human rights organization in the         capital, Quito, that the eight environmentalists, Luis    Muñoz, David Conrado, Juan Pablo Barragán, Jose Luis      Proaño, Alberto Saltos, Stalin Saltos, Ivon Ramos and     Julia Butterfly, have all been released. Julia Butterfly, a US citizen, has been deported to the USA. Amnesty       International is investigating the terms of their         release."
