"Further information on UA 175/94 (MDE 12/09/94, 3 May) - Egypt: death following torture: 'Abd al-Haris Mohammad Madani 
 Further information on UA 175/94 (MDE 12/09/94, 3 May) - Egypt: death following torture: 'Abd al-Haris Mohammad Madani | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 'Abd al-Haris Mohammad Madani reportedly died on 6 May 1994 apparently following torture. He was arrested on 26 April in Cairo and was taken to Qasr al-'Aini hospital after being severely tortured at the Giza branch of the State Security Intelligence (SSI). It is not clear whether he died in hospital or in police custody."
