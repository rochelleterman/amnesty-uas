"UA 39/94 - Bosnia-Herzegovina: deliberate and arbitrary killings / fear for safety: Shelling of civilians queuing for aid in the besieged town of Sarajevo 
 UA 39/94 - Bosnia-Herzegovina: deliberate and arbitrary killings / fear for safety: Shelling of civilians queuing for aid in the besieged town of Sarajevo | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 According to recent press reports, nine people including three children, were killed on 4 February 1994 when shells hit a group of civilians waiting for the distribution of humanitarian aid, in the Dobrinja suburb of Sarajevo. 15 people were wounded in the incident. Bosnian Serbs are alleged to have been responsible for the attack, although they have denied any involvement."
