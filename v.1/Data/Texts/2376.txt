"Further information on UA 412/93 (AFR 53/42/93, 22 November) - South Africa: ""disappearance"" / fear for physical safety: Clare Stewart 
 Further information on UA 412/93 (AFR 53/42/93, 22 November) - South Africa: ""disappearance"" / fear for physical safety: Clare Stewart | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 The body of Clare Stewart, a 34-year-old development worker was found on 24 November 1993. She had been abducted on 10 November. A post mortem is to be performed in Durban and final confirmation of identity and cause of death is expected sometime next week. There are no reports of any arrests in connection with Clare Stewart's abduction and murder."
