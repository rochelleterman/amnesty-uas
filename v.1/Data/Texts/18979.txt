"Morocco: Death penalty: Mohamed Ben Abdellah 
 Morocco: Death penalty: Mohamed Ben Abdellah | Amnesty International 
 On 26 November 1997 Mohamed Ben Abdellah was sentenced to death by a court in Oujda in eastern Morocco. He can now appeal for his case to be reviewed by the Supreme Court.
