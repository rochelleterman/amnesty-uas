 Indonesia: Incommunicado detention / fear of torture / ill-treatment | Amnesty International 
 T Jamaluddin, alias Bit; Muzarni and Adnan: The three men, arrested on 26 February 1998, appear to be held in incommunicado military detention and could be at risk of torture or ill-treatment. They were arrested apparently in connection with allegations of criminal activity.
