"UA 370/92 - Equatorial Guinea: death penalty: Norberto Ndong Abia 
 UA 370/92 - Equatorial Guinea: death penalty: Norberto Ndong Abia | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Norberto Ndong Abia, a member of the Presidential Security Guard, was sentenced to death by a military court in Bata on 23 or 24 November 1992. He had been convicted of shooting dead 22-year-old Pedro Ateba during an argument on 26 October 1992. AI does not have details of the trial. AI is opposed to the use of the death penalty and is calling for the sentence to be commuted. "
