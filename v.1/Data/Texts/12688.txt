"Nepal: Further information on: Fear for safety/ possible ""disappearance"", Hari Prasad Acharya 
 Nepal: Further information on: Fear for safety/ possible ""disappearance"", Hari Prasad Acharya | Amnesty International 
 According to his family Hari Prasad Acharya is still missing following his reported arrest by a group of about 20 uniformed soldiers on 6 November 2003. They are extremely concerned for his safety and some fear he may be dead.
