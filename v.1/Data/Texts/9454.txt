 USA (Illinois): Death penalty: Raymond Lee Stewart | Amnesty International 
 Raymond Lee Stewart, black, is scheduled to be executed on 17 September 1996 in Illinois. He was sentenced to death in 1981 and in 1982 received a second death sentence.
