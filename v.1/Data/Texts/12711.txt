"Nepal: Fear for safety/fear of ""disappearance"" 
 Nepal: Fear for safety/fear of ""disappearance"" | Amnesty International 
 Five named people were reportedly arrested by armed security forces personnel in Janakpur Municipality, Dhanusha district, on 8 October. Their whereabouts are unknown and there are fears for their safety. The five young men were arrested at around 2pm in the Kataiya Chowri residential area of Janakpur, reportedly on suspicion of being involved in Maoist activities.
