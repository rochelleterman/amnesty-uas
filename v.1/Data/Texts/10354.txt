"Bangladesh: Fear of imminent execution 
 Bangladesh: Fear of imminent execution | Amnesty International 
 Six men (Abdur Rahman, Siddiqul Islam Bangla Bhai, Ataur Rahman Sunny, Abdul Awal, Khaled Saifullah and Iftekhar Mamun), who are members of the Islamist group Jama'atul Mujahideen Bangladesh (JMB), are facing imminent execution, convicted of involvement in a November 2005 suicide bomb attack which killed two judges in the southern city of Jhalakathi.
