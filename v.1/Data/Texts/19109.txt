"Further information on UA 277/93 (MDE 30/08/93, 18 August; and follow-up MDE 30/10/93, 27 August) - Tunisia: fear of torture: Samir Moussa, and new name: Salaheddine Zikikout 
 Further information on UA 277/93 (MDE 30/08/93, 18 August; and follow-up MDE 30/10/93, 27 August) - Tunisia: fear of torture: Samir Moussa, and new name: Salaheddine Zikikout | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Salaheddine Zikikout, a 30-year-old student at the Conservatoire national des arts et metiers (National Conservatory for Arts and Crafts) in Paris, was arrested on 8 August 1993 upon arrival at the port in Tunis, and is being held in illegally prolonged incommunicado detention. Samir Moussa, arrested on 25 July, continues to be held in illegally prolonged incommunicado detention. Despite repeated attempts by his family, the Tunisian authorities have not revealed his whereabouts. "
