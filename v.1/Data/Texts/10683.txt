"People's Republic of China: death penalty: Pu Pinliang, Shen Jian, Zhang Chunming 
 People's Republic of China: death penalty: Pu Pinliang, Shen Jian, Zhang Chunming | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 The above named were sentenced to death on 1 March 1995 by the Suzhou Municipal Intermediate People's Court at Taicang Courthouse. Zhang Chunming was sentenced with a two-year reprieve. They were convicted of corruption. It is not known if they have appealed against their sentences."
