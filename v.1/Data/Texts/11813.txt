 Indonesia: Possible ""disappearances"" / fear for safety | Amnesty International 
 Antonio da Silva Monteiro (29), Paulo da Costa (17), Armindo da Silva (31), Armindo Monteiro (22), Jose Monteiro (31), Jose da Silva, Chiquito da Silva, Aquiles da Silva, Gilberto da Silva and one other (name unknown): Amnesty International is seriously concerned for the safety of 10 East Timorese men who have not been seen since military operations took place in their village on 15 November 1996. Six of the men were reportedly arrested at their houses and taken to a military post at Buanurak hamlet.
