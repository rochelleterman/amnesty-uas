"Peru: Further information on death penalty: proposed extension of death penalty laws 
 Peru: Further information on death penalty: proposed extension of death penalty laws | Amnesty International 
 In an encouraging development, a draft bill to put into effect the death penalty for terrorist offences was voted down by Congress by a majority of 49 to 26 on 10 January. However, three other bills that would widen the reach of the death penalty are still before Congress.
