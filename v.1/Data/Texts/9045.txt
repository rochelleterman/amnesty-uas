"Further information on EXTRA 31/94 (AMR 51/40/94, 20 May) - USA (Texas): death penalty: Willie Williams, Robert West 
 Further information on EXTRA 31/94 (AMR 51/40/94, 20 May) - USA (Texas): death penalty: Willie Williams, Robert West | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Willie Williams, who was scheduled to be executed in Texas on 24 May, has been granted a stay of execution. The reason for the stay is unknown. Robert West is still scheduled to be executed on 7 June 1994."
