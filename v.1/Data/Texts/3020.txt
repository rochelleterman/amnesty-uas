 Swaziland: Fear for safety: Jan Sithole, trade union leader | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Jan Sithole, the secretary general of the Swaziland Federation of Trade Unions (SFTU), has received anonymous death threats in July 1995. These threats come in the context of increasing tension between the government and trade unions. AI is disturbed by a number of incidents in the past year, indicating that Jan Sithole's life is in danger."
