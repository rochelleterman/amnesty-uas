 Sudan: Rights activist detained, at risk of torture: Bushra Gamar Hussein Rahma | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Sudanese human rights activist, Bushra Gamar Hussein Rahma, is being detained in an unknown location. He was arrested on 25 June in Omdurman, Sudan, and has no access to his family or a lawyer. He is at risk of torture or other ill-treatment."
