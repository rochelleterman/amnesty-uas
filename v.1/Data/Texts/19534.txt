 Mauritania: Incommunicado detention /Fear of torture or ill-treatment | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Mohamed Ould Semane, Mohamed Khouna Ould Brahim Salem and at least 30 others are being held incommunicado at unknown locations and are at risk of torture or ill-treatment. They are all believed to be detained in relation to attacks which the authorities blame on terrorists."
