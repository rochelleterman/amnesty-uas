 Mexico: Further Information on Fear for safety | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Environmental activists and members of a coalition protesting against the activities of a mining company in the municipality of Cerro San Pedro, San Luis Potosí state, have been threatened and attacked. On 16 July 2008 a group of activists and members of Frente Amplio Opositor demonstrated and briefly blocked the access road to the mine’s office. According to demonstrators, a municipal police van attempted to run over two protesters. "
