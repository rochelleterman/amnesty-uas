 Afghanistan: Death Penalty: Sayed Perwiz Kambakhsh | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 University student and journalist Perwiz Kambakhsh was sentenced to death on a charge of blasphemy on 22 January 2008 in the northern city of Mazar-e-Sharif in Balkh province. Before the trial began, local religious leaders had called for him to be executed for causing offence to Islam. His trial took place in a closed session of the provincial lower court and he had no legal representation. "
