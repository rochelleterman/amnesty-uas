"UA 442/94 - Tunisia: fear of torture / ill-treatment: Jalel Maalej 
 UA 442/94 - Tunisia: fear of torture / ill-treatment: Jalel Maalej | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Jalel Maalej, a Tunisian academic living in Paris, was arrested on 4 December 1994 upon his arrival in Tunisia from France. AI fears that he may have been arrested on suspicion of political activities and may be a prisoner of conscience. He is being held incommunicado without access to his lawyer or family and is at risk of torture or ill-treatment."
