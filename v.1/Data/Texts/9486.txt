"USA: Further information on: Fear of torture or ill-treatment/Incommunicado Detention, Ahmed Abu 'Ali 
 USA: Further information on: Fear of torture or ill-treatment/Incommunicado Detention, Ahmed Abu 'Ali | Amnesty International 
 Further evidence has emerged which suggests that Ahmed Abu 'Ali was tortured and ill-treated while detained in Saudi Arabia, with the knowledge of US authorities. In addition, the conditions in which he is currently held in the USA may amount to cruel, inhuman or degrading treatment. Amnesty International is concerned for his health.
