 Nepal: Fear for safety/possible ""disappearance"" | Amnesty International 
 Three named people were reportedly arrested by plain clothed members of the security forces from different locations in Kathmandu and Lalitpur, between 27 August and 23 September. Their whereabouts are unknown and they may have ""disappeared"". Amnesty International is concerned for their safety.
