 Egypt: Further information on death penalty / legal concern | Amnesty International 
 Sabir Farahat Abu al-'Ala and Mahmoud Farahat Abu al-'Ala: The death sentences passed by the Supreme Military Court in Cairo on 30 October 1997 against the two brothers named above have been confirmed by the Military Appeals Bureau and ratified by the President of the Republic.
