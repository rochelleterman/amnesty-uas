"USA: Death penalty / legal concern: Anthony Porter 
 USA: Death penalty / legal concern: Anthony Porter | Amnesty International 
 Anthony Porter is scheduled to be executed on 23 September 1998 for a double murder committed in 1982, despite having an IQ of 51.  By this measurement, he would be the most severely mentally retarded inmate to be put to death in the USA since executions resumed there in 1977.
