"USA (Ohio): Further information on Rocky Barton (m) 
 USA (Ohio): Further information on Rocky Barton (m) | Amnesty International 
 Rocky Barton was executed in Ohio on 12 July 2006. He was sentenced to death for the murder of his wife, Kimbirli Barton. He had given up his appeals against his conviction and sentence.
