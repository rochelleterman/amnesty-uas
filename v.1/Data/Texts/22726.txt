 Syria: Two Syrians at risk after deportation: Hassan Baroudi, Kutiba al-Issa  | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Two Syrian men who were deported to Syria from Malaysia have not been seen since their arrival in the Syrian capital, Damascus, in mid-March. They may have been subjected to enforced disappearance and are in grave danger of torture and other ill-treatment."
