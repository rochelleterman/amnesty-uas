 Russian Federation: Extrajudicial execution, torture, ill-treatment, disappearance, arbitrary detention | Amnesty International 
 Russian forces surrounded the village of Alkhan Khala, Chechen Republic, near the Chechen capital Grozny, on 20 July and the village remains cut off. Amnesty International is concerned for the safety of the civilian population who may be facing torture and ""disappearance"".
