 USA (Texas): Death penalty, Emerson Edward Rudd | Amnesty International 
 Emerson Rudd is scheduled to be executed in Texas on 15 November 2001. He was sentenced to death in 1989 for a murder committed in 1988. At the time of the murder he had just turned 18.
