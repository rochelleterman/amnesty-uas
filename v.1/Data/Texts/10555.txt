 Myanmar: Fear of ill-treatment of torture: Ye Htut (revised version) | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 AI fears that Ye Htut, reportedly arrested on 27 September by military intelligence personnel, may be facing ill-treatment or torture in prison. According to state-run newspapers he faces charges of ""concocting news stories"" about Myanmar and sending them to dissident Burmese groups abroad. His place of detention is not known."
