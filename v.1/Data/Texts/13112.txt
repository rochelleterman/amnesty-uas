"UA 144/93 - Philippines: possible extrajudicial execution: Exquito Lasquite 
 UA 144/93 - Philippines: possible extrajudicial execution: Exquito Lasquite | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Amnesty International is concerned at the killing of Exquito Lasquite, a 33-year-old local coordinator of the National Federation of Sugar Workers (NFSW) on 17 April 1993. Members of a Citizen Armed Forces Geographical Unit (CAFGU), a government-backed militia, are believed to have been responsible. "
