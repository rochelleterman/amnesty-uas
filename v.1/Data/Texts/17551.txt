 Iraq: Death penalty/Fear of imminent execution | Amnesty International 
 Ahmad al-Jaf, 'Uday Dawud al-Dulaimi, Jasim 'Abbas and Tahsin 'Ali Mattar have been sentenced to death in two separate trials. If the death sentences are carried out, the men may be the first people to be executed by the new Iraqi government.
