"Turkey: Further information on fear of torture: Serpil Polat (f) 
 Turkey: Further information on fear of torture: Serpil Polat (f) | Amnesty International 
 On 14 January 1998 Serpil Polat was formally arrested and committed to prison in Bingol to await trial. No information has been available so far regarding the charges and her treatment in police custody.
