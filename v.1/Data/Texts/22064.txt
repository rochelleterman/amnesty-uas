 Iran: Further information on prisoner of conscience: Maryam Malek (f) | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Maryam Malek was released on 29 April 2009 after five days in detention. Her bail had been set at 200 million rials (about US$20,000), but her family could not afford this amount. She was released instead with a third party guaranteeing her bail. She is likely to face trial in the future."
