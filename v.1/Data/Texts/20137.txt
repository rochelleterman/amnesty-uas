 Haiti: The consequences of impunity: attack on Judge Napela Saintil | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Judge Napela Saintil was severely beaten in Port-au-Prince on the evening of 30 March 2004. His attacker also reportedly threatened him for the part he played in convicting Louis Jodel Chamblain in the trial of 1994 Raboteau massacre, in which an estimated 20 people were killed."
