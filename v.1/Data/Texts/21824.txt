 Bahrain: Further information: Bahraini activist remains in prison: Nabeel Rajab | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Prominent Bahraini human rights activist, Nabeel Rajab, was acquitted of the charge of libel by the Appeals Court in Manama on 23 August 2012. He remains in prison serving a three-year prison sentence for taking part in anti-government protests. He is a prisoner of conscience."
