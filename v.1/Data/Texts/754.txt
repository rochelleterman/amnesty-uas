 Ethiopia:  Fear for safety Opposition demonstrators | Amnesty International 
 Opposition parties are planning a major demonstration in the capital, Addis Ababa, on 10 February. Police have previously used excessive force to break up demonstrations and Amnesty International is concerned for the safety of the demonstrators.
