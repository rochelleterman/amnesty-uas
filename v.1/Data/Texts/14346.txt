"Turkey: Further information on Arbitrary detention/fair trial concern/ ill-treatment 
 Turkey: Further information on Arbitrary detention/fair trial concern/ ill-treatment | Amnesty International 
 The six Kurdish children were released from prison after a trial hearing on 15 February. However, they and seven of the children arrested with them are facing charges under which they could be imprisoned for several years. If convicted, Amnesty International would consider them prisoners of conscience.
