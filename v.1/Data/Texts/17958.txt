"Jordan: Further information on Death penalty: Fateh Suba'i 
 Jordan: Further information on Death penalty: Fateh Suba'i | Amnesty International 
 Fateh Suba'i was executed by hanging at dawn on 12 August 1998. He had been sentenced to death after being found guilty of murdering two adults and their child in June 1997.
