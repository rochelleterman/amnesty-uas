 USA: Condemned man's mental health in question: Jonathan Marcus Green | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Jonathan Marcus Green, a 42-year-old man, is due to be executed in the US state of Texas on 30 June. He was sentenced to death in 2002 for the murder of a 12-year-old girl in 2000. His lawyers have claimed that he suffers from mental illness and possible ‘mental retardation’."
