 Democratic Republic of Congo: Prisoner of conscience/ill-treatment/detention without charge, Donatien Kisangani Mukatamwina (known as Kisangani) | Amnesty International 
 Human rights defender Donatien Kisangani Mukatamwina, known as Kisangani, is being held without charge by the armed political group, the Rassemblement congolais pour la d_mocratie (RCD-Goma), Congolese Rally for Democracy. He has allegedly been ill-treated in detention. Amnesty International believes he is a prisoner of conscience, detained solely on account of his human rights activities.
