"China: Further information on Possible disappearance/fear for safety, Dr Wan Yanhai (m), aged 38 
 China: Further information on Possible disappearance/fear for safety, Dr Wan Yanhai (m), aged 38 | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International has received reports suggesting that Wan Yanhai is being detained on suspicion of ""leaking state secrets"", apparently in connection with the distribution on the internet of a government report containing information about the spread of AIDS in Henan province.
