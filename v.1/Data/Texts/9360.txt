"USA: Further information on: Prisoner of conscience: Abdullah William Webster 
 USA: Further information on: Prisoner of conscience: Abdullah William Webster | Amnesty International 
 Abdullah Webster was released from prison on 30 April. His release, on grounds of ""good conduct"", comes three months before the completion of his 14-month sentence. He is back at home with his family in the USA and has been able to spend time with his wife and two-year-old daughter.
