"Turkmenistan: Fear for safety / death in custody 
 Turkmenistan: Fear for safety / death in custody | Amnesty International 
 Khudayberdi Amandurdyyev and Charymyrat Gurov: Khudayberdi Amandurdyyev, who is in the third year of a five-year prison sentence imposed after he was involved in an anti-government demonstration on 12 July 1995, is reportedly suffering constant beatings in prison. Charymyrat Gurov, one of his co-defendants, died in prison at the beginning of January 1998 following sustained beatings. AI is seriously concerned for the safety of Khudayberdi Amandurdyyev.
