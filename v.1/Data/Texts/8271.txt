"UA 283/92 - Peru: ""disappearance"": Ana Rosario Celis Laureano 
 UA 283/92 - Peru: ""disappearance"": Ana Rosario Celis Laureano | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Amnesty International is concerned at the reported ""disappearance"" of 16- year-old Ana Rosario Celis Laureano from Huacho, province of Huacha, Lima department on 13 August 1992, after being taken from her grandfather's house at 1 a.m. by four hooded men, one of whom was seen wearing army uniform and boots.
