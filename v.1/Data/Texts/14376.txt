"Turkey: Fear for Safety 
 Turkey: Fear for Safety | Amnesty International 
 Eight members of the Kurdish party HADEP (People's Democracy Party) were arrested by gendarmes on 1 and 2 April and Amnesty International is concerned that they may be tortured in custody. They are held at rnak Provincial Gendarmerie Headquarters, in southeastern Turkey.
