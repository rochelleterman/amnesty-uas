 Francisco Abarca Verales, Florencia Abarca (F) aged 10, Bernardo Sanchez Cruz, Catholic priest, human rights defender and several others: Members of an indigenous community in Guerrero State raided by the Mexican army on 1 September 1997, and members of a local human rights organization who have filed a complaint about the raid, are feared to be at risk of reprisals by the military.
