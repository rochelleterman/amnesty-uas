"UA 431/94 - Colombia: fear for safety / possible ""disappearance"": Gerardo Estrada 
 UA 431/94 - Colombia: fear for safety / possible ""disappearance"": Gerardo Estrada | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Gerardo Estrada was abducted on 5 December 1994 by armed men from his home in Tuquerres municipality, Narino department. His whereabouts are unknown and there is serious concern for his safety. He is a member of the governing committee of the Indigenous Authorities of Colombia movement, Junta Directiva del Movimiento de Autoridades Indigenas de Colombia (AICO), and was recently elected to the departmental municipal assembly."
