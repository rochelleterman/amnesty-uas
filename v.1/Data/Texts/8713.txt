 USA (Georgia): Further information on Death penalty, Ronald Keith Spivey, white, aged 62 | Amnesty International 
 Ronald Spivey was put to death by lethal injection in Georgia after more than two decades on death row. He had been sentenced to death in 1977 for the murder of Billy Watson, an off-duty police officer.
