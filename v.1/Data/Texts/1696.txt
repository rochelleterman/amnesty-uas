"Nigeria: Possible excessive use of force / fear for safety / fear of ill-treatment 
 Nigeria: Possible excessive use of force / fear for safety / fear of ill-treatment | Amnesty International 
 Some 100 activists were arrested by security forces a pro-democracy rally called by the United Action for Democracy in Nigeria.  While in detention they are at risk of ill-treatment. At least two protesters were killed and many others seriously injured.  Some 100 other protesters were carried away to an undisclosed location.
