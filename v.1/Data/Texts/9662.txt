"USA (Virginia): Further Information on: Death penalty - Bobby Wayne Swisher. 
 USA (Virginia): Further Information on: Death penalty - Bobby Wayne Swisher. | Amnesty International 
 Bobby Swisher was executed on 22 July 2003 in Virginia after the state Supreme Court refused to hear his final appeal and Governor Warner refused to intervene. Bobby Swisher was sentenced to death for the murder of Dawn McNees Snyder in February 1997.
