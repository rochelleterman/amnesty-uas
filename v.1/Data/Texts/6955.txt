"EXTRA 50/94 - Haiti: legal concern / ill-treatment: Gardy Le Blanc 
 EXTRA 50/94 - Haiti: legal concern / ill-treatment: Gardy Le Blanc | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Gardy Le Blanc, an engineer who was employed in public works under former president Aristide, was arrested on 17 August 1994. He has reportedly been beaten in detention. He remains in custody but apparently has not been formally charged."
