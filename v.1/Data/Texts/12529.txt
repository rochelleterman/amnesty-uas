"Nepal: Further information on torture: Keshav Timilsina. 
 Nepal: Further information on torture: Keshav Timilsina. | Amnesty International 
 Lawyers were permitted to visit Keshav Timilsina on 20 May and accompanied him to hospital for a medical examination. Since his hospital visit, he has reportedly not been subjected to any further torture or ill-treatment.
