"Chile: Further information on Death threats 
 Chile: Further information on Death threats | Amnesty International 
 Sola Sierra Henriquez (f), Viviana Diaz Caro (f), Mariana Guzman Nunez (f): No further threats against these members of the Association of Relatives of ""Disappeared"" Prisoners have been reported to Amnesty International. Those affected by the threats in early June 1997 were granted police protection.
