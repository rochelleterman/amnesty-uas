"Indonesia: Fear for safety, Martinus Daisiwa (21) 
 Indonesia: Fear for safety, Martinus Daisiwa (21) | Amnesty International 
 Martinus Daisiwa has been tortured and then shot in the leg by police in Papua. He is now in hospital, reportedly handcuffed to his bed, and he may be at risk of further torture or ill-treatment. He has not been charged.
