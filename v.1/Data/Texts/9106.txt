"USA (Georgia): Further information on Death penalty / Legal concern - Larry Eugene Moon 
 USA (Georgia): Further information on Death penalty / Legal concern - Larry Eugene Moon | Amnesty International 
 Larry Eugene Moon was executed in Georgia on 25 March 2003. He was sentenced to death in Catoosa County in January 1988 for the murder of Ricky Callahan in November 1984.
