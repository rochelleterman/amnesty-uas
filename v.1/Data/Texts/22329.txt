 Iran: Further Information on Arbitrary arrest/Fear of Torture: Negin Sheikholeslami (f) | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Negin Sheikholeslami was released from Section 209 of Evin Prison in the capital, Tehran, on bail of 500,000,000 rials, equivalent to almost US$50,000, on 23 November 2008. During her detention, Negin Sheikholeslami was able to meet with family members twice."
