 Colombia: torture / fear for safety | Amnesty International 
 Jaime Hernandez Diaz, oil worker, Orlando Hernandez, his brother: there are serious concerns for the safety of the above.  On 1 March 1996, members of a paramilitary group reportedly tortured Orlando Hernandez and stated that they were searching for Jaime Hernandez.
