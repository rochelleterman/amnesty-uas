"USA: Death penalty / legal concern : Tyrone Gilliam 
 USA: Death penalty / legal concern : Tyrone Gilliam | Amnesty International 
 Tyrone Gilliam, black, is scheduled to be executed sometime during the week beginning 16 November 1998. According to information received by Amnesty International, Gilliam did not receive adequate legal representation during his trial and was seriously ill when he confessed to the murder.
