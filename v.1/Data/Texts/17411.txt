"Iran: Prisoner of Conscience/Fear of torture or ill-treatment/Medical concern: Saleh Kamrani (m) 
 Iran: Prisoner of Conscience/Fear of torture or ill-treatment/Medical concern: Saleh Kamrani (m) | Amnesty International 
 Human rights defender Saleh Kamrani, aged about 35, was arrested on 18 August, and is held in Section 209 of Evin Prison in Tehran, run by the Ministry of Intelligence. He is at risk of torture or ill-treatment. He needs regular medication for a heart condition.
