 It is reported that in July 1995 specialists of the Institute of Forensic Medicine in Pristina carried out an autopsy on Shefki Latifi, who died on 4 July 1995 following alleged ill-treatment in Podujevo police station. The results of the autopsy are reportedly due to be made known in September."
