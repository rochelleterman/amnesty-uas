"USA: Further Information on Death Penalty, Robert A. Buell. 
 USA: Further Information on Death Penalty, Robert A. Buell. | Amnesty International 
 Robert Buell was executed in Ohio on the morning of 25    September, local time. He had been on death row for 18    years, having been sentenced to death in 1984 for the     sexual assault and murder of 11-year-old Krista Harrison  in 1982.
