"USA: Further information on death penalty / legal concern: Andrew Kokoraleis 
 USA: Further information on death penalty / legal concern: Andrew Kokoraleis | Amnesty International 
 Andrew Kokoraleis was executed by lethal injection in the early hours of 17 March 1999. He had been sentenced to    death in 1987 for the rape, aggravated kidnapping and     murder of Lorraine Borowski in Chicago in 1982.
