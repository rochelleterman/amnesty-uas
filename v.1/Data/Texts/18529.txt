"Syria: Further information on fear of torture / medical concern / possible prisoner of conscience: Nouh Rasul Mustafa 
 Syria: Further information on fear of torture / medical concern / possible prisoner of conscience: Nouh Rasul Mustafa | Amnesty International 
 Nouh Rasul Mustafa, a disabled Syrian Kurd, was released on 8 May 1997. While detained, he was reportedly beaten and tortured and threatened that he would be killed if his brother Nasreddin Mustafa did not report to the security forces.
