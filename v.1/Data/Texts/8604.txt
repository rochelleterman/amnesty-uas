 Further information on EXTRA 97/93 (AMR 51/95/93, 8 November; and follow-up AMR 51/100/93, 6 December) - USA (Tennessee): death penalty: Wayne Bates | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Wayne Bates, who was scheduled to be executed in Tennessee on 28 February 1994 after abandoning his appeals, has decided to resume those appeals, and will not now be executed on 28 February."
