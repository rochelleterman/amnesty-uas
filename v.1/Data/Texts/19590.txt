 Zimbabwe: Torture and ill-treatment/ /harassment | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Activists from the organisation Women of Zimbabwe Arise (WOZA), including WOZA leaders Jenni Williams and Magadonga Mahlangu were arrested on 28 May 2008 at a peaceful demonstration in the Zimbabwean capital, Harare. They are in grave danger of torture or other ill-treatment and are being held under harsh prison conditions."
