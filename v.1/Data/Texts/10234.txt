"Venezuela: Fear for safety / fear of ill-treatment: Inmates at El Dorado prison 
 Venezuela: Fear for safety / fear of ill-treatment: Inmates at El Dorado prison | Amnesty International 
 Violence in El Dorado prison on 28 August 1997 is reported to have left 29 inmates dead and three wounded. Apparently one group of inmates led a violent attack against another group. This latest incident suggests that conditions and prisoners' human rights are deteriorating in Venezuelan prisons.
