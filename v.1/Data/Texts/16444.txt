"Federal Republic of Yugoslavia: Ill-treatment / excessive use of force 
 Federal Republic of Yugoslavia: Ill-treatment / excessive use of force | Amnesty International 
 Ethnic Albanians in Kosovo Province: Following reports of large-scale ill-treatment and excessive use of force by police during mass demonstrations in several towns in Kosovo Province on 9 March 1998, there is serious concern that ethnic Albanians and others who participate in forthcoming demonstrations, such as the one planned for 13 March, may also be at risk of police violence.
