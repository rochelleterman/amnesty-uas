"Kenya: Further information on illegal detention / fear for safety / fear of refoulement / health concern: Dima Noggo 
 Kenya: Further information on illegal detention / fear for safety / fear of refoulement / health concern: Dima Noggo | Amnesty International 
 Dima Noggo who was held incommunicado since his arrest in Nairobi on 16 April 1998 has been released and allowed to return to his family in Germany. During his detention he fell ill but was denied medical treatment for some days.
