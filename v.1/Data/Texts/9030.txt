 USA (Indiana): death penalty: Tommie J. Smith (aka Ziyon Yisrayah) | Amnesty International 
 The above, a black man, is scheduled to be executed in Indiana on 14 June 1996. He was one of two men convicted of the murder of a white police officer in Indianapolis in 1980. His co-defendant, Gregory Resnover, also black, was executed in 1994.
