"Nigeria: Fear of ill-treatment / health concern / possible prisoners of conscience: 
 Nigeria: Fear of ill-treatment / health concern / possible prisoners of conscience: | Amnesty International 
 Kemi Sobodu (f), Dr Wale Balogun, Dr Beko Ransome-Kuti and Mr Oboro: The three people named above appear to have been arrested solely on account of their association with a poster campaign calling for the release of prisoners of conscience. They are being held incommunicado, possibly in harsh conditions and AI fears for the health of Mr Oboro.
