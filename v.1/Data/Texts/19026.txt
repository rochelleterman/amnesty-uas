"Tunisia: Further information on fear for safety: Radhia Nasraoui 
 Tunisia: Further information on fear for safety: Radhia Nasraoui | Amnesty International 
 On 10 February 1999, Radhia Nasraoui was  charged in a    local court with failing to comply with the authorities'  ruling banning her from leaving the capital. She received a 15 days' suspended sentence and had to pay a small      fine.
