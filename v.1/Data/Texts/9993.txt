"USA (Georgia): Further information on Death penalty / Legal concern, Jose Martinez High 
 USA (Georgia): Further information on Death penalty / Legal concern, Jose Martinez High | Amnesty International 
 Jose Martinez High was executed in Georgia on 6 November 2001 for the murder of 11-year-old Bonnie Bulloch in July 1976. He had been on death row since 1978 - well over half his life. He had a history of serious mental illness and had also been assessed as having borderline mental retardation.
