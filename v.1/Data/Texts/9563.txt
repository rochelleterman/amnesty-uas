"USA (Arizona): Further information on death penalty: Luis Mata 
 USA (Arizona): Further information on death penalty: Luis Mata | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Luis Mata has received a stay of execution from Arizona Supreme Court. The stay was granted partly on the grounds that he received poor legal representation at an earlier sentencing. These arguments and others are due to be heard before the Court on 11 October 1995."
