 Egypt: Fear of execution / fear of torture | Amnesty International 
 Ahmad Ibrahim al-Sayyid al-Naggar is at imminent risk of execution after being forcibly returned to Egypt by the Albanian authorities along with Mohammad Huda, Mohammad Hassan Mahmoud and Magid Mostafa. He was sentenced to death in absentia in October 1997.
