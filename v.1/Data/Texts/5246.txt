"Colombia: Further information on fear for safety 
 Colombia: Further information on fear for safety | Amnesty International 
 There are fears for the safety of Betty Vallejo, who worked closely with Father Alcides Jimenez who was murdered on 1 September 1998. It has been reported that two gunmen were looking for her on the day of the murder.
