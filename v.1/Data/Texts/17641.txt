"Israel/OT: Fear for Safety: Palestinians in Nablus and Jenin. 
 Israel/OT: Fear for Safety:  Palestinians in Nablus and Jenin. | Amnesty International 
 The Israel Defence Forces (IDF) have been attacking Palestinian refugee camps in Nablus and Jenin on West Bank. Three Palestinians have reportedly been killed and 124 injured, 20 of them seriously in Balata Refugee Camp near Nablus. Seven Palestinians were killed and seven injured in Jenin refugee camp. Amnesty International is seriously concerned for the safety of Palestinians in these camps.
