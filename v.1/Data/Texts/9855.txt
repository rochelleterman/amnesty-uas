"USA (Georgia): Death penalty / Legal concern, James Willie Brown 
 USA (Georgia): Death penalty / Legal concern, James Willie Brown | Amnesty International 
 James Willie Brown is scheduled for execution in Georgia on 4 November. He was sentenced to death at a retrial in 1990 for the rape and murder of Brenda Watson in 1975. He has a clemency hearing in front of the state parole board on 31 October.
