"UA 397/93 - Iran: fear of torture: Ghobad Veysi, Ali Soleimani, Jalal Mohammad-Rezaie, Abbas Feyzi, Moussa Maleki 
 UA 397/93 - Iran: fear of torture: Ghobad Veysi, Ali Soleimani, Jalal Mohammad-Rezaie, Abbas Feyzi, Moussa Maleki | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 The above five men were reportedly detained near Sanandaj, in Iranian Kurdistan, on or around 21 October 1993. All five are alleged members of an illegal Kurdish political organization, Komala. It is believed that they are being held incommunicado in Sanandaj, and may be subjected to torture."
