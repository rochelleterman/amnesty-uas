 Indonesia: Further information on imminent execution: Karta Cahyadi, alias Yongki, 32, married with children | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Karta Cahyadi was executed early on the morning of 26 April in Kali Sari village, East Jakarta. He had been found guilty of robbery and murder and sentenced to death in September 1990 in the Surakarta District Court, Central Java, together with Tugiman bin Sikin, whose fate is unknown."
