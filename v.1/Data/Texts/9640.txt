"USA (Texas): Further information on death penalty / legal concern: Cathy Lynn Henderson (f) 
 USA (Texas): Further information on death penalty / legal concern: Cathy Lynn Henderson (f) | Amnesty International 
 On 11 June, the Texas Court of Criminal Appeals issued an indefinite stay of execution for Cathy Henderson, aged    50, and     remanded the case to the trial court for      consideration of newly available evidence that she is     innocent of capital  murder. She was sentenced to death   in May 1995 for the    murder of a                        three-and-a-half-month-old baby, Brandon      Baugh, in   January 1994. She was scheduled to be executed  in Texas  on 13 June 2007.
