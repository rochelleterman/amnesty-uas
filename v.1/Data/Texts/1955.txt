 Rwanda: Extrajudicial executions / fear for safety: Several thousand unarmed civilians in a cave | Amnesty International 
 Soldiers of the Rwandese Patriotic Army reportedly sealed off a cave where they are reported to have killed between 5,000 and 8,000 civilians.  The cave remains sealed and it is not known if anyone is still alive.  Amnesty International has written to the government .
