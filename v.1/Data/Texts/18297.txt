"Saudi Arabia: Further information on fear of imminent execution 
 Saudi Arabia: Further information on fear of imminent execution | Amnesty International 
 Hadi Sa'eed Al-Muteef, aged 31, has reportedly attempted suicide twice in eight days, by swallowing nails. It is not clear where the nails came from. He was hospitalised on both occasions, but then returned to solitary confinement. He reportedly suffers from depression and other mental illness.
