"Uzbekistan: Fear of imminent execution: Rinat Nazipov 
 Uzbekistan: Fear of imminent execution: Rinat Nazipov | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Rinat Nazipov, aged 27, was sentenced to death by Tashkent City Court on 23 June 1994 for premeditated, aggravated murder. The Presidium of the Supreme Court turned down his appeal on 5 September 1994. A petition for clemency has been lodged with the President of Uzbekistan."
