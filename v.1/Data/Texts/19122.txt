 Tunisia: Further information on: fear of ill-treatment or torture / probable prisoner of conscience: Frej Fenniche | Amnesty International 
 The above was released from incommunicado detention on 14 May 1996. He was allegedly charged with possessing documents deemed to be damaging to national interests, disturbing public order and insulting the authorities. He is free pending the completion of the judicial inquiry.
