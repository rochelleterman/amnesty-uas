"Indonesia: Fear for safety/ Intimidation, human rights 
 Indonesia: Fear for safety/ Intimidation, human rights | Amnesty International 
 Activists working with the Institute for Human Rights     Study and Advocacy (ELS-HAM) in Papua Province and        Jakarta have been subjected to intimidation and           harassment. In the past, activists involved in            investigating and publicizing human rights cases have     received death threats, been accused of defamation and    summoned for questioning by the police.
