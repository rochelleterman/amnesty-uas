 Iran: Prisoner at risk of torture and ill-treatment: Abdollah Momeni | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 There has been no news of male Iranian prisoner of conscience Abdollah Momeni since his family visited him at Evin Prison in the capital, Tehran, on 8 August 2009, a month after his arrest. After she saw him, his wife expressed concerned about his health as he had lost weight, was barely able to walk and appeared disorientated. He remains at risk of torture and other ill-treatment."
