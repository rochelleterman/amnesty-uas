"Palestinian Authority areas: Death penalty: Tha'er Fares 
 Palestinian Authority areas: Death penalty: Tha'er Fares | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Tha'er Muhammad Fares, a Palestinian policeman, was sentenced to death on 2 May by a Palestinian Military Court in Gaza after being convicted of murder, without the right of appeal. This is the first death sentence imposed since the Palestinian Authority was set up in May 1994."
