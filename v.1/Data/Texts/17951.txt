 Jordan: Incommunicado detention / Legal concern / Fear of torture, Ahmad Hikmat Shakir | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International is concerned for the safety of Iraqi citizen Ahmad Hikmat Shakir, who is being held by the Jordanian General Intelligence Department at an unknown detention centre. He is held incommunicado detention and is at risk of torture or ill-treatment.
