"USA (Alabama): Further information on Death penalty / legal concern: Pernell Ford 
 USA (Alabama): Further information on Death penalty / legal concern: Pernell Ford | Amnesty International 
 Pernell Ford was executed in Alabama's electric chair in  the first few minutes of 2 June 2000.  Ford, who had      suffered from serious mental health problems and suicidal tendencies since he was a child, had given up his         appeals.
