"Angola: Extrajudicial executions - fear of further killings 
 Angola: Extrajudicial executions - fear of further killings | Amnesty International 
 Following the reported recent extrajudicial execution, by government soldiers, of at least eight villagers in the Miconje district in the north of Cabinda, there are fears that such killings will continue unless the government takes immediate remedial and preventative action.
