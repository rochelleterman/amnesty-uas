 USA (Georgia): Further information on death penalty/ legal concern:  Troy Anthony Davis  | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 On 12 September 2008, the Georgia Board of Pardons and Paroles voted to deny clemency to Troy Davis. He remains scheduled to be executed at 7pm local time on 23 September. He has been on death row for 17 years for a murder he maintains he did not commit. "
