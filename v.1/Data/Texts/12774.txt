"Nepal: Further Information on Fear for safety/ possible ""disappearance"", Hari Prasad Acharya 
 Nepal: Further Information on Fear for safety/ possible ""disappearance"", Hari Prasad Acharya | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International remains very concerned for the safety of Hari Prasad Acharya. There are unconfirmed reports that he is being held at Jagdal Ghan, Chhauni Army Barracks in Kathmandu. He was arrested on 6 November 2003, at his home in Dhading district and his family were afraid that he was dead as they had not heard any news about his whereabouts.
