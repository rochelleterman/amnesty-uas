 Guatemala: Fear for safety | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 A member of a trade union has been killed in the village of El Semillero in the department of Escuintla, some 150 kilometres from Guatemala City. Several of his colleagues are believed to have been followed by unidentified men, and in one case escaped a possible attack. Amnesty International believes their lives are in serious and imminent danger. "
