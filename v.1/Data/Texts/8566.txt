"USA (Texas): Further information on death penalty: Larry Keith Robison 
 USA (Texas): Further information on death penalty: Larry Keith Robison | Amnesty International 
 Larry Robison was executed in Texas on 21 January 2000.   He had been sentenced to death for the murder of Bruce    Gardner in 1982. Several years before the crime he had    been diagnosed as suffering from paranoid schizophrenia.
