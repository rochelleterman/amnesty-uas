"Cameroon: Further information on fear of torture and ill-treatment: three students 
 Cameroon: Further information on fear of torture and ill-treatment: three students | Amnesty International 
 Roger Alexis Wamba, Armand Mbe, and Blaise Ngoune were conditionally released from detention on 22 November 1996. Two other students arrested with them, Alfred Ondona and Zefac Tonang, were also subsequently released.
