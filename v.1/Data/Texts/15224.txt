"Turkey: Further information on fear of torture / fear of ""disappearance"": M. Hanefi Isik 
 Turkey: Further information on fear of torture / fear of ""disappearance"": M. Hanefi Isik | Amnesty International 
 On 14 August 1996 the family of M. Hanefi Isik, a 46-year-old teacher and member of Turkish Human Rights Association, was informed that he was being held at Diyarbakir Police Headquarters. As M. Hanefii Isik had been detained on 8 August, the Turkish authorities failed to inform his family of his whereabouts for five days.
