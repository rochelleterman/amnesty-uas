"UA 202/93 - Kuwait: death penalty: Walid Jassem Mahdi 
 UA 202/93 - Kuwait: death penalty: Walid Jassem Mahdi | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 On 19 June 1993 Kuwait's State Security Court sentenced to death an Iraqi national charged with ""collaboration"" with Iraqi forces during their occupation of Kuwait. It is the seventeenth death sentence passed by the State Security Court this month. "
