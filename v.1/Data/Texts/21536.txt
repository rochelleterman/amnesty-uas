 Belarus: Further information: Journalist released on bail still faces prison: Andrzej Poczobut  | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 Andrzej Poczobut was released on bail on 30 June in Belarus, however he still faces a prison sentence of almost eight years on charges of “libelling the President”. He has been targeted for his work as a journalist and for the legitimate exercise of his right to freedom of expression. "
