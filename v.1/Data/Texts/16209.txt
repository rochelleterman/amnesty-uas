"Uzbekistan: Further Information on Fear of imminent execution/torture and ill-treatment 
 Uzbekistan: Further Information on Fear of imminent execution/torture and ill-treatment | Amnesty International 
 Amnesty International has learned that the death sentence of Ilkhom Karimov has been commuted to 20 years' imprisonment. However, the death sentences of Azamat Uteev and Evgeny Gugnin were upheld by the Supreme Court. Their death sentences could be carried out at any time.
