"Indonesia: Fear for safety 
 Indonesia: Fear for safety | Amnesty International 
 There is concern for the safety of at least 17 people,    including Yacomina Isir (f), and possibly as many as 44   people, who were arrested by police in Sorong, Irian      Jaya, during disturbances which followed the break-up of  a flag-raising ceremony on 5 July 1999.
