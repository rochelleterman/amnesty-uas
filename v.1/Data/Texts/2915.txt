 Sudan: Fear for safety /fear of torture or ill-treatment/ incommunicado detention | Amnesty International 
 Four named men from the Nuba ethnic group have been detained incommunicado by security forces in the town of Dongola, Northern Sudan, since 19 May. Amnesty International is gravely concerned for their safety after a fifth man arrested with them was reportedly tortured to death by security forces.
