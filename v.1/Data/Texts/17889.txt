"Jordan: Death penalty: Fateh Suba'i 
 Jordan: Death penalty: Fateh Suba'i | Amnesty International 
 This Syrian national was sentenced to death by the Amman Criminal Court on 21 January 1998 after being found guilty of murdering two adults and their child. The death sentence must now be ratified by the Court of Cassation and King Hussein bin Talal before it can be carried out. AI asks for the commutation of the death sentence.
