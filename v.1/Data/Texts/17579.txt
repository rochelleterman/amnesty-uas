"Iraq: Further information on Death Penalty 
 Iraq: Further information on Death Penalty | Amnesty International 
 Three men ('Ali Hassan al-Majid, Sultan Hashim Ahmad      al-Ta'I and Hussain Rashid al-Tikriti), all senior        officials under the government of Saddam Hussain, who     were sentenced to death on 24 June 2007, had their        appeals rejected by the Supreme Iraqi Criminal Tribunal   (SICT) on 4 September. They face execution within 30 days of this ruling. They cannot now be pardoned by the        President, who has the power of clemency for prisoners    condemned by other courts
