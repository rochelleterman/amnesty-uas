"Yemen: Death penalty / legal concern: 3 soldiers (names unknown) 
 Yemen: Death penalty / legal concern: 3 soldiers (names unknown) | Amnesty International 
 Three members of Yemen's armed forces are reported to have been sentenced to death after a trial which Amnesty International fears may have violated internationally recognized standards for fair trial. The three men were convicted of killing six civilians and injuring two others.
