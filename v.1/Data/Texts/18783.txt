"United Arab Emirates (UAE): Further information on death penalty: Touran Ibrahim Abbas (f) 
 United Arab Emirates (UAE): Further information on death penalty: Touran Ibrahim Abbas (f) | Amnesty International 
 The Supreme Court in Dubai has upheld the death sentence against Touran Ibrahim Abbas for drug trafficking. It will now be passed to the President for ratification. An appeal court has also upheld the death sentence against Qamar Ali Khan, also for drug trafficking.
