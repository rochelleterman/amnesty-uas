"EXTRA 71/94 - Belize: death penalty: Alfred Codrington, Lindsberth Logan, Nicolas Antonio Guevara 
 EXTRA 71/94 - Belize: death penalty: Alfred Codrington, Lindsberth Logan, Nicolas Antonio Guevara | Amnesty International 
 NA 
 On 25 November 1994 the Attorney General of Belize announced that the three men named above would be hanged on 9 December 1994. All men had been sentenced to death for murder, which carries a mandatory death penalty. If carried out, these would be the first executions in Belize for more than nine years."
